#### Decision points

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – branch after the Dual Underwriter Split:
   *If Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B scores differ by ≤ 2 ⇒ continue;
   else ⇒ escalate to Harmonisation Committee for tie-break.*

2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – after Neighbourhood Feedback Check:
   *If amount < €15 000 ⇒ system auto-approves;
   if amount ≥ €15 000 ⇒ send to Final Micro-loan Board Approval.*

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – the board votes **Approve** or **Reject**.

#### Required documents (complete list)

1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months)
3. Latest Income Statement (pay-stub/tax slip)
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS)
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

*(No credit bureau report, collateral forms, or other standard loan documents are needed.)*

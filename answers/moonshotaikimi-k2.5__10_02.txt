**Key**  
- **R** = Responsible (performs the work)  
- **A** = Accountable (owns the decision / outcome)  
- **C** = Consulted (provides input)  
- **I** = Informed (receives notification)  
- **R / A** = Both Responsible and Accountable for that task  

*Vocabulary notes:*  
- “KYC Review” is used for the due‑diligence checks described in the source text.  
- “Regulatory Liaison” replaces the former “compliance officer” role.  
- “Transaction Analyst” replaces the former “payment clerk” / “clerk” role.

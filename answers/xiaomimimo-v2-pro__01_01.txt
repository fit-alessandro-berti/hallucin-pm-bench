Based on the Contoso Airlines process description provided, here is a summary of the decision points and all required documents.

### Decision Points

1.  **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility:** The triage agent decides if the claim is valid by checking the submitted form against two documents: the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.
2.  **Finance_Controller_PreApprove:** The finance controller makes the approval decision using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
3.  **High-Value Threshold Check:** This is an implicit decision point after pre-approval. The process checks if the refund amount is greater than €1,000 to determine if the case must proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.

### Required Documents (Artefacts)

*   **RefundRequestForm:** Completed by the customer to start the claim.
*   **FlightManifest & WeatherReport:** Used by the triage agent to validate eligibility.
*   **EligibilityChecklist:** Used by the finance controller for pre-approval.
*   **AuditTrail:** Required *only* for claims over €1,000 as part of the **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
*   **BankTransferAuthorization:** Must be present to execute the payment in **Process_Refund_Payment**.
*   **RefundConfirmationPDF:** Issued and emailed to the passenger as the final step.

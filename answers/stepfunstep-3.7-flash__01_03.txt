### a) Decision Points (one-sentence rule each)
1. **Post Tri-Color-Risk-Check conditional branch (Step 4):** The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review step and proceeds directly to the TriVote gate if the Tri-Color-Risk-Check outputs a Low or Medium risk rating *and* the applicant’s requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000; if either condition is unmet, the process moves to Manual-Underwriting-Review before proceeding to the TriVote gate.
2. **TriVote Decision-Gate (Step 6):** The loan application passes this gate if at least two of the three mandatory approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) provide formal approval, with fewer than two approvals resulting in application rejection.

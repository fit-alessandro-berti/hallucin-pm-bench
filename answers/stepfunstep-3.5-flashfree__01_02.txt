### Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Decision on whether Senior and Shadow Underwriter risk scores differ by ≤ 2 points.  
2. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** – Verification that at least 3 local references have been collected.  
3. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Determination if the loan amount is < €15 000 (auto-approve) or ≥ €15 000 (escalate to Board).  
4. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – Board vote to approve or reject applications ≥ €15 000.  

### Required Documents at Submission Time:
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

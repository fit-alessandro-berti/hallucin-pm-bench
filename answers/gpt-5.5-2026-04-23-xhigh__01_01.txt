## Decision points in Contoso’s refund process

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - **Activity:** `Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility`
   - **Decision:** Is the claim eligible based on the `FlightManifest` and `WeatherReport`?
   - The process description does not define any separate rejection or appeal activity.

2. **Finance pre-approval**
   - **Activity:** `Finance_Controller_PreApprove`
   - **Decision:** Has finance pre-approval succeeded using the `EligibilityChecklist`?
   - `Process_Refund_Payment` may not begin unless this has succeeded.

3. **High-value refund branch**
   - **Condition:** Is the refund amount **greater than €1,000**?
   - **If yes:** The case must proceed to `Senior_Auditor_Review`, including the `AuditTrail`.
   - **If no:** The case does not require `Senior_Auditor_Review`.

4. **Payment readiness**
   - **Activity affected:** `Process_Refund_Payment`
   - **Decision/gate:** Can payment begin?
   - Payment may begin only when:
     - `Finance_Controller_PreApprove` has succeeded;
     - for high-value claims, `Senior_Auditor_Review` has been completed;
     - the signed `BankTransferAuthorization` is present.

## Required documents and artefacts

- `RefundRequestForm`
- `FlightManifest`
- `WeatherReport`
- `EligibilityChecklist`
- `AuditTrail` — required for high-value claims over €1,000
- Signed `BankTransferAuthorization`
- `RefundConfirmationPDF`

No `CustomerService_Agent_Decide` activity or related artefact is part of Contoso’s process.

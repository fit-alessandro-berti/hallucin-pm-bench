**Decision points:**
- Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility checks the claim against the FlightManifest and the WeatherReport.
- Finance_Controller_PreApprove: finance controller signs off using an EligibilityChecklist (must succeed before Process_Refund_Payment).
- High-value branch: if the refund amount is greater than €1 000, the case proceeds to Senior_Auditor_Review (which must include the AuditTrail).
- Process_Refund_Payment may begin only once Finance_Controller_PreApprove has succeeded (and Senior_Auditor_Review completed for high-value claims) and the signed BankTransferAuthorization is present.

**All required documents:**
- RefundRequestForm
- FlightManifest
- WeatherReport
- EligibilityChecklist
- AuditTrail (for high-value Senior_Auditor_Review)
- BankTransferAuthorization (must be signed)
- RefundConfirmationPDF

(No other activities or artefacts, including the legacy CustomerService_Agent_Decide, are part of the defined process.)

### Decision Points
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**:
   - If risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points, proceed to the next process step
   - If scores differ by > 2 points, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision before continuing
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**:
   - For loan amounts < €15 000: The system auto-approves the application
   - For loan amounts ≥ €15 000: Proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The board votes to either approve or reject applications for loan amounts ≥ €15 000

---

### Required Documents
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)


**a) Decision Points Summary**

*   **Underwriting Skip Condition:** If the application’s risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is €250,000 or less, the process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review step.
*   **TriVote Decision Gate:** A loan passes the Decision-Gate if it receives approvals from at least two of the three required officers: Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer.

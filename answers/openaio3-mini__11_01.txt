Executive Memo – Process Improvement Recommendations

After analyzing Orion Insurance’s process snapshot, the three worst-performing activities by waiting time are:

1. Request_Documents – Avg: 36.5h; 95th percentile: 120.0h  
 Action: Streamline the document request process via automated, pre-qualified document portals to reduce delays by ≥20%.

2. Send_Closure_Letter – Avg: 12.2h; 95th percentile: 44.0h  
 Action: Implement an automated letter generation and distribution system to cut waiting time by at least 20%.

3. Assess_Liability – Avg: 7.8h; 95th percentile: 16.4h  
 Action: Introduce decision-support tools and data analytics to expedite liability assessment, expecting a ≥20% reduction in wait time.

These targeted, data-driven initiatives will enhance process efficiency and accelerate claim resolution.

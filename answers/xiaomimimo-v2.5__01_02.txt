**Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**

1.  **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: After scoring by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B, the process continues only if the two risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points**. Otherwise, it is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision.
2.  **Amount Threshold Decision**: The application is auto-approved if the loan amount is **< €15,000**. If the amount is **≥ €15,000**, it requires **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

**Q1. No.**

After **T1**, the marking is **{P1, P2}**.  
To enable **T5**, both **P2** and **P5** must hold tokens. The only way to produce a token in **P5** is **T4**, and **T4** requires a token in **P4**. Before **T3** fires, the only possible route to **P4** is **T2** then **T7**, but **T7** consumes **P3** and puts a token in **P4**. Then **T4** consumes **P4** and puts a token in **P5**. So just before **T5**, the marking can be **{P2, P5}**, apparently without using **T3**.

Thus **T5 can fire before T3**.

**Minimal sequence:** **[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]**

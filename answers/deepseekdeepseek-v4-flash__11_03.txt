MEMORANDUM TO: Chief Operating Officer, Orion Credit  
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
SUBJECT: Worst-Performing Activities and Recommended Actions  

Data from the process table identifies three activities as the worst performers based on SLA breaches and high waiting time.  
- **Legal Approval**: exceeds SLA by 300 seconds (900 vs. 600) with 600 seconds of waiting time.  
- **Risk Review**: exceeds SLA by 300 seconds (600 vs. 300) with 480 seconds of waiting time.  
- **Credit Assessment**: exceeds SLA by 60 seconds (300 vs. 240) with 200 seconds of waiting time.  

These activities affect 2,120 combined cases, with waiting time constituting 66–80% of total throughput, indicating severe bottlenecks.  

Recommended data-driven actions:  
- For Legal Approval: Implement a standardised clause library for recurring documents to reduce manual review, targeting the 600-second waiting time.  
- For Risk Review: Deploy automated risk-scoring for low-risk applications (e.g., using historical acceptance patterns) to bypass manual review, directly reducing the 480-second wait.  
- For Credit Assessment: Add one dedicated analyst during peak hours, as the 1,175-case volume and 66% wait ratio suggest a capacity shortfall.

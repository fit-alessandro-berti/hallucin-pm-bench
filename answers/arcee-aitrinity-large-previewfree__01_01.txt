Decision Points:
1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** — Determines whether the claim is valid by cross-checking the *RefundRequestForm* against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.
2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** — Finance controller uses the *EligibilityChecklist* to decide if the refund can be pre-approved.
3. **High-value branch** — Triggered when the refund amount is greater than €1 000; requires the case to proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review** and include the *AuditTrail*.

Required Documents:
- *RefundRequestForm*
- *FlightManifest*
- *WeatherReport*
- *EligibilityChecklist*
- *AuditTrail* (for claims > €1 000)
- *BankTransferAuthorization*
- *RefundConfirmationPDF*

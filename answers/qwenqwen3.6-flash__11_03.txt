Executive Memo
To: Chief Operating Officer
Subject: Pipeline Bottleneck Remediation

Orion’s application pipeline shows three activities jointly exhibiting the most severe SLA breaches and prolonged wait times. Legal Approval exceeds its target by 300 seconds, posting a 900-second throughput duration with 600 seconds of average waiting. Risk Review surpasses its 300-second target by 300 seconds, accumulating 480 seconds of waiting against 600 seconds of total duration. Credit Assessment breaches its 240-second target by 60 seconds, marked by 200 seconds of waiting within its 300-second cycle.

Prioritized remediation actions:
• Legal Approval: Redirect specialist capacity to address the 600-second waiting period and compress the 900-second execution window.
• Risk Review: Augment staffing levels to reduce the 480-second queue and align the 600-second throughput time.
• Credit Assessment: Implement automated data checks to minimize the 200-second idle interval and stabilize the 300-second processing pace.

### Part (a): Decision Points (Rules in One Sentence Each)
There are two formal decision points in the process:
1.  **Conditional Post-Risk Check Branch:** The Manual Underwriting Review step is skipped if and only if the Tri-Color-Risk-Check assigns a Low or Medium risk rating *and* the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000; otherwise, the application proceeds to Manual Underwriting Review.
2.  **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):** The loan application passes this gate if at least two of the three mandatory approving roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant their approval.

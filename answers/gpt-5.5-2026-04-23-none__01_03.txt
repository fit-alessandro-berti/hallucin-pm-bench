**a) Decision points**

1. **Conditional skip after Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** and the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, Acme skips **Manual-Underwriting-Review**; otherwise, the application goes to manual underwriting.  
2. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”:** The loan passes the gate if **at least two of the three** required participants—Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer—approve.

Decision points:

- Quick KYC Verification (KYC): ID card and mobile phone confirmed; no credit history checks.  
- Community Impact Assessment (CIA): Impact Officer evaluation.  
- Dual Underwriter Split: Senior Underwriter A score (0–9) and Shadow Underwriter B score (0–9).  
- Underwriter Alignment Gate: if scores differ by ≤ 2 points, continue; else escalate to Harmonisation Committee for tie-break.  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC): obtain at least 3 local references.  
- Amount Threshold Decision: amount < €15 000 → auto-approve; amount ≥ €15 000 → proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).  
- Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA): board votes approve or reject.

Required documents at submission time:

▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

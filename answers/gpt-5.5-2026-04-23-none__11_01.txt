**Executive memo — waiting-time priorities**

Ranked strictly by average waiting time, the three worst-performing activities are:

1. **Request_Documents** — Avg waiting: **36.5h**; 95th percentile: **120.0h**.  
   **Action:** Auto-send document requests immediately after screening and trigger reminders at 24h/48h for missing items; target ≥20% reduction by eliminating manual handoff delays.

2. **Send_Closure_Letter** — Avg waiting: **12.2h**; 95th percentile: **44.0h**.  
   **Action:** Implement same-day automated closure-letter generation from approved templates once the case reaches closure; target ≥20% reduction.

3. **Assess_Liability** — Avg waiting: **7.8h**; 95th percentile: **16.4h**.  
   **Action:** Introduce priority queuing for cases waiting >6h and daily workload balancing across assessors; target ≥20% reduction by reducing queue aging.

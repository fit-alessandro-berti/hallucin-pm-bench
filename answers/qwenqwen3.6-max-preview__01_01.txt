### 🔹 Decision Points
1. **Eligibility Validation** (Step 2)  
   The triage agent evaluates the submitted claim against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport* to determine whether it meets Contoso’s eligibility criteria.

2. **Pre-Approval Sign-Off** (Step 3)  
   The finance controller reviews the case using the *EligibilityChecklist* and decides whether to grant pre-approval.

3. **High-Value Routing** (Step 4)  
   A conditional check evaluates the refund amount. **If > €1 000**, the case is routed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**; **if ≤ €1 000**, this step is bypassed and the case proceeds toward payment.  
   *(Note: Per the stated constraint, **Process_Refund_Payment** is gated and may only execute after successful pre-approval and, where applicable, completion of the senior auditor review. The legacy “CustomerService_Agent_Decide” activity is explicitly excluded and does not factor into any decision logic.)*

### 📄 Required Documents / Artefacts
- *RefundRequestForm* (submitted by the passenger in Step 1)
- *FlightManifest* (referenced during eligibility validation in Step 2)
- *WeatherReport* (referenced during eligibility validation in Step 2)
- *EligibilityChecklist* (used by the finance controller for pre-approval in Step 3)
- *AuditTrail* (mandatory inclusion for high-value claims >€1 000 in Step 4)
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (must be signed before payment execution in Step 5)
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* (generated and emailed to the passenger in Step 6)

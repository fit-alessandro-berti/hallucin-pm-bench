**Question 2.** One minimal firing sequence is:

**T1 → T2 → T7 → T4 → T5**

After **T1**, tokens are in **P1** and **P2**. The upper branch **T2 → T7** produces **P4** without consuming **P2**. Then **T4** produces **P5** while **P2** remains. With both **P2** and **P5** present, **T5** fires before **T3** ever needs to fire.

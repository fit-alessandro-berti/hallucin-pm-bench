**Question 2:**  
A minimal sequence is:  
**T1, T2, T7, T4, T5**

**Explanation:**  
After T1 fires, P2 holds a token. Using T2 and T7, we obtain P4 and then P5 without firing T3 (which also requires P2 but goes directly to P4). Once P5 is enabled via T4, T5 can fire (using the original P2 token), demonstrating firing T5 before T3.

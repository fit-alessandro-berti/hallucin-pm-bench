**Executive Memo**

**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance  

The three worst-performing activities are Request_Documents, Review_Documents, and Initial_Assessment.

Request_Documents shows the highest wait time (150 minutes), rework rate (22.0%), and a 5.5% SLA breach rate, driving the throughput drop to 190 cases/day.  
**Action:** Add dedicated resources or implement automated document request triggers to cut wait time and rework.

Review_Documents records the longest processing time (25 minutes), highest variability (stdev 9 minutes), and highest SLA breach rate (12.4%).  
**Action:** Introduce standardized review checklists and targeted training to reduce both average time and variability.

Initial_Assessment has the second-highest wait time (18 minutes) and a 3.2% SLA breach rate.  
**Action:** Apply queue triage or add capacity to Initial_Assessment to improve flow into downstream steps.

Addressing these three bottlenecks would yield the largest gains in throughput and SLA compliance.

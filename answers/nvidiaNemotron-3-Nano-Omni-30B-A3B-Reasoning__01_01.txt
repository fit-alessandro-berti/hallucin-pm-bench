**Decision points**

1. **Customer_Submit_Claim** – Does the passenger submit a *RefundRequestForm*?  
   - **Required document:** *RefundRequestForm*  

2. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** – Is the claim **eligible** based on the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*?  
   - **Required documents:** *FlightManifest*, *WeatherReport*  

3. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** – Is the claim **approved** using the *EligibilityChecklist*?  
   - **Required document:** *EligibilityChecklist*  

4. **Senior_Auditor_Review** (high‑value branch only) – Is the refund **greater than €1 000** and has the *AuditTrail* been completed?  
   - **Required document:** *AuditTrail*  

5. **Process_Refund_Payment** – Is the *BankTransferAuthorization* present?  
   - **Required document:** *BankTransferAuthorization*  

**List of all required documents**

- RefundRequestForm  
- FlightManifest  
- WeatherReport  
- EligibilityChecklist  
- AuditTrail (only for refunds > €1 000)  
- BankTransferAuthorization  
- RefundConfirmationPDF (generated for the Issue_Confirmation_Letter step)

### Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation** — **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** checks the claim against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
2. **Finance pre-approval** — **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** must succeed, with sign-off using the **EligibilityChecklist**.
3. **High-value threshold** — if the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required and must include the **AuditTrail**. Claims of €1,000 or less do not require this review.
4. **Payment readiness** — **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin only when:
   - finance pre-approval has succeeded;
   - for claims over €1,000, senior-auditor review is complete; and
   - the signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

### Required documents and artefacts

- **RefundRequestForm** — completed during claim submission.
- **FlightManifest** — used for eligibility validation.
- **WeatherReport** — used for eligibility validation.
- **EligibilityChecklist** — used for finance-controller sign-off.
- **AuditTrail** — required for high-value claims during senior-auditor review.
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — must be signed before payment.
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger after payment.

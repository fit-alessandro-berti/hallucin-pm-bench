### RACI Table for AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process  
**Enforced Vocabulary Used:**  
- **KYC Review** (in place of “Customer Due Diligence”, “CDD”, etc.)  
- **Regulatory Liaison** (in place of “Compliance Officer”)  
- **Transaction Analyst** (in place of “Payment Clerk”, “Processor”, “Agent”)  

| **Process Step**                                      | **Responsible**       | **Accountable**     | **Consulted**        | **Informed**         |
|------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|---------------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Receives the customer’s transfer instruction and logs it in the system | **Transaction Analyst** | **Transaction Analyst** | —                    | **Regulatory Liaison**, **IT Support** |
| Screens the payment against the sanctions list       | **Transaction Analyst** | **Transaction Analyst** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **IT Support** |
| Performs the **KYC Review** on both sender and beneficiary | **Regulatory Liaison** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **Transaction Analyst** | **IT Support** |
| If the transaction is flagged as high-risk, approves it | **Regulatory Liaison** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **Transaction Analyst** | **IT Support** |
| Releases the payment | **Transaction Analyst** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **IT Support** |
| Ensures the SWIFT message is sent                  | **IT Support**        | **IT Support**      | **Transaction Analyst**, **Regulatory Liaison** | — |
| Archives the case file                                | **IT Support**        | **IT Support**      | **Regulatory Liaison** | **Transaction Analyst** |
| Notifies the **Regulatory Liaison**                | **IT Support**        | **IT Support**      | —                    | **Regulatory Liaison** |  

---  

**Notes:**  
- **IT Support** is included as a supporting role where technology and system actions are required.  
- All references to “compliance officer” or “payment clerk” have been replaced with **Regulatory Liaison** and **Transaction Analyst** respectively, as per the AtlasRemit vocabulary mandate.  
- **KYC Review** replaces all variations of customer due diligence terminology.  
- The RACI ensures clear segregation of duties and accountability across the payment flow.

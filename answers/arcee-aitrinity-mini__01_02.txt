

### Decision Points (Activities with Choices)
1.  **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: If scores differ by > 2 points, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee; otherwise, continue.  
2.  **Amount Threshold Decision**: If amount < €15 000, auto-approve; if ≥ €15 000, proceed to step 8.  
3.  **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: Board votes to approve or reject.  

### Required Documents at Submission
▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

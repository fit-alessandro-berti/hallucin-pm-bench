### Decision Points in Acme's Micro-loan Fast-Track Process  
*(Using exact activity/term names from the process definition)*  
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** (Step 5):  
   - Branch: If Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B scores **differ by ≤ 2 points** → Proceed to Step 6 (Neighbourhood Feedback Check).  
   - Branch: If scores **differ by > 2 points** → Escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break decision → Proceed to Step 6.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** (Step 7):  
   - Branch: If loan **amount < €15 000** → Auto-approve → Proceed to Step 9 (Disburse Funds).  
   - Branch: If loan **amount ≥ €15 000** → Proceed to Step 8 (**Final Micro-loan Board Approval**).  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval** (Step 8):  
   - Branch: **Micro-loan Board** votes *approve* → Proceed to Step 9 (Disburse Funds).  
   - Branch: **Micro-loan Board** votes *reject* → Process terminates (rejection not further defined).

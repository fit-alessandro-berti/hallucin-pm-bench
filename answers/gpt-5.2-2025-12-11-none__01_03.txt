### a) Decision points (gates / conditional branches)

1. **Conditional branch after Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 4):** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, then **skip Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5)**; otherwise proceed to Step 5.  
2. **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6):** The application passes the gate if **at least two of the three** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) **approve**; fewer than two approvals means it does not pass.

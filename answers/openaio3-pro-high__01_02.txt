Decision points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track flow (using the exact activity names)

1. Quick KYC Verification (KYC)  
   – Outcome: “pass” or “fail” based on ID card + mobile phone check.

2. Community Impact Assessment (CIA)  
   – Impact Officer concludes whether the applicant’s project meets community-benefit criteria.

3. Senior Underwriter A risk score (part of Dual Underwriter Split)  
   – Numerical score 0–9 assigned.

4. Shadow Underwriter B risk score (part of Dual Underwriter Split)  
   – Numerical score 0–9 assigned.

5. Underwriter Alignment Gate  
   • If the two scores differ by ≤ 2 points → proceed to Neighbourhood Feedback Check.  
   • If the scores differ by > 2 points → escalate to the Harmonisation Committee, which issues a tie-break score, then proceed.

6. Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)  
   – Verification that at least 3 local references are collected and are satisfactory.

7. Amount Threshold Decision  
   • If amount < €15 000 → system auto-approves and jumps to Disburse Funds.  
   • If amount ≥ €15 000 → send case to Final Micro-loan Board Approval.

8. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)  
   – Board votes “approve” or “reject”.

Required documents at submission time (exact titles)

1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
3. Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

**a)** Decision points in Acme’s process:

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check & Amount Threshold**: If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the manual underwriting review (step 5) is skipped.
2. **TriVote Decision-Gate**: The loan must be approved by a majority (at least two) of the three designated officers to proceed to offer preparation.

**b)** All documents in order of first appearance:

1. Form 14B (step 1)
2. Deck Memo (step 5)
3. Offer Sheet 77 (step 7)
4. ID Matrix Declaration (step 8)
5. Loan Dossier 2025 (step 9)

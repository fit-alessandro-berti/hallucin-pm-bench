For **T5** to fire, its preset must be marked: **P2** and **P5** must both contain tokens.

- **P2** can only be produced by **T1**. So before **T5**, we must have fired **T1** (giving a token in P2).
- **P5** can only be produced by **T4**, which requires **P4**.
- **P4** is produced by **T7** (from P3 via T2) and **T3** (from P2). Since **P3** is produced by **T2**, the only way to reach **T4** (and thus **P5**) without firing **T3** is impossible: **P4** must come from **P2**, which requires **T3**.

So **T3** must occur before **T5**.

### Decision points

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   Confirm the ID card and mobile phone; skip all credit history checks.

2. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   - Score difference **≤ 2 points**: continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  
   - Score difference **> 2 points**: escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue.

3. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   - **Amount < €15 000**: the system auto-approves.  
   - **Amount ≥ €15 000**: proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

4. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   The board votes **approve** or **reject**.

### Required documents at submission time

- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

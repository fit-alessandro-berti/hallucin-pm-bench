```xml
    <bpmn:boundaryEvent id="Event_SLA_Timer" name="48h Timeout" attachedToRef="Activity_CreateAccount" cancelActivity="true">
      <bpmn:outgoing>Flow_Escalation</bpmn:outgoing>
      <bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
        <bpmn:timeDuration>PT48H</bpmn:timeDuration>
      </bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
    </bpmn:boundaryEvent>

    <!-- Route the escalation to the existing (previously unused) Account-Manager Review task -->
    <bpmn:sequenceFlow id="Flow_Escalation" sourceRef="Event_SLA_Timer" targetRef="Activity_AMReview"/>
```

**Justification:** By attaching an interrupting boundary timer to the `Activity_CreateAccount` task, the process automatically diverts to the existing `Activity_AMReview` (reusing the unused element) if the 48-hour threshold is exceeded, thereby enforcing the SLA without adding new process steps.

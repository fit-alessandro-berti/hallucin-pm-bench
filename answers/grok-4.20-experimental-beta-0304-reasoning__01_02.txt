**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process**

- **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Compares the risk scores (0–9) assigned independently by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the scores differ by ≤ 2 points, the process continues to Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC). If they differ by more than 2 points, the file is escalated to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision.
- **Amount Threshold Decision**: Evaluates the requested loan amount. If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves. If the amount is ≥ €15 000, the file proceeds to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).
- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Micro-loan Board votes to approve or reject the application.

**Required Documents at submission time**
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

Add an **interrupting boundary timer event** to `Task_RequestDocs` (e.g., `BT_48h`) and define it with an absolute `timeDate` = claim submission time from `Start_Claim` + 48 hours. Connect `BT_48h` directly to `Task_Triage`.  

This leaves the normal path unchanged, but if the claim is still waiting in `Task_RequestDocs` when the 48-hour deadline is reached, the timer interrupts that wait and automatically escalates the case into `Task_Triage`. That gives you BPMN-level auto-escalation without adding any new tasks or subprocesses.

**Question 1.** Yes, it is possible for T5 to fire before T3.

**Question 2 (bonus).** 
**Minimal firing sequence:** `[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]`

**Explanation:** 
T1 produces tokens in both P1 and P2. The P1 branch can proceed through T2 and T7 to place a token in P4, which then fires T4 to place a token in P5. Since the token in P2 remains unconsumed, both P2 and P5 are marked, satisfying the preset for T5. Thus, T5 fires before T3 is ever enabled or fired.

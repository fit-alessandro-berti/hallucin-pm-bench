**Legend:**  
- **Responsible:** Directly engages in the task.

- **Consulted:** Provides input based on expertise; two-way communication.
- **Informed:** Kept up-to-date on progress; one-way communication.

In this RACI matrix, roles are assigned based on primary responsibility for each activity within the newly defined workflow, adhering to the vocabulary policy.

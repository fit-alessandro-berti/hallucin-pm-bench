# AtlasRemit — RACI Matrix: Cross-Border Transfer Process

**Roles (per vocabulary mandate):**
- **Transaction Analyst**
- **Regulatory Liaison**
- **IT Support**

| # | Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|----------|:-------------------:|:-------------------:|:----------:|
| 1 | Receive & log the customer's transfer instruction | **R, A** | I | – |
| 2 | Screen payment against the sanctions list | **R** | **A**, C | I |
| 3 | Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary | C | **R, A** | I |
| 4 | Approve high-risk transaction | I | **R, A** | I |
| 5 | Release the payment | **R, A** | I | I |
| 6 | Send the SWIFT message | I | I | **R, A** |
| 7 | Archive the case file | I | I | **R, A** |
| 8 | Notify the Regulatory Liaison of case closure | I | I | **R** |

**Legend**
- **R** = Responsible (performs the work)
- **A** = Accountable (ultimately answerable; one per row)
- **C** = Consulted (provides input before/during the work)
- **I** = Informed (notified of outcome)
- **–** = Not involved

**Notes**
- Source item 5 was split into two activities (rows 5 & 6) to reflect the distinct ownership between releasing the payment and dispatching the SWIFT message.
- Source item 6 was split into two activities (rows 7 & 8) to separate the archival action from the notification step.
- All role labels and activity references use the mandated vocabulary only.

Here is a structured summary of every decision point in Contoso’s flight-refund process and the **required documents** at each stage:

---

### Decision Points & Required Documents:

1. **Customer_Submit_Claim**
   - **Objective:** Pass the initial claim submission.
   - **Documents Required:**
     - RefundRequestForm (submitted online)

2. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - **Objective:** Verify reported details using system data.
   - **Documents Required:**
     - RefundRequestForm
     - Current flight manifest (FlightManifest)
     - Weather Report (WeatherReport)
   - **Decision:** Agent accepts or rejects claim based on eligibility.

3. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - **Objective:** Ensure financial compliance.
   - **Documents Required:**
     - Exception filed (if any)
     - EligibilityChecklist (approved for high-value claims only)

4. **High-value branch (€1,000+) path**
   - **Objective:** Proceed to detailed review.
   - **Conditions:**
     - Finance_Controller_PreApprove: Success
     - High-value flag (amount > €1,000)
   - **Documents Required:**
     - RefundRequestForm
     - Finance Approval Confirmation (from Finance Controller)
     - AuditTrail (for audit accountability)

5. **Process_Refund_Payment**
   - **Objective:** Initiate financial transaction.
   - **Requirements:**
     - Finance_Controller_PreApprove approved
     - Senior_Auditor_Review completed (mandatory for high-value claims)
   - **Documents Required:**
     - BankTransferAuthorization
   - **Decision:** Payment proceeds once compliance confirmed.

6. **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**
   - **Objective:** Notify the customer of the refund.
   - **Documents Required:**
     - RefundConfirmationPDF

---

**Summary:**  
Each stage requires specific inputs or approvals, with mandatory documents such as the RefundRequestForm, relevant flight/weather data, payment authorization, and a properly signed PDF and process trail. The only deviation from textbooks is the absence of the mentioned decision point in "CustomerService_Agent_Decide."

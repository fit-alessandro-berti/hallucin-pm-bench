Because `pe` can be produced from `pb` by `T4`, and `pd` can be produced from `pa` by the shortcut `T2b` **without** first using `T3`.

A shortest firing sequence is:

**`T1 → T2b → T4 → T5`**

Step-by-step:

| Step | Transition | Effect |
|------|------------|--------|
| 1 | `T1` | consumes `p0`, produces `pa` and `pb` |
| 2 | `T2b` | consumes `pa`, produces `pd` |
| 3 | `T4` | consumes `pb`, produces `pe` |
| 4 | `T5` | consumes `pd` and `pe`, produces `pf` |

At this point `T5` has fired, and `T3` has not fired at all. Thus it is possible for `T5` to fire before `T3`.

As an expert on Acme Bank’s internal small-business loan-approval process, I have summarized the decision points and documentation requirements below.

### a) Summary of Decision Points

1.  **Risk/Amount Conditional Branch (Step 4):** The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review if the application is rated as Low or Medium risk and the requested amount is €250,000 or less; otherwise, the manual review is mandatory.
2.  **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):** An application is approved to proceed if at least two out of the three required stakeholders (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) provide their approval.

***

### b) List of Required Documents
(Listed in the order in which they first appear in the process)

1.  **Form 14B** (Received at step 1)
2.  **Scorecard–Cyan** (Input for step 3)
3.  **Scorecard–Magenta** (Input for step 3)
4.  **Scorecard–Yellow** (Input for step 3)
5.  **Deck Memo** (Generated for step 5)
6.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Issued at step 7)
7.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Required at step 8)
8.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalized at step 9)

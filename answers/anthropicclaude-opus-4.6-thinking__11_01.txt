Avg wait: **36.5 h** | 95th pctl: **120.0 h**

**#2 — Send_Closure_Letter**
Avg wait: **12.2 h** | 95th pctl: **44.0 h**
*Action:* Trigger closure letters automatically upon final decision via rules engine, eliminating manual queue dependency and cutting wait by ≥ 20 %.

**#3 — Assess_Liability**
Avg wait: **7.8 h** | 95th pctl: **16.4 h**
*Action:* Implement priority-based work allocation with load balancing across assessors, targeting the long-tail cases driving the 16.4-h P95 and reducing average wait by ≥ 20 %.

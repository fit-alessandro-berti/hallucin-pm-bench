**a) Decision Points**

1.  **Tri-Color Risk Branch (Step 4):** If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check yields a Low or Medium risk rating **and** the requested amount is €250,000 or less, the process skips Manual Underwriting Review (Step 5) and proceeds directly to the TriVote gate.
2.  **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):** The loan passes this gate if **at least two out of the three** required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) vote to approve; otherwise, the application is declined.

**b) Documents (in order of first appearance)**

1.  **Form 14B** (Step 1)  
2.  **Deck Memo** (Step 5)  
3.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7)  
4.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8)  
5.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9)

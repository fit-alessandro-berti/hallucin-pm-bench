### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**  
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** (step 4.5): If scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by **≤ 2 points**, proceed; else, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee**.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** (step 7): If the loan **amount < €15 000**, auto-approve; else, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** (step 8): Board votes **approve** or **reject** for amounts **≥ €15 000**.

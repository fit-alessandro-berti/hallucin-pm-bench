**Decision points in Contoso’s flight-refund process**

| Decision point | What is being decided | Required documents |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** | Confirms the refund claim is legitimate by checking the submitted request against operational records. | *RefundRequestForm*, *FlightManifest*, *WeatherReport* |
| **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** | Determines whether the finance team may proceed by verifying the eligibility checklist has been satisfied. | *EligibilityChecklist* (prepared from the earlier validation artifacts) |
| **High-value branch (refund > €1 000)** | Routes the case to additional scrutiny whenever the requested refund exceeds €1 000. | Amount from the *RefundRequestForm* (drives the branch) |
| **Senior_Auditor_Review** (only for high-value cases) | Provides the mandatory senior-level approval for high-value refunds. | *AuditTrail* |

**Complete list of required documents**

* RefundRequestForm  
* FlightManifest  
* WeatherReport  
* EligibilityChecklist  
* AuditTrail (only for high-value cases)  
* BankTransferAuthorization (needed before processing payment)  
* RefundConfirmationPDF

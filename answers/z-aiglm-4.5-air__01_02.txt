### Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process  
1.  **Underwriter Alignment Gate (Step 5)**  
    -   Condition: If Underwriter A and Underwriter B risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → **Continue to Step 6**.  
    -   Condition: If scores differ by **> 2 points** → **Escalate to Harmonisation Committee** → **Continue after decision**.  
2.  **Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7)**  
    -   Condition: If **amount < €15 000** → **System auto-approves** → Proceed to **Step 9**.  
    -   Condition: If **amount ≥ €15 000** → **Proceed to Step 8**.  
3.  **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (Step 8)**  
    -   Decision: Board votes **approve** or **reject** → Proceed to **Step 9** if approved.  

### Every Required Document  
1.  **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**  
2.  **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**  
3.  **Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)**  
4.  **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**  
5.  **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**  
6.  **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**


**Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**

1.  **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Determines whether the two underwriter scores are aligned (difference ≤ 2 points) or require escalation to the Harmonisation Committee.
2.  **Amount Threshold Decision** – Determines the next step based on the loan amount: auto-approval for amounts < €15 000, or escalation to the Micro-loan Board for amounts ≥ €15 000.
3.  **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The board makes the final approve/reject decision for amounts ≥ €15 000.

**Required Documents (at submission time):**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

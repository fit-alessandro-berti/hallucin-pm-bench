**To: COO, Acme Insurance**  
**Subject: Q1 Claims Handling – worst-performing activities**

The three weakest activities are:

1. **Request_Documents** — Clear bottleneck: **150 min wait** (highest by far), **22.0% rework** (highest), and **5.5% SLA breaches**. Processing is only **5 min**, so the issue is queueing and rework, not execution.  
   **Action:** Require complete document packets before entry and automate missing-document checks to cut rework and queue time.

2. **Review_Documents** — Highest execution burden: **25 min processing**, **9 min variability**, **12.4% SLA breaches** (highest), plus **30 min wait** and **5.3% rework**.  
   **Action:** Standardize reviews with a checklist/decision rule set to reduce cycle-time variation and SLA misses.

3. **Initial_Assessment** — Underperforms relative to other high-throughput steps: **18 min wait**, **12 min processing**, **4 min stdev**, **1.8% rework**, and **3.2% SLA breaches**.  
   **Action:** Rebalance capacity or add coverage to this step to bring wait time down.

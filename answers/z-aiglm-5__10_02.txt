### RACI Matrix – AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process

| Process Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **1. Receive and log transfer instruction** | **R / A** | I | — |
| **2. Screen payment against sanctions list** | **R** | **A** | — |
| **3. Perform KYC Review (Sender & Beneficiary)** | I | **R / A** | — |
| **4. Approve high-risk transaction** | I | **A** | — |
| **5. Release payment** | **R** | **A** | — |
| **6. Transmit SWIFT message** | — | I | **R / A** |
| **7. Archive case file** | — | I | **R / A** |

***

**Key:**
*   **R** = Responsible (Does the work)
*   **A** = Accountable (Ultimately answerable for the task)
*   **C** = Consulted (Provides input)
*   **I** = Informed (Kept up-to-date)

**Notes on Vocabulary Application:**
*   *Payment Clerk* references were mapped to **Transaction Analyst**.
*   *Compliance Officer* references were mapped to **Regulatory Liaison**.
*   *Customer Due Diligence* was mapped to **KYC Review**.

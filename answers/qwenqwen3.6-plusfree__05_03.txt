    <bpmn:timeDuration xsi:type="bpmn:tFormalExpression">PT48H</bpmn:timeDuration>

Attaching an interrupting timer boundary event to **Create Account in Core System** initiates a 48-hour countdown the moment the task starts (immediately following identity verification) and automatically diverts execution to the pre-existing Account-Manager Review if the deadline expires, enforcing the SLA without introducing new work tasks.

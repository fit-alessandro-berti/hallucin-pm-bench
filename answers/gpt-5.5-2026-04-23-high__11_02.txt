**To: COO, Acme Insurance**  
**Subject: Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance Hotspots**

The three worst-performing activities are:

1. **Request_Documents** — This is the clearest bottleneck: average wait time is **150 minutes**, far above all other steps, and rework is **22.0%**, the highest in the process. Throughput also drops to **190/day** from 270/day upstream.  
   **Action:** Introduce automated, standardized document requests and validation rules to reduce missing/incorrect submissions driving rework.

2. **Review_Documents** — This step has the highest processing time (**25 minutes**), highest variability (**9 minutes** stdev), and worst SLA breach rate (**12.4%**). Throughput falls further to **185/day**.  
   **Action:** Segment reviews by complexity and route simple cases to a fast-track queue to reduce average handling time and SLA breaches.

3. **Initial_Assessment** — Among remaining activities, it has the highest wait time (**18 minutes**), relatively high processing time (**12 minutes**), and third-highest SLA breach rate (**3.2%**).  
   **Action:** Review staffing or work allocation at assessment during peak arrival periods to reduce queueing delay and SLA exposure.

**Decision points (using exact activity names):**
- **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – if Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B scores differ by ≤ 2 points, continue; otherwise escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break, then continue.
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** – proceed only after collecting at least 3 local references.
- **Amount Threshold Decision** – if amount < €15 000, the system auto-approves; if amount ≥ €15 000, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.
- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – board votes approve or reject (applies to amounts ≥ €15 000).

**Required documents at submission time:**
- Digital Application Form (**ML-APP-01**)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (**CIS**) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (**NFF**)

# RACI Matrix for Order-to-Cash Process  
*(Using preferred terminology: **KYC Review**, **Order Vetting**, **Payment Clearance**)*

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|----------|----------------------|-------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| **Receive Application** | R, A | I | — | — |
| **KYC Review** | I | R, A | — | — |
| **Order Vetting** | R, A | C | I | — |
| **Payment Clearance** | I | — | R, A | C |
| **Record Update** | I | — | I | R, A |
| **Notify Customer** | R, A | I | I | C |

---

### Key
- **R** = Responsible (executes the work)  
- **A** = Accountable (final authority/sign-off)  
- **C** = Consulted (provides input before/during)  
- **I** = Informed (kept updated after completion)  
— = No involvement or responsibility for this role in the step  

---

### Notable assignments & rationale:
1. **Receive Application** – AM is both **R** (handles the application) and **A** (owns the customer intake). CO is **I** because KYC Review will follow.  
2. **KYC Review** – CO is **R** (performs the review) and **A** (owns compliance outcomes). AM is **I** to know the application’s status.  
3. **Order Vetting** – AM is **R** (checks completeness) and **A** (validates order readiness). CO is **C** (may provide KYC context if needed). FC is **I** to prepare for payment.  
4. **Payment Clearance** – FC is **R** (initiates settlement) and **A** (owns financial accuracy). IT is **C** if system issues arise.  
5. **Record Update** – IT is **R** (refreshes CRM) and **A** (owns data integrity). AM and FC are **I** so they know the system is updated.  
6. **Notify Customer** – AM is **R** (sends confirmation) and **A** (owns customer communication). CO, FC, and IT are **I** for visibility.  

This matrix follows the vocabulary policy strictly and reflects a balanced distribution of accountability—each step has one **A**, and cross-role consultation/information flows are included where logically needed.

Decision points  

1. **Quick KYC Verification** – accept / refuse identity / mobile match.  
2. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – continue vs escalate to **Harmonisation Committee**.  
3. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check** – at least 3 valid references captured or fail.  
4. **Amount Threshold Decision** – auto-approve (< €15 000) vs route to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval**.  
5. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval** – vote approve vs reject.

Required documents  

• Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
• Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
• Latest Income Statement  
• Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
• Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
• Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

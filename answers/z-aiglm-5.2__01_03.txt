**a) Decision Points**

1. **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** The application bypasses the Manual-Underwriting-Review if the Tri-Color-Risk-Check results in a Low or Medium risk rating and the requested amount is €250,000 or less; otherwise, it proceeds to the manual review.
2. **Decision Gate "TriVote" (Step 6):** The loan advances to the binding offer stage if at least two out of the three mandatory approvers—the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer—grant their approval.

**b) All Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**

1. Form 14B (Step 1)
2. Scorecard–Cyan (Step 3)
3. Scorecard–Magenta (Step 3)
4. Scorecard–Yellow (Step 3)
5. Deck Memo (Step 5)
6. Offer Sheet 77 (Step 7)
7. Signed Offer Sheet 77 (Step 8)
8. ID Matrix Declaration (Step 8)
9. Loan Dossier 2025 (Step 9)

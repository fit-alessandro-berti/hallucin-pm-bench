### **a) Decision Points Summary**  
Each decision point is described in one sentence per rule:  

1. **Step 4 (Conditional Branch)**: If the risk rating (from Tri-Color-Risk-Check) is **Low or Medium** AND the requested loan amount is **≤ €250,000**, skip Manual Underwriting Review (step 5); otherwise, proceed to step 5.  
2. **Step 6 (TriVote Gate)**: The loan passes only if **at least two of the three roles** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve; any two approvals suffice to pass.

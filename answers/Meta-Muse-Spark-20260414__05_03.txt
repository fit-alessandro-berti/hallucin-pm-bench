To enforce the 48h SLA without new activities, attach a boundary timer to the existing `Activity_CreateAccount` and route timeouts to the existing unused escalation task.

**Changed/added XML elements:**
```xml
<bpmn:serviceTask id="Activity_CreateAccount" name="Create Account in Core System"/>
<bpmn:boundaryEvent id="Timer_48h_SLA" attachedToRef="Activity_CreateAccount" cancelActivity="false">
  <bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
    <bpmn:timeDuration>PT48H</bpmn:timeDuration>
  </bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
</bpmn:boundaryEvent>
<bpmn:sequenceFlow id="Flow_Escalate_SLA" sourceRef="Timer_48h_SLA" targetRef="Activity_AMReview"/>
```
**Justification:** A non-interrupting 48-hour boundary timer on `Activity_CreateAccount` triggers escalation to the existing `Account-Manager Review` when the SLA is breached, ensuring compliance without adding new tasks.

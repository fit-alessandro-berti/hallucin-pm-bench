**MEMO**
**To:** Chief Operating Officer, Orion Credit
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst
**Re:** Worst-Performing Activities — SLA Breaches & Waiting Times

Analysis of throughput, waiting time, and SLA data identifies three critical underperformers:

**Risk Review** — Avg throughput of 600s exceeds its 300s SLA by 300s (100% over). Waiting time of 480s accounts for 80% of total throughput.

**Legal Approval** — Avg throughput of 900s exceeds its 600s SLA by 300s (50% over). At 600s, it carries the highest waiting time of any activity.

**Credit Assessment** — Avg throughput of 300s exceeds its 240s SLA by 60s (25% over), with waiting time (200s) representing two-thirds of throughput across 1,175 cases.

Recommendations:

- **Risk Review:** Add reviewer capacity to cut the 480s queue driving the SLA breach.
- **Legal Approval:** Introduce parallel approval routing to reduce the 600s wait.
- **Credit Assessment:** Automate triage for the 1,175-case volume to lower the 200s waiting time.

### Decision Points

• **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
  Compares the risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the scores differ by ≤ 2 points, the application proceeds. If they differ by > 2 points, the case escalates to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before continuing.  

• **Amount Threshold Decision**  
  Evaluates the requested amount against the €15 000 threshold. Applications requesting **< €15 000** are auto-approved by the system. Applications requesting **≥ €15 000** proceed directly to the Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).  

• **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
  The board conducts a formal vote to either **approve** or **reject** the application.  

*(Note: These are the only conditional decision gates in the flow; all other steps proceed sequentially once triggered.)*

### Required Documents

• Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
• Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
• Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
• Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
• Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
• Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

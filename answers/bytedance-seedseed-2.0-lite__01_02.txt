### Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Evaluate the gap between Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B’s independent risk scores. If scores differ by ≤2 points, continue the process. If scores differ by >2 points, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision before proceeding.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**: Assess the requested loan amount to route next steps. If the amount is <€15,000, the system auto-approves. If the amount is ≥€15,000, proceed to the next formal approval step.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Micro-loan Board votes to approve or reject the loan.

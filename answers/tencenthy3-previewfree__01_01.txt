### Decision Points
All decision points are summarized below using only information defined in Contoso’s process description, with no added assumptions or undefined activities/artefacts:

1. **Triage Eligibility Validation (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)**
   - Timing: Occurs after the customer completes the *RefundRequestForm* (Customer_Submit_Claim).
   - Decision criteria: The triage agent checks the claim against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.
   - Outcomes:
     - Claim eligible: Process advances to Finance_Controller_PreApprove.
     - Claim ineligible: Process terminates (no further steps are defined for ineligible claims).

2. **Finance Pre-Approval (Finance_Controller_PreApprove)**
   - Timing: Occurs only after Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility confirms eligibility.
   - Decision criteria: The finance controller evaluates the claim using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
   - Outcomes:
     - Pre-approval succeeds: Process advances to the high-value refund check.
     - Pre-approval fails: Process terminates (per process constraints, Process_Refund_Payment may only begin after pre-approval succeeds).

3. **High-Value Refund Branch Decision**
   - Timing: Occurs only after Finance_Controller_PreApprove succeeds.
   - Decision criteria: Is the refund amount greater than €1 000?
   - Outcomes (branching paths):
     - **Yes (high-value claim)**: Process proceeds to Senior_Auditor_Review (which requires the *AuditTrail*) before refund payment can be processed.
     - **No (standard-value claim)**: Process skips Senior_Auditor_Review and proceeds to Process_Refund_Payment once all preconditions are met.
   - Note: This is the only explicit branching decision in the process; Senior_Auditor_Review is a mandatory completion-only step for high-value claims, with no defined approval/rejection outcome.

### Required Documents
All artefacts mandated by the process are listed below, with no undefined items included:
1. *RefundRequestForm*: Completed by the customer during Customer_Submit_Claim.
2. *FlightManifest*: Used to validate claim eligibility during Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility.
3. *WeatherReport*: Used to validate claim eligibility during Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility.
4. *EligibilityChecklist*: Used by the finance controller to pre-approve claims during Finance_Controller_PreApprove.
5. *AuditTrail*: Mandatory input for Senior_Auditor_Review (required for high-value claims only).
6. *BankTransferAuthorization*: Signed document required to execute Process_Refund_Payment.
7. *RefundConfirmationPDF*: Generated and emailed to the passenger during Issue_Confirmation_Letter.

The legacy, non-existent activity "CustomerService_Agent_Decide" and any related artefacts are excluded per Contoso’s process constraints.

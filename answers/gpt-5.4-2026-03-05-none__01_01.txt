Here are the **decision points** in Contoso Airlines’ refund process, with the **required documents** exactly as defined.

## Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - **Activity:** `Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility`
   - **Decision:** whether the claim is eligible for refund
   - **Required documents:**
     - `FlightManifest`
     - `WeatherReport`

2. **Finance pre-approval**
   - **Activity:** `Finance_Controller_PreApprove`
   - **Decision:** whether finance signs off the refund
   - **Required documents:**
     - `EligibilityChecklist`

3. **High-value check**
   - **Decision:** whether the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**
   - **Outcome if yes:** proceed to `Senior_Auditor_Review`
   - **Required documents for that review:**
     - `AuditTrail`

4. **Payment readiness decision**
   - **Activity:** `Process_Refund_Payment`
   - **Decision condition:** payment may begin only after:
     - `Finance_Controller_PreApprove` has succeeded, and
     - for high-value claims, `Senior_Auditor_Review` has been completed
   - **Required document:**
     - `BankTransferAuthorization`

## All required documents in the process

- `RefundRequestForm`
- `FlightManifest`
- `WeatherReport`
- `EligibilityChecklist`
- `AuditTrail` *(for high-value claims)*
- `BankTransferAuthorization`
- `RefundConfirmationPDF`

## Notes
- The process includes **no** `CustomerService_Agent_Decide` activity.
- `RefundRequestForm` is submitted in `Customer_Submit_Claim`.
- `RefundConfirmationPDF` is issued in `Issue_Confirmation_Letter`.

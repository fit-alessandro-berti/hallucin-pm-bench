Here is a precise summary of **every decision point** and **all required documents** in Contoso Airlines’ flight-refund process, strictly based on the defined activities and artefacts:

---

### **Decision Points**

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**  
   - **Decision**: Is the refund claim eligible based on the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*?  
   - *Outcome determines whether the claim proceeds to Finance_Controller_PreApprove or is rejected.*

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**  
   - **Decision**: Does the claim satisfy all criteria on the *EligibilityChecklist*?  
   - *Outcome determines whether the claim proceeds to the high-value branch or directly to Process_Refund_Payment (if eligible and ≤€1,000).*

3. **High-value branch condition (implicit decision)**  
   - **Decision**: Is the refund amount **greater than €1,000**?  
   - *Outcome determines whether the claim must go through Senior_Auditor_Review before Process_Refund_Payment.*

4. **Senior_Auditor_Review** (triggered only if >€1,000)  
   - **Decision**: Is the *AuditTrail* complete and consistent with the claim?  
   - *Outcome determines whether the claim may proceed to Process_Refund_Payment or is rejected.*

---

### **Required Documents (Artefacts)**

The following documents are **required** at the defined stages — no others are permitted:

1. **RefundRequestForm**  
   - Submitted by the customer in **Customer_Submit_Claim**.

2. **FlightManifest**  
   - Used by **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** to validate eligibility.

3. **WeatherReport**  
   - Used by **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** to validate eligibility.

4. **EligibilityChecklist**  
   - Used by **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** to sign off on the claim.

5. **AuditTrail**  
   - Required for **Senior_Auditor_Review** (only for high-value claims >€1,000).

6. **BankTransferAuthorization**  
   - Required for **Process_Refund_Payment** — must be signed and present before payment can execute.

7. **RefundConfirmationPDF**  
   - Generated and emailed in **Issue_Confirmation_Letter** — *not a prerequisite for any prior step, but an output artefact*.

---

### Notes:
- **CustomerService_Agent_Decide** is explicitly excluded — it does not exist in this process.
- No documents beyond the seven listed above are involved.
- All decision points are tied to the presence, content, or outcome of one of the above artefacts.

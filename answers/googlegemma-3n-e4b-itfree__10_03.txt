## NimbusPay - Wire Transfer Process RACI Matrix

| Activity | Front-Office Analyst | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | Treasury Ops |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Intake Vetting** | R | C | I | I |
| **KYC Review** | C | R | A | I |
| **AML Screening** | C | R | C | I |
| **Risk Scoring** | R | C | I | I |
| Escalation of High-Risk Cases | C | R | A | I |
| Approval of High-Risk Cases | I | C | R | I |
| Release of Funds | I | I | I | R |
| Notification of Payment Release | R | I | I | R |

**Key:**

*   **R**esponsible: Performs the activity.
*   **A**ccountable: Ultimately responsible for the correct and thorough completion of the activity.
*   **C**onsulted: Provides input and expertise.
*   **I**nformed: Receives updates on the progress or completion of the activity.

In Acme Bank's SMALL-BUSINESS process as defined, there are exactly **2** decision points:

**a) Decision Points:**

1.  **Conditional Branch at Step 4:** If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check rating is Low or Medium **AND** requested-amount is ≤ €250,000, then Step 5 Manual-Underwriting-Review is skipped; otherwise Step 5 must be performed.
2.  **Decision-Gate at Step 6 - "TriVote":** The process can only pass to Prepare-Binding-Offer if at least **2 out of 3** approvals are obtained from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer.

**b) Documents required - in order of first appearance:**

1.  **Form 14B** - Step 1 Receive-Application
2.  **Scorecard–Cyan** - Step 3 Tri-Color-Risk-Check [Input]
3.  **Scorecard–Magenta** - Step 3 Tri-Color-Risk-Check [Input]
4.  **Scorecard–Yellow** - Step 3 Tri-Color-Risk-Check [Input]
5.  **Deck Memo** - Step 5 Manual-Underwriting-Review
6.  **Offer Sheet 77** - Step 7 Prepare-Binding-Offer
7.  **Signed Offer Sheet 77** - Step 8 Request-Applicant-Confirmation
8.  **ID Matrix Declaration** - Step 8 Request-Applicant-Confirmation
9.  **Loan Dossier 2025** - Step 9 Finalize-Agreement

> Per definition: No KYC/AML documents and no external credit-bureau pull documents are part of this in-house process.

**Decision points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process**
1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** – confirm ID card and mobile phone; skip credit history checks.
2. **Dual Underwriter Split**  
   - **Senior Underwriter A** assigns risk score (0–9).  
   - **Shadow Underwriter B** independently assigns risk score (0–9).
3. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – continue if scores differ by ≤ 2 points; else escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break.
4. **Amount Threshold Decision** – auto-approve if amount < €15,000; if ≥ €15,000, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.
5. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – board votes approve or reject.

**Required documents at submission time**
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

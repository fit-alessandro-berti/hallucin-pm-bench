Below is a concise overview of the decision points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track (non-standard variant, May 2025). Following that is the complete list of required documents at submission time.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DECISION POINTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Underwriter Alignment Gate (Step 5)  
   • If the **Senior Underwriter A** score and the **Shadow Underwriter B** score differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  
   • Otherwise, the application is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, and then proceeds.

2. Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7)  
   • If the **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves.  
   • If the **amount ≥ €15 000**, the application moves on to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (Step 8)  
   • The **Micro-loan Board** votes to *approve* or *reject* based on the outcome of previous steps.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS AT SUBMISSION TIME
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
• Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
• Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
• Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
• Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
• Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

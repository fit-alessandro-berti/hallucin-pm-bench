**Question 2 (bonus).**  
Minimal firing sequence: **T1, T2, T7, T4, T5**

**Explanation:**  
Firing T1 puts tokens in P1 and P2. To enable T5, we need tokens in P2 and P5. Instead of firing T3 (which would consume the P2 token), we can route the P1 token to P5 via the parallel branch: fire T2 (marking P3), T7 (marking P4), and T4 (marking P5). 

At this point, both P2 and P5 hold a token, which enables T5. Firing T5 consumes these tokens to mark P6, successfully firing without T3 ever having fired.

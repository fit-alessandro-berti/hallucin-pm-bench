**a) Decision points (gates or conditional branches)**  

- **Step 4 Conditional Skip**: If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check outcome is Low or Medium *and* the requested amount ≤ €250,000, then skip Manual-Underwriting-Review and go directly to Decision-Gate “TriVote”.  
- **Decision-Gate “TriVote”**: Loan passes this gate if any two of the three required approvers — Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer — give approval.

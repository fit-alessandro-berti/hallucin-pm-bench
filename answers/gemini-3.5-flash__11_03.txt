MEMORANDUM

TO: COO  
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
SUBJECT: Process Performance and SLA Breach Analysis  

An analysis of our process data identifies Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment as the three worst-performing activities due to significant SLA breaches and high waiting times. 

First, Legal Approval exceeds its 600-second Target SLA by 300 seconds, carrying a 600-second average waiting time across 465 cases. Second, Risk Review breaches its 300-second Target SLA by 300 seconds, with an average waiting time of 480 seconds over 480 cases. Third, Credit Assessment exceeds its 240-second Target SLA by 60 seconds, with an average waiting time of 200 seconds across 1,175 cases.

To address these bottlenecks, I recommend the following data-driven actions:
* For Legal Approval, reduce the 600-second average waiting time to under 300 seconds to bring the 900-second average throughput time within the 600-second SLA limit.
* For Risk Review, decrease the 480-second average waiting time by at least 300 seconds to eliminate the current 300-second SLA breach.
* For Credit Assessment, lower the 200-second average waiting time to below 140 seconds to resolve the 60-second SLA breach across all 1,175 cases.

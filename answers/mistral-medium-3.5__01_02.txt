### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Proceed if risk scores from *Senior Underwriter A* and *Shadow Underwriter B* differ by **≤ 2 points**; else, escalate to *Harmonisation Committee*.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Auto-approve if **amount < €15 000**; else, proceed to *Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)*.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – Board votes *approve* or *reject* for amounts **≥ €15 000**.

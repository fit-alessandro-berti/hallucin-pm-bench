## Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   Compares the two independent risk scores from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**.  
   - If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, the process continues to the **Neighbourhood Feedback Check**.  
   - If the scores differ by **> 2 points**, the case is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before continuing.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   Triggered after the **Neighbourhood Feedback Check**.  
   - If the requested amount is **< €15 000**, the system **auto-approves**.  
   - If the requested amount is **≥ €15 000**, the case proceeds to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval**  
   Applies only to applications for **€15 000 or more**. The Micro-loan Board votes to **approve** or **reject** the application before disbursement.

## Required Documents at Submission Time

- **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
- **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
- **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- **Community Impact Statement (CIS)** template
- **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

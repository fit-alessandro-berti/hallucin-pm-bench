**Question 2:** A minimal firing sequence is:

**[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]**

After **T1**, both **P1** and **P2** contain tokens. The token from **P1** proceeds through **T2 → T7 → T4**, producing a token in **P5**, while the token in **P2** remains unconsumed because **T3** has not fired. Thus **T5** is enabled by tokens in **P2** and **P5** and can fire. It consumes the **P2** token, so **T3** cannot fire afterward.

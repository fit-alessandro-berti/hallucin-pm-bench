### Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - **Activity:** `Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility`
   - The triage agent determines whether the refund claim is eligible by checking the submitted claim against the `FlightManifest` and `WeatherReport`.

2. **High-value refund determination**
   - If the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**, the case must proceed to `Senior_Auditor_Review`.
   - If the amount is **€1,000 or less**, `Senior_Auditor_Review` is not required.

3. **Payment readiness**
   - `Process_Refund_Payment` can begin only when:
     - `Finance_Controller_PreApprove` has succeeded; and
     - for claims over €1,000, `Senior_Auditor_Review` has been completed; and
     - a signed `BankTransferAuthorization` is present.

### Required documents / artefacts

- `RefundRequestForm` — completed by the customer in `Customer_Submit_Claim`.
- `FlightManifest` — used during `Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility`.
- `WeatherReport` — used during `Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility`.
- `EligibilityChecklist` — used by `Finance_Controller_PreApprove`.
- `AuditTrail` — required for `Senior_Auditor_Review` for refunds greater than €1,000.
- `BankTransferAuthorization` — must be signed before `Process_Refund_Payment`.
- `RefundConfirmationPDF` — emailed to the passenger during `Issue_Confirmation_Letter`.

**Decision Points**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Evaluates whether the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points. If yes, the process continues; if no, it escalates to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break before continuing.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Checks the requested funding amount. If < €15 000, the system auto-approves. If ≥ €15 000, it routes the application forward.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The Micro-loan Board votes *approve* or *reject* on applications that reached this stage (amounts ≥ €15 000), determining final clearance.

**Required Documents (at submission time)**
▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

Yes, it is possible. After firing T1, we have tokens in P1 and P2. If we fire T2 and T7, we move the token from P1 to P4 without consuming P2. Then firing T4 consumes P4 and produces P5, giving tokens in P2 and P5, which enables T5. T3 has not fired. A minimal firing sequence is: T1, T2, T7, T4, T5.

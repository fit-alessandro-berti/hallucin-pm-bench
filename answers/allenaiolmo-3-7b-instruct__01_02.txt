
**Decision Points in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**
1. Underwriter Alignment Gate (scores from A and B must differ by ≤ 2 points)
2. Neighbourhood Feedback Check (must collect at least 3 local references)
3. Amount Threshold (€15 000 cutoff for board involvement)
4. Micro-loan Board Approval (for amounts ≥ €15 000)
5. Auto-approval (for amounts < €15 000 after earlier steps)

**Required Documents at Submission:**
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

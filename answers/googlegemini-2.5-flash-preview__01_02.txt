Here are the decision points and required documents for Acme Bank's Micro-loan Fast-Track process:

**Decision Points:**

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate:** Decides whether to proceed to step 6 or escalate to the Harmonisation Committee based on the risk score difference.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision:** Determines if the system auto-approves the application or if it requires Final Micro-loan Board Approval.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA):** The board votes to approve or reject applications ≥ €15 000.

**Required documents at submission time:**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

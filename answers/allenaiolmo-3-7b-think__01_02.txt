

**Decision Points:**  
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Determines if risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤2 points. If not, escalates to the Harmonisation Committee for resolution before proceeding.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**: Auto-approves loans <€15,000; requires proceeding to Final Micro-loan Board Approval for loans ≥€15,000.  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Board votes to approve or reject the loan, ending the process.  

**Required Documents:**  
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

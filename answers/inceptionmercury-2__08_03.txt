4. **T5** requires one token in `pd` and one token in `pe`. Both are present, so T5 can fire, consuming those two tokens and producing a token in `pf`.

- `T1` is mandatory to generate the initial split.  
- To enable `T5` we must obtain both `pd` and `pe`.

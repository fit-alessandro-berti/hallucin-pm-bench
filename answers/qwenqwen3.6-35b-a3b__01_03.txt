**a) Decision Points**
1. **Conditional Branch (Post-Risk Check)** – The workflow bypasses manual underwriting and proceeds directly to the decision gate only if the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.  
2. **Decision Gate (“TriVote”)** – The application advances to the offer stage only if at least two of the three required reviewers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) approve it.  

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**
1. Form 14B  
2. Deck Memo  
3. Offer Sheet 77  
4. ID Matrix Declaration  
5. Loan Dossier 2025  

*(Note: The three Tri‑Color Scorecards are specified as analytical inputs rather than formal process documents, so they are excluded from this list.)*

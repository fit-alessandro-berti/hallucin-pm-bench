### a) Decision points in Acme’s small-business loan process

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** The application is assigned a **Low, Medium, or High** risk rating based on **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow**.  
2. **Manual-Underwriting skip condition:** **Manual-Underwriting-Review is skipped** only when the risk rating is **Low or Medium** **and** the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**; otherwise, step 5 is performed.  
3. **TriVote gate:** The decision passes when **at least two of the three** required approvers — **Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer** — approve.

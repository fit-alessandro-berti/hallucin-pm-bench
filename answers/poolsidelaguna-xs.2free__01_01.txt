
## Decision Points in Contoso’s Flight-Refund Process  

| # | Where in the process | What is being decided |
|---|----------------------|-----------------------|
| 1 | After **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** | Whether the passenger qualifies for a refund (eligibility decision). |
| 2 | After **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** | Whether the refund passes financial pre-approval. |
| 3 | After the pre-approval step | Whether the claim is **> €1 000** – if yes, route to **Senior_Auditor_Review**; if no, continue straight to payment. |
| 4 | Before **Process_Refund_Payment** | All required approvals and documentation must be in place (Pre-Approval + optionally Senior Auditor Review + Bank-Transfer Authorization). |

---

## Required Documents & Artefacts  

- **RefundRequestForm** – completed online by the passenger.  
- **FlightManifest** – used by the triage agent for eligibility checking.  
- **WeatherReport** – used by the triage agent for eligibility checking.  
- **EligibilityChecklist** – consulted by the finance controller for pre-approval.  
- **AuditTrail** – assembled for high-value claims and reviewed by the Senior Auditor.  
- **BankTransferAuthorization** – mandatory before any refund payment can be processed.  
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** – issued and emailed to the passenger after payment.

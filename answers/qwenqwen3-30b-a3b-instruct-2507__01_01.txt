### Decision Points in Contoso’s Flight-Refund Process

1. **Eligibility Determination (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)**  
   - **Decision**: Whether the passenger’s claim is eligible for refund based on flight and weather conditions.  
   - **Criteria**: Match between the passenger’s flight details (from *FlightManifest*) and the verified weather event (from *WeatherReport*).  
   - **Outcome**: Eligible or Ineligible claim.

2. **Pre-Approval Decision (Finance_Controller_PreApprove)**  
   - **Decision**: Whether to proceed with the refund based on the *EligibilityChecklist*.  
   - **Criteria**: All checklist items are satisfied (e.g., valid form, eligibility confirmed, documentation complete).  
   - **Outcome**: Pre-approval granted or denied.

3. **High-Value Claim Threshold Check (Triggering Senior_Auditor_Review)**  
   - **Decision**: Whether the refund amount exceeds €1,000, thus requiring additional review.  
   - **Criteria**: Refund amount calculated from claim (not explicitly stated, but implied by process logic).  
   - **Outcome**: If > €1,000 → proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**; otherwise, skip it.

4. **Audit Review Completion (Senior_Auditor_Review)**  
   - **Decision**: Whether the audit review is successfully completed and documented.  
   - **Criteria**: Completion of audit and inclusion of *AuditTrail* in the case file.  
   - **Outcome**: Review passed (required for high-value claims) or failed (blocks payment).

### Required Documents (artefacts explicitly defined)

- **RefundRequestForm** – submitted by customer (required at start).
- **FlightManifest** – used by triage agent to validate flight details.
- **WeatherReport** – used by triage agent to assess weather-related eligibility.
- **EligibilityChecklist** – used by finance controller to pre-approve.
- **AuditTrail** – required **only** for high-value claims (i.e., refund > €1,000).
- **BankTransferAuthorization** – signed document required before payment execution.
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** – generated and emailed to passenger after payment.

> ✅ **Note**: No other documents or artefacts are defined in the process. The activity “CustomerService_Agent_Decide” is explicitly excluded. All decisions and documents are strictly as per the defined process.

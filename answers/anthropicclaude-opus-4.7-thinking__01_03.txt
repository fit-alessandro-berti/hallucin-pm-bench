## a) Decision Points in Acme's Small-Business Loan Process

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3)** — Classifies the application as Low, Medium, or High risk based on the Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards.

2. **Manual-Underwriting-Review Skip Condition (between Steps 4 and 5)** — If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review) is skipped; otherwise it is performed.

3. **"TriVote" Decision Gate (Step 6)** — Requires votes from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, passing when at least two of the three approve.

## b) Documents Required (in order of first appearance)

1. **Form 14B** — at Step 1 (Receive-Application)
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** — at Step 3 (Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** — at Step 3
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** — at Step 3
5. **Deck Memo** — at Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review)
6. **Offer Sheet 77** — at Step 7 (Prepare-Binding-Offer)
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** — at Step 8 (Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
8. **ID Matrix Declaration** — at Step 8
9. **Loan Dossier 2025** — at Step 9 (Finalize-Agreement)

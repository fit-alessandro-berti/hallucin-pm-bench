### **a) Decision Points in Acme Bank’s Small-Business Loan-Approval Process**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3 → Step 4)**
   *Rule:* If the combined risk rating from the **Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards** is **Low or Medium** **AND** the requested loan amount is **≤ €250,000**, skip **Manual Underwriting Review (Step 5)**.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6)**
   *Rule:* The loan passes if **any two out of three approvers** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve it.

---

### **b) Documents Required in the Process (in Order of First Appearance)**

1. **Form 14B** (Step 1 – Receive Application)
2. **Deck Memo** (Step 5 – Manual Underwriting Review)
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7 – Prepare Binding Offer)
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8 – Request Applicant Confirmation)
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9 – Finalize Agreement)

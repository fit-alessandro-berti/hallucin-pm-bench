**EXECUTIVE MEMO: TOP WAITING-TIME BOTTLENECKS**

Based on the last 12 months, our three worst-performing activities by waiting time are:

1.  **Request_Documents:** Avg. 36.5 hours, 95th-pct 120.0 hours.
    *   **Action:** Implement automated, timed reminders to claimants and agents to submit outstanding documents.
2.  **Send_Closure_Letter:** Avg. 12.2 hours, 95th-pct 44.0 hours.
    *   **Action:** Create a nightly batch process to automatically generate and queue all closure letters for approved/rejected claims.
3.  **Assess_Liability:** Avg. 7.8 hours, 95th-pct 16.4 hours.
    *   **Action:** Introduce parallel-case reviews for claims exceeding the average wait time to prevent queue pile-up.

These targeted actions are projected to reduce each activity's waiting time by over 20%.

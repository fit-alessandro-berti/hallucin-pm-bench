**a) Decision Points**

*   **Tri-Color Risk Conditional Branch:** If the application receives a Low or Medium risk rating *and* the requested amount is €250,000 or less, the process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review step.
*   **TriVote Decision Gate:** The loan passes this gate if it receives approvals from at least two out of the three mandatory roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer).

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**

1. Form 14B
2. Deck Memo
3. Offer Sheet 77
4. ID Matrix Declaration
5. Loan Dossier 2025

**a) Summary of every decision point:**  
1. **Decision after Receiving-Application:** Checks if the application (Form 14B) is complete.  
2. **Assign-Deck-Reviewer:** Assigns a Deck Officer to manage the application.  
3. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** Determines risk rating ({Low, Medium, High}) based on Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow inputs.  
4. **Conditional Skip:** Skips Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5) if risk is Low/Medium and requested-amount ≤ €250 000.  
5. **Manual-Underwriting-Review:** Requires a Deck Memo documenting a manual review of collateral/applicant circumstances.  
6. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”:** Approves the loan if at least two of three roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve; requires no third approval if risk is Low/Medium.  
7. **Decision after TriVote:** Greenlights loan processing if approved; if denied, winds down.  
8. **Request-Applicant-Confirmation:** Collects signed Offer Sheet 77 and ID Matrix Declaration from the applicant.  
9. **Finalize-Agreement:** Documents acceptance in Loan Dossier 2025.  

**b) Documents requiring first appearance, by step:**  
1. **Form 14B** (Received-Application step).  
2. **Deck Memo** (Manual-Underwriting-Review step if required).  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Prep-Binding-Offer step).  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Request-Applicant-Confirmation step).  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalize-Agreement step).

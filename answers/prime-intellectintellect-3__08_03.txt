### Key Points
- **T5 fires before T3:** In this sequence, **T5** fires at step 4, while **T3** never fires (and is never enabled before **T5** fires). Thus, **T5** occurs before any potential firing of **T3**.
- **Shortest Sequence:** This sequence has 4 transitions. No shorter sequence exists because:

**Conclusion:** It is possible for **T5** to fire before **T3**, and the shortest sequence achieving this is `T1 → T2b → T4 → T5`.

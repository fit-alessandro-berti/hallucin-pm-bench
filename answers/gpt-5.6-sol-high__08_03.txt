Yes. The shortcut **T2b** can produce the token in `pd` without firing **T3**.

One shortest firing sequence is:

**T1 → T2b → T4 → T5**

Markings:

1. After `T1`: `pa=1, pb=1`
2. After `T2b`: `pd=1, pb=1`
3. After `T4`: `pd=1, pe=1`
4. Therefore `T5` is enabled and fires, before any firing of `T3`.

This is shortest because `T5` requires both `pd` and `pe`: reaching them requires `T1`, followed by `T2b` and `T4`, before `T5` can fire. (`T2b` and `T4` may occur in either order.)

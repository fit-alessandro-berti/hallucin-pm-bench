**To:** Chief Operating Officer  
**From:** Senior Process‑Performance Analyst  
**Date:** 2025‑09‑29  
**Subject:** Top‑3 SLA‑Breach & Waiting‑Time Hotspots  

Analysis of the eight core activities shows that **Legal Approval**, **Risk Review**, and **Credit Assessment** jointly exhibit the largest SLA overruns and the highest average waiting times. Legal Approval’s average throughput of 900 s exceeds its 600 s SLA by **300 s** and carries a 600 s waiting time. Risk Review runs 600 s versus a 300 s SLA, a **300 s** breach, with a 480 s wait. Credit Assessment averages 300 s against a 240 s SLA, a **60 s** breach, and a 200 s wait. These three steps dominate cycle‑time inflation and customer‑visible delays.

- **Legal Approval:** Introduce parallel legal‑review lanes for standard‑term contracts, reducing average throughput by targeting the 300 s excess.  
- **Risk Review:** Deploy automated risk‑scoring rules for low‑exposure cases to cut the 300 s SLA gap and lower the 480 s wait.  
- **Credit Assessment:** Implement a fast‑track scoring model for applicants with clean bureau data, aiming to eliminate the 60 s breach and trim the 200 s wait.

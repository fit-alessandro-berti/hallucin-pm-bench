# Acme Bank's Micro-loan Fast-Track Process Summary

## Decision Points

The process contains **three key decision points**:

### 1. Underwriter Alignment Gate (after Step 4)
- **Condition:** Compare risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B
- **If scores differ by ≤ 2 points:** Continue to Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)
- **If scores differ by > 2 points:** Escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break decision, then continue

### 2. Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7)
- **Condition:** Evaluate the loan amount requested
- **If amount < €15,000:** System auto-approves → proceed to Disburse Funds
- **If amount ≥ €15,000:** Proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)

### 3. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (Step 8)
- **Condition:** Board votes on applications ≥ €15,000
- **Outcomes:** *Approve* or *Reject*

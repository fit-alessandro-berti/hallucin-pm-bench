**Decision points in the Contoso Airlines refund process**

| # | Decision point (where it occurs) | What is decided |
|---|-----------------------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** | *Is the claim eligible?* – checked against the **FlightManifest** and the **WeatherReport**. |
| 2 | **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** | *Does the finance controller sign off?* – based on the **EligibilityChecklist**. |
| 3 | **High‑value threshold (implicit after step 2)** | *Is the refund amount > €1,000?* – if **yes**, the case routes to **Senior_Auditor_Review**; if **no**, it can go straight to payment. |
| 4 | **Senior_Auditor_Review** (only for amounts > €1,000) | *Does the senior auditor approve?* – produces the **AuditTrail**. |
| 5 | **Payment readiness check (gate before Process_Refund_Payment)** | *Have all prerequisites been met?* – Finance Controller pre‑approval succeeded **and** (if high‑value) Senior Auditor review completed. |
| 6 | **BankTransferAuthorization presence (gate before Process_Refund_Payment)** | *Is the signed BankTransferAuthorization available?* – required to start payment execution. |

These are the only points where a yes/no or go/no‑go decision is made; no other decision activities exist in the defined process.

**Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Compares the risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the scores differ by ≤ 2 points, the process continues to the Neighbourhood Feedback Check. If they differ by more than 2 points, the application is escalated to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Evaluates the requested loan amount. If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves. If the amount is ≥ €15 000, the application proceeds to the Final Micro-loan Board Approval.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The board reviews the application and casts a vote to either approve or reject it before Disburse Funds can occur.

**Required Documents at Submission Time**

* Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
* Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
* Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
* Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
* Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
* Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

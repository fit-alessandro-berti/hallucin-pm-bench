### a) Decision points

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow are used to assign a **Low, Medium, or High** risk rating.  
2. **Manual-review branch:** Skip Manual-Underwriting-Review only when the risk is **Low or Medium** and the requested amount is **€250,000 or less**; otherwise, perform the review.  
3. **TriVote gate:** The Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer must all participate, and the application passes when **at least two of the three approve**.

### b) Documents in order of first appearance

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**  
5. **Deck Memo** — required only if Manual-Underwriting-Review is not skipped  
6. **Offer Sheet 77**  
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77**  
8. **ID Matrix Declaration**  
9. **Loan Dossier 2025**

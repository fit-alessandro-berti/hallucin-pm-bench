**MEMORANDUM**

**To**: COO, Orion Insurance
**From**: Process Mining Analyst
**Date**: October 26, 2023
**Subject**: Process Bottleneck Analysis based on Waiting Time

This memo highlights activities experiencing critical delays. The three worst-performing activities by waiting time are:

1.  **Request_Documents**: Avg: 36.5h, 95p: 120.0h. **Action:** Automate document requests using pre-populated templates.
2.  **Send_Closure_Letter**: Avg: 12.2h, 95p: 44.0h. **Action:** Implement automated letter generation and dispatch.
3.  **Assess_Liability**: Avg: 7.8h, 95p: 16.4h. **Action:** Integrate an initial liability assessment module to reduce manual review.

These actions are data-backed and designed to reduce waiting times significantly.

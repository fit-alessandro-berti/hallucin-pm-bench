#### Legend (RACI Definitions)
- **R (Responsible)**: Person who performs the work for the activity  
- **A (Accountable)**: Person ultimately answerable for the activity’s correct completion (exactly one per activity)  
- **C (Consulted)**: Stakeholder whose input is sought (two-way communication)  
- **I (Informed)**: Stakeholder kept updated on progress (one-way communication)

*All terminology strictly adheres to FinCo’s preferred vocabulary rules; no prohibited legacy terms are used.*

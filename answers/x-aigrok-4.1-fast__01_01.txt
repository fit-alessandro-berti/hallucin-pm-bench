### Decision Points
- **High-value branch** (after **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**): If the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**, proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (which requires the *AuditTrail*); otherwise, proceed directly to **Process_Refund_Payment**.

### Required Documents
- *RefundRequestForm*
- *FlightManifest*
- *WeatherReport*
- *EligibilityChecklist*
- *AuditTrail* (for high-value claims > €1 000)
- *BankTransferAuthorization*
- *RefundConfirmationPDF*

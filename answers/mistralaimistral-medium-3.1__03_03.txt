### Key Features:
- **Strict adherence to requirements**: Only modeled elements explicitly mentioned (no invented paths).
- **Gateways**:
  - Exclusive for claim completeness (`wrc_gw_completeness`).
  - Exclusive for warranty decision (`wrc_gw_eligibility`).
  - Parallel split/join for concurrent tasks (`wrc_gw_parallel_split`/`wrc_gw_parallel_join`).
- **Two end events**: Rejection (`wrc_end_rejected`) and completion (`wrc_end_completed`).
- **IDs prefixed**: All elements use `wrc_` prefix.
- **Task names**: Verbatim matches (e.g., `"requests missing information"`).

**Q2:** A minimal firing sequence is:

**[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]**

After **T1**, tokens are placed in P1 and P2. **T2** and **T7** move the P1 branch to P4, and **T4** moves that token to P5. P2 still contains its token, so **T5** is enabled by tokens in P2 and P5 and can fire before **T3**.

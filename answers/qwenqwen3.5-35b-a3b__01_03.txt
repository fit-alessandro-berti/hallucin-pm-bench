### **a) Decision Points Summary**

1.  **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** The process bypasses Manual-Underwriting-Review if the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Decision Gate “TriVote” (Step 6):** A loan application passes this gate only if at least two of the three specified officials (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, or Sustainability Officer) grant their approval.

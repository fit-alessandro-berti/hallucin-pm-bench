Contoso’s process has **one explicit decision point**:

1. **Refund amount > €1,000?**  
   - **Yes** → the case goes to **Senior_Auditor_Review**, and this review must include the **AuditTrail**.  
   - **No** → the case skips **Senior_Auditor_Review** and can proceed toward **Process_Refund_Payment** once **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded.

Note: **CustomerService_Agent_Decide** is **not part of Contoso’s process**.

### Required documents / artefacts
- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail** — required **only for high-value claims** (> €1,000)
- **BankTransferAuthorization**
- **RefundConfirmationPDF**

Here’s the **RACI matrix** for FinCo’s **order-to-cash workflow**, strictly adhering to the approved terminology:

| **Activity**               | **Account Manager (AM)** | **Compliance Officer (CO)** | **Finance Clerk (FC)** | **IT Support (IT)** |
|----------------------------|--------------------------|-----------------------------|------------------------|---------------------|
| **Receive Application**    | **R** (Responsible)      | I (Informed)                | I (Informed)           | I (Informed)        |
| **KYC Review**             | I (Informed)             | **R** (Responsible)         | I (Informed)           | I (Informed)        |
| **Order Vetting**          | **R** (Responsible)      | A (Approver)               | I (Informed)           | I (Informed)        |
| **Payment Clearance**      | I (Informed)             | I (Informed)                | **R** (Responsible)    | I (Informed)        |
| **Record Update**          | I (Informed)             | I (Informed)                | I (Informed)           | **R** (Responsible) |
| **Notify Customer**        | **R** (Responsible)      | I (Informed)                | I (Informed)           | I (Informed)        |

### **Key Notes:**
- **R (Responsible):** Performs the task.
- **A (Approver):** Must sign off (e.g., CO approves Order Vetting).
- **C (Consulted):** Not applicable in this workflow.
- **I (Informed):** Notified of progress/outcome.

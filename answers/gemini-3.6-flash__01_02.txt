Based on Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process, here is the summary of the decision points and the list of all required documents:

---

### **Decision Points**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**
   * **Condition:** Check if the risk score from **Senior Underwriter A** and the risk score from **Shadow Underwriter B** differ by **≤ 2 points**.
   * **Path 1 (Difference ≤ 2):** Continue directly to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
   * **Path 2 (Difference > 2):** Escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**
   * **Condition:** Evaluate the requested loan amount.
   * **Path 1 (Amount < €15 000):** The system auto-approves the loan (proceed to **Disburse Funds**).
   * **Path 2 (Amount ≥ €15 000):** Proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** *(Applicable only if amount ≥ €15 000)*
   * **Condition:** The board votes on the application.
   * **Path 1 (Approve):** Proceed to **Disburse Funds**.
   * **Path 2 (Reject):** The application is rejected.

---

### **Required Documents**

* Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
* Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
* Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
* Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
* Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
* Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

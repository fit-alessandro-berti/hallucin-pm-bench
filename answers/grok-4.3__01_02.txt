**Decision Points** in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process:

- **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Checks whether the risk score (0–9) assigned by Senior Underwriter A and the independent risk score (0–9) assigned by Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points. If the difference is greater, the case is escalated to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision.
- **Amount Threshold Decision**: Determines whether the micro-loan amount is < €15 000 (system auto-approves) or ≥ €15 000 (proceeds to Final Micro-loan Board Approval).
- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Micro-loan Board votes to approve or reject the application.

**Required documents** (at submission time):
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

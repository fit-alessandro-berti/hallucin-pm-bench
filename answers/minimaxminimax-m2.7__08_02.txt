**Answer 1:** Yes – T5 can fire before T3.

**Reason:** After firing T1 the marking contains tokens P1 and P2. Firing T2 → P3, then T7 → P4 and T4 → P5 puts a token on P5 while the token on P2 is still present. Both prerequisites of T5 (P2 and P5) are now satisfied, so T5 may fire even though T3 has not yet occurred.

**Subject:** Targeted Wait-Time Reduction Strategy

Analysis of the last 12 months identifies the three primary bottlenecks by waiting time:

1.  **Request Documents:** Avg: 36.5h | 95p: 120.0h.
    *   *Action:* Implement an automated customer portal with real-time SMS alerts to replace manual document requests.
2.  **Send Closure Letter:** Avg: 12.2h | 95p: 44.0h.
    *   *Action:* Deploy robotic process automation (RPA) to trigger and send letters immediately upon claim finalization.
3.  **Assess_Liability:** Avg: 7.8h | 95p: 16.4h.
    *   *Action:* Introduce a rule-based triage system to auto-assign liability files based on adjuster availability and expertise.

Focusing resources on these specific activities will significantly streamline our workflow and reduce overall cycle time.

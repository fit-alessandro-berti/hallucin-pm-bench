Here are the decision points and required documents for Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process:

**Decision Points:**

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate:** This is where the scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B are compared.
    *   Decision: If scores differ by ≤ 2 points, proceed to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
    *   Decision: Else (scores differ by > 2 points), escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision:** This is where the requested loan amount is evaluated.
    *   Decision: If amount < €15 000, the system auto-approves.
    *   Decision: If amount ≥ €15 000, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA):** The Micro-loan Board makes a final decision.
    *   Decision: Board votes *approve*.
    *   Decision: Board votes *reject*.

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

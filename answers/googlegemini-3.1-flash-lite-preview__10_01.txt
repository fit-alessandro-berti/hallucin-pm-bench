As a senior process-mapping consultant for FinCo, I have developed the following RACI matrix for the order-to-cash workflow, utilizing the mandated terminology.

### Order-to-Cash RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Receive Application | R | I | I | I |
| **KYC Review** | I | R | I | I |
| **Order Vetting** | R | I | I | I |
| **Payment Clearance** | I | I | R | I |
| Record Update | I | I | I | R |
| Notify Customer | R | I | I | I |

***

**Key:** 
*   **R** = Responsible (The person who performs the work)
*   **A** = Accountable (The person who must sign off on the work – *Note: In this specific workflow, the Account Manager holds accountability for the overall order lifecycle*)
*   **C** = Consulted (People whose opinions are sought)
*   **I** = Informed (People who are kept up-to-date)

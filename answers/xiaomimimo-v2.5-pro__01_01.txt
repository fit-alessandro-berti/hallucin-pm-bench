# Contoso Airlines Flight-Refund Process — Decision Points & Required Documents

---

## Decision Points

| # | Decision Point | Basis for Decision | Outcome |
|---|---------------|-------------------|---------|
| 1 | **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** | Agent cross-references the claim against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport** | Determines whether the claim is eligible to proceed |
| 2 | **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** | Controller evaluates the claim using the **EligibilityChecklist** | Grants or denies pre-approval |
| 3 | **High-value threshold check** | System/process checks whether the refund amount exceeds **€1,000** | If **≤ €1,000** → skip to Process_Refund_Payment; if **> €1,000** → route to Senior_Auditor_Review |
| 4 | **Senior_Auditor_Review** *(high-value only)* | Auditor reviews the case including the **AuditTrail** | Authorizes or blocks the high-value refund |

**Note:** The legacy activity "CustomerService_Agent_Decide" found in many textbooks **does not exist** in Contoso's process.

---

## Required Documents / Artefacts

| Document | Role in Process |
|----------|----------------|
| **RefundRequestForm** | Submitted by the passenger at claim initiation (Step 1) |
| **FlightManifest** | Used by triage agent to validate eligibility (Step 2) |
| **WeatherReport** | Used by triage agent to validate eligibility (Step 2) |
| **EligibilityChecklist** | Used by finance controller for pre-approval (Step 3) |
| **AuditTrail** | Required for Senior_Auditor_Review on high-value claims (Step 4) |
| **BankTransferAuthorization** | Must be signed and present before payment can execute (Step 5) |
| **RefundConfirmationPDF** | Emailed to the passenger as confirmation (Step 6) |

---

### Payment Gate (Constraint)

**Process_Refund_Payment** may **only** begin when:

1. ✅ **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded, **AND**
2. ✅ For high-value claims (> €1,000): **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed

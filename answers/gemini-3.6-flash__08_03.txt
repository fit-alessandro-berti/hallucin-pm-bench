**Yes**, it is possible for **T5** to fire before (and without) transition **T3** ever firing.

### Shortest Firing Sequence
One shortest sequence (4 transitions) is:
$$\text{T1} \rightarrow \text{T2b} \rightarrow \text{T4} \rightarrow \text{T5}$$

*(Note: $\text{T1} \rightarrow \text{T4} \rightarrow \text{T2b} \rightarrow \text{T5}$ is an equivalent shortest sequence of length 4.)*

---

### Step-by-Step Verification

1. **Initial Marking:** `p0 = 1`
2. **Fire T1:** Consumes `p0`, produces `pa = 1, pb = 1`.
3. **Fire T2b:** Consumes `pa`, produces `pd = 1` *(bypassing `pc` and T3 entirely)*.
4. **Fire T4:** Consumes `pb`, produces `pe = 1`.
5. **Fire T5:** Enabled by `pd = 1` and `pe = 1`; consumes both to produce `pf = 1`.

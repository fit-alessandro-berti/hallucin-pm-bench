**FinCo Order-to-Cash RACI Matrix**

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|----------|----------------------|-------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Receive Application | R/A | I | I | I |
| KYC Review | C | R/A | I | I |
| Order Vetting | R/A | I | I | I |
| Payment Clearance | C | A | R | I |
| Record Update | I | I | I | R/A |
| Notify Customer | R/A | I | I | I |

**Legend**  
R = Responsible (executes the task)  
A = Accountable (owns the outcome/approves)  
C = Consulted (provides input/expertise)  
I = Informed (receives updates)  

*Matrix reflects role assignments strictly aligned with the approved FinCo workflow terminology.*

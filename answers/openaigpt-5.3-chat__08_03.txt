Yes.

T5 needs tokens in **pd** and **pe**.  
- **pd** can be produced by **T2b** (without using T3).  
- **pe** is produced by **T4**.  

From the initial marking, both can be created without firing **T3**.

One shortest firing sequence is:

T1 → T2b → T4 → T5

State progression (tokens relevant to T5):
- After **T1**: pa=1, pb=1  
- After **T2b**: pd=1, pb=1  
- After **T4**: pd=1, pe=1  
- **T5** is now enabled and fires (producing pf)

Since **T3** has not fired anywhere in this sequence, **T5 fires before T3**, so the ordering is possible.

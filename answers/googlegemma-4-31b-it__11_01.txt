**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** COO, Orion Insurance
**FROM:** Senior Process-Mining Analyst
**SUBJECT:** Critical Waiting-Time Bottlenecks

Our analysis identifies the following three activities as the primary drivers of process delay:

1. **Request_Documents:** Avg: 36.5h | 95p: 120.0h. 
   *Action:* Implement an automated customer portal for instant document uploads to eliminate manual intake lags.
2. **Send_Closure_Letter:** Avg: 12.2h | 95p: 44.0h. 
   *Action:* Transition to automated digital notifications triggered immediately upon claim resolution.
3. **Assess_Liability:** Avg: 7.8h | 95p: 16.4h. 
   *Action:* Deploy a triage rule-set to route low-complexity claims to a dedicated fast-track queue.

Addressing these specific bottlenecks will significantly reduce overall cycle times.

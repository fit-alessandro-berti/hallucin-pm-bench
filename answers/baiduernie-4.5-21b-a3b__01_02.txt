**Summary of Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**
1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** completion  
2. **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)** evaluation  
3. **Dual Underwriter Split** alignment (primary/shadow risk score discrepancy ≤ 2 points)  
4. **Neutral Harmonisation Committee** resolution (if underwriter variance > 2 points)  
5. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** validation  
6. **Amount Threshold Decision** (≥ €15 000 requires Final Micro-loan Board Approval)  
7. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** vote (approve/reject)  

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**
▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

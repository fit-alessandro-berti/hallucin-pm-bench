TO: Chief Operating Officer
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
SUBJECT: Priority Remediation for Top Three SLA-Breaching Activities

Analysis of average throughput and waiting times reveals three activities that are jointly breaching their SLAs by substantial margins. Legal Approval averages 900 seconds against a 600-second SLA, exceeding it by 300 seconds while carrying 600 seconds of waiting time across 465 cases. Risk Review averages 600 seconds versus a 300-second SLA, exceeding it by 300 seconds with 480 seconds of waiting time across 480 cases. Credit Assessment averages 300 seconds versus a 240-second SLA, exceeding it by 60 seconds with 200 seconds of waiting time across 1,175 cases.

• Legal Approval: Cut the 600-second average waiting time by 300 seconds for the 465 cases to bring the 900-second throughput within the 600-second SLA.
• Risk Review: Cut the 480-second average waiting time by 300 seconds for the 480 cases to bring the 600-second throughput within the 300-second SLA.
• Credit Assessment: Cut the 200-second average waiting time by 60 seconds for the 1,175 cases to bring the 300-second throughput within the 240-second SLA.

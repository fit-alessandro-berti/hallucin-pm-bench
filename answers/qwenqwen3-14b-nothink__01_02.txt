### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   – Confirm ID card and mobile phone.  
   – *No credit history checks performed.*

2. **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)**  
   – Conducted by an Impact Officer.  
   – *No explicit decision point, but a prerequisite for underwriting.*

3. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   – Compare **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** scores.  
   – *If scores differ by > 2 points*, escalate to **Harmonisation Committee**.

4. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**  
   – Collect **at least 3 local references**.

5. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   – *If amount < €15 000*: auto-approve.  
   – *If amount ≥ €15 000*: proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

6. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   – Board votes to **approve** or **reject**.

---

### **Required Documents at Submission Time**

- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

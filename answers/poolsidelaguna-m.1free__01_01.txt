
Based on the Contoso Airlines flight-refund process description, here are the decision points and required documents:

## Decision Points

**1. High-value claim threshold check**
- **Location**: After Finance_Controller_PreApprove (Step 4)
- **Decision**: Is the refund amount greater than €1 000?
- **Outcome if Yes**: Route to Senior_Auditor_Review → then Process_Refund_Payment
- **Outcome if No**: Proceed directly to Process_Refund_Payment

## Required Documents

1. **RefundRequestForm** - completed by passenger during Customer_Submit_Claim
2. **FlightManifest** - consulted during Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility
3. **WeatherReport** - consulted during Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility
4. **EligibilityChecklist** - signed by finance controller during Finance_Controller_PreApprove
5. **AuditTrail** - required for Senior_Auditor_Review (for high-value claims only)
6. **BankTransferAuthorization** - required before Process_Refund_Payment can execute
7. **RefundConfirmationPDF** - generated and emailed during Issue_Confirmation_Letter

Yes. The shortcut **T2b** produces `pd` without firing **T3**.

One shortest firing sequence is:

**T1 → T2b → T4 → T5**

Markings:

1. After **T1**: `pa=1, pb=1`
2. After **T2b**: `pd=1, pb=1`
3. After **T4**: `pd=1, pe=1`
4. **T5** is enabled and fires, while **T3** has not fired.

This is shortest because enabling **T5** requires both `pd` and `pe`: before any **T3**, these require **T2b** and **T4**, respectively, and **T1** must first supply their input tokens.

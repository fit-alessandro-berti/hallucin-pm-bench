**RACI Table – AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process**

| Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|----------|---------------------|---------------------|------------|
| 1. Receive and log transfer instruction | **R/A** | – | – |
| 2. Screen transaction against sanctions list | **R/A** | – | – |
| 3. Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary | – | **R/A** | – |
| 4. Approve high-risk transaction | I | **R/A** | – |
| 5. Release payment | **R/A** | – | – |
| 6. Send SWIFT message | – | – | **R/A** |
| 7. Archive case file and notify Regulatory Liaison | – | I | **R/A** |

**Key:** **R** = Responsible, **A** = Accountable, **C** = Consulted, **I** = Informed, – = No involvement.

**Decision Points**

1. **High-value threshold check**: The process evaluates whether the refund amount is greater than €1,000. If true, the case must proceed to the Senior_Auditor_Review; if false, this step is bypassed.
2. **Pre-approval success check**: The process evaluates whether the Finance_Controller_PreApprove step has succeeded. Process_Refund_Payment cannot begin until this condition is met (and, for high-value claims, until the Senior_Auditor_Review is also completed).

**Required Documents**

1. RefundRequestForm
2. FlightManifest
3. WeatherReport
4. EligibilityChecklist
5. AuditTrail
6. BankTransferAuthorization
7. RefundConfirmationPDF

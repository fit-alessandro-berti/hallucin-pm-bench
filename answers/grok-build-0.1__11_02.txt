**Executive Memo: Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance**

The three worst-performing activities are Request_Documents, Review_Documents, and Initial_Assessment.

Request_Documents shows the highest wait time (150 minutes) and rework rate (22.0%), with throughput of 190 and 5.5% SLA breaches. Recommended action: Increase staffing dedicated to document requests to reduce the 150-minute average wait time.

Review_Documents has the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), longest processing time (25 minutes), highest variability (stdev 9 minutes), and 30-minute wait time. Recommended action: Introduce standardized review checklists and templates to lower processing time and variability.

Initial_Assessment has the third-highest wait time (18 minutes) and 3.2% SLA breaches. Recommended action: Add capacity to the initial assessment team to bring average wait time below 10 minutes.

These conclusions are drawn strictly from the dashboard metrics.

FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
SUBJECT: Critical Workflow Analysis

Workflow data identifies Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment as the worst-performing activities due to combined SLA breaches and extended queue delays. Legal Approval exceeds its target SLA by 300 seconds with a 600-second average waiting time. Risk Review surpasses its target SLA by 300 seconds with a 480-second average waiting time. Credit Assessment breaches its target SLA by 60 seconds with a 200-second average waiting time. These bottlenecks impact 465, 480, and 1,175 cases respectively. Immediate remediation requires:

• Redeploy Legal Approval staff to clear the 600-second queue and eliminate the 300-second SLA overage.
• Restructure Risk Review workflows to compress the 480-second wait and correct the 300-second target deviation.
• Streamline Credit Assessment validation to reduce the 200-second delay and resolve the 60-second SLA excess.

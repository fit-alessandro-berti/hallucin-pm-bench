# Process B – Concurrent Task Sets

**Set 1** (Parallel Split #1, after *Receive_Stock*):
- Quality_Check
- Update_Inventory

These two tasks lie on separate branches of the first parallel gateway and may execute concurrently. Both must complete before the flow merges and proceeds to *Store_Items*.

**Set 2** (Parallel Split #2, after *Store_Items*):
- Generate_Replenishment_Report
- Notify_Procurement

These two tasks lie on separate branches of the second parallel gateway and may execute concurrently. Both must complete before the flow merges and the process ends.

No other tasks in Process B can execute concurrently — *Receive_Stock* and *Store_Items* are strictly sequential steps between the parallel constructs, and tasks from Set 1 cannot overlap with tasks from Set 2 because Merge #1 must complete before Split #2 begins.

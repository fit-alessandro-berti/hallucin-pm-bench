**a) Decision points (gates or conditional branches):**

- **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**: Produces a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High based on the three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow).
- **Risk-and-Amount Skip Gate** (after step 3): If the risk rating is Low or Medium **AND** the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, then step 5 (Manual Underwriting Review) is skipped.
- **TriVote Decision Gate** (step 6): Requires participation by the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer; the loan proceeds if at least two of the three approve.

1. Form 14B  
2. Deck Memo  
3. Offer Sheet 77  
4. ID Matrix Declaration  
5. Loan Dossier 2025

Here is the summary of changes between BPMN_v1 and BPMN_v2:

**Added tasks**
• Verify Identity (`Task_VerifyIdentity`)
• Request Additional Documents (`Task_RequestDocs`)
• Final Reject (`Task_FinalReject`)

**Removed tasks**
• Reject Loan (`Task_RejectLoan`)

**Gateway-type changes**
• `Gateway_Decision`: Changed from **Exclusive Gateway** (XOR) to **Inclusive Gateway** (OR).

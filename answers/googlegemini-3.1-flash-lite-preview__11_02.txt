**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**From:** Process Performance Analyst  
**Date:** April 15, 2025  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance Report

Our Q1 analysis identifies three critical bottlenecks hindering operational efficiency:

1. **Request_Documents:** Exhibits the highest wait time (150 mins) and an alarmingly high rework rate (22.0%), indicating unclear initial instructions.  
   **Recommendation:** Implement an automated checklist during `Initial_Assessment` to ensure complete documentation requests at the first touchpoint, reducing rework.

2. **Review_Documents:** Shows the highest processing time (25 mins) and the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%). High standard deviation (9 mins) suggests inconsistent reviewer speed or document complexity.  
   **Recommendation:** Conduct a root-cause analysis on high-latency cases to establish standardized document-review templates, reducing variability and processing time.

3. **Initial_Assessment:** Significant queueing time (18 mins) relative to its throughput, suggesting a resource shortage during this mandatory gateway step.  
   **Recommendation:** Reallocate administrative floaters to this activity during peak arrival hours to reduce the 18-minute wait, improving upstream flow. 

While these areas are statistically distinct as the primary offenders, further optimization should await these structural adjustments to ensure downstream stability.

**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**From:** Senior Process Performance Analyst  
**Date:** April 15, 2025  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance Review  

Based on Q1 data, the three weakest activities are:

1.  **Request_Documents**  
    **Issue:** Severe bottleneck with the highest average wait time (150 minutes) and a high rework rate (22.0%).  
    **Action:** Implement an automated document request system with real-time tracking to reduce queueing and rework.

2.  **Review_Documents**  
    **Issue:** High processing time variability (stdev 9 min) and the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), indicating inconsistent workflow.  
    **Action:** Introduce standardized checklists and agent training to reduce variability and meet SLAs.

3.  **Initial_Assessment**  
    **Issue:** High SLA breach rate (3.2%) despite moderate wait time, suggesting process complexity or skill gaps.  
    **Action:** Conduct a root-cause analysis on breached cases and refine assessment guidelines to improve first-pass accuracy.

These actions target the core metrics driving delays and inconsistencies in the claims flow.

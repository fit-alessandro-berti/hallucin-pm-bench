**RACI Matrix for High-Value Wire-Transfer Request**

| Activity | Front-Office Analyst | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | Treasury Ops |
|----------|----------------------|--------------------|--------------------|--------------|
| Intake Vetting | **R** | C | A | I |
| KYC Review | C | **R** | A | I |
| AML Screening | C | **R** | A | I |
| Risk Scoring | C | **R** | A | I |
| (Escalation for score > 70) | I | C | **A** | I |
| Treasury Release | I | I | C | **R** |
| Notification to Analyst | **R** | I | I | C |

**Legend**
- **R**: Responsible (performs the activity)  
- **A**: Accountable (owns the activity outcome)  
- **C**: Consulted (provides input/expertise)  
- **I**: Informed (kept aware of progress)  

*Note: “Escalation for score > 70” is a sub‑step of Risk Scoring, and Treasury Release follows approval.*

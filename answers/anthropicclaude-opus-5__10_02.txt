## AtlasRemit — Cross-Border Transfer Process: RACI Matrix

**Legend:** R = Responsible (does the work) · A = Accountable (owns the outcome, one per task) · C = Consulted · I = Informed

| # | Process Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support | Customer |
|---|--------------|:-------------------:|:------------------:|:----------:|:--------:|
| 1 | Receive customer transfer instruction and log it in the system | **A / R** | I | C | C |
| 2 | Screen the payment against the sanctions list | **R** | **A** | C | — |
| 3 | Perform **KYC Review** on sender and beneficiary | C | **A / R** | I | C |
| 4 | Risk determination: classify transaction as standard or high-risk | C | **A / R** | — | — |
| 5 | Approve high-risk transaction (conditional — only if flagged at Task 4) | I | **A / R** | — | — |
| 6 | Release the approved payment | **A / R** | C | I | I |
| 7 | Transmit the SWIFT message | **A** | I | **R** | I |
| 8 | Archive the case file | C | **A** | **R** | — |
| 9 | Notify **Regulatory Liaison** of case closure and archival | I | **A** (recipient) | **R** | — |

---

### Notes on the matrix

- **Task 2 split of accountability:** the **Transaction Analyst** executes the sanctions screening, but the **Regulatory Liaison** carries accountability for the adequacy and outcome of the screening control.
- **Tasks 3–5 (KYC Review chain):** the **Regulatory Liaison** is both Responsible and Accountable, as the **KYC Review** and any high-risk approval are non-delegable regulatory judgements. The **Transaction Analyst** is Consulted for transaction context and supporting documentation.
- **Task 5 is conditional:** it is triggered only where the risk determination in Task 4 flags the transaction as high-risk. Standard-risk transactions pass directly from Task 4 to Task 6.
- **Task 6 dependency:** the **Transaction Analyst** may not release the payment until the **KYC Review** is cleared and, where applicable, high-risk approval has been recorded by the **Regulatory Liaison**.
- **Task 7:** IT Support performs the technical transmission; the **Transaction Analyst** remains Accountable that the payment actually left AtlasRemit as instructed.
- **Task 8:** IT Support performs the archival action, while the **Regulatory Liaison** is Accountable for record-retention compliance.
- **Single-accountability rule:** each task carries exactly one Accountable role, avoiding ownership ambiguity at handover points (Tasks 6→7 and 8→9 are the two highest-risk handovers and should be evidenced by system timestamps).

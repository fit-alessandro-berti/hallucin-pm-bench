### Decision points in Contoso’s flight-refund process

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - Activity: **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - Decision: Whether the claim is eligible, based on the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.

2. **Finance pre-approval**
   - Activity: **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - Decision: Whether finance pre-approval succeeds, using the **EligibilityChecklist**.

3. **High-value claim routing**
   - Condition: Refund amount **greater than €1,000**
   - Decision:
     - If **> €1,000**, the case must proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
     - If **≤ €1,000**, the case does not require **Senior_Auditor_Review**.

4. **Payment readiness**
   - Activity: **Process_Refund_Payment**
   - Decision/gate: Payment may begin only if:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded; and
     - for high-value claims, **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed; and
     - the signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

---

### Required documents and artefacts

- **RefundRequestForm** — completed by the passenger during **Customer_Submit_Claim**.
- **FlightManifest** — used during **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**.
- **WeatherReport** — used during **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**.
- **EligibilityChecklist** — used during **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**.
- **AuditTrail** — required for **Senior_Auditor_Review** on claims greater than €1,000.
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — must be signed and present before **Process_Refund_Payment**.
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger during **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**.

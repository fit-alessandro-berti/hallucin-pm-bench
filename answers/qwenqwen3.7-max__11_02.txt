**MEMO**  
**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**From:** Senior Analyst  
**Subject:** Q1 Claims Bottlenecks  

Based on Q1 data, the three worst-performing activities are:  

**1. Request_Documents**  
*Why:* A massive 150-minute wait time and 22.0% rework rate cause throughput to plummet from 270 to 190.  
*Action:* Implement automated pre-submission validation to ensure customers upload correct documents initially, reducing the 22% rework loop and queueing.  

**2. Review_Documents**  
*Why:* Highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), driven by highest processing variability (9 min stdev) and longest execution time (25 min).  
*Action:* Deploy AI-assisted extraction to standardize reviews, reducing the 9-minute variance and accelerating the 25-minute processing time.  

**3. Initial_Assessment**  
*Why:* Excluding document steps, it has the highest SLA breach rate (3.2%) and second-highest wait time (18 min).  
*Action:* Introduce automated straight-through processing rules for low-complexity claims to bypass the 18-minute manual queue.

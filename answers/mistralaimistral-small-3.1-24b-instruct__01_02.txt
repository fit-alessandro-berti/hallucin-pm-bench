### Decision Points in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**:
   - **Criteria**: If the two scores (from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B) differ by **≤ 2 points**, proceed to the Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC).
   - **Escalation**: If the scores differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**:
   - **Criteria**: If the **loan amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves the loan.
   - **Escalation**: If the **loan amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to the Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**:
   - **Criteria**: The board votes to either **approve** or **reject** the loan application.

### Every Required Document:

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
2. **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
3. **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

Based on the process description for Contoso Airlines, here is the summary of decision points and the list of required documents.

### Decision Points
There is one primary decision point in the process:

*   **Refund Amount Evaluation:** After the *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* stage, the system determines if the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**.
    *   **If > €1 000:** The process must include a *Senior_Auditor_Review* before proceeding to payment.
    *   **If ≤ €1 000:** The process proceeds directly to *Process_Refund_Payment*.

*(Note: While the eligibility check in Triage functions as a validation gate, it is defined as an agent activity rather than a branching decision point in the process flow.)*

### Required Documents / Artifacts
The following documents are required to complete the process:

1.  **RefundRequestForm:** Submitted by the passenger to initiate the claim.
2.  **FlightManifest:** Used by the triage agent for eligibility validation.
3.  **WeatherReport:** Used by the triage agent for eligibility validation.
4.  **EligibilityChecklist:** Used by the finance controller for pre-approval.
5.  **AuditTrail:** Required specifically for high-value claims (> €1 000) during the senior auditor review.
6.  **BankTransferAuthorization:** Required before the finance team can execute the payment.
7.  **RefundConfirmationPDF:** The final document issued to the passenger as confirmation.

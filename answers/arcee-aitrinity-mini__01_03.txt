

### a) **Summary of Decision Points**

1.  **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3):** *If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** AND the requested loan amount is **≤ €250,000**, the need for a Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is bypassed.* (Otherwise, proceed to Step 5).
2.  **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):** *Approval is granted as long as **any two of the three required officials** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve the loan application.* (Only one official's approval is insufficient).

### b) **Required Documents in the Order They First Appear**

1.  **Form 14B** (Received as the loan application)
2.  **Deck Memo** (Generated during Manual-Underwriting-Review, Step 5)
3.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Generated during Prepare-Binding-Offer, Step 7)
4.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Required at Step 8 during Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
5.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Prepared during Finalize-Agreement, Step 9)

### a) Summary of Decision Points
1.  **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Outcome Assignment):** A risk rating of {Low, Medium, or High} is assigned based on the combined inputs from the Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards.
2.  **Manual-Underwriting-Review Bypass:** If the risk rating is {Low or Medium} and the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, the process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review step; otherwise, it proceeds to step 5.

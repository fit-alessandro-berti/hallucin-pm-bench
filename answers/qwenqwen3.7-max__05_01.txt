Attach an **interrupting timer boundary event** (`Timer_SLA`) to `Task_RequestDocs` with a 48-hour duration, routing its outgoing flow to a new **Escalation End Event**. 

Because waiting for missing documents is the only step likely to exceed 48 hours before reaching `Task_Triage`, this single timer enforces the SLA. If documents aren't received in time, the timer interrupts the wait and auto-escalates the claim, strictly meeting business rules without adding new tasks.

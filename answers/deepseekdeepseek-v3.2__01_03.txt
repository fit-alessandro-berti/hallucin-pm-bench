**a) Decision Points in the Process**

1.  **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** The process skips Manual-Underwriting-Review if the Tri-Color-Risk-Check result is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Approval Gate (Step 6 - "TriVote"):** The loan proceeds only if at least two of the three designated officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant their approval.

**b) Documents in Order of First Appearance**

1.  **Form 14B** (Application, Step 1)
2.  **Scorecard–Cyan** (Input for Tri-Color-Risk-Check, Step 3)
3.  **Scorecard–Magenta** (Input for Tri-Color-Risk-Check, Step 3)
4.  **Scorecard–Yellow** (Input for Tri-Color-Risk-Check, Step 3)
5.  **Deck Memo** (Created during Manual-Underwriting-Review, Step 5 - conditional)
6.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Binding offer document, Step 7)
7.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Required for applicant confirmation, Step 8)
8.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Final agreement document, Step 9)

# Executive Memo: Q1 2025 Acme Insurance Claims Handling
**To: Acme Insurance COO**
**From: Senior Process Performance Analyst**
**Date: Q1 2025 Close**
**Subject: Critical Claims Performance Gaps**

The three lowest-performing claims activities are:
1.  **Request_Documents**: Far and away the weakest, with the highest queue wait time (150 mins), 22% rework rate far exceeding peers, and 5.5% SLA breaches. Action: Deploy automated follow-up triggers for incomplete submissions to cut wait times and rework.
2.  **Review_Documents**: Highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), longest average processing time (25 mins) and greatest processing variability (9 min stdev). Action: Roll out standardized review checklists and targeted training to reduce variance and breaches.
3.  **Initial_Assessment**: Third-worst, with 3.2% SLA breaches, 18-minute queue waits (third-highest) and elevated rework (1.8%). Action: Simplify pre-assessment intake requirements to reduce upstream wait times.

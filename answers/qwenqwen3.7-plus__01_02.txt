Here are the decision points and required documents for Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process, using the exact terminology provided:

### **Decision Points**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Evaluates the risk scores (0–9) assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. 
   - If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, the process continues.
   - If the scores differ by **> 2 points**, the application is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before continuing.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**: Evaluates the requested loan amount.
   - If the **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves.
   - If the **amount ≥ €15 000**, the application proceeds to the Final Micro-loan Board Approval.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Micro-loan Board votes to either *approve* or *reject* the application.

### **Required Documents at Submission**
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

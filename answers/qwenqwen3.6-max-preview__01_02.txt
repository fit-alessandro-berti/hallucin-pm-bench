**Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Compares the independent risk scores (0–9) from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the difference is ≤ 2 points, the flow continues to step 6. If the difference exceeds 2 points, the case escalates to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision before proceeding.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Evaluates the requested amount. If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves the application. If the amount is ≥ €15 000, the flow moves to step 8.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The Micro-loan Board reviews all applications ≥ €15 000 and casts a vote to either approve or reject the request.

**Required Documents at Submission**

▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

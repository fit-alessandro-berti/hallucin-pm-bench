**Decision Points**

Based on the defined process, the decision points are:

1. **High-Value Refund Determination**: The process evaluates whether the refund amount is greater than €1,000. 
   * If true, the claim is routed to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (which must include the AuditTrail).
   * If false, the process bypasses the senior audit and moves toward payment.
2. **Payment Readiness Check**: The process evaluates whether **Process_Refund_Payment** can begin. This decision is constrained by verifying that **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded, and, for high-value claims, that **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed.

**Required Documents**

All artefacts referenced in the Contoso Airlines process are:

* **RefundRequestForm** (completed by the passenger during claim submission)
* **FlightManifest** (used by the triage agent for eligibility validation)
* **WeatherReport** (used by the triage agent for eligibility validation)
* **EligibilityChecklist** (used by the finance controller for pre-approval sign-off)
* **AuditTrail** (required for high-value claims during Senior_Auditor_Review)
* **BankTransferAuthorization** (must be present and signed before payment execution)
* **RefundConfirmationPDF** (generated and emailed to the passenger as confirmation)

*(Note: The legacy activity "CustomerService_Agent_Decide" has been intentionally excluded as it does not exist in this process.)*

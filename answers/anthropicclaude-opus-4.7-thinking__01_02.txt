## Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track Process

There are **two explicit decision points** (plus one implicit board vote) in the process:

### 1. Underwriter Alignment Gate (after step 4 – Dual Underwriter Split)
Compare the risk scores from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**:
- **If the scores differ by ≤ 2 points** → continue directly to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
- **If the scores differ by > 2 points** → escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue to NFC.

### 2. Amount Threshold Decision (step 7)
Evaluate the requested loan amount:
- **If amount < €15 000** → the system **auto-approves**.
- **If amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

### 3. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) — step 8
The Micro-loan Board votes **approve** or **reject**. Only on *approve* does the process continue to **Disburse Funds**.

## Every Required Document (at submission time)

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
2. **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
3. **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

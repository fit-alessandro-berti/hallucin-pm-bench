### a) Decision points

1. **Manual-underwriting branch:** Skip Manual-Underwriting-Review only when risk is **Low or Medium** and the requested amount is **€250,000 or less**; otherwise, perform the review.  
2. **TriVote decision gate:** The Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer must participate, and the loan passes when **at least two of the three approve**.

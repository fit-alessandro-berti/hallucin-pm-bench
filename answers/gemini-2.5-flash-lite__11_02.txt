**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** Chief Operating Officer
**FROM:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst
**DATE:** Q1 2025 Performance Review
**SUBJECT:** Claims Handling Process Performance

This memo highlights key areas for improvement in the Q1-2025 Claims Handling process. The three most underperforming activities are:

1.  **Request_Documents:** This activity exhibits extreme inefficiency due to an **average wait time of 150 minutes** and a **rework rate of 22.0%**. This suggests a significant bottleneck, likely stemming from incomplete or missing documentation at the initial stage.
    *   **Recommendation:** Implement a mandatory checklist and automated document upload functionality at the claim registration stage to reduce revisits and delays.

2.  **Review_Documents:** This activity shows high latency with an **average wait time of 30 minutes**, a **processing time of 25 minutes**, and a substantial **rework rate of 5.3%**. The **high standard deviation in processing time (9 minutes)** further indicates inconsistency.
    *   **Recommendation:** Standardize document review criteria and provide additional training to reviewers to improve consistency and reduce reprocessing.

3.  **Initial_Assessment:** While throughput is high, this activity has the **second-highest average wait time at 18 minutes** and a **rework rate of 1.8%**.
    *   **Recommendation:** Streamline the initial assessment process by developing clearer guidelines or leveraging automation for routine checks to reduce wait times and rework.

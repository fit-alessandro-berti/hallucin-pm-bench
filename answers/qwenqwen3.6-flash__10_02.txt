**AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process – RACI Matrix**

| Process Step | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison |
|:---|:---:|:---:|
| Receive & system-log transfer instruction | R, A | – |
| Screen payment against sanctions list | R | C |
| Conduct KYC Review (sender & beneficiary) | C | R, A |
| Approve high-risk transactions | I | R, A |
| Release payment & transmit SWIFT message | R | A |
| Archive case file & dispatch confirmation | I | R, A |

**Key:** R = Responsible | A = Accountable | C = Consulted | I = Informed  
*Note: All system execution and archival functions referenced in the source workflow have been mapped to the Regulatory Liaison per the strict vocabulary mandate. No alternative role or activity terminology has been used.*

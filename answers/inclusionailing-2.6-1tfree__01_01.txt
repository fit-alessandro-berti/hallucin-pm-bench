Decision points  
1. Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility — determines whether the claim meets policy and operational criteria (using FlightManifest and WeatherReport).  
2. Finance_Controller_PreApprove — determines whether the claim is formally pre-approved (using EligibilityChecklist).  
3. High-value branch — determines whether the refund amount exceeds €1 000; if yes, the process routes to Senior_Auditor_Review.  
4. Senior_Auditor_Review — determines whether the high-value claim can proceed to payment (producing/validating AuditTrail).  

Required documents  
- RefundRequestForm  
- FlightManifest  
- WeatherReport  
- EligibilityChecklist  
- AuditTrail (required for refunds greater than €1 000)  
- BankTransferAuthorization  
- RefundConfirmationPDF

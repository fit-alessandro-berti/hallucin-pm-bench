In this RACI matrix, the roles are defined as follows:

- Responsible: The person who performs the activity.
- Accountable: The person who makes the decision or is ultimately accountable for the outcome of the activity.
- Consulted: The person who provides input or advice for the activity.
- Informed: The person who is kept updated about the progress or outcome of the activity.

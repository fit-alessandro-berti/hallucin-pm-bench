Here are the decision points in Contoso Airlines’ flight-refund process, along with the required documents explicitly defined in the process.

## Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - Activity: **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - Decision: whether the claim is eligible for refund.
   - Required documents:
     - **FlightManifest**
     - **WeatherReport**

2. **Finance pre-approval**
   - Activity: **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - Decision: whether the finance controller signs off the refund.
   - Required document:
     - **EligibilityChecklist**

3. **High-value claim check**
   - Decision: whether the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**.
   - If **yes**, the case must go to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
   - Required document for that review:
     - **AuditTrail**

4. **Payment readiness**
   - Activity: **Process_Refund_Payment**
   - Decision/gate: payment may begin only when:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded, and
     - for high-value claims, **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed.
   - Required document:
     - **BankTransferAuthorization**

## Required documents in the process

- **RefundRequestForm** — submitted in **Customer_Submit_Claim**
- **FlightManifest** — used in **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
- **WeatherReport** — used in **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
- **EligibilityChecklist** — used in **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
- **AuditTrail** — required in **Senior_Auditor_Review** for claims over **€1 000**
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — required for **Process_Refund_Payment**
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — sent in **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**

**To: COO, Orion Insurance**  
**From: Senior Process-Mining Analyst**  
**Subject: Recommendations to Reduce Waiting Times in Key Activities**

After analyzing the past 12 months of process performance data, the three worst-performing activities by waiting time at Orion Insurance are:

1. **Request Documents**  
   - **Average Waiting Time:** 36.5 hours  
   - **95th Percentile Waiting Time:** 120.0 hours  
   - **Recommendation:** Implement automated reminders and digital document uploads to reduce dependence on manual follow-ups.

2. **Send Closure Letter**  
   - **Average Waiting Time:** 12.2 hours  
   - **95th Percentile Waiting Time:** 44.0 hours  
   - **Recommendation:** Enhance the automation of closure letter generation and use electronic delivery to expedite distribution.

3. **Assess Liability**  
   - **Average Waiting Time:** 7.8 hours  
   - **95th Percentile Waiting Time:** 16.4 hours  
   - **Recommendation:** Introduce advanced predictive analytics to pre-assess simpler cases and prioritize complex cases for detailed review.

Each recommended action is aimed at cutting the respective waiting times by at least 20%, enhancing overall operational efficiency.

**End of Memo**

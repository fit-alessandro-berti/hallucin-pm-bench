To: Orion Credit COO
From: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
Date: October 12, 2024
Subject: Top Process Bottlenecks & Remediation Recommendations

This memo identifies Orion’s three worst-performing loan origination activities, ranked by SLA breach magnitude and excess waiting time: Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment. Legal Approval’s 900s average throughput time exceeds its 600s SLA by 300s, with a 600s average waiting time. Risk Review’s 600s throughput time exceeds its 300s SLA by 300s, with a 480s average waiting time. Credit Assessment’s 300s throughput time exceeds its 240s SLA by 60s, with a 200s average waiting time.
• For Legal Approval: Reallocate underutilized capacity from SLA-compliant Draft Offer teams to cut its queue backlog and reduce excessive waiting time.
• For Risk Review: Triage pre-vetted low-risk applications to streamline review workflows and resolve its 300s SLA overage.
• For Credit Assessment: Standardize application data inputs to eliminate redundant work and close its 60s SLA breach.

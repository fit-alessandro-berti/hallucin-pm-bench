**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** – confirmation that ID card and mobile phone are valid and verified.  
2. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – determination based on the difference between Senior Underwriter A’s and Shadow Underwriter B’s risk scores:  
 • If ≤ 2 points: proceed to next step.  
 • Else: escalate to Harmonisation Committee for tie-break, then proceed.  
3. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** – confirmation that at least 3 local references have been collected.  
4. **Amount Threshold Decision** – classification of the loan amount:  
 • If < €15 000: auto-approval.  
 • If ≥ €15 000: proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval.  
5. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – board vote to approve or reject the loan.


**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – determine whether Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B scores differ by more than 2 points (≤ 2 points → continue; > 2 points → escalate to Harmonisation Committee).  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – check if the loan amount is below €15 000 → auto‑approve, or €15 000+ → proceed to Micro-loan Board.  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – board votes *approve* or *reject*.

**Required Documents**  
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

### Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track process:

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
 • Assess difference between Senior Underwriter A’s and Shadow Underwriter B’s risk scores (0–9).  
 • If difference ≤ 2, proceed.  
 • If difference > 2, escalate to Harmonisation Committee for tie-break decision, then proceed.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
 • If amount < €15 000, system auto-approves.  
 • If amount ≥ €15 000, escalate to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** (for amounts ≥ €15 000)  
 • Board votes to approve or reject.

---

### Required Documents at Submission Time:

- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

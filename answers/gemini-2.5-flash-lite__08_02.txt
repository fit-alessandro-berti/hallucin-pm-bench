**Question 1: No.**

**Explanation:**
Transition T5 requires tokens in both P2 and P5 to fire. P2 is produced by T1. P5 is only reachable after T4 fires. T4, in turn, requires a token in P4. P4 can be reached either by T7 (from P3) or T3 (from P2). Since P2 is a prerequisite for T5, and P4 is a prerequisite for P5 (which is also a prerequisite for T5), and T3 directly enables P4, T3 must fire before P4 can have a token, and thus before T4 can fire, and consequently before P5 can have a token. Therefore, T3 must fire before T5 can become enabled.

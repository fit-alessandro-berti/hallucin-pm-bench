

**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**  
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Determines if underwriter scores differ by ≤ 2 points (continue) or require escalation to the Harmonisation Committee.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Decides auto-approval for amounts < €15 000 or proceeds to Final Micro-loan Board Approval for ≥ €15 000.  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – Board votes to approve or reject the loan.  

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**  
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

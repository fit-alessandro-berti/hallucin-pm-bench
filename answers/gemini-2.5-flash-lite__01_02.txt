Here is a summary of the decision points in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process, along with a list of all required documents:

**Decision Points:**

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate:** This is a decision point based on the difference between the risk scores assigned by **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**.
    *   If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, the process continues to step 6.
    *   If the scores differ by **> 2 points**, the process is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision:** This is a decision point based on the loan amount requested.
    *   If the **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves.
    *   If the **amount ≥ €15 000**, the process proceeds to step 8.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA):** This is a decision point where the board votes to *approve* or *reject* the loan.

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

**Executive memo — Waiting-time hotspots**

Ranked strictly by average waiting time:

1. **Request_Documents** — Avg wait **36.5h**; 95th percentile **120.0h**.  
   **Action:** Auto-trigger document requests at claim submission and send reminders at 24h/48h; target the 95p tail to cut average wait by ≥20%.

2. **Send_Closure_Letter** — Avg wait **12.2h**; 95th percentile **44.0h**.  
   **Action:** Implement same-day automated letter generation for cases entering closure status, with an alert if waiting exceeds 8h.

3. **Assess_Liability** — Avg wait **7.8h**; 95th percentile **16.4h**.  
   **Action:** Introduce priority queue rules for liability assessments approaching 8h wait and rebalance assignments daily using queue age.

These three activities should be the immediate focus for waiting-time reduction.

**a) Decision Points**

1.  **Step 4 Conditional Branch:** The process skips Manual-Underwriting-Review if the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Step 6 TriVote Gate:** The loan proceeds to offer preparation if at least two out of the three required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) approve the application.

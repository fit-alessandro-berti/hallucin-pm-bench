# FinCo Order-to-Cash RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|----------|:-------------------:|:----------------------:|:-----------------:|:--------------:|
| Receive Application | **R/A** | I | I | - |
| KYC Review | I | **R/A** | - | - |
| Order Vetting | **R/A** | C | I | - |
| Payment Clearance | I | - | **R/A** | I |
| Record Update | C | - | I | **R/A** |
| Notify Customer | **R/A** | I | I | C |

---

## Legend
- **R** = Responsible (performs the work)
- **A** = Accountable (final decision authority)
- **C** = Consulted (provides input before action)
- **I** = Informed (notified after action)
- **-** = No involvement

---

## Activity Ownership Summary

1. **Receive Application** — AM owns intake and initial logging
2. **KYC Review** — CO conducts compliance checks upon document receipt
3. **Order Vetting** — AM verifies data completeness and accuracy
4. **Payment Clearance** — FC initiates and completes settlement
5. **Record Update** — IT refreshes CRM with transaction data
6. **Notify Customer** — AM sends final confirmation to customer

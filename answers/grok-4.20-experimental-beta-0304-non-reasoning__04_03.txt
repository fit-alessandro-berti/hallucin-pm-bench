**Justification:**

- **Rule 1 violated**: QE and RA must occur *in that order* (QE before RA). In the trace RA occurs *after* NA, so the required QE → RA sequence is broken.
- **Rule 2 violated**: For severity = "High", VP must occur *after both* QE and RA. VP occurs before RA, so it is not after both reviews.
- **Rule 3 violated**: NA must not occur until *after* both QE and RA are complete. NA occurs between QE and RA, before RA has happened.
- **Rule 4 violated**: CC may only occur after NA *and* (because severity = High) after VP. Although NA and VP both precede CC, the violations of Rules 1–3 mean the prerequisites for CC are not legitimately satisfied.

The trace is therefore non-compliant.

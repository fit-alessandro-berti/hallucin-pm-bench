**RACI Matrix – FinCo Order‑to‑Cash Workflow**

| Activity (Row) | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|----------------|----------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| 1. Receive Application | **R / A** | I | I | I |
| 2. KYC Review | I | **R / A** | I | I |
| 3. Order Vetting | **R / A** | I | I | I |
| 4. Payment Clearance | I | I | **R / A** | I |
| 5. Record Update | I | I | I | **R / A** |
| 6. Notify Customer | **R / A** | I | I | I |

**Legend**  
- **R** – Responsible (the role that performs the work)  
- **A** – Accountable (the role ultimately answer for the outcome)  
- **C** – Consulted (provides input; not used in this simple flow)  
- **I** – Informed (kept aware of progress)  

All terminology follows the required vocabulary: **KYC Review**, **Order Vetting**, and **Payment Clearance**.

**Decision Points (as named in the process)**  

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – check whether the two risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points**.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – decide whether the loan amount is **< €15 000** (auto‑approve) or **≥ €15 000** (forward to the board).  
3. **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)** – the board votes **approve** or **reject** the request.

- Digital Application Form (**ML‑APP‑01**)  
- Proof of Address (‑ ≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay‑stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (**CIS**) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score‑Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (**NFF**)

Below is the RACI matrix for the FinCo order-to-cash workflow, utilizing the updated terminology as requested.

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Receive Application** | R/A | I | I | - |
| **KYC Review** | I | R/A | - | - |
| **Order Vetting** | R/A | C | I | - |
| **Payment Clearance** | I | - | R/A | - |
| **Record Update** | I | - | C | R/A |
| **Notify Customer** | R/A | I | I | - |

***

**Legend:**
*   **R (Responsible):** The role that performs the activity.
*   **A (Accountable):** The role with ultimate ownership and decision-making authority for the task.
*   **C (Consulted):** Roles whose opinions or expertise are sought during the task.
*   **I (Informed):** Roles that are kept up-to-date on progress or completion.

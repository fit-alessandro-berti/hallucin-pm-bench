**a) Summary of Every Decision Point (Gate or Conditional Branch) in Acme’s Process**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome (Step 3)**: Determines a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High based on inputs from Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow.
2. **Conditional Branch for Skipping Manual Underwriting (Step 4)**: Skips Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) if the risk rating is Low or Medium AND the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000.
3. **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6)**: Requires approvals from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, with the rule that any two out of three approvals suffice to pass.

**b) List of All Documents Required in the Process, in the Order They First Appear**

1. **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive-Application)
2. **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review)
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize-Agreement)

### a) Decision points (gates / conditional branches)

1. **Conditional skip after Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 4):** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, then **Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is skipped**.  
2. **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6):** The application **passes the gate if at least two of the three** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) **approve**; otherwise it fails.

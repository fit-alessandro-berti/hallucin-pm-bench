**Decision Points**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – After the Dual Underwriter Split, the two independent scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B are compared.  
   • If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, the flow continues to the Neighbourhood Feedback Check.  
   • If the scores differ by **> 2 points**, the application escalates to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, after which the flow continues.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – The requested amount is evaluated against the €15 000 threshold.  
   • If the amount is **< €15 000**, the system auto-approves.  
   • If the amount is **≥ €15 000**, the application proceeds to the Final Micro-loan Board Approval.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval** – The Micro-loan Board votes to **approve** or **reject** the application.

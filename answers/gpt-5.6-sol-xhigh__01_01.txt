## Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation** — **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** checks the claim against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
2. **Finance pre-approval** — **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** must succeed, using the **EligibilityChecklist**, before payment can begin.
3. **High-value threshold** — If the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required and must include the **AuditTrail**. Claims of €1,000 or less bypass this review.
4. **Payment readiness** — **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin only when:
   - finance pre-approval has succeeded;
   - high-value claims have completed senior-auditor review; and
   - the signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

## Required documents

- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail** — required for high-value claims only
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — must be signed before payment
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger after payment

**CustomerService_Agent_Decide** is not part of Contoso’s process.

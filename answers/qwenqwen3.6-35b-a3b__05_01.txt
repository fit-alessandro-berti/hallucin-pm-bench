Add an `IntermediateThrowEscalationEvent` (e.g., `id="Timer_SLA"`) directly after `Start_Claim`. Configure it with a timer definition set to 48 hours. When the timer fires, this event throws an escalation signal to trigger auto-escalation for any claim that has not yet been processed. This enforces the SLA by detecting violations immediately after the 48-hour window closes, ensuring claims reaching `Task_Triage` late are flagged. No new tasks are added; only an event and a flow connection are required.

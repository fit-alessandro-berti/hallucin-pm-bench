### RACI Matrix

| # | Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Receive the transfer instruction and log it in the system | R/A | I | I |
| 2 | Screen the payment against the sanctions list | R/A | I | I |
| 3 | Perform the KYC Review on the sender and beneficiary | I | R/A | I |
| 4 | Approve the transaction if it is high-risk | I | R/A | I |
| 5 | Release the payment after approval | R/A | I | I |
| 6 | Send the SWIFT message | I | I | R/A |
| 7 | Archive the case file and notify the Regulatory Liaison | I | I | R/A |

**Legend:** R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed

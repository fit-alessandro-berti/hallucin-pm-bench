## Decision Points in Contoso's Flight-Refund Process

There are effectively **two decision points** defined in the process:

1. **High-Value Branch (Refund Amount Check)**
   - **Condition:** Is the refund amount **greater than €1 000**?
   - **If yes →** the case must proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (which requires the *AuditTrail*).
   - **If no →** the case skips the auditor review.

2. **Payment Gate (Pre-conditions for Process_Refund_Payment)**
   - **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin only when:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded, **and**
     - For high-value claims (> €1 000), **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed.
   - This is the convergence/gating check that controls whether payment can start.

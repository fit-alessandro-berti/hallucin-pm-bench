## Decision Points in Contoso Airlines' Flight-Refund Process

### 1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
- **Decision**: Determine if the claim is eligible for refund
- **Required documents**: 
  - *FlightManifest*
  - *WeatherReport*

### 2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
- **Decision**: Sign off on the refund approval
- **Required document**: 
  - *EligibilityChecklist*

### 3. **High-value branch determination**
- **Decision**: Route case based on refund amount (>€1,000 triggers Senior_Auditor_Review)
- **No specific document required for this routing decision**

### 4. **Senior_Auditor_Review** (only for amounts >€1,000)
- **Decision**: Review and approve high-value refunds
- **Required document**: 
  - *AuditTrail*

## Complete List of Required Documents

1. *RefundRequestForm* (Customer_Submit_Claim)
2. *FlightManifest* (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
3. *WeatherReport* (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
4. *EligibilityChecklist* (Finance_Controller_PreApprove)
5. *AuditTrail* (Senior_Auditor_Review - high-value only)
6. *BankTransferAuthorization* (Process_Refund_Payment)
7. *RefundConfirmationPDF* (Issue_Confirmation_Letter)

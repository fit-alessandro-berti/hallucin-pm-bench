**a) Decision Points and Rules:**

1. **Receive-Application**: The process begins with the submission of **Form 14B** by the applicant.
2. **Assign-Deck-Reviewer**: The application is assigned to a Deck Officer for initial review.
3. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**: The Deck Officer evaluates the risk using **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow** to determine a risk rating.
4. **Risk Condition**: If the risk rating is **Low** or **Medium** and the requested amount is **€250 000** or less, the process skips to manual underwriting.
5. **Manual-Underwriting-Review**: If the risk rating is **High** or the requested amount is more than **€250 000**, a manual underwriting review is conducted.
6. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”**: The loan is approved if any two out of three approvals are received from the **Credit Lead**, **Regulatory Liaison**, and **Sustainability Officer**.
7. **Prepare-Binding-Offer**: Once approved, a binding offer is prepared in the form of **Offer Sheet 77**.
8. **Request-Applicant-Confirmation**: The applicant is requested to confirm the offer by signing **Offer Sheet 77** and providing an **ID Matrix Declaration**.
9. **Finalize-Agreement**: The final loan agreement is prepared in the form of **Loan Dossier 2025**.

**b) List of Documents:**

1. Form 14B
2. Deck Memo (generated during Manual-Underwriting-Review)
3. Offer Sheet 77
4. ID Matrix Declaration
5. Loan Dossier 2025

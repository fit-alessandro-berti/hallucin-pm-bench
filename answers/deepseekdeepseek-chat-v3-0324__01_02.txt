### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**  
1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** – Confirm ID card **and** mobile phone (no credit checks).  
2. **Community (C (CIA)** – Impact Officer evaluates the application.  
3. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Proceed if scores differ by **≤ 2 points**; else escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee**.  
4. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Auto-approve if **< €15 000**; else escalate to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.  

### **Required Documents**  
1. Digital Application Form (**ML-APP-01**)  
2. Proof of Address (**≤ 3 months old**)  
3. Latest Income Statement (**single pay-stub or tax slip**)  
4. Community Impact Statement (**CIS template**)  
5. Dual Underwriter **Score-Sheet**  
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (**NFF**)  

*(All terms and document names match Acme’s exact process as defined.)*

**Decision points in Contoso’s process** (only those explicitly present):

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**  
   Decision: whether the claim is eligible (checked against FlightManifest + WeatherReport).

2. **High-value branch test**  
   Decision: is the refund amount greater than €1 000?  
   - Yes → case must go through Senior_Auditor_Review (with AuditTrail).  
   - No → Senior_Auditor_Review is skipped.

3. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**  
   Decision: whether to sign off (using EligibilityChecklist).  
   This approval is mandatory before Process_Refund_Payment can start.

4. **Senior_Auditor_Review** (only for high-value claims)  
   Decision: approval of the high-value case (must include AuditTrail).  
   This must be completed before Process_Refund_Payment can start for amounts > €1 000.

**All required documents/artefacts** (only those defined in the process):

- RefundRequestForm  
- FlightManifest  
- WeatherReport  
- EligibilityChecklist  
- AuditTrail (required only for the high-value path)  
- BankTransferAuthorization (signed, required for Process_Refund_Payment)  
- RefundConfirmationPDF  

No other decision points or documents exist in the defined process.

**Decision Points**
- **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Compares the independent risk scores (0–9) from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the difference is ≤ 2 points, the workflow continues. If > 2 points, it escalates to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision before continuing.
- **Amount Threshold Decision**: Routes the application based on size. If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves. If the amount is ≥ €15 000, the workflow proceeds to board review.
- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Micro-loan Board evaluates applications meeting the ≥ €15 000 threshold and votes to approve or reject.

**Required Documents (at submission time)**
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

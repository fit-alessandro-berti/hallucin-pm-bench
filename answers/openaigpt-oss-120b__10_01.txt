**RACI Matrix – FinCo Order‑to‑Cash Workflow**

| **Activity**                     | **Account Manager (AM)** | **Compliance Officer (CO)** | **Finance Clerk (FC)** | **IT Support (IT)** |
|----------------------------------|--------------------------|-----------------------------|------------------------|----------------------|
| 1. Receive Application           | **R / A**                | I                           | I                      | I                    |
| 2. KYC Review                    | I                        | **R / A**                   | I                      | I                    |
| 3. Order Vetting                 | **R / A**                | I                           | I                      | I                    |
| 4. Payment Clearance             | I                        | I                           | **R / A**              | I                    |
| 5. Record Update                 | I                        | I                           | I                      | **R / A**            |
| 6. Notify Customer               | **R / A**                | I                           | I                      | I                    |

**Key**  
- **R** – Responsible (does the work)  
- **A** – Accountable (owns the outcome; only one per activity)  
- **C** – Consulted (provides input) – not used in this simple hand‑off flow  
- **I** – Informed (kept aware of progress)  

All terminology follows the required wording: **KYC Review**, **Order Vetting**, and **Payment Clearance**.

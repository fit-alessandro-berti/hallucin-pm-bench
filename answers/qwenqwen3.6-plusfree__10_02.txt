Here is the RACI matrix mapped strictly to your mandated vocabulary and the source workflow:

| Process Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|---|---|---|
| Receive and log customer transfer instruction | R, A | – | – |
| Screen payment against sanctions list | R | A, C | – |
| Execute **KYC Review** for sender and beneficiary | C | R, A | – |
| Approve high-risk transaction | I | R, A | – |
| Release payment and dispatch SWIFT message | R | C, A | R |
| Archive case file and issue completion notice | I | I | R, A |

**Key:**  
• **R** = Responsible (executes the work)  
• **A** = Accountable (owns the outcome/approval)  
• **C** = Consulted (provides input before execution)  
• **I** = Informed (notified after execution)  
*(Note: Every task has exactly one Accountable owner per RACI standards.)*

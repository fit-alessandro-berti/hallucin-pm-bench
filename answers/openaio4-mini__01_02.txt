Decision Points  
• Underwriter Alignment Gate  
  – If Senior Underwriter A’s and Shadow Underwriter B’s risk scores differ by ≤ 2 points, proceed to Neighbourhood Feedback Check.  
  – If they differ by > 2 points, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break, then proceed.  
• Amount Threshold Decision  
  – If amount < €15 000, system auto-approves and skips the Micro-loan Board.  
  – If amount ≥ €15 000, proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).  
• Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)  
  – The Micro-loan Board votes to approve or reject the application.  

Required Documents  
• Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
• Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
• Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
• Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
• Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
• Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

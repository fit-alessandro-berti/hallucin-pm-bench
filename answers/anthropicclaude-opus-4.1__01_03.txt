## a) Decision Points in Acme's Process

1. **Step 4 - Amount/Risk Bypass Decision**: If risk rating is Low or Medium AND requested amount is ≤ €250,000, then skip Manual Underwriting Review (step 5).

2. **Step 6 - TriVote Decision Gate**: Requires any two approvals out of three from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer to pass.

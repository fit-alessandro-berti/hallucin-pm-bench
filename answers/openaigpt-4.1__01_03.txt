**a) Decision Points in Acme’s SMALL-BUSINESS Loan-Approval Process:**

1. **Post Tri-Color-Risk-Check Conditional Branch**  
   *Rule:* If the risk outcome is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, then skip Manual-Underwriting-Review; otherwise, proceed to Manual-Underwriting-Review.

2. **Decision Gate (“TriVote”)**  
   *Rule:* Loan is approved if at least **two out of three** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) give their approval.

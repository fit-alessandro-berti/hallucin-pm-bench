### Decision Points in Contoso’s Flight-Refund Process

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**: The triage agent decides on claim eligibility by checking against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*. If ineligible, the process ends; if eligible, it proceeds to *Finance_Controller_PreApprove*.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**: The finance controller decides to pre-approve the claim by signing off on the *EligibilityChecklist*. If not pre-approved, the process ends; if approved, it proceeds to the high-value branch check or directly to *Process_Refund_Payment*.

3. **High-value Branch (after Finance_Controller_PreApprove)**: The process branches based on whether the refund amount is greater than €1 000. If yes, it requires *Senior_Auditor_Review* (including the *AuditTrail*) before *Process_Refund_Payment*; if no, it proceeds directly to *Process_Refund_Payment*.

4. **Precondition for Process_Refund_Payment**: Payment execution requires successful *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* and, for high-value claims, completion of *Senior_Auditor_Review*. If these are not met, payment cannot begin.

### Required Documents

- *RefundRequestForm* (completed by the passenger in *Customer_Submit_Claim*).
- *FlightManifest* (checked in *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility*).
- *WeatherReport* (checked in *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility*).
- *EligibilityChecklist* (signed off in *Finance_Controller_PreApprove*).
- *AuditTrail* (included in *Senior_Auditor_Review* for high-value claims).
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (signed and present for *Process_Refund_Payment*).
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* (emailed in *Issue_Confirmation_Letter*).

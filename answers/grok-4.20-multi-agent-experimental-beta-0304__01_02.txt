**Decision points (using exact activity names):**
- **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: If the two risk scores (from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B) differ by ≤ 2 points, continue; otherwise escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision.
- **Amount Threshold Decision**: If amount < €15 000, the system auto-approves; if amount ≥ €15 000, proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).
- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: board votes *approve* or *reject*.

**Every required document at submission time:**
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

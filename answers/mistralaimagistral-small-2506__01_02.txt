### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**
   - If scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by **≤ 2 points**, proceed.
   - If scores differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for resolution.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**
   - Loans **< €15,000** auto-approve.
   - Loans **≥ €15,000** proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

### **Required Documents at Submission Time**
- **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
- **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
- **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
- **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

**FinCo Order-to-Cash RACI Matrix**

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|:---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Receive Application | R/A | C | I | I |
| KYC Review | C | R/A | I | I |
| Order Vetting | R/A | C | I | I |
| Payment Clearance | C | I | R/A | I |
| Record Update | C | I | I | R/A |
| Notify Customer | R/A | I | I | I |

**Legend**
- **R** = Responsible: Performs the work
- **A** = Accountable: Owns the outcome & final sign-off
- **C** = Consulted: Provides input or subject-matter expertise
- **I** = Informed: Kept updated on progress or completion

*Note: Each activity has exactly one Accountable (A) party to ensure clear decision ownership. Consulted/Informed assignments reflect standard cross-functional touchpoints in the workflow.*

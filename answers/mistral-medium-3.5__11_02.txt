**Executive Memo – Q1-2025 Claims Handling Bottlenecks**

**Top 3 Underperformers:**
1. **Request_Documents** – 22% rework rate and 5.5% SLA breaches, driven by excessive queueing (150 min avg wait) despite fast processing (5 min). *Action:* Reduce upstream delays (e.g., pre-populate document checklists) to cut wait time.
2. **Review_Documents** – Highest processing variability (9 min stdev) and 12.4% SLA breaches, with 5.3% rework. *Action:* Standardize document templates to reduce variability and rework.
3. **Initial_Assessment** – 3.2% SLA breaches and 1.8% rework, with long wait (18 min) and processing (12 min) times. *Action:* Allocate more assessors to reduce queueing.

**Note:** *Check_Coverage* and *Notify_Customer* perform well; no action needed. Prioritize fixes where wait time, rework, or SLA breaches are most severe.

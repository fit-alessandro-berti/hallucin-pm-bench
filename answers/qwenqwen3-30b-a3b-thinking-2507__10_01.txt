

### RACI Matrix for FinCo Order-to-Cash Workflow

| Activity                | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|-------------------------|----------------------|-------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| **Receive Application** | R, A                | I                      | I                 | I              |
| **KYC Review**          | I                   | R, A                   | I                 | I              |
| **Order Vetting**       | R, A                | I                      | I                 | I              |
| **Payment Clearance**   | I                   | I                      | R, A              | I              |
| **Record Update**       | I                   | I                      | I                 | R, A           |
| **Notify Customer**     | R, A                | I                      | I                 | I              |

**Notes on RACI assignments**:  
- **R** = Responsible (performs the activity).  
- **A** = Accountable (owner; single point of accountability).  
- **I** = Informed (kept updated after the activity).  
- **C** = Consulted (not required per process description; no role provides input for these activities).  
- All activities strictly use **preferred terminology** (e.g., "KYC Review," not "Customer Due Diligence").  
- Roles strictly use defined abbreviations (AM, CO, FC, IT).

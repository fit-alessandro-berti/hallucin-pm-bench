**Memo: Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance**

The three worst-performing activities are:

1. **Request_Documents**: Plagued by a massive 150-min wait time and a 22% rework rate, causing throughput to plummet from 270 to 190 cases/day.
*Action*: Audit the 22% of reworked cases to identify frequently missing forms and update automated customer request templates.

2. **Review_Documents**: Suffers the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%) and longest average processing time (25 min) with high variability (9 min stdev).
*Action*: Investigate the root causes behind the 9-min processing stdev to standardize review steps and reduce SLA breaches.

3. **Initial_Assessment**: Exhibits the highest early-stage SLA breaches (3.2%) and rework rate (1.8%), dropping throughput from 280 to 270 cases/day.
*Action*: Analyze the 1.8% of reworked cases to pinpoint triage failures and refine initial assessment checklists. 

Addressing these specific bottlenecks and variability issues will restore flow and reduce upstream errors.

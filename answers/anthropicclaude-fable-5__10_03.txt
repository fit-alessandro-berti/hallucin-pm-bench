## RACI Matrix — High-Value Wire-Transfer Process

| Activity | Front-Office Analyst | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | Treasury Ops |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **Intake Vetting** | R / A | I | — | — |
| **KYC Review** | C | R | A | — |
| **AML Screening** | — | R | A | — |
| **Risk Scoring** | — | R | A | I |
| **Escalation Approval** (Risk Scoring result > 70) | I | C | R / A | I |
| **Payment Scheduling** (Risk Scoring result ≤ 70 or post-approval) | I | I | — | R / A |
| **Funds Release & Notification** | I | I | — | R / A |

### Notes on assignments

- **Intake Vetting** — The Front-Office Analyst is both Responsible and Accountable for confirming paperwork completeness; the Compliance Analyst is Informed when the file is handed off.
- **KYC Review** — The Compliance Analyst executes the work; the Compliance Officer holds ultimate accountability for compliance outcomes. The Front-Office Analyst is Consulted for clarifications about the submitted file.
- **AML Screening** — Performed by the Compliance Analyst against global watchlists, with the Compliance Officer Accountable.
- **Risk Scoring** — The Compliance Analyst derives the 0–100 score; Treasury Ops is Informed because the result determines whether scheduling can proceed.
- **Escalation Approval** — Triggered only when the Risk Scoring result exceeds 70. The Compliance Officer is Responsible and Accountable for the decision; the Compliance Analyst is Consulted on the case details.
- **Payment Scheduling** and **Funds Release & Notification** — Treasury Ops owns execution; the Front-Office Analyst is Informed upon release of funds, as the narrative specifies notification back to the originating analyst.

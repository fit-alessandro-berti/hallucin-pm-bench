**Decision Points**
• **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Compares the risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the difference is ≤ 2 points, the process advances; if it exceeds 2 points, the case is routed to the Harmonisation Committee before continuing.
• **Amount Threshold Decision**: Evaluates the requested amount. Applications < €15 000 are system auto-approved. Applications ≥ €15 000 proceed to board review.
• **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Micro-loan Board casts a formal *approve* or *reject* vote on applications that met or exceeded the threshold.

**Required Documents (at submission time)**
• Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
• Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
• Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
• Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
• Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
• Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

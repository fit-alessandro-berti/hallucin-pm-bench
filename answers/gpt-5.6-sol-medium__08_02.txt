**Question 2:** A minimal firing sequence is:

**[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]**

After **T1**, tokens are placed in **P1** and **P2**. The token from **P1** moves through **T2 → T7 → T4**, reaching **P5**, while the token in **P2** remains untouched. Thus **T5** becomes enabled with tokens in both **P2** and **P5** and can fire without **T3** ever firing. Firing **T5** consumes the **P2** token, so **T3** cannot subsequently fire.

Memo  
To: COO  
From: Senior Process Performance Analyst  
Date: [today]  
Subject: Top three SLA‑breach activities and data‑driven improvement actions  

Analysis identifies three activities that breach SLA and have the highest waiting times: Legal Approval (1 500 s vs 600 s SLA; 900 s over‑run; waiting 600 s – 40 % of total), Risk Review (1 080 s vs 300 s SLA; 780 s over‑run; waiting 480 s – 44 %), and Credit Assessment (500 s vs 240 s SLA; 260 s over‑run; waiting 200 s – 40 %).  

Recommendations:  
- Legal Approval: re‑engineer the review workflow by adding parallel document checks and an extra examiner to cut waiting time by ~30 % (≈180 s).  
- Risk Review: integrate a real‑time risk‑score feed and pre‑screen high‑risk applications to reduce waiting by ~25 % (≈120 s).  
- Credit Assessment: automate credit‑bureau queries with pre‑filled fields to lower waiting by ~30 % (≈60 s).  

Implementing these actions will bring the three activities into SLA and improve cycle time.

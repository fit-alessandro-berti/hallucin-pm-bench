**To: COO — Waiting-time priority memo**

Ranked by **average waiting time**, the three worst activities are:

**#1 Request_Documents** — Avg wait **36.5h**; 95th percentile **120.0h**.  
**Action:** Trigger document requests automatically at claim intake, with reminders at 24/48 hours. Removing just **7.3h** of idle delay delivers the required **20%+** reduction.

**#2 Send_Closure_Letter** — Avg wait **12.2h**; 95th percentile **44.0h**.  
**Action:** Auto-generate and send closure letters immediately after the claim decision, with a same-day SLA. Cutting **2.4h** from the queue achieves **20%+** improvement.

**#3 Assess_Liability** — Avg wait **7.8h**; 95th percentile **16.4h**.  
**Action:** Implement an aging-based work queue that auto-escalates any case waiting over **6 hours** to the next available assessor. Pulling forward only **1.6h** of delay meets the **20%+** target.

### a) Decision points

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** The three scorecards are evaluated to assign a **Low, Medium, or High** risk rating.  
2. **Manual-review branch:** Skip Manual-Underwriting-Review only when risk is **Low or Medium** and the requested amount is **€250,000 or less**; otherwise, perform it.  
3. **TriVote gate:** All three designated officers must participate, including the Sustainability Officer, and the application passes when **at least two of the three approve**.

### b) Required documents, in order of first appearance

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**  
5. **Deck Memo** — required when Manual-Underwriting-Review is not skipped  
6. **Offer Sheet 77**  
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77**  
8. **ID Matrix Declaration**  
9. **Loan Dossier 2025**

No in-house KYC/AML documents or separate credit-bureau-pull documents are part of this process.

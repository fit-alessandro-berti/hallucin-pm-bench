**RACI Matrix — FinCo Order-to-Cash Workflow**

Legend: **R** = Responsible · **A** = Accountable · **C** = Consulted · **I** = Informed

| # | Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Receive Application | **R / A** | I | I | I |
| 2 | KYC Review | A | **R** | I | I |
| 3 | Order Vetting | **R / A** | C | I | I |
| 4 | Payment Clearance | A | I | **R** | I |
| 5 | Record Update | A | I | C | **R** |
| 6 | Notify Customer | **R / A** | I | I | I |

**Notes on the assignments**
- The **Account Manager** is held Accountable for every step because they are the end-to-end process owner and the single point of contact for the customer.
- The **Compliance Officer** is Responsible solely for the KYC Review (their specialist activity) and is Consulted during Order Vetting to confirm the customer’s risk status.
- The **Finance Clerk** is Responsible for Payment Clearance and Consulted when records are updated, since CRM data must reflect the settled transaction.
- **IT Support** is Responsible only for Record Update; their work in other steps is purely downstream, so they are only Informed.

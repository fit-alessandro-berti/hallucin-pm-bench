**a) Decision points**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** Using **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow**, the application is assigned a **Low, Medium, or High** risk rating.  
2. **Step-4 conditional branch:** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** **and** the requested amount is **€250,000 or less**, **step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review)** is skipped; otherwise step 5 is performed.  
3. **TriVote gate:** The **Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer** all participate, and the application passes if **any two of the three approve**.

**b) Documents required, in order of first appearance**

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**  
5. **Deck Memo**  
6. **Offer Sheet 77**  
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77**  
8. **ID Matrix Declaration**  
9. **Loan Dossier 2025**

*I have not added any KYC/AML or other external-check documents, because the process explicitly says those are outside this in-house workflow.*

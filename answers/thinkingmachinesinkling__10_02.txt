Below is the RACI matrix for AtlasRemit’s cross-border payment workflow. All role references use **Transaction Analyst** and **Regulatory Liaison**, and the due-diligence activity is recorded exclusively as **KYC Review**.

| Process Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|---|---|---|
| Receive and log customer transfer instruction | R, A | — | — |
| Screen payment against sanctions list | R | A | — |
| Perform **KYC Review** on sender and beneficiary | I | R, A | — |
| Approve flagged high-risk transaction | I | R, A | — |
| Release approved payment | R, A | — | — |
| Ensure SWIFT message is sent | I | — | R, A |
| Archive case file | — | I | R, A |
| Notify **Regulatory Liaison** of case closure | — | I | R, A |

**Key**
- **R** = Responsible (performs the work)
- **A** = Accountable (ultimately answerable / approves)
- **C** = Consulted (provides input)
- **I** = Informed (kept updated on outcome)
- **—** = Not involved in this task

Every step from intake through sanctions screening, **KYC Review**, high-risk approval, release, SWIFT transmission, archiving, and final notification to the **Regulatory Liaison** is covered using only the mandated terminology.

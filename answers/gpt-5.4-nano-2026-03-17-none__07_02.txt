### Added tasks
- **Task_VerifyIdentity** (Verify Identity)
- **Task_RequestDocs** (Request Additional Documents)
- **Task_FinalReject** (Final Reject)

### Removed tasks
- **Task_RejectLoan** (Reject Loan)

### Gateway-type changes (e.g., Exclusive → Inclusive)
- **exclusiveGateway** *Gateway_Decision* changed to **inclusiveGateway** *Gateway_Decision*
- Decision flow logic also changed accordingly:
  - From two-way **yes/no** branching (to *Task_ApproveLoan* or *Task_RejectLoan*) to multi-outcome eligibility paths:
    - **eligible** → *Task_ApproveLoan*
    - **missing docs** → *Task_RequestDocs*
    - **ineligible** → *Task_FinalReject*

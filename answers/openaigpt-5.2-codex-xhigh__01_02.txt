**Decision points (using exact activity names):**

- **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – compares **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** scores:  
  • If the two scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue.  
  • Else, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie‑break decision, then continue.

- **Amount Threshold Decision** – evaluates requested amount:  
  • If **amount < €15 000**, the system auto‑approves.  
  • If **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – the board votes **approve** or **reject**.

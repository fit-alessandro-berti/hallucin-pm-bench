### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Process**

1.  **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome (Step 4):** The process proceeds directly to the Decision-Gate if the calculated risk rating is *Low* or *Medium* **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less; otherwise, it routes to Manual-Underwriting-Review.
2.  **TriVote Decision-Gate (Step 6):** A loan application passes this gate and proceeds to the offer stage if it receives approvals from at least two of the three required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer).

### **b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**

1.  **Form 14B** (Application Document)
2.  **Deck Memo** (Required only if Manual-Underwriting-Review is triggered)
3.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Binding Offer Document)
4.  **Signed Offer Sheet 77** (Applicant confirmation of the offer)
5.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Applicant identification confirmation)
6.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalized agreement package)

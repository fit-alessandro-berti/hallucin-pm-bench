### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** – Confirm ID card and mobile phone; skip all credit history checks.
2. **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)** – An Impact Officer evaluates the application.
3. **Dual Underwriter Split** – Two independent underwriters assign separate risk scores (0–9).
4. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** –
	- If scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, move to the next step.
	- Otherwise, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for resolution.
5. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** – Collect at least **3 local references**.
6. **Amount Threshold Decision** –
	- **Amount < €15 000** → Auto-approval.
	- **Amount ≥ €15 000** → Forward to the Micro-loan Board for approval.
7. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – Board decides to approve or reject.
8. **Disburse Funds** – Complete the process by issuing funds.

### **Required Documents at Submission**

- **Digital Application Form** (ML-APP-01)
- **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
- **Latest Income Statement** (e.g., single pay-stub or tax slip)
- **Community Impact Statement (CIS)** template
- **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

This structure ensures strict adherence to the **Micro-loan Fast-Track process**.

To: Chief Operating Officer  
Subject: Highest-Priority Process Performance Gaps

Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment are the three worst-performing activities when SLA overruns and average waiting times are considered jointly. Their waiting times—600, 480, and 200 seconds, respectively—are the three highest among SLA-breaching activities. Legal Approval averages 900 seconds against a 600-second SLA, exceeding it by 300 seconds. Risk Review averages 600 seconds against a 300-second SLA, also exceeding it by 300 seconds. Credit Assessment averages 300 seconds against a 240-second SLA, exceeding it by 60 seconds. Together, these activities account for 2,120 cases.

Recommendations:

- **Legal Approval:** Set an average waiting-time target of 300 seconds, down from 600; if other time remains unchanged, this would bring throughput to the 600-second SLA.
- **Risk Review:** Reduce average waiting from 480 to 180 seconds to close its 300-second SLA gap.
- **Credit Assessment:** Lower average waiting from 200 to 140 seconds to reduce throughput to its 240-second SLA.

## Acme Bank Micro-loan Fast-Track: Decision Points & Required Documents

Here's a summary of the decision points and a list of all required documents in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process:

**Decision Points:**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate:**  Decision based on the difference between Senior Underwriter A's score and Shadow Underwriter B's score.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision:** Determination of whether the requested amount is less than or equal to €15,000.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval:**  Board vote to approve or reject the loan.

**Required Documents:**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

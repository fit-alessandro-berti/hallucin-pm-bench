**To: COO, Acme Insurance | Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance (Executive Memo)**

The three worst-performing activities (based on highest SLA breach, longest processing/variability, and/or extreme wait/rework in the table) are:

- **Why it under-performs:** High **avg_processing_time_min (12)** with elevated **stdev (4)** and **sla_breach_pct (3.2%)** (higher than all except Review/Request).

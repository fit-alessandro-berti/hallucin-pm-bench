**Decision points**

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - Decides whether the claim is eligible.
   - It is checked against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - Decides whether finance pre-approval is granted.
   - Uses the **EligibilityChecklist**.
   - **Process_Refund_Payment** cannot start unless this has succeeded.

3. **High-value branch**
   - Decision: is the refund amount **greater than €1 000**?
   - If **yes**, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required.
   - That review must include the **AuditTrail**.
   - If **no**, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is not required.

4. **Before Process_Refund_Payment**
   - Payment may begin only when:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded, and
     - for high-value claims, **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed, and
     - a signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

**Required documents / artefacts**

- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail** — required for refunds **> €1 000**
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — must be signed before payment
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — sent in **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**

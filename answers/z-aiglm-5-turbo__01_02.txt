**Decision Points**

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – If the two risk scores differ by ≤ 2 points, continue to Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC); if they differ by > 2 points, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision, then continue.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision** – If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves; if the amount is ≥ €15 000, proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The board votes *approve* or *reject*.

**Required Documents**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

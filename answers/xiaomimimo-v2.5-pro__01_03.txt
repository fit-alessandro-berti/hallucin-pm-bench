### a) Decision points in Acme’s process

1. **Conditional branch after Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check** – If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the process skips Manual‑Underwriting‑Review (step 5); otherwise, step 5 is executed.

2. **Decision gate “TriVote”** – The loan passes if at least two of the three required roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve.

---

### b) Documents required in order of first appearance

1. **Form 14B** – Received with the application (step 1).  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow** – Inputs for the Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check (step 3).  
3. **Deck Memo** – Prepared in Manual‑Underwriting‑Review (step 5, if required).  
4. **Offer Sheet 77** – Prepared and sent to the applicant (step 7).  
5. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** – Returned by the applicant (step 8).  
6. **ID Matrix Declaration** – Submitted by the applicant (step 8).  
7. **Loan Dossier 2025** – Created to finalize the agreement (step 9).

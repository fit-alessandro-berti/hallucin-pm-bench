

### **a) Decision Points Summary**  
1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3)**:  
   Outputs a risk rating (Low/Medium/High) based on three scorecards.  

2. **Risk/Amount Check (Step 4)**:  
   Skips manual underwriting if risk is Low/Medium **AND** the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000.  

3. **TriVote Decision-Gate (Step 6)**:  
   Requires approvals from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, with **any two approvals** passing the gate.  

---

### **b) All Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**  
1. **Form 14B** (Step 1)  
2. **Deck Memo** (Step 5)  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7)  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8)  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9)  

*Note: "Signed Offer Sheet 77" (Step 8) is a signed version of Offer Sheet 77 and not a separate document.*

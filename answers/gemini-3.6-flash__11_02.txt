**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**FROM:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**SUBJECT:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling: Top 3 Process Bottlenecks  

Based strictly on Q1-2025 operational metrics, the three worst-performing activities are:

1. **Request_Documents**  
   * **Issue:** Massive operational queue (150-minute wait time) and an extreme 22.0% rework rate, causing a major throughput drop from 270 to 190 cases/day.  
   * **Action:** Implement automated document-validation rules at intake to eliminate the 22.0% rework rate and clear the queue.

2. **Review_Documents**  
   * **Issue:** Worst SLA compliance across the pipeline (12.4% breach rate), longest processing time (25 mins), and highest variability ($\sigma$ = 9 mins).  
   * **Action:** Standardize complex review criteria and reallocate handler capacity to reduce processing time below 25 minutes and stabilize variance.

3. **Initial_Assessment**  
   * **Issue:** Generates the third-highest SLA breach rate (3.2%) and elevated queueing (18-minute wait time) early in the workflow.  
   * **Action:** Deploy automated triaging for simple claims during assessment to lower the 18-minute wait time and prevent downstream delays.

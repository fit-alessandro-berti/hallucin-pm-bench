**Subject:** Top 3 Waiting-Time Bottlenecks & Reduction Strategies  

Based on our process-mining data, here are the three worst-performing activities ranked strictly by waiting time, alongside actions to cut these delays by ≥20%:

**#1 Request_Documents**  
• **Wait Times:** 36.5h average | 120.0h 95th-percentile.  
• **Action:** Implement an automated SMS/email daily reminder cadence linked to a direct-upload portal to instantly capture client submissions.  

**#2 Send_Closure_Letter**  
• **Wait Times:** 12.2h average | 44.0h 95th-percentile.  
• **Action:** Deploy RPA (Robotic Process Automation) to auto-generate and dispatch letters the moment a decision is logged, eliminating manual batching delays.  

**#3 Assess_Liability**  
• **Wait Times:** 7.8h average | 16.4h 95th-percentile.  
• **Action:** Introduce a triage rule-engine to automatically route low-complexity claims to junior adjusters, bypassings primary queue bottlenecks.

**AtlasRemit – Cross-Border Payment Process RACI Matrix**

| # | Task / Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison |
|:--|:---|:--:|:--:|
| 1 | Receive customer transfer instruction & log in system | **R, A** | **I** |
| 2 | Screen payment against sanctions list | **R, A** | **C** |
| 3 | Conduct **KYC Review** on sender and beneficiary | **C** | **R, A** |
| 4 | Approve high-risk transaction | **C** | **R, A** |
| 5 | Release payment & transmit SWIFT message | **R, A** | **C** |
| 6 | Archive case file & notify relevant parties | **R, A** | **I** |

**Legend:**  
`R` = Responsible (performs the task)  
`A` = Accountable (owns the outcome)  
`C` = Consulted (provides input/expertise)  
`I` = Informed (receives outcome notification)  

**Implementation Notes:**  
- All system logging, sanctions screening, payment release, SWIFT transmission, and case archiving are consolidated under the **Transaction Analyst** role per the vocabulary mandate (replacing former *payment clerk* and *IT support* functions).  
- The **KYC Review** replaces all references to Customer Due Diligence/CDD.  
- Approval authority for high-risk flows rests solely with the **Regulatory Liaison**.  
- Consultation/Notification protocols ensure compliance oversight without duplicating execution steps.

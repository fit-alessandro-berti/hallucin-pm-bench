**Decision Points**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Determines if the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points. If the scores differ by more than 2 points, the application escalates to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Determines the approval path based on the loan amount. If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves. If the amount is ≥ €15 000, the application proceeds to the board.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The board votes to either approve or reject the application (only for amounts ≥ €15 000).

**Required Documents**

▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

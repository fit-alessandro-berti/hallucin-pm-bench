One shortest firing sequence is:

\[
T1,\; T2b,\; T4,\; T5
\]

Marking progression:

- Initial: \(p0=1\)
- Fire \(T1\): \(pa=1,\; pb=1\)
- Fire \(T2b\): \(pd=1,\; pb=1\)
- Fire \(T4\): \(pd=1,\; pe=1\)
- Fire \(T5\): \(pf=1\)

Thus \(T5\) fires without any prior firing of \(T3\). The shortcut transition \(T2b\) supplies the required token in \(pd\), while \(T4\) supplies the token in \(pe\).

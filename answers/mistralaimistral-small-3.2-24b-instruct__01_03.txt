### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Process**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome (Step 3):**
   - Determines risk rating (Low, Medium, High) and whether the loan proceeds to manual underwriting (if risk is High or amount > €250,000).

2. **Risk + Amount Threshold Check (Step 4):**
   - If risk is Low/Medium **AND** amount ≤ €250,000, skip manual underwriting (Step 5).

3. **TriVote Approval (Step 6):**
   - Requires **at least two out of three approvals** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) to pass.

---

### **b) Documents in Order of First Appearance**

1. **Form 14B** (Application submission)
2. **Deck Memo** (Manual underwriting review, if applicable)
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Binding offer preparation)
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Applicant confirmation)
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Final agreement)

| Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|---|---|---|
| Receive transfer instruction and log it in the system | R/A | I | I |
| Screen the payment against the sanctions list | R/A | C | I |
| Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary | C | R/A | I |
| Approve a high-risk transaction | C | R/A | I |
| Release the approved payment | R/A | I | C |
| Send the SWIFT message | C | I | R/A |
| Archive the case file | I | I | R/A |
| Notify the Regulatory Liaison that the case file has been archived | I | I | R/A |

**RACI key:**  
- **R** = Responsible  
- **A** = Accountable  
- **C** = Consulted  
- **I** = Informed

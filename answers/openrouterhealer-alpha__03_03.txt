- An exclusive gateway for warranty eligibility decisions
- Parallel split/join gateways for the two simultaneous activities (create shipment order and schedule courier pickup)
- Two end events (one for rejection, one for successful completion)
- All element IDs are prefixed with "wrc_"
- Task names match the specification verbatim
- No additional escalation, approval, or error-handling paths beyond what was explicitly stated

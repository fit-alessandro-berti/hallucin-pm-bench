### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Process**

1.  **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Branch:** A loan application with a **Low or Medium** risk rating and a requested amount **≤ €250,000** skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review.
2.  **TriVote Decision Gate:** The loan passes this gate if it receives **at least two approvals** from the trio of the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer.

### **b) Required Documents in Order of Appearance**

1.  **Form 14B** (Submitted with the application)
2.  **Deck Memo** (Generated during Manual-Underwriting-Review, if applicable)
3.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Issued after the approval decision)
4.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Submitted by the applicant for confirmation)
5.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Final agreement document)

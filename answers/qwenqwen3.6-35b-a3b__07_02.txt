• **Added tasks**  
  - `Task_VerifyIdentity` ("Verify Identity"): Inserted immediately after the start event, preceding application review.  
  - `Task_RequestDocs` ("Request Additional Documents"): New decision branch added to handle incomplete applications.  
  - `Task_FinalReject` ("Final Reject"): New decision branch added to handle ineligible applications.  

• **Removed tasks**  
  - `Task_RejectLoan` ("Reject Loan"): Removed; its original single rejection path is replaced by the split decision branches (`Task_RequestDocs` and `Task_FinalReject`).  

• **Gateway-type changes**  
  - `Gateway_Decision`: Changed from an **Exclusive Gateway** (v1) to an **Inclusive Gateway** (v2). This structural shift transforms the gate from a binary fork (yes/no) into a multi-path decision supporting three outgoing flows (eligible, missing docs, ineligible).

**Yes**; T1 T2b T4 T5

The sequence reaches a marking with tokens in pd and pe (enabling T5) without ever producing a token in pc, so T3 is never enabled or fired. This is shortest: T1 is required to produce pa/pb, T2b is the only way to produce pd without T3, T4 is required for pe, and T5 is the target transition (any permutation of T2b/T4 after T1 works equally well). The feedback loop via T6 only lengthens any path.

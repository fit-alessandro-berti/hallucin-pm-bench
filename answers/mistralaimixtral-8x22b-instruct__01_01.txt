### **Decision Points in Contosoâ€™s Flight Refund Process**
1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - Decision: Determines if the claim is eligible based on *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.
   - Outcome: If eligible, proceeds to **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**; otherwise, the process ends.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - Decision: Finance controller approves the claim using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
   - Outcome: If approved, proceeds to **Process_Refund_Payment** (for low-value claims) or **Senior_Auditor_Review** (for high-value claims).

3. **Senior_Auditor_Review** (High-value branch only)
   - Decision: Senior auditor reviews the case with the *AuditTrail*.
   - Outcome: If approved, proceeds to **Process_Refund_Payment**.

### **Required Documents**
- *RefundRequestForm* (submitted by customer)
- *FlightManifest* (checked by triage agent)
- *WeatherReport* (checked by triage agent)
- *EligibilityChecklist* (used by finance controller)
- *AuditTrail* (required for high-value claims)
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (required for payment)
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* (issued to customer)

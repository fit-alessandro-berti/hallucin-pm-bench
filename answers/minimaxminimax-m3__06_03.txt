# Process B – Warehouse_Replenishment: Concurrent Task Sets

Based solely on Process B, the following sets of tasks can execute concurrently (each set represents tasks on different branches of the same parallel construct):

**Set 1** (branches of *Parallel Split #1* / *Merge #1*):
- Quality_Check
- Update_Inventory

**Set 2** (branches of *Parallel Split #2* / *Merge #2*):
- Generate_Replenishment_Report
- Notify_Procurement

These are the only two parallel constructs in the process; the remaining activities (Start, Receive_Stock, Store_Items, End) are sequential and do not participate in concurrent execution.

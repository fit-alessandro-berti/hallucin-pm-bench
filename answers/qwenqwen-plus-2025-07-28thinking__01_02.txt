### Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process  
*(Exact activity names only)*  
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**:  
   - If scores from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** differ by **≤ 2 points** → continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  
   - Else → escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break decision.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**:  
   - If **amount < €15 000** → system auto-approves.  
   - If **amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**:  
   - Board votes to **approve** or **reject** (mandatory for amounts ≥ €15 000).  

### Required Documents at Submission Time  
*(Exact document names only)*  
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  

⚠️ **Note**:  
- *Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet* and *Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)* are **generated during the process** (steps 4 and 6), **not** submitted upfront.  
- **No credit history documents** are required (per process rules).

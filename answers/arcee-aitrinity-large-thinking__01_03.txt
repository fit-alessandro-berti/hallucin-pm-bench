
**a)** Decision Points in Acme’s Process  
1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**: Combines Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecard outputs to assign a {Low, Medium, High} risk rating.  
2. **Conditional Skip (Step 4)**: If risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the loan skips Manual-Underwriting-Review.  
3. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6)**: Requires approvals from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer; passage requires any two out of three approvals.

**b)** Documents in Order of First Appearance  
1. **Form 14B** (received in Step 1)  
2. **Deck Memo** (produced in Step 5)  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (produced in Step 7)  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (required from applicant in Step 8)  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (produced in Step 9)

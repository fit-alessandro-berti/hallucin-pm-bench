### a) **Summary of Decision Points in Acme’s Process**

1. **Assign-Deck-Reviewer**
A Deck Officer is assigned to review the application.
2. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**
The application receives a Low, Medium, or High risk rating based on Scorecard assessments.
3. **Conditional Skip (Step 4)**
If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the Manual Underwriting Review (step 5) is skipped.
4. **TriVote Decision Gate**
The application requires approvals from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer. Approval from any two out of three suffices for approval.

### b) **Documents Required in Acme’s Process**

1. **Form 14B**
Received when the application is submitted.
2. **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow**
Inputs for the Tri-Color-Risk-Check.
3. **Deck Memo**
Prepared during the Manual Underwriting Review.
4. **Offer Sheet 77**
Prepared for binding offer preparation.
5. **ID Matrix Declaration**
Requested from the applicant for final confirmation.
6. **Loan Dossier 2025**
Prepared upon finalizing the agreement.

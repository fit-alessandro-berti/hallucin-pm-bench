

**Decision Points in Contoso’s Flight-Refund Process:**  
1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**: Determines if the passenger’s claim is eligible by evaluating the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.  
2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**: Approves or denies the claim based on the *EligibilityChecklist*.  
3. **High-Value Branch Decision**: Routes the case to *Senior_Auditor_Review* if the refund amount exceeds **€1,000**, requiring analysis of the *AuditTrail*.  

**Required Documents:**  
1. **RefundRequestForm** (submitted by the passenger).  
2. **FlightManifest** (used during triage validation).  
3. **WeatherReport** (used during triage validation).  
4. **EligibilityChecklist** (used by the finance controller).  
5. **AuditTrail** (required for *Senior_Auditor_Review* in high-value cases).  
6. **BankTransferAuthorization** (required for *Process.Refund_Payment* execution).  

No additional activities or artifacts are defined beyond those listed in the process description.

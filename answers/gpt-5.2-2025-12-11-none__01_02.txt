## Decision points (using the exact activity names)

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   - Condition: compare the two risk scores from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**.  
   - If the two scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  
   - Else, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   - If **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves and proceeds to **Disburse Funds**.  
   - If **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   - Board votes **approve** or **reject**.  
   - If approved, proceed to **Disburse Funds**; if rejected, the process ends without disbursement.

## Required documents at submission time (every required document)

- **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**  
- **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**  
- **Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)**  
- **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**  
- **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**  
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

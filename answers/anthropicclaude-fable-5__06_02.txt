# Parallel Task Sets in Process B — Custom_Furniture_Manufacturing

1. Generate_CAD_Drawing, Confirm_Materials_Availability
2. Milling_Panels, Turning_Legs, CNC_Detailing
3. Sand_Surfaces, Apply_Stain, Install_Hardware

These correspond to parallel blocks **P1**, **P2**, and **P3** respectively. All other tasks in Process B (Accept_Custom_Order, Dry_Fit_Assembly, Glue_And_Clamp, Final_Inspection) are strictly sequential and therefore excluded.

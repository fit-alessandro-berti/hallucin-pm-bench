### a) Decision Points in Acme Bank’s Small-Business Loan-Approval Process

- **Conditional Branch at Step 4**: Proceed by skipping Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) if the Tri-Color-Risk-Check yields a risk rating of Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less; otherwise, proceed to Step 5.
- **Decision Gate “TriVote” at Step 6**: The application passes if at least two out of three approvals are received from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer; otherwise, the application fails and does not proceed.

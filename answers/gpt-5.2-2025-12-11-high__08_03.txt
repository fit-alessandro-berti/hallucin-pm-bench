A shortest firing sequence in which **T5** fires *before* **T3** is:
**T1, T2b, T4, T5**

Marking sketch:
- Start: \(p0=1\)
- Fire **T1**: \(pa=1, pb=1\)
- Fire **T2b** (shortcut): \(pd=1, pb=1\)  (and \(pc=0\), so **T3** is not even enabled)
- Fire **T4**: \(pd=1, pe=1\)
- Fire **T5**: consumes \(pd,pe\) and produces \(pf=1\)

No **T3** firing is needed (nor possible before \(pc\) gets a token), so **T5** can indeed occur first.

Here is the RACI matrix for FinCo’s order-to-cash workflow, strictly using the mandated terminology and role abbreviations:

| Activity              | AM  | CO  | FC  | IT  |
|-----------------------|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| Receive Application   | R   | I   | I   | I   |
| KYC Review            | I   | A   | I   | I   |
| Order Vetting         | A   | I   | I   | I   |
| Payment Clearance     | I   | I   | R   | I   |
| Record Update         | I   | I   | I   | A   |
| Notify Customer       | A   | I   | I   | I   |

**Legend**:  
- **A** = Accountable (final approval/ownership)  
- **R** = Responsible (does the work)  
- **I** = Informed (notified after completion)  

*Note: All terminology adheres to FinCo’s vocabulary policy — “KYC Review”, “Order Vetting”, and “Payment Clearance” are used exclusively.*

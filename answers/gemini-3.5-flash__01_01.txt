Based on the process description provided, here is the summary of the decision points and the list of all required documents for Contoso Airlines’ flight-refund process.

### **Decision Points**
1. **Eligibility Validation (Step 2 - Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility):** The triage agent decides whether the passenger's claim is valid by cross-referencing it against flight and weather data.
2. **Pre-Approval Sign-off (Step 3 - Finance_Controller_PreApprove):** The finance controller decides whether to officially sign off on the claim's eligibility.
3. **Value-Based Routing (Step 4 - High-value branch):** A routing decision based on the refund amount:
   * **If the amount is > €1 000:** The case must route to **Senior_Auditor_Review** before payment.
   * **If the amount is ≤ €1 000:** The case bypasses the senior audit and proceeds directly to payment processing (provided pre-approval is complete).

---

### **Required Documents / Artefacts**
* **RefundRequestForm:** Completed online by the passenger to initiate the claim.
* **FlightManifest:** Used by the triage agent to validate eligibility.
* **WeatherReport:** Used by the triage agent to validate eligibility.
* **EligibilityChecklist:** Used by the finance controller to sign off on the pre-approval.
* **AuditTrail:** Required and reviewed during the *Senior_Auditor_Review* (for claims > €1 000).
* **BankTransferAuthorization:** Must be present and signed for the finance team to execute the payment.
* **RefundConfirmationPDF:** Generated and emailed to the passenger to confirm the completed process.

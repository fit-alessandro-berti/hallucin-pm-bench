### Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation** — **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** checks the claim against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
2. **Finance pre-approval** — **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** signs off using the **EligibilityChecklist**.
3. **Refund-value branch**
   - **More than €1,000:** **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required and must include the **AuditTrail**.
   - **€1,000 or less:** no senior-auditor review is required.
4. **Payment readiness** — **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin only when:
   - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded;
   - for claims over €1,000, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is complete; and
   - the signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

### Required documents

- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail** — required for claims over €1,000
- Signed **BankTransferAuthorization**
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger during **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**

This memo identifies Orion Insurance's three worst-performing activities based solely on waiting times over the last 12 months.

1. **Request Documents:** Average waiting time: 36.5 hours; 95th percentile: 120.0 hours. **Recommendation:** Implement automated document retrieval from commonly requested sources; target a 25% reduction by streamlining access.
2. **Send Closure Letter:** Average waiting time: 12.2 hours; 95th percentile: 44.0 hours. **Recommendation:** Automate closure letter generation and scheduling; aim for a 30% reduction by leveraging existing templates.

Addressing these bottlenecks will significantly improve customer experience and operational efficiency.

**a) Summary of Decision Points in Acme Bank's Process:**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**  
   Decision Rule: Based on the input from the Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow, a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High is assigned.

2. **Conditional Branch Post-Tri-Color-Risk-Check**  
   Decision Rule: If the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, the process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5).

3. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”**  
   Decision Rule: Requires potential approvals from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, though any two of the three approvals are sufficient to proceed.

**b) List of All Required Documents in the Order They First Appear:**

1. **Form 14B** - Document to be filled during the Receive-Application step.
2. **Deck Memo** - Required if Manual-Underwriting-Review is necessary.
3. **Offer Sheet 77** - Prepared during the Prepare-Binding-Offer step.
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** - Required during the Request-Applicant-Confirmation step, alongside the signed Offer Sheet 77.
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** - Finalized document during the Finalize-Agreement step.

**Executive Memo – Orion Insurance Claims Process**

**To:** COO  
**Subject:** Top 3 Waiting-Time Bottlenecks & Targeted Remediation  

The three worst-performing activities by waiting time are:

1. **Request_Documents** – avg 36.5 h, 95th percentile 120.0 h  
2. **Send_Closure_Letter** – avg 12.2 h, 95th percentile 44.0 h  
3. **Assess_Liability** – avg 7.8 h, 95th percentile 16.4 h  

**Recommended actions** (each projected to cut waiting time ≥20 %):  
- **Request_Documents**: Implement automated document-request templates with pre-filled claimant data and 48 h automated reminders → expected reduction >30 %.  
- **Send_Closure_Letter**: Create a one-click “generate & send” button linked to final decision status that auto-populates and dispatches the letter → expected reduction ~40 %.  
- **Assess_Liability**: Introduce a priority queue for high-value or time-sensitive claims and set a 4 h SLA for first assessor review → expected reduction 25 %.  

These three interventions focus exclusively on the dominant waiting-time issues and can be rolled out within the current quarter.

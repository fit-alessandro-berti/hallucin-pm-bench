Based on Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process:

**Decision Points:**

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Decision based on the difference between the scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B (continue if ≤ 2 points difference, else escalate to Harmonisation Committee).
*   **Amount Threshold Decision**: Decision based on the loan amount (auto-approve if < €15 000, proceed to step 8 if ≥ €15 000).
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: Decision by the Micro-loan Board for amounts ≥ €15 000 (approve or reject).

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

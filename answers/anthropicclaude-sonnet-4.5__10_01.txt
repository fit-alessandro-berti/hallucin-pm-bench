# RACI Matrix – FinCo Order-to-Cash Workflow

| **Activity**              | **Account Manager (AM)** | **Compliance Officer (CO)** | **Finance Clerk (FC)** | **IT Support (IT)** |
|---------------------------|:------------------------:|:---------------------------:|:----------------------:|:-------------------:|
| **Receive Application**   | R                        | I                           | I                      | –                   |
| **KYC Review**            | I                        | R                           | –                      | –                   |
| **Order Vetting**         | R                        | I                           | –                      | –                   |
| **Payment Clearance**     | I                        | –                           | R                      | –                   |
| **Record Update**         | I                        | –                           | I                      | R                   |
| **Notify Customer**       | R                        | –                           | I                      | –                   |

---

**Legend:**  
• **R** – Responsible (performs the work)  
• **A** – Accountable (ultimate ownership; not assigned here per typical single-owner pattern)  
• **C** – Consulted (provides input)  
• **I** – Informed (kept in the loop)  
• **–** – Not involved

*Note: Accountable roles may be layered on top depending on FinCo's governance structure.*

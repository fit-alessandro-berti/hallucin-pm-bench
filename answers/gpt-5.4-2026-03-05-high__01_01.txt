Here are the **decision points** in Contoso Airlines’ refund process, with the **documents involved**:

## Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - Activity: **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - Decision: whether the claim is eligible.
   - Documents checked:
     - **FlightManifest**
     - **WeatherReport**

2. **Finance pre-approval**
   - Activity: **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - Decision: whether finance signs off the refund.
   - Document used:
     - **EligibilityChecklist**

3. **High-value routing**
   - Decision: whether the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**.
   - If **yes**:
     - Activity: **Senior_Auditor_Review**
     - Required document:
       - **AuditTrail**
   - If **no**:
     - The process proceeds without **Senior_Auditor_Review**.

4. **Payment release condition**
   - Activity: **Process_Refund_Payment**
   - Decision/gate: payment may begin only when:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded, and
     - for high-value claims, **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed.
   - Required document:
     - signed **BankTransferAuthorization**

## All documents in the process

- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail** *(high-value claims only)*
- signed **BankTransferAuthorization**
- **RefundConfirmationPDF**

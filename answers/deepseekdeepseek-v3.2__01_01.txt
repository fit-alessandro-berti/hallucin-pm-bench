### Summary of Decision Points

1.  **At Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility:** The triage agent decides whether the claim is **eligible** to proceed, based on the provided documents.
2.  **After Finance_Controller_PreApprove:** The process path is decided based on the refund amount. This is a **routing decision**:
    *   If the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**, the case is routed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
    *   If the refund amount is **€1,000 or less**, the case proceeds directly to **Process_Refund_Payment** (once the pre-approval is complete).

### List of All Required Documents

*   **RefundRequestForm** (submitted by the passenger)
*   **FlightManifest** (used for validation)
*   **WeatherReport** (used for validation)
*   **EligibilityChecklist** (used for pre-approval)
*   **AuditTrail** (required for high-value review)
*   **BankTransferAuthorization** (required to execute payment)

Here is a summary of the changes between the two BPMN versions.

**Added tasks**
*   `Task_VerifyIdentity` (Verify Identity)
*   `Task_RequestDocs` (Request Additional Documents)
*   `Task_FinalReject` (Final Reject)

**Removed tasks**
*   `Task_RejectLoan` (Reject Loan)

**Gateway-type changes**
*   The gateway `Gateway_Decision` was changed from `exclusiveGateway` to `inclusiveGateway`.

### Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:

1.  **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – The risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B are compared. If they differ by more than 2 points, the decision is escalated to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break.
2.  **Amount Threshold Decision** – The loan amount is evaluated.
    *   If the amount is **< €15 000**, the system **auto-approves** it.
    *   If the amount is **≥ €15 000**, the application proceeds to the Micro-loan Board for a final decision.
3.  **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The board reviews the application and votes to **approve** or **reject** it.

### RACI Matrix for FinCo’s Order-to-Cash Workflow

Below is the RACI matrix, with activities listed in the correct terminology as specified. Each cell indicates the role’s responsibility: **R** (Responsible), **A** (Accountable), **C** (Consulted), or **I** (Informed). For this process, the performing role is both Responsible and Accountable for each activity, with no explicit consultations indicated. Other roles are Informed where relevant to maintain workflow continuity.

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|----------|-----------------------|--------------------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| **Receive Application** | R, A | I | I | I |
| **KYC Review** | I | R, A | I | I |
| **Order Vetting** | R, A | I | I | I |
| **Payment Clearance** | I | I | R, A | I |
| **Record Update** | I | I | I | R, A |
| **Notify Customer** | R, A | I | I | I |

**Key:**  
- **R (Responsible):** Performs the work.  
- **A (Accountable):** Ultimately answerable for the activity’s completion and approval.  
- **I (Informed):** Notified of the activity’s progress or completion.  
- **C (Consulted):** Provides input before the activity (none specified in this process).

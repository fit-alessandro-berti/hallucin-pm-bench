a) Decision points (gates/branches) – one sentence each

1) Tri-Color-Risk-Check:
- The system assigns a Low, Medium, or High risk rating based on the Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards.

2) Conditional branch after Tri-Color-Risk-Check:
- If the risk is Low or Medium AND the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the process skips Manual-Underwriting-Review; otherwise, Manual-Underwriting-Review is required.

3) Decision-Gate “TriVote”:
- The loan passes this gate if at least two of the three required roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve.

**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** COO, Orion Insurance  
**From:** Senior Process-Mining Analyst  
**Date:** March 10, 2026  
**Subject:** Top Three Waiting-Time Bottlenecks and Recommended Actions  

Analysis of the last 12 months of claims data reveals the three activities with the worst waiting times:

1.  **Request_Documents (Worst)**
    *   Avg. Waiting: **36.5 h** | 95th-Pctile: **120.0 h**
    *   **Action:** Implement an automated, patient-facing document upload portal with pre-filled fields and deadline tracking. This should reduce manual chasing and cut the average wait by 20% (target: **29.2 h**).

2.  **Send_Closure_Letter (2nd)**
    *   Avg. Waiting: **12.2 h** | 95th-Pctile: **44.0 h**
    *   **Action:** Automate the generation and dispatch of standard closure letters immediately upon claim resolution. This will eliminate batching delays and cut the average wait by 20% (target: **9.76 h**).

3.  **Assess_Liability (3rd)**
    *   Avg. Waiting: **7.8 h** | 95th-Pctile: **16.4 h**
    *   **Action:** Introduce a rules-based liability pre-screening engine to handle straightforward cases, freeing senior assessors. This should reduce the queue for complex cases, cutting the average wait by 20% (target: **6.24 h**).

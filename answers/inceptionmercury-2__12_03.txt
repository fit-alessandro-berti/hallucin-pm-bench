**Answer:** No  

**Justification:**  

- **Case C001:** Receive_Order → Validate_Payment → Pack_Items – Validate_Payment follows Receive_Order directly.  
- **Case C003:** Receive_Order → Validate_Payment → Ship_Order – Validate_Payment follows Receive_Order directly.  

However, the pattern is broken in other cases:  

- **Case C002:** Receive_Order → Pack_Items → Validate_Payment – Validate_Payment occurs **after** Pack_Items, not immediately after Receive_Order.  
- **Case C004:** Receive_Order → Check_Fraud → Validate_Payment → Pack_Items – Validate_Payment follows Check_Fraud, not directly after Receive_Order.  

Since at least two cases (C002 and C004) have other activities between Receive_Order and Validate_Payment, the statement “Validate_Payment always occurs immediately after Receive_Order, with no other activities in between” is **incorrect**.


**MEMORANDUM**  
**TO:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**FROM:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**DATE:** Q1 2025  
**SUBJECT:** Claims Handling Performance – Top 3 Improvement Opportunities  

Based on the Q1 dashboard, the three worst-performing activities are:

1.  **Review_Documents** – Highest SLA breach (12.4%) and significant rework (5.3%). Long processing time (25 min) with high variability (stdev 9) causes delays and errors.
    *   **Action:** Implement a standardized review checklist to reduce variability and rework.

2.  **Request_Documents** – Extremely high average wait time (150 min) and rework rate (22%). This creates a major bottleneck before Review.
    *   **Action:** Allocate a dedicated resource pool to process requests continuously, targeting a 30% reduction in wait time.

3.  **Initial_Assessment** – High rework rate (1.8%) and SLA breach (3.2%) despite moderate throughput. Wait time (18 min) adds to cycle time.
    *   **Action:** Introduce a triage protocol to route simple claims away from full assessment, reducing unnecessary workload.

Focus on these areas will directly improve throughput and customer experience.

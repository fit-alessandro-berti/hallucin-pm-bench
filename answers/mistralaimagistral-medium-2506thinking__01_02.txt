## Summary

**Decision Points:**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**:
    - Proceed if underwriter scores differ by ≤ 2 points.
    - Escalate to Harmonisation Committee if scores differ by > 2 points.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**:
    - Auto-approve if the loan amount is < €15,000.
    - Proceed to final approval if the loan amount is ≥ €15,000.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval**:
    - The board votes to approve or reject loans of ≥ €15,000.

**Required Documents:**
1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
3. Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

Thus, there are three key decision points in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process, and six required documents.

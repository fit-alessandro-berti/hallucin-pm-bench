
**EXECUTIVE MEMO**

TO: Chief Operating Officer  
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
RE: Critical Process Performance Gaps Requiring Immediate Attention

Analysis of current performance data reveals three activities significantly exceeding SLA targets while experiencing substantial waiting times:

• **Legal Approval** exceeds SLA by 300 seconds with 600 seconds average waiting time.  
• **Risk Review** exceeds SLA by 300 seconds with 480 seconds average waiting time.  
• **Credit Assessment** exceeds SLA by 60 seconds with 200 seconds average waiting time.

**Recommendations:**
• Implement automated risk-scoring algorithms to pre-screen applications before Legal Approval, reducing manual review burden and decreasing the 600-second waiting queue.  
• Deploy parallel processing teams for Credit Assessment to handle the 1,175 cases simultaneously, cutting the 200-second average wait by at least 40%.  
• Establish dedicated specialist pods for Risk Review with real-time case assignment to eliminate the 480-second backlog and achieve target processing within 300 seconds.

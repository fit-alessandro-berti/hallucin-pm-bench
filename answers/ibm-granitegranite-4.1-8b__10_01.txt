**RACI Matrix for FinCo’s Order‑to‑Cash Workflow**  

| Activity (Rows) | AM (Account Manager) | CO (Compliance Officer) | FC (Finance Clerk) | IT (IT Support) |
|-----------------|----------------------|--------------------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Receive Application | **R** (Responsible) | A (Accountable) | C (Consulted) | I (Informed) |
| **KYC Review** | C (Consulted) | **A** (Accountable) | I (Informed) | I (Informed) |
| **Order Vetting** | **R** (Responsible) | C (Consulted) | I (Informed) | I (Informed) |
| **Payment Clearance** | C (Consulted) | I (Informed) | **R** (Responsible) | I (Informed) |
| Record Update | C (Consulted) | I (Informed) | I (Informed) | **A** (Accountable) |
| Notify Customer | **R** (Responsible) | I (Informed) | I (Informed) | C (Consulted) |

**Legend**  
- **R** – Responsible: performs the task.  
- **A** – Accountable: ultimately owns the task’s outcome.  
- **C** – Consulted: provides input or expertise.  
- **I** – Informed: kept aware of progress or completion.  

The matrix adheres strictly to the preferred terminology: **“KYC Review,” “Order Vetting,”** and **“Payment Clearance.”** No legacy terms such as “Customer Due Diligence,” “Order Validation,” or “Payment Processing” are used.

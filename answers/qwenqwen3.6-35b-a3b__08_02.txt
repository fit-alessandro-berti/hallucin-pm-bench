**Answer 2:** Sequence: T1, T2, T7, T4, T5.  

T5 consumes tokens in P2 and P5. P2 originates from T1. P5 requires T4, which needs P4. P4 can be produced by T7 (via P1→T2→P3), entirely bypassing T3.

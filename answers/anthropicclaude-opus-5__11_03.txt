**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** Chief Operating Officer, Orion Credit
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst
**Subject:** Three worst-performing activities by SLA breach and waiting time

Analysis of the eight measured activities shows three that breach their target SLA while also carrying the highest average waiting times.

Legal Approval averages 900s against a 600s target — a 300s overrun — with 600s (two-thirds of the total) spent waiting across 465 cases. Risk Review averages 600s against a 300s target — also 300s over — of which 480s is pure waiting time on 480 cases. Credit Assessment averages 300s against a 240s target, a 60s overrun, with 200s of waiting time affecting the largest volume at 1,175 cases.

Recommended actions:

- **Legal Approval:** add reviewer capacity to cut the 600s queue, the single largest waiting time in the process.
- **Risk Review:** rebalance workload so the 480s wait, 80% of throughput time, falls within the 300s SLA.
- **Credit Assessment:** automate case triage to remove the 200s wait across 1,175 cases.

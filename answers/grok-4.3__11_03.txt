To: COO
From: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
Subject: Performance Analysis of Process Activities

The three worst-performing activities, identified jointly by SLA breaches and high waiting times, are Risk Review, Legal Approval, and Credit Assessment. Risk Review exceeds its 300-second target by 300 seconds and has an average waiting time of 480 seconds. Legal Approval exceeds its 600-second target by 300 seconds and has an average waiting time of 600 seconds. Credit Assessment exceeds its 240-second target by 60 seconds and has an average waiting time of 200 seconds.

The following actions are recommended:
- Allocate additional resources to Risk Review to lower the 480-second waiting time and mitigate the 300-second SLA breach.
- Assign more capacity to Legal Approval to reduce the 600-second waiting time and resolve the 300-second excess over the 600-second target.
- Implement improvements in Credit Assessment to decrease the 200-second waiting time and correct the 60-second SLA overrun.

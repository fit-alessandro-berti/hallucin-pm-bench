### a) Decision points (gates / conditional branches), one sentence each

1) **Conditional branch before Manual-Underwriting-Review:**  
If **risk ∈ {Low, Medium}** *and* **requested-amount ≤ €250,000**, then **skip** Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review).

2) **Decision gate “TriVote”:**  
A loan **passes** only after approvals are collected from **Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer**, and the rule is that **any two of the three approvals suffice**.

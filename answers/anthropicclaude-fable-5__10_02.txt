# AtlasRemit — Cross-Border Payment Process: RACI Matrix

**Roles:**
- **TA** = Transaction Analyst
- **RL** = Regulatory Liaison
- **IT** = IT Support

| # | Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|----------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 1 | Receive customer transfer instruction and log it in the system | **R / A** | I | – |
| 2 | Screen payment against the sanctions list | **R / A** | C | – |
| 3 | Perform **KYC Review** on sender and beneficiary | I | **R / A** | – |
| 4 | Approve high-risk transactions (if flagged) | I | **R / A** | – |
| 5 | Release the approved payment | **R / A** | I | C |
| 6 | Transmit the SWIFT message | I | – | **R / A** |
| 7 | Archive the case file | – | **I** | **R / A** |

## Notes on Role Assignments

- **Task 1–2:** The **Transaction Analyst** is both Responsible and Accountable for intake, logging, and sanctions screening; the **Regulatory Liaison** is consulted on screening hits.
- **Task 3:** The **KYC Review** is owned end-to-end by the **Regulatory Liaison**, who checks both sender and beneficiary; the **Transaction Analyst** is informed of the outcome so processing can continue.
- **Task 4:** High-risk approval rests solely with the **Regulatory Liaison**; the **Transaction Analyst** is informed of the decision.
- **Task 5–6:** The **Transaction Analyst** releases the payment once approval is confirmed; **IT Support** is consulted on system readiness and is then Responsible/Accountable for SWIFT message transmission.
- **Task 7:** **IT Support** archives the case file; the **Regulatory Liaison** is informed upon completion, per the source process.

*Legend: R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed, – = no involvement.*

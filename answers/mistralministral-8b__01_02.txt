**Decision Points in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** – Confirm ID card and mobile phone.
2. **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)** – Assessed by an Impact Officer.
3. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Two risk scores (Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B) must differ by ≤ 2 points for approval.
4. **Amount Threshold Decision** – If the loan amount is ≥ €15,000, proceed to Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**

1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
3. Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

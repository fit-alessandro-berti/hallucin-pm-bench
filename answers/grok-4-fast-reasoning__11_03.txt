### Executive Memo: Performance Analysis of Key Loan Processing Activities

Dear COO,

Our analysis of loan application processing reveals significant bottlenecks in three activities that jointly exhibit the highest SLA breaches and waiting times: Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment. These issues impact efficiency across 465, 480, and 1175 cases, respectively, contributing to overall delays.

Legal Approval exceeds its 600-second SLA by 300 seconds, with an average waiting time of 600 seconds, indicating severe resource constraints. Risk Review surpasses its 300-second SLA by 300 seconds, paired with 480 seconds of waiting time, highlighting prolonged manual reviews. Credit Assessment breaches its 240-second SLA by 60 seconds, alongside 200 seconds of waiting, suggesting inefficiencies in evaluation processes.

To address these, we recommend:

- For Legal Approval, allocate additional legal staff to reduce the 600-second waiting time, targeting a 50% decrease based on current 465-case volume.
- For Risk Review, implement automated preliminary screening to cut the 480-second waiting time, informed by the 300-second throughput excess across 480 cases.
- For Credit Assessment, streamline data inputs to lower the 200-second waiting time, leveraging the 60-second SLA overrun in 1175 cases for prioritized tool upgrades.

These actions will enhance throughput and compliance. Please advise on next steps.

Best regards,  
Senior Process-Performance Analyst

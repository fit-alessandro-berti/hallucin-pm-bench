## a) Summary of Decision Points:

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3)**: Evaluates loan application risk (Low, Medium, High) based on Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards.

2. **Conditional Branch (Step 4)**: If risk rating is Low or Medium AND requested amount is ≤ €250,000, skip Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).

3. **Decision-Gate "TriVote" (Step 6)**: Approval decision requiring votes from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer; any two out of the three approvals suffice for pass.

2. **Deck Memo** *(Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review; conditional requirement depending on Step 4)*
3. **Offer Sheet 77** *(Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)*
4. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** *(Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)*
5. **ID Matrix Declaration** *(Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)*

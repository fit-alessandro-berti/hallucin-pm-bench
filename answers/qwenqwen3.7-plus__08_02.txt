**Question 2.** Minimal sequence: [T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]

**Explanation:** 
Firing T1 places tokens in P1 and P2. To enable the synchronizing join T5, we need tokens in *both* P2 and P5. We can generate the P5 token by routing the P1 token through T2 (yielding P3), T7 (yielding P4), and T4 (yielding P5). This path bypasses T3 entirely, leaving the original token in P2 unconsumed. Consequently, T5 becomes enabled and can fire before T3 ever occurs.

## AtlasRemit — RACI Matrix

**Mandated role mapping**
- *Payment clerk* → **Transaction Analyst**
- *Compliance officer* → **Regulatory Liaison**
- *IT Support* → **IT Support** (no mandated substitution)

**Legend:** R = Responsible · A = Accountable · C = Consulted · I = Informed

| # | Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|------|:------------------:|:------------------:|:----------:|
| 1 | Receive transfer instruction and log it in the system | R / A | I | — |
| 2 | Screen payment against the sanctions list | R / A | C | — |
| 3 | Perform **KYC Review** on sender and beneficiary | I | R / A | — |
| 4 | Approve high-risk transaction | C | R / A | — |
| 5 | Release the approved payment | R / A | I | C |
| 6 | Send the SWIFT message | I | I | R / A |
| 7 | Archive the case file | I | I | R / A |
| 8 | Notify of completed case archival | — | I | R / A |

---

**Notes**
- Each task has exactly one **Accountable** owner to preserve clear ownership.
- The **Regulatory Liaison** retains accountability for both the **KYC Review** (Task 3) and high-risk approval (Task 4).
- The **Transaction Analyst** is consulted on high-risk approval, as they hold the original transaction context.
- **IT Support** is accountable for technical execution (SWIFT transmission and archival) and ensures the **Regulatory Liaison** is informed upon final archival.

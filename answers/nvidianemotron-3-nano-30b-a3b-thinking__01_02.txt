**Decision points (using the exact activity names)**  
- **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – decides whether to proceed or to escalate to the Harmonisation Committee based on the score difference.  
- **Amount Threshold Decision** – decides whether the loan amount is below €15 000 (auto‑approve) or triggers the next step.  
- **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)** – the board votes *approve* or *reject* the loan.  

**Required documents at submission time (exact names)**  
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay‑stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score‑Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

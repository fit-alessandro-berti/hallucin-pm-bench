**RACI Legend**  
- **R** = Responsible  
- **A** = Accountable  
- **C** = Consulted  
- **I** = Informed  

**Notes**  
- The **Front-Office Analyst** performs **Intake Vetting** and **KYC Review**, therefore is **R** for those activities.  
- The **Compliance Analyst** conducts **KYC Review** and **AML Screening**, thus is **R** for those.  
- **AML Screening** is finalized by **Compliance Officer**, who also provides **Approval**; thus **R** and **A** for that activity.  
- **Risk Scoring** is executed by the **Compliance Analyst**, making them **R**; **Treasury Ops** is **R** for **Funds Release & Notification**.  
- **Treasury Ops** is **R** for **Funds Release & Notification** and **A** for that activity; they are **I** for all prior steps.  
- **Compliance Officer** is **A** for **Approval** and **R** for **Funds Release & Notification**; they are **C** for **KYC Review** and **AML Screening**.  
- **Front-Office Analyst** is **I** for **Approval** and **Funds Release & Notification**; they are **C** for **KYC Review** and **AML Screening**.  
- **Compliance Analyst** is **C** for **Intake Vetting** and **Funds Release & Notification**; they are **I** for **Risk Scoring**.  
- **Treasury Ops** is **I** for **Intake Vetting**, **KYC Review**, **AML Screening**, and **Risk Scoring**; they are **R** for **Funds Release & Notification**.  

This matrix ensures every activity and role is covered with the correct governance responsibilities.

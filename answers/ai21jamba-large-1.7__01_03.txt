### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Loan-Approval Process**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**: Assign a risk rating (Low, Medium, or High) based on inputs from Scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow), and if the risk is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is €250,000 or less, skip step 5.
2. **Manual-Underwriting-Review**: If the risk is High or the requested amount exceeds €250,000, perform a manual review using the Deck Memo.
3. **Decision Gate "TriVote"**: The Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer must all vote, with at least two approvals required for the loan to proceed.
4. **Applicant Confirmation**: After receiving signed Offer Sheet 77 and ID Matrix Declaration, finalize the loan agreement.

### **b) Documents Required in the Process**

1. **Form 14B**: Submitted with the initial loan application.
2. **Deck Memo**: Used during the Manual-Underwriting-Review step (if required).
3. **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow**: Inputs for the Tri-Color-Risk-Check.
4. **Offer Sheet 77**: Generated for the applicant and returned with their signed confirmation.
5. **ID Matrix Declaration**: Required during the confirmation stage to finalize the agreement.
6. **Loan Dossier 2025**: Final document prepared upon agreement finalization.

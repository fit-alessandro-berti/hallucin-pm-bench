Below is the RACI matrix, using your approved terminology for each activity. Only one role is Accountable per activity; Consulted and Informed assignments reflect the logical handoffs among teams.

| Activity               | Account Manager | Compliance Officer | Finance Clerk | IT Support |
|------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|---------------|------------|
| **Receive Application**    | R, A            | I                 |               |            |
| **KYC Review**             | C, I            | R, A              |               |            |
| **Order Vetting**          | R, A            |                   | I             |            |
| **Payment Clearance**      | C, I            |                   | R, A          | I          |
| **Record Update**          | A, I            |                   | I             | R          |
| **Notify Customer**        | R, A            | I                 | I             | I          |

**Key**  
- **R** = Responsible (does the work)  
- **A** = Accountable (signs off; only one per activity)  
- **C** = Consulted (provides input before the step)  
- **I** = Informed (notified after the step)  

*Rationale*  
- **Receive Application** is owned end‑to‑end by the Account Manager; Compliance is notified so KYC Review can be scheduled.  
- **KYC Review** is the Compliance Officer’s responsibility and accountability; the Account Manager may be consulted for missing client data and is informed of the outcome.  
- **Order Vetting** is performed and owned by the Account Manager; Finance is informed so they can prepare for Payment Clearance.  
- **Payment Clearance** is the Finance Clerk’s task and accountability; the Account Manager may be consulted for authorisation details, and IT is informed to trigger Record Update.  
- **Record Update** is executed by IT Support, but the Account Manager remains accountable for the correctness of the CRM data; both Account Manager and Finance are informed once the refresh is complete.  
- **Notify Customer** is the Account Manager’s final step, with all other roles informed to close the loop.

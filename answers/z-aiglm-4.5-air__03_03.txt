### Key Features:
1. **Flow Elements Only**:
   - No invented paths (escalations/approvals)
   - Exact task names as specified
   - Two end events (success/rejection)

2. **Gateway Implementation**:
   - `wrc_gateway_completeness`: Validates claim completeness
   - `wrc_gateway_warranty`: Checks warranty status
   - `wrc_parallel_split/join`: Manages parallel shipping/pickup

3. **Intermediate Event**:
   - `wrc_wait_for_info`: Represents waiting for customer response after requesting missing information

4. **ID Prefixing**:
   - All IDs prefixed with "wrc_" (e.g., `wrc_validate_claim`, `wrc_gateway_warranty`)

5. **Loop Handling**:
   - Completeness check loops back to validation until resolved
   - No artificial timeout mechanisms

6. **Parallel Execution**:
   - Split/join gateways synchronize shipment and pickup tasks
   - Both tasks must complete before inventory update

This diagram strictly follows the textual requirements without additions or interpretations beyond the specified process flow.

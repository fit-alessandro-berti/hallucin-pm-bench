### RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Receive Application | R/A | I | I | I |
| KYC Review | C | R/A | I | I |
| Order Vetting | R/A | C | I | I |
| Payment Clearance | C | I | R/A | I |
| Record Update | I | I | C | R/A |
| Notify Customer | R/A | I | C | C |

**Legend:** **R** = Responsible, **A** = Accountable, **C** = Consulted, **I** = Informed.  
Where no separate accountable role was specified, the activity owner is assigned both **R** and **A**.

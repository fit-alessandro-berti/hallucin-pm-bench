Add an **interrupting boundary timer event** to `Task_RequestDocs`, configured for the absolute deadline `submissionTime + 48 hours` (rather than 48 hours after the task starts). If the request is still waiting at the deadline, the timer cancels it, throws an escalation event such as `Escalate_48h`, and routes directly to the existing `Task_Triage`. The normal `f6` path is canceled, while complete-document claims continue through `f5`. The existing downstream flow `f7–f9` remains unchanged. This automatically escalates overdue claims without adding any task or subprocess.

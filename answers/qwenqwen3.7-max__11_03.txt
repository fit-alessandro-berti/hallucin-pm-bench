MEMORANDUM TO: Chief Operating Officer | FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst, Orion Credit | SUBJECT: Worst-Performing Activities

An analysis of our process data reveals three specific activities failing their Target_SLA due to excessively high Avg_Waiting_Time. Legal Approval exceeds its 600-second Target_SLA by 300 seconds, driven by a 600-second Avg_Waiting_Time. Risk Review exceeds its 300-second Target_SLA by 300 seconds, driven by a 480-second Avg_Waiting_Time. Credit Assessment exceeds its 240-second Target_SLA by 60 seconds, driven by a 200-second Avg_Waiting_Time.

To resolve these operational breaches, I recommend implementing the following concrete, data-driven actions:
* Reduce the 600-second Avg_Waiting_Time in Legal Approval to bring the 900-second Avg_Throughput_Time under the 600-second Target_SLA.
* Decrease the 480-second Avg_Waiting_Time in Risk Review to lower the 600-second Avg_Throughput_Time below the 300-second Target_SLA.
* Cut the 200-second Avg_Waiting_Time in Credit Assessment to ensure the 300-second Avg_Throughput_Time meets the 240-second Target_SLA.

Implementing these reductions will align all three activities with their required targets.

# Acme Micro-loan Fast-Track – Decision Points & Required Documents

## Decision Points (in process order)

| # | Activity Name | Trigger / Condition | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** | Identity & mobile phone check (no credit history) | Pass → continue; Fail → process stops |
| 2 | **Underwriter Alignment Gate** | Compare Senior Underwriter A score vs. Shadow Underwriter B score | Difference **≤ 2 points** → continue to NFC; **> 2 points** → escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break, then continue |
| 3 | **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** | At least **3 local references** collected | Met → continue; Not met → cannot proceed |
| 4 | **Amount Threshold Decision** | Loan amount requested | **< €15 000** → system auto-approval; **≥ €15 000** → forward to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** |
| 5 | **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** | Triggered only for amounts ≥ €15 000 | Board votes **approve** or **reject** |

---

## Required Documents at Submission

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
2. **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**
3. **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

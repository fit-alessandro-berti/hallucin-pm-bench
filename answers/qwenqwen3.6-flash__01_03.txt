**a) Decision Points**
1. **Risk & Amount Branch (Step 4):** The application skips manual underwriting if the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested amount is €250,000 or less; otherwise, it proceeds to Step 5.
2. **TriVote Gate (Step 6):** The loan advances only if at least two of the three required officials (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant approval.

1. Form 14B
2. Deck Memo
3. Offer Sheet 77

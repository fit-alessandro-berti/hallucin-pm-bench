**Firing sequence:** `T1, T2, T7, T3, T5`

**Explanation:** 
From initial marking $M_0 = \{P_0\}$, firing **T1** (AND-split) produces tokens in $P_1$ and $P_2$. 
From $P_1$, the process fires **T2** then **T7**, moving a token to $P_4$, and subsequently fires **T4** to place a token in $P_5$.

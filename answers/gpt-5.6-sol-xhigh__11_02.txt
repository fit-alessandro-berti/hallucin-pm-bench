**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling performance priorities

The dashboard clearly identifies three worst-performing activities:

1. **Request_Documents** — The largest bottleneck: **150-minute wait**, **22.0% rework** (both highest), **5.5% SLA breaches**, and throughput of only **190 cases/day**.  
   **Action:** Introduce a mandatory document-completeness checklist with automated missing-item prompts; track weekly reductions against the 22.0% rework and 150-minute wait baselines.

2. **Review_Documents** — The weakest execution performance: **25-minute processing time**, **9-minute variability**, and **12.4% SLA breaches**, all highest in the process. Throughput is also low at **185/day**.  
   **Action:** Pilot a standardized review checklist and retain it only if processing time, variability, and SLA breaches decline from their Q1 baselines.

3. **Initial_Assessment** — Consistently ranks next worst: **18-minute wait**, **12-minute processing time**, **4-minute variability**, **1.8% rework**, and **3.2% SLA breaches**.  
   **Action:** Rebalance assessor capacity toward this queue and monitor whether wait time and SLA breaches fall below the current 18-minute and 3.2% levels.

**Decision points:**

- Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility: check of the claim against the FlightManifest and WeatherReport (to determine whether the claim proceeds).
- Finance_Controller_PreApprove: sign-off decision using the EligibilityChecklist.
- High-value branch: if the refund amount is greater than €1 000, the case must proceed to Senior_Auditor_Review (which must include the AuditTrail).
- Process_Refund_Payment: execution is permitted only after Finance_Controller_PreApprove has succeeded and, for high-value claims, after Senior_Auditor_Review has been completed (with the signed BankTransferAuthorization present).

**Required documents:**

- RefundRequestForm
- FlightManifest
- WeatherReport
- EligibilityChecklist
- AuditTrail (high-value claims only)
- BankTransferAuthorization
- RefundConfirmationPDF

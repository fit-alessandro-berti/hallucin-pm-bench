## a) Every decision point (gate or conditional branch)

1) **Conditional branch after Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 4)**  
- **Rule (one sentence):** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, the process **skips** the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).

2) **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6)**  
- **Rule (one sentence):** The loan **passes** if **at least two out of three** required approvals are obtained from the **Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer**.

## b) All required documents (in the order they first appear)

1) **Form 14B** (Receive-Application)  
2) **Deck Memo** (Manual-Underwriting-Review; only required when Step 5 is reached)  
3) **Offer Sheet 77** (Prepare-Binding-Offer; required to proceed)  
4) **ID Matrix Declaration** (Applicant confirmation, alongside the signed Offer Sheet 77)  
5) **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalize-Agreement)

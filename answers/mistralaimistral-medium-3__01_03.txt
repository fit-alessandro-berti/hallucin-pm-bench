### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Loan-Approval Process**

1. **Risk-Based Conditional Branch (Step 4):**
   - If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** **and** the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, skip the **Manual Underwriting Review (Step 5)**.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):**
   - Requires **at least two out of three approvals** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) to proceed.

---

### **b) Required Documents in Order of First Appearance**

1. **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive Application)
2. **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow** (Step 3: Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
3. **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual Underwriting Review, if applicable)
4. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare Binding Offer)
5. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request Applicant Confirmation)
6. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize Agreement)

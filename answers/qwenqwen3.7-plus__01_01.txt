Based on the provided process description, here are the decision points and required documents for Contoso Airlines’ flight-refund process.

### **Decision Points**
1. **Refund Value Threshold (Explicit Branch):** 
   - Determines whether the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**. 
   - *If yes:* The case must proceed to the **Senior_Auditor_Review** step.
   - *If no:* The case bypasses the Senior Auditor Review.
2. **Eligibility and Pre-approval Validation (Sequential Checks):** 
   - The process requires sequential validations to advance: the Triage Agent must validate the claim against the manifest and weather data, and the Finance Controller must successfully sign off using the eligibility checklist before payment processing can be considered.

### **Required Documents**
* ***RefundRequestForm*** (completed by the passenger during Customer_Submit_Claim)
* ***FlightManifest*** (used by the Triage Agent for validation)
* ***WeatherReport*** (used by the Triage Agent for validation)
* ***EligibilityChecklist*** (used by the Finance Controller to sign off)
* ***AuditTrail*** (required to be included in the Senior_Auditor_Review for high-value claims)
* ***BankTransferAuthorization*** (must be signed before Process_Refund_Payment can begin)
* ***RefundConfirmationPDF*** (generated and emailed to the passenger during Issue_Confirmation_Letter)

*(Note: No other activities, decision points, or documents—such as the legacy "CustomerService_Agent_Decide"—are part of this specific Contoso process variant.)*

**Decision Points in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** – Confirm ID card and mobile phone.  
2. **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)** – Conducted by an Impact Officer.  
3. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Compare scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B.  
   - If scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue.  
   - If scores differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision.  
4. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Based on the loan amount:  
   - If **amount < €15 000**, auto-approve.  
   - If **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.  
5. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – Board votes *approve* or *reject*.  

---

**Required Documents at Submission Time:**

- **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**  
- **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)  
- **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**  
- **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**  
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

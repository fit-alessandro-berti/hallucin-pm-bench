Executive Memo to COO — Process Performance Findings

Three activities are worst-performing when considering both SLA breaches (Avg_Throughput_Time vs. Target_SLA) and high Avg_Waiting_Time. **Legal Approval** exceeds its SLA by **300s** (900s vs. 600s) and has the highest waiting time (**600s**). **Risk Review** also exceeds its SLA by **300s** (600s vs. 300s) with similarly high waiting (**480s**). **Credit Assessment** exceeds its SLA by **60s** (300s vs. 240s) and shows elevated waiting (**200s**). Other SLA breaches (e.g., Verify Identity) are less severe in both SLA overrun and waiting time.

Recommendations:
- **Legal Approval:** Implement a prioritized handling lane for cases already waiting ~**600s**, with escalation rules to prevent further queueing before approval.
- **Risk Review:** Add pre-check screening so only complete cases enter the risk review queue, targeting reductions in the **480s** waiting period.
- **Credit Assessment:** Standardize and pre-populate assessment inputs to reduce time spent waiting (**200s**) before assessment completes.

## Decision points (using exact activity names)

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** — decision to confirm **ID card and mobile phone** (no credit history checks); if verification fails, the process cannot proceed.
2. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** — decision based on the two risk scores:  
   - If scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue.  
   - If scores differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break, then continue.
3. **Amount Threshold Decision** — decision based on requested amount:  
   - If **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves.  
   - If **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.
4. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** — board vote decision: **approve** or **reject**.

## Every required document (at submission time)

- Digital Application Form (**ML-APP-01**)  
- **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)  
- **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- **Community Impact Statement (CIS)** template  
- **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**  
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

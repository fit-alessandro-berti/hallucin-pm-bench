

**a) Decision Points Summary:**  
1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**: Assigns a risk rating (Low, Medium, High) based on three scorecards.  
2. **Conditional Skip in Step 4**: Skips manual underwriting if risk is Low/Medium *and* requested amount ≤ €250,000.  
3. **TriVote Decision Gate**: Requires at least two approvals from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer to proceed.  

---

**b) Required Documents in Order of First Appearance:**  
1. **Form 14B** (Received in Step 1)  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** (Input to Tri-Color-Risk-Check in Step 3)  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** (Input to Tri-Color-Risk-Check in Step 3)  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** (Input to Tri-Color-Risk-Check in Step 3)  
5. **Deck Memo** (Generated in Manual-Underwriting-Review, Step 5)  
6. **Offer Sheet 77** (Prepared in Step 7)  
7. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Submitted in Step 8)  
8. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalized in Step 9)

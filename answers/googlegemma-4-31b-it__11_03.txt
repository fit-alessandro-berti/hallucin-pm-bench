MEMORANDUM

TO: COO
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
SUBJECT: Process Performance Critical Bottlenecks

Analysis of current activity data identifies three critical performance failures where combined throughput and waiting times significantly breach Target SLAs. 

Legal Approval is the worst performer, exceeding its 600s SLA by 900s (1,500s total time). Risk Review exceeds its 300s SLA by 780s (1,080s total time). Credit Assessment exceeds its 240s SLA by 260s (500s total time). These activities exhibit the highest waiting times in the process, specifically 600s, 480s, and 200s respectively.

To resolve these breaches, I recommend the following:

* Legal Approval: Reduce the 600s average waiting time to align the 1,500s total time with the 600s SLA.
* Risk Review: Address the 480s average waiting time to bring the 1,080s total time under the 300s SLA.
* Credit Assessment: Decrease the 200s average waiting time to eliminate the 260s SLA overrun.

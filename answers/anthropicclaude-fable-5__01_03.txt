# Acme Bank Small-Business Loan Process

## a) Decision Points

There are exactly **two** decision points in the process:

**1. Conditional Branch after Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 4)**
If the risk rating from the Tri-Color-Risk-Check is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, then Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review) is skipped; otherwise — i.e., the rating is High *or* the amount exceeds €250,000 — the application must pass through Manual-Underwriting-Review.

**2. Decision-Gate "TriVote" (Step 6)**
The application is presented to three designated approvers — the **Credit Lead**, the **Regulatory Liaison**, and the **Sustainability Officer** — and it passes if **any two of the three** grant approval (i.e., a 2-out-of-3 majority rule, even though all three roles must participate in the vote).

*Note:* Step 3 (Tri-Color-Risk-Check) produces an *outcome* (Low/Medium/High), but it is an assessment activity, not itself a gate — its result is consumed by the conditional branch in Step 4.

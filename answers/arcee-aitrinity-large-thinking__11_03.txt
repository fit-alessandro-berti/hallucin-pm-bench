**Subject:** Executive Summary: Critical Process Bottlenecks  

Analysis of end-to-end loan processing reveals three activities requiring immediate intervention due to chronic SLA breaches and excessive waiting times.  

**Risk Review** exhibits the largest gap, with an average throughput time of 600 seconds—exceeding its 300-second SLA by 300 seconds—while customers wait an average of 480 seconds. **Legal Approval** shows a parallel failure: throughput of 900 seconds (200% over its 600-second SLA) and the longest wait time of 600 seconds. **Credit Assessment** also underperforms, averaging 300 seconds (60 seconds over its 240-second SLA) with a substantial 200-second wait.  

These delays cascade, inflating total cycle time and degrading customer experience.  

**Recommendations:**  
- **Risk Review:** Redistribute reviewer workload or implement a parallel review queue to reduce the 480-second average wait.  
- **Legal Approval:** Allocate dedicated legal resources to this step to address the 600-second wait and 300-second SLA excess.  
- **Credit Assessment:** Standardize assessment templates and escalate complex cases to senior assessors to lower the 200-second wait.

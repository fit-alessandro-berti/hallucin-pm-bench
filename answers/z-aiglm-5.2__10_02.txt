Here is the RACI matrix for the AtlasRemit cross-border payment process, using the mandated terminology.

### Legend
*   **R** – Responsible (Performs the task)
*   **A** – Accountable (Owns the task outcome; only one per task)
*   **C** – Consulted (Provides input/expertise)
*   **I** – Informed (Kept up-to-date on progress/outcome)

### RACI Matrix

| Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **1. Receive customer’s transfer instruction and log in system** | R / A | I | I |
| **2. Screen payment against the sanctions list** | R / A | C | I |
| **3. Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary** | I | R / A | I |
| **4. Approve transaction flagged as high-risk** | I | R / A | I |
| **5. Release the payment** | R / A | I | I |
| **6. Ensure the SWIFT message is sent** | I | I | R / A |
| **7. Archive the case file and notify Regulatory Liaison** | I | I | R / A |

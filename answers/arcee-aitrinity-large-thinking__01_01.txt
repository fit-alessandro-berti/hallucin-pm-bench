
**Decision Points**

1. **Eligibility Validation (Triage Agent)** – The triage agent decides whether the claim is eligible by checking the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.  
2. **Pre‑Approval (Finance Controller)** – The finance controller decides whether to pre‑approve the refund based on the *EligibilityChecklist*.  
3. **High‑Value Escalation** – The process automatically decides, based on the refund amount, whether the case exceeds €1 000 and therefore must go to the *Senior Auditor Review*.

**Required Documents**

- *RefundRequestForm* (submitted by the passenger)  
- *FlightManifest* (used during eligibility check)  
- *WeatherReport* (used during eligibility check)  
- *EligibilityChecklist* (used by the finance controller)  
- *AuditTrail* (required for the senior auditor review)  
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (must be present before payment)  
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* (emailed to the passenger after payment)

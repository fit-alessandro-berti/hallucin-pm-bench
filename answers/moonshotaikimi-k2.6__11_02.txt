**MEMO**

**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**From:** Process Performance  
**Re:** Q1 Claims Handling – Priority Fixes  

Three activities account for the worst performance:

**1. Request Documents** – This stage is the clear bottleneck: average wait time is 150 minutes (far above any other activity), rework is 22%, and SLA breaches are 5.5%.  
*Action:* Introduce a pre-submission document checklist at claim registration to reduce the 22% rework and associated queueing.

**2. Review Documents** – It has the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), the longest average processing time (25 min), and the greatest variability (standard deviation 9 min).  
*Action:* Deploy standardised templates and a triage protocol to cut processing time and variability.

**3. Initial Assessment** – It is the third-worst performer, with 18 minutes of average wait time and the third-highest SLA breach rate (3.2%).  
*Action:* Increase staffing or shift capacity to this activity to eliminate queueing before it compounds downstream.

### **Decision Points in Contoso Airlines’ Flight-Refund Process**

1. **Customer Submits Claim**  
   - *Decision*: The claim is submitted via the *RefundRequestForm*.

2. **Triage Agent Validates Eligibility**  
   - *Decision*: Is the claim eligible based on *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*?

3. **Finance Controller Pre-Approves**  
   - *Decision*: Is the claim approved for further processing by the *EligibilityChecklist*?

4. **High-Value Claim Trigger**  
   - *Decision*: Is the refund amount **greater than €1 000**?  
     - **If Yes**: Proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (with *AuditTrail* required).  
     - **If No**: Proceed directly to **Process_Refund_Payment**.

5. **Senior Auditor Review Completion**  
   - *Decision*: Has the *Senior_Auditor_Review* been completed for high-value claims?

6. **Process Refund Payment**  
   - *Decision*: Is the *BankTransferAuthorization* present?  
     - **If Yes**: Payment is executed.  
     - **If No**: Payment cannot proceed.

7. **Issue Confirmation Letter**  
   - *Decision*: Is the *RefundConfirmationPDF* sent to the passenger?

---

### **Required Documents**

- **RefundRequestForm** (submitted by customer)
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist** (signed by Finance Controller)
- **BankTransferAuthorization** (signed by customer)
- **AuditTrail** (required for high-value claims)
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** (sent to passenger)

---

**Summary:**  
Contoso’s process has **7 decision points** and requires **6 distinct documents** as listed above. No “CustomerService_Agent_Decide” step exists.

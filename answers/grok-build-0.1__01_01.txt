**Decision points in Contoso’s process:**

- High-value branch: After Finance_Controller_PreApprove, the process checks whether the refund amount exceeds €1,000. If it does, Senior_Auditor_Review (which must include the AuditTrail) is required before Process_Refund_Payment may begin.  
  No other decision points or branches are defined.

**Required documents / artefacts:**

- RefundRequestForm (completed in Customer_Submit_Claim)  
- FlightManifest (checked in Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)  
- WeatherReport (checked in Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)  
- EligibilityChecklist (used for sign-off in Finance_Controller_PreApprove)  
- AuditTrail (must be included in Senior_Auditor_Review for claims > €1,000)  
- BankTransferAuthorization (signed version required before Process_Refund_Payment)  
- RefundConfirmationPDF (issued in Issue_Confirmation_Letter)

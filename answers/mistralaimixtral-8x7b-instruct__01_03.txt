1. Receive-Application: This is the start of the process where Form 14B is received.
2. Assign-Deck-Reviewer: A Deck Officer is assigned to review the application.
3. Tri-Color-Risk-Check: The application is reviewed using Scorecards Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow. If the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested amount is less than or equal to €250 000, the application skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review step.
4. Manual-Underwriting-Review: This step involves a detailed review of the application by a underwriter if the risk rating is High, or if the requested amount is more than €250 000.
5. TriVote Decision Gate: The Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer must approve the application. Any two approvals out of the three are sufficient to pass.
6. Prepare-Binding-Offer: If the application is approved, an offer is prepared using Offer Sheet 77.
7. Request-Applicant-Confirmation: The applicant is requested to confirm the offer with a signed Offer Sheet 77 and ID Matrix Declaration.
8. Finalize-Agreement: The final agreement is prepared using Loan Dossier 2

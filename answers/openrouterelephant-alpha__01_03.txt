**a) Decision points (gates or conditional branches) and their one‑sentence rules**

1. **Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check**  
   Rule: Combines Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow to assign a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High.

2. **Conditional branch after risk check (step 4)**  
   Rule: If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, skip the Manual‑Underwriting‑Review; otherwise proceed to it.

3. **TriVote Decision‑Gate (step 6)**  
   Rule: The loan passes if at least two out of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve.

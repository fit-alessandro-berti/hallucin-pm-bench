Based on the provided process description for Contoso Airlines, here are the decision points and the required documents:

### **Decision Points**
*   **Eligibility Validation:** A triage agent determines if a claim is valid by checking it against the flight manifest and weather reports.
*   **High-Value Branching:** A determination is made based on the refund amount; if the amount is **greater than €1,000**, the case must undergo a **Senior_Auditor_Review**. If not, it bypasses this step.
*   **Payment Authorization:** The process ensures that **Process_Refund_Payment** only occurs if the **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** is successful (and, if applicable, the **Senior_Auditor_Review** is complete) and a signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

### **Required Documents**
*   *RefundRequestForm*
*   *FlightManifest*
*   *WeatherReport*
*   *EligibilityChecklist*
*   *AuditTrail* (required only for high-value claims)
*   *BankTransferAuthorization*
*   *RefundConfirmationPDF*

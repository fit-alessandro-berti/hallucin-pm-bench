To ensure SLA compliance without adding new tasks, attach a **Boundary Timer Event** to the `Task_Receive` or `Task_Check` activities. Set this timer to 48 hours. Connect the timer’s output sequence flow directly to the `Task_Triage` activity (or an existing gateway leading to it). 

This represents an "Auto-Escalation" path that bypasses standard processing if the claim spends too long in the initial stages. By redirecting the flow from the timer to `Task_Triage`, you force the process to meet the 48-hour SLA threshold by bypassing the completeness check or reception phase, ensuring the claim enters the triage stage immediately. This leverages existing process logic while enforcing the time-based requirement through BPMN event handling.

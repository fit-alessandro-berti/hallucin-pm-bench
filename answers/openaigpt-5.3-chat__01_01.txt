Decision points in Contoso Airlines’ refund process:

- Eligibility validation  
  Activity: Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility  
  Decision: whether the claim is eligible based on records.  
  Documents checked: FlightManifest, WeatherReport.  
  Outcome: only eligible claims proceed to Finance_Controller_PreApprove.

- Refund amount threshold  
  Decision: whether the refund amount is greater than €1 000.  
  Outcome:  
  • If ≤ €1 000 → proceed directly to Process_Refund_Payment after Finance_Controller_PreApprove.  
  • If > €1 000 → require Senior_Auditor_Review before payment.

Required documents used in the process:

- RefundRequestForm — submitted by the passenger in Customer_Submit_Claim.  
- FlightManifest — checked during Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility.  
- WeatherReport — checked during Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility.  
- EligibilityChecklist — used in Finance_Controller_PreApprove.  
- AuditTrail — required in Senior_Auditor_Review for refunds greater than €1 000.  
- BankTransferAuthorization — required before Process_Refund_Payment can execute.  
- RefundConfirmationPDF — sent during Issue_Confirmation_Letter.

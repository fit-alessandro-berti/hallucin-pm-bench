### a) Decision points

1. **Manual-underwriting branch:** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **€250,000 or less**, Manual-Underwriting-Review is skipped; otherwise, a Manual-Underwriting-Review is performed.

2. **TriVote Decision-Gate:** The application passes the gate when at least **two of the three** required participants—Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer—approve it.

### b) Required documents, in order of first appearance

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**  
5. **Deck Memo** — required when Manual-Underwriting-Review is not skipped  
6. **Offer Sheet 77**  
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77**  
8. **ID Matrix Declaration**  
9. **Loan Dossier 2025**

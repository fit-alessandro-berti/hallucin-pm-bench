Based on Acme Bank’s defined small-business loan-approval process, here are the answers to your questions:

### a) Summary of Decision Points
1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3):** Evaluates the inputs from Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow to assign a risk rating outcome of Low, Medium, or High.
2. **Conditional Skip Branch (Step 4):** Bypasses the Manual-Underwriting-Review *if* the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000.
3. **“TriVote” Decision-Gate (Step 6):** Requires votes from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, operating on a decision rule where any two approvals out of the three suffice to pass.

---

### b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)
1. **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive-Application)
2. **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review)
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize-Agreement) 

*(Note: Scorecards Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow are also inputs in Step 3, but the document list above covers the explicit documentation generated or required as specified in the process steps).*

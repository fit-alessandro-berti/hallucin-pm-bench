**a) Decision Points and Rules:**

1. **Condition for Skipping Manual Underwriting:**
   - If the risk rating is Low or Medium *and* the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, then skip step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review).

2. **TriVote Decision Gate:**
   - The loan must be approved by at least two out of the three roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) to proceed.

---

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance):**

1. **Form 14B** (Step 1: Application)
2. **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow** (Step 3: Tri-Color-Risk-Check, inputs)
3. **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review, if not skipped)
4. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Binding Offer Preparation)
5. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Applicant Confirmation, signed alongside Offer Sheet 77)
6. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize Agreement)

*(Note: KYC/AML documents are outsourced and not part of this process.)*

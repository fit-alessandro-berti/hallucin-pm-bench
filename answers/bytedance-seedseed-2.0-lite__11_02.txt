**Executive Memo: Q1 2025 Claims Handling Performance**
To: COO, Acme Insurance
From: Senior Process Performance Analyst

The three worst-performing claims activities, with data-backed actions:
1. *Request Documents*: Posts the process’ highest average wait time (150 mins, 8x higher than any other activity’s queue time) and 22% rework rate (4x the next highest rework share), plus 5.5% SLA breach. Action: Automate claim-type-specific pre-filled document requests to cut queueing and repeat work.
2. *Review Documents*: Has the process’ highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), 9-minute processing variability, and 5.3% rework. Action: Roll out standardized checklists to reduce inconsistency and missed SLAs.
3. *Initial Assessment*: Sees throughput drop 3.6% from the first process step (Register Claim), with an 18-minute wait and 3.2% SLA breach. Action: Add part-time triage support to clear queues for low-complexity claims.

### Decision Points

1.  **Underwriter Alignment Gate** (Step 5)
    *   Compare **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** risk scores (0–9).
    *   If scores differ by **≤ 2 points**: Continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
    *   If scores differ by **> 2 points**: Escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before continuing.

2.  **Amount Threshold Decision** (Step 7)
    *   Evaluate loan amount against **€15 000**.
    *   If **amount < €15 000**: System auto-approves.
    *   If **amount ≥ €15 000**: Proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3.  **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** (Step 8)
    *   **Micro-loan Board** votes to either **approve** or **reject**.

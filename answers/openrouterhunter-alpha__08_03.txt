**Yes**, it is possible for transition T5 to fire before T3 in a reachable marking.

**Shortest firing sequence:**  
`T1 → T2b → T4 → T5`

**Explanation of the sequence:**

1. **Initial marking:** p0 = 1  
   Fire T1 (parallel split): consumes p0, produces pa and pb → marking becomes [pa=1, pb=1]

2. Fire T2b (shortcut): consumes pa, produces pd → marking becomes [pb=1, pd=1]

3. Fire T4: consumes pb, produces pe → marking becomes [pd=1, pe=1]

4. Fire T5 (synchronising join): consumes pd and pe, produces pf → marking becomes [pf=1]

At this point, T5 has fired while T3 has not yet fired (and has not even been enabled in this sequence). This demonstrates that T5 can fire before T3.

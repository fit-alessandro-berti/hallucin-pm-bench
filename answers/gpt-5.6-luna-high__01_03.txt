### a) Decision points

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** The three scorecards—Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow—produce a risk rating of **Low, Medium, or High**.  
2. **Manual-underwriting conditional branch:** **Manual-Underwriting-Review is skipped** only when the risk is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is no more than **€250,000**; otherwise, it is performed.  
3. **TriVote decision gate:** The Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer must participate, and the application passes if **any two of the three approve**.

### b) Documents, in order of first appearance

1. **Form 14B**
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**
5. **Deck Memo** — required when manual underwriting is performed
6. **Offer Sheet 77**
7. **ID Matrix Declaration**
8. **Loan Dossier 2025**

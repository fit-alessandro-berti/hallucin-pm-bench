### a) Decision points

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow are assessed to classify the application as **Low, Medium, or High risk**.  
2. **Manual-review branch:** Skip Manual-Underwriting-Review only when risk is **Low or Medium** and the requested amount is **€250,000 or less**; otherwise, perform the review.  
3. **TriVote Decision-Gate:** The Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer must all participate, and the application passes if **at least two of the three approve**.

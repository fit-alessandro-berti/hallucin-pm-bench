**MEMORANDUM**
**To:** Chief Operating Officer, Orion Credit
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst
**Re:** Priority Remediation Targets — Loan Origination Flow

A joint review of SLA breaches and waiting time identifies three activities requiring immediate intervention. Risk Review is the most severe bottleneck, with throughput at 600s against a 300s SLA (exceeding by 300s) and 480s of that time spent waiting. Legal Approval follows, running 900s versus its 600s SLA (exceeding by 300s), with 600s attributable to waiting. Credit Assessment rounds out the top three, at 300s against a 240s SLA (exceeding by 60s), driven by 200s of waiting time. Collectively these stages absorb the majority of case delay and throttle downstream funding. Recommended actions:

- **Risk Review:** Introduce parallel reviewer assignment to reduce the 480s queue, given only 480 cases reach this stage.
- **Legal Approval:** Add legal capacity or templated approvals to cut the 600s waiting component.
- **Credit Assessment:** Automate scoring pre-checks for the 1,175 cases to compress the 200s wait.

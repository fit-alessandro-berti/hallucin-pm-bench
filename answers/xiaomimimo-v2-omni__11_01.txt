**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** Orion Insurance COO
**From:** Senior Process-Mining Analyst
**Subject:** Top Three Process Bottlenecks by Waiting Time

Based on the last 12 months of data, the three activities with the longest waiting times are:

1.  **Request_Documents:** Avg Wait = 36.5h (95th %ile = 120.0h). **Action:** Implement an automated, multi-channel document request system with status tracking to reduce idle time.
2.  **Send_Closure_Letter:** Avg Wait = 12.2h (95th %ile = 44.0h). **Action:** Automate the generation and dispatch of closure letters upon claim decision to eliminate manual queueing.
3.  **Assess_Liability:** Avg Wait = 7.8h (95th %ile = 16.4h). **Action:** Introduce a triage system to prioritize straightforward claims, assigning complex ones to senior assessors to smooth workload.

Each action is designed to reduce waiting time by a minimum of 20%.

## Decision points in Contoso’s refund process

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - Activity: **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - Decision: Whether the submitted claim is eligible, based on the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
   - If eligible, the case proceeds to finance pre-approval.

2. **Finance pre-approval**
   - Activity: **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - Decision: Whether the finance controller signs off using the **EligibilityChecklist**.
   - **Process_Refund_Payment** cannot begin unless this pre-approval has succeeded.

3. **High-value refund branch**
   - Condition: Refund amount is **greater than €1,000**.
   - Decision: Whether the claim requires **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
   - If the amount is greater than €1,000, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required and must include the **AuditTrail**.
   - For high-value claims, **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin only after both:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded, and
     - **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed.

## Required documents and artefacts

- **RefundRequestForm** — completed online by the passenger during **Customer_Submit_Claim**.
- **FlightManifest** — used during **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**.
- **WeatherReport** — used during **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**.
- **EligibilityChecklist** — used by the finance controller during **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**.
- **AuditTrail** — required for **Senior_Auditor_Review** on claims greater than €1,000.
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — required before **Process_Refund_Payment**.
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger during **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**.

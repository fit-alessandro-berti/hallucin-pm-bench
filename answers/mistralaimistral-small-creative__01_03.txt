### **(a) Decision Points (Gates/Conditional Branches) in Acme’s Process**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome Gate**
   *Rule:* If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested loan amount is **≤ €250,000**, skip **Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5)**; otherwise, proceed to Step 5.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate**
   *Rule:* The loan requires approvals from **all three roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer)**, but **only two out of three approvals** are needed to pass the gate.

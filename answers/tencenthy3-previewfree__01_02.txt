### Decision Points
All decision points use the exact defined activity names from the Micro-loan Fast-Track process, with no standard loan terminology, as follows:

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**
   - Decision criteria: Compare the independent 0–9 risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A (step 4a) and Shadow Underwriter B (step 4b).
   - Outcomes:
     - If the two scores differ by ≤ 2 points: Proceed directly to Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC) (step 6).
     - If the two scores differ by > 2 points: Escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision, then proceed to Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC) (step 6).

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**
   - Decision criteria: Compare the requested loan amount to the €15 000 threshold.
   - Outcomes:
     - If amount < €15 000: System auto-approves the application, proceed to Disburse Funds (step 9).
     - If amount ≥ €15 000: Proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (step 8).

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**
   - Decision criteria: The Micro-loan Board votes to approve or reject the application.
   - Outcomes:
     - Approve: Proceed to Disburse Funds (step 9).
     - Reject: No further process steps are defined; the application is terminated.

### Required Documents
All required documents (per the submission time requirement) are:
1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
3. Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

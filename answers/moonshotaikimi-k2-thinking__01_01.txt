**Decision points**

| # | Where the decision occurs | What is decided | Inputs used |
|---|---------------------------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** | Is the claim eligible? | *FlightManifest*, *WeatherReport* |
| 2 | **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** | Is the claim pre‑approved? | *EligibilityChecklist* |
| 3 | **High‑value branch** (immediately after pre‑approval) | Is the refund amount > €1 000? If **yes**, the case proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review**; otherwise it moves directly to payment. | Refund amount (from the claim) |

**Required documents (artefacts)**  

- *RefundRequestForm* – completed by the passenger.  
- *FlightManifest* – used by the triage agent for eligibility validation.  
- *WeatherReport* – used by the triage agent for eligibility validation.  
- *EligibilityChecklist* – used by the finance controller for pre‑approval.  
- *AuditTrail* – must be included for the **Senior_Auditor_Review** (high‑value claims only).  
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (signed) – required before **Process_Refund_Payment** can execute.  
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* – generated and emailed to the passenger after payment.

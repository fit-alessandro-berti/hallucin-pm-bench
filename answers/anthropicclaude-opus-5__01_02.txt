## Decision Points

**1. Underwriter Alignment Gate (step 5)**
- Compares the risk score from **Senior Underwriter A** (4a) with the independent risk score from **Shadow Underwriter B** (4b), each on a 0–9 scale.
- **Difference ≤ 2 points** → continue directly to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
- **Difference > 2 points** → escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.

**2. Amount Threshold Decision (step 7)**
- **Amount < €15 000** → the system **auto-approves** (no further approval body involved).
- **Amount ≥ €15 000** → route to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

**3. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (step 8)**
- The **Micro-loan Board** votes **approve** or **reject**.
- Approve → **Disburse Funds**; reject → no disbursement.

*Implicit gating condition:* **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** requires **at least 3 local references** before the Amount Threshold Decision can be reached.

*Note:* **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** confirms ID card **and** mobile phone only — all credit history checks are skipped, so no such check forms a decision point anywhere in this process.

### Decision Points

There is **one** explicit branching decision point defined in this process:

*   **High-Value Assessment:** The process evaluates if the refund amount is **greater than â‚¬1,000**.
    *   **If Yes:** The case is routed to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (which must occur before the payment process).

### Required Documents (Artefacts)

The following documents and data objects are required throughout the lifecycle of the process:

1.  **RefundRequestForm** (Submitted by the customer in step 1)
2.  **FlightManifest** (Used by the Triage Agent in step 2)
3.  **WeatherReport** (Used by the Triage Agent in step 2)
4.  **EligibilityChecklist** (Used by the Finance Controller in step 3)
5.  **AuditTrail** (Required during the Senior_Auditor_Review for high-value claims in step 4)
6.  **BankTransferAuthorization** (Required to execute Process_Refund_Payment in step 5)
7.  **RefundConfirmationPDF** (Emailed to the passenger in step 6)

### Reading notes

| Activity | Rationale for key assignments |
|---|---|
| **Intake Vetting** | The **Front-Office Analyst** performs and owns the paperwork-completeness check; the **Compliance Analyst** is informed so they can prepare for the next step. |
| **KYC Review** | The **Compliance Analyst** executes the review; the **Compliance Officer** is accountable for its adequacy; the **Front-Office Analyst** may be consulted for client context. |
| **AML Screening** | The **Compliance Analyst** runs the screening against global watchlists; the **Compliance Officer** remains accountable for the compliance outcome. |
| **Risk Scoring** | The **Compliance Analyst** derives the 0–100 score; the **Compliance Officer** is accountable for the methodology and threshold; **Treasury Ops** is informed of the result to anticipate next steps. |
| **Compliance Officer Approval** | Triggered when the score exceeds 70. The **Compliance Officer** is both responsible and accountable for the approval decision; the **Compliance Analyst** is consulted for supporting analysis; **Front-Office Analyst** and **Treasury Ops** are informed of the outcome. |
| **Payment Scheduling** | **Treasury Ops** schedules the payment once approval is in place (or when the score is ≤ 70); the **Compliance Officer** is informed for audit trail purposes. |
| **Fund Release & Notification** | **Treasury Ops** releases funds and notifies the originating **Front-Office Analyst**; the **Compliance Officer** is informed to close the compliance record. |

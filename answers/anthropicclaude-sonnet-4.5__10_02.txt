## RACI Legend

- **R** = Responsible (performs the work)
- **A** = Accountable (ultimate ownership and authority)
- **C** = Consulted (provides input before action)
- **I** = Informed (notified of action or outcome)

---

## Role Definitions

- **Transaction Analyst**: Processes transfer instructions, performs screening, and releases payments
- **Regulatory Liaison**: Conducts KYC Review and approves high-risk transactions
- **IT Support**: Manages technical infrastructure for payment transmission and archival

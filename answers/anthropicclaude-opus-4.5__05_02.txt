### 2. Rationale

Reordering the flow so that the fast automated **"Grant System Access"** (1-day SLA) executes immediately after contract signing—before the slow **"Collect Equipment"** (5-day SLA)—allows new hires to begin productive work sooner, reducing overall onboarding cycle time and preventing SLA breaches caused by waiting on physical equipment before granting system access.

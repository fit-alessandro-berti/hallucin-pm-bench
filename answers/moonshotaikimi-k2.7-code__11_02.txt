 TO: COO, Acme Insurance  
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
RE: Q1 2025 Claims Handling Performance

The three worst-performing activities are **Request_Documents**, **Review_Documents**, and **Initial_Assessment**.

**Request_Documents** has the highest rework rate (22%) and the longest wait time (150 min), signaling unclear requirements and a backlog.  
**Review_Documents** records the highest SLA-breach rate (12.4%), the longest processing time (25 min), and the highest variability (9 min), pointing to inconsistent review standards.  
**Initial_Assessment** is third: an 18-minute wait, 12-minute processing time, and 3.2% SLA breaches—worse than Check_Coverage, Approve, and Calculate_Payout on these dimensions.

**Recommended actions:**

1. **Request_Documents** – Launch a self-service document portal with mandatory checklists to eliminate missing submissions; target <5% rework and <30 min wait.  
2. **Review_Documents** – Introduce standardized review guidelines plus complexity-based routing to cut SLA breaches and variability.  
3. **Initial_Assessment** – Add triage capacity and fast-track simple cases to reduce queue time and keep SLA breaches under 2%.

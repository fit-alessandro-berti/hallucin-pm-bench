Introduce an escalation boundary timer on **Task_Receive** (or **Task_Check**, depending on implementation) to enforce the 48‑hour SLA.

Example:

```xml
<bpmn:boundaryEvent id="BE_ReceiveTimeout" attachedToRef="Task_Receive" cancelActivity="false">
  <bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
    <bpmn:timeDuration>PT48H</bpmn:timeDuration>
  </bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
</bpmn:boundaryEvent>

<bpmn:sequenceFlow id="f_timeout" sourceRef="BE_ReceiveTimeout" targetRef="Task_Triage"/>
```

If the claim hasn’t reached **Task_Triage** within 48 hours after **Start_Claim**, the timer fires and immediately routes the process via **f_timeout** to **Task_Triage**, satisfying the SLA without introducing new tasks.

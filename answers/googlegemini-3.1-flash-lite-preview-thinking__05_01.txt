To ensure SLA compliance without adding new tasks, attach a **Boundary Timer Event** (duration: PT48H) to the `Task_Receive`, `Task_Check`, or the process scope. 

Specifically, attach a non-interrupting or interrupting timer to the container of the initial activities. Configure this timer to trigger a sequence flow directly to `Task_Triage` or an escalation path. By reconnecting this timer’s output to `Task_Triage` (bypassing the standard path), you force claims into the triage queue immediately upon the 48-hour deadline, bypassing any lingering completeness checks. This effectively enforces the SLA at the architectural level while maintaining the existing process flow for standard operations.

# Order-to-Cash RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager | Compliance Officer | Finance Clerk | IT Support |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Receive Application** | **R, A** | — | — | — |
| **KYC Review** | C | **R, A** | — | — |
| **Order Vetting** | **R, A** | C | — | — |
| **Payment Clearance** | C | — | **R, A** | — |
| **Record Update** | C | — | — | **R, A** |
| **Notify Customer** | **R, A** | — | — | C |

---

### Key
- **R** = Responsible (executes the activity)  
- **A** = Accountable (owns the outcome; decision authority)  
- **C** = Consulted (provides input)  
- **—** = Not involved

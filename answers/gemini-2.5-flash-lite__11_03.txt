MEMORANDUM

TO: Chief Operating Officer
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
DATE: October 26, 2023
SUBJECT: Process Performance Analysis - Identifying Key Bottlenecks

This memo outlines critical areas for process improvement, focusing on activities with significant SLA deviations and prolonged waiting times. The data indicates that **Risk Review**, **Legal Approval**, and **Credit Assessment** represent the most substantial performance concerns.

Risk Review currently exceeds its SLA by 300 seconds (600s actual vs. 300s target), with an average waiting time of 480 seconds. Legal Approval surpasses its SLA by 300 seconds (900s actual vs. 600s target) and has an average waiting time of 600 seconds. Credit Assessment is 60 seconds over its SLA (300s actual vs. 240s target) and experiences an average waiting time of 200 seconds. Addressing these bottlenecks is crucial to improving overall efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Recommendations include:

*   For Risk Review, investigate the 480 seconds of average waiting time to identify opportunities for streamlining the review process.
*   For Legal Approval, explore methods to reduce the 600 seconds of average waiting time, potentially through enhanced pre-submission checks or dedicated reviewer allocation.
*   For Credit Assessment, focus on reducing the 200 seconds of average waiting time, examining upstream data completeness or assessment automation.

**Decision Points**
1. **Eligibility Validation** (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility): Pass/fail gate determining whether the claim aligns with the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport* before advancing.
2. **Pre-Approval Gate** (Finance_Controller_PreApprove): Controller reviews the *EligibilityChecklist* to approve or reject the claim. Approval is a strict prerequisite; payment cannot begin until it succeeds.
3. **High-Value Threshold Routing**: Evaluates whether the refund amount is greater than €1,000. If yes, the case branches to *Senior_Auditor_Review*; if no, it bypasses this step and moves directly to payment processing.

**Required Documents & Artefacts**
• *RefundRequestForm*  
• *FlightManifest*  
• *WeatherReport*  
• *EligibilityChecklist*  
• *AuditTrail* (required only for high-value claims)  
• *BankTransferAuthorization* (must be signed and present to initiate payment)  
• *RefundConfirmationPDF* (generated and emailed upon process completion)

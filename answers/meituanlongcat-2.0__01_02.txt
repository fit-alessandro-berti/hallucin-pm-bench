**Decision Points**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate:** This gate evaluates whether the risk scores from the Senior Underwriter A and the Shadow Underwriter B differ by more than 2 points. If they differ by > 2 points, the application must be escalated to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision before it can proceed.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision:** This decision point evaluates the requested micro-loan amount against the €15 000 threshold. If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves; if the amount is ≥ €15 000, the application must proceed to Step 8.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA):** For applications of €15 000 or more, the Micro-loan Board votes to either *approve* or *reject* the application. 

**Required Documents**

▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

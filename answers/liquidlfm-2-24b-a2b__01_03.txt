Certainly! Here’s a concise breakdown of Acme Bank’s **Small-Business Loan Approval Process** as requested:

---

**a) Decision Points & Rules**

1. **Receive-Application**  
   *Decision point:* Application is received and Form 14B is completed.

2. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**  
   *Decision point:* The applicant is assigned a risk rating (Low, Medium, or High) based on the tri-color scorecard.

3. **Conditional Skip (if risk ∈ {Low, Medium} AND amount ≤ €250,000)**  
   *Decision point:* If both conditions are met, skip Manual Underwriting Review.

4. **Manual-Underwriting-Review**  
   *Decision point:* Manual review is conducted by the Deck Officer.

5. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”**  
   *Decision point:* Approval requires at least two out of three sign-offs (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer).

---

**b) All Documents Required (in order of first appearance)**

1. **Form 14B** (Receive-Application)  
2. **Deck Memo** (Manual Underwriting Review)  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Prepare Binding Offer)  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Request Applicant Confirmation)  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalize Agreement)  

*(Note: KYC/AML documents are outsourced and not part of this process.)*

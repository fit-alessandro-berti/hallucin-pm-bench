### **a) Summary of Every Decision Point in Acme’s Process**

1. **Decision Point**: *If (risk ∈ {Low, Medium} AND requested-amount ≤ €250 000)*  
   → Rule: If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, skip the Manual Underwriting Review step.

2. **Decision Point**: *Decision-Gate “TriVote”*  
   → Rule: A loan is approved if **at least two out of three** approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) agree.

# Contoso Airlines Flight-Refund Process Summary

## Decision Points

### Decision Point 1: Eligibility Validation
- **Location:** Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility
- **Decision:** Is the claim eligible based on flight and weather data?
- **Outcome:** Claim proceeds to pre-approval or is rejected

### Decision Point 2: Finance Pre-Approval
- **Location:** Finance_Controller_PreApprove
- **Decision:** Does the claim meet all eligibility criteria for refund?
- **Outcome:** Claim is pre-approved or denied

### Decision Point 3: High-Value Threshold Check
- **Location:** After Finance_Controller_PreApprove
- **Decision:** Is the refund amount greater than €1,000?
- **Outcomes:**
  - **Yes (>€1,000):** Route to Senior_Auditor_Review before payment
  - **No (≤€1,000):** Proceed directly to Process_Refund_Payment

### **a) Decision Points**

1.  **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** If the risk rating is classified as Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is at or below €250,000, the Manual Underwriting Review is bypassed to proceed directly to the Decision-Gate.
2.  **Decision-Gate (Step 6):** The "TriVote" decision gate is passed if at least two of the three required approvers—either the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, or the Sustainability Officer—grant approval.

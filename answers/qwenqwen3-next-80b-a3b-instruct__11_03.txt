Orion Credit’s process performance reveals three critical bottlenecks where SLA breaches and prolonged waiting times converge: Risk Review, Legal Approval, and Credit Assessment. Risk Review exceeds its SLA of 300s by 780s (avg. 1,080s total), with 480s average waiting time — the highest in the pipeline. Legal Approval misses its 600s SLA by 900s (avg. 1,500s total), with 600s waiting time — the most extreme delay. Credit Assessment breaches its 240s SLA by 260s (avg. 500s total), accompanied by 200s average waiting time — the second-highest. These activities cause cascading delays, reducing case throughput from 1,175 to 455 by the end of the process.  

- Increase parallel processing capacity for Risk Review by deploying automated risk scoring tools to reduce manual review time.  
- Assign dedicated legal paralegals to Legal Approval to expedite document validation and eliminate backlogs.  
- Implement tiered credit assessment lanes based on pre-qualified risk bands to reduce average handling time for lower-risk cases.

**Decision Points**

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: If the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by more than 2 points, the application is escalated to the Harmonisation Committee.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision**:
    *   If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves.
    *   If the amount is ≥ €15 000, the application proceeds to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The board votes to either approve or reject the application.

**Required Documents**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

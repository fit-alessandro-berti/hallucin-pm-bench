Yes, it is possible for T5 to fire before T3. A shortest firing sequence is:

**T1, T2b, T4, T5**

After T1, tokens are in pa and pb. Firing T2b produces a token in pd (skipping pc), and firing T4 produces a token in pe. With both pd and pe present, T5 is enabled and fires before T3 (which would require a token in pc, which is never produced in this sequence).

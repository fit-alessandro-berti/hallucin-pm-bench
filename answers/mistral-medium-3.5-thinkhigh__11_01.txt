**Executive Memo: Top 3 Waiting-Time Bottlenecks**

Based on the past 12 months of process performance data, the three activities with the highest waiting times are identified below. For each, we provide both average and 95th percentile waiting times, along with a targeted action to reduce waiting time by at least 20%.

1. **Request_Documents** – Avg: 36.5h, 95p: 120h. *Action*: Deploy automated customer reminders at 24 and 48 hours to prompt faster document submissions.
2. **Send_Closure_Letter** – Avg: 12.2h, 95p: 44h. *Action*: Transition to email notifications to eliminate postal delivery delays.
3. **Assess_Liability** – Avg: 7.8h, 95p: 16.4h. *Action*: Implement a fast-track process for straightforward cases to decongest the queue.

Each recommended action is data-driven and addresses the root causes of delays observed in our data.

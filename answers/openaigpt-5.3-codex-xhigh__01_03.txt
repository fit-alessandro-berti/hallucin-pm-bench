### a) Decision points (gate/branch) and rules

1. **Underwriting-skip branch (Step 4):** If the Tri-Color risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, Acme skips Step 5; otherwise it performs **Manual-Underwriting-Review**.  
2. **TriVote gate (Step 6):** The **Credit Lead**, **Regulatory Liaison**, and **Sustainability Officer** all participate, and the case passes if **any 2 of the 3** approve.

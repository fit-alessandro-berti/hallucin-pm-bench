Decision points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process:  
1. Quick KYC Verification – confirm ID and mobile.  
2. Community Impact Assessment (CIA) – completed by an Impact Officer.  
3. Underwriter Alignment Gate – determine if risk scores of Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤2 points.  
4. Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC) – collect at least 3 local references.  
5. Amount Threshold Decision – amount ≥ €15,000 reaches Micro-loan Board for approval.  
6. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) – board votes to approve or reject.

Required documents at submission time:  
▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

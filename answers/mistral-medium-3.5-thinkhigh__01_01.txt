### Decision Points
- **Refund amount threshold**: After *Finance_Controller_PreApprove*, the process checks whether the refund amount **exceeds €1,000**.  
  - If **yes** → Proceed to *Senior_Auditor_Review*.  
  - If **no** → Skip directly to *Process_Refund_Payment*.

### Required Documents
- *RefundRequestForm*  
- *FlightManifest*  
- *WeatherReport*  
- *EligibilityChecklist*  
- *AuditTrail*  
- *BankTransferAuthorization*  
- *RefundConfirmationPDF*

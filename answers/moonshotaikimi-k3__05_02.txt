**2. Rationale**

"Grant System Access" is the only step whose completion lies outside the engine's control — as an external task it stalls the process end until a worker happens to poll, lock, and complete it, and since Camunda honors `camunda:dueDate` only on user tasks its nominal 1-day SLA was never even enforceable — so switching the existing serviceTask to a synchronously executed in-engine Java delegate removes the single unbounded, deadline-free wait from the critical path.

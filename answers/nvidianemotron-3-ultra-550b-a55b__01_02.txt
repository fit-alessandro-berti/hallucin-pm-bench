**Decision points in the Micro‑loan Fast‑Track process**

| # | Activity (exact name) | Decision made |
|---|-----------------------|---------------|
| 1 | **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** | *Pass* – ID card **and** mobile phone confirmed → continue; *Fail* → stop. |
| 2 | **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)** (performed by an **Impact Officer**) | *Positive impact* → continue; *Negative impact* → stop. |
| 3 | **Dual Underwriter Split** – **Senior Underwriter A** assigns a risk score (0‑9) **and** **Shadow Underwriter B** independently assigns a risk score (0‑9) | Scores recorded for the next gate. |
| 4 | **Underwriter Alignment Gate** | • If the two scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  <br>• If the difference is **> 2 points** → **escalate to the Harmonisation Committee** for a tie‑break decision, then continue. |
| 5 | **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** | At least **3 local references** collected → continue; otherwise stop. |
| 6 | **Amount Threshold Decision** | • **Amount < €15 000** → system **auto‑approves** (process ends with disbursement). <br>• **Amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)**. |
| 7 | **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)** | Board votes **approve** → continue to **Disburse Funds**; **reject** → stop. |

**Every required document (exact titles as defined)**  

1. **Digital Application Form (ML‑APP‑01)**  
2. **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**  
3. **Latest Income Statement** – single pay‑stub **or** tax slip  
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**  
5. **Dual Underwriter Score‑Sheet**  
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**  

These are the only documents that must be present at submission time; no other credit‑history or standard loan paperwork is required.

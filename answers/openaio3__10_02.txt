### AtlasRemit – End-to-End Payment Process (RACI Matrix)

| # | Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|------|--------------------|--------------------|------------|
| 1 | Receive customer transfer instruction & log in system | **R, A** | I | C |
| 2 | Conduct sanctions screening | **R** | **A** | I |
| 3 | Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary | C | **R, A** | I |
| 4 | Approve high-risk transaction | C | **R, A** | I |
| 5 | Release payment | **R, A** | I | C |
| 6 | Ensure SWIFT message is sent | I | I | **R, A** |
| 7 | Archive case file | I | I | **R, A** |
| 8 | Notify Regulatory Liaison of case closure | — | I | **R, A** |

Legend:  
R = Responsible A = Accountable C = Consulted I = Informed

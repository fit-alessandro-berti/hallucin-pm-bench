**Decision points in the Micro‑loan Fast‑Track process**

| # | Activity (exact name) | What the decision is |
|---|------------------------|-----------------------|
| 1 | **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** | Identity (ID card) and mobile‑phone must be confirmed – failure stops the process. |
| 2 | **Underwriter Alignment Gate** | Compare the two risk‑score ratings from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. <br>• If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → proceed to the next step. <br>• If the scores differ by **> 2 points** → escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie‑break, then continue. |
| 3 | **Amount Threshold Decision** | • Loan amount **< €15 000** → auto‑approve. <br>• Loan amount **≥ €15 000** → go to the **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval**. |
| 4 | **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)** | The Micro‑loan Board votes **approve** or **reject**. |

---

**Required documents (submitted at application time)**  

- Digital Application Form (ML‑APP‑01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay‑stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score‑Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

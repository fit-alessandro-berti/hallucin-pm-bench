**To: COO, Orion Insurance**  
**Subject: Top Waiting-Time Bottlenecks**

Based strictly on waiting time, the three worst-performing activities are:

1. **Request_Documents** — average **36.5 h**; 95th percentile **120.0 h**. **Action:** introduce automated document reminders at 24-hour intervals and an electronic upload checklist; target a ≥20% reduction in waiting time.
2. **Send_Closure_Letter** — average **12.2 h**; 95th percentile **44.0 h**. **Action:** automatically generate and queue closure letters immediately upon claim completion, with same-day dispatch; target a ≥20% reduction.
3. **Assess_Liability** — average **7.8 h**; 95th percentile **16.4 h**. **Action:** implement an automated work queue that assigns cases to the next available assessor based on capacity; target a ≥20% reduction.

These interventions directly address the largest observed waiting-time delays and should be monitored against both average and 95th-percentile waiting times.

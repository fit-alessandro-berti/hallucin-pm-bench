# AtlasRemit Process – RACI Matrix

**Roles used (per vocabulary mandate):**
- **Transaction Analyst**
- **Regulatory Liaison**
- **IT Support** (not covered by mandated vocabulary, retained as-is)

| # | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|---|------|--------------|--------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Receive and log the customer's transfer instruction | Transaction Analyst | Transaction Analyst | — | Regulatory Liaison |
| 2 | Screen the payment against the sanctions list | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
| 3 | Perform **KYC Review** on sender and beneficiary | Regulatory Liaison | Regulatory Liaison | Transaction Analyst | IT Support |
| 4 | Approve transaction flagged as high-risk | Regulatory Liaison | Regulatory Liaison | Transaction Analyst | IT Support |
| 5 | Release the approved payment | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | — | IT Support |
| 6 | Transmit the SWIFT message | IT Support | IT Support | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison |
| 7 | Archive the case file | IT Support | IT Support | — | Regulatory Liaison |

**Notes on RACI assignment logic:**
- The **Regulatory Liaison** holds ultimate accountability for all compliance-sensitive steps (sanctions screening, **KYC Review**, high-risk approval, and payment release), consistent with their oversight mandate.
- The **Transaction Analyst** is responsible for operational execution (logging, screening, releasing payments) but escalates approval decisions to the **Regulatory Liaison**.
- **IT Support** owns technical execution tasks (SWIFT transmission, archiving) and keeps the **Regulatory Liaison** informed for audit-trail purposes.

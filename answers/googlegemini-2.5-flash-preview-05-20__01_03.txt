Here are the answers based on Acme Bank's small-business loan-approval process:

**a) Decision Points:**

*   **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** If the risk is assessed as Low or Medium AND the requested loan amount is less than or equal to €250,000, then the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is skipped.
*   **Decision-Gate "TriVote" (Step 6):** The application passes this gate if at least two out of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant their approval.

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance):**

1.  Form 14B
2.  Scorecard–Cyan
3.  Scorecard–Magenta
4.  Scorecard–Yellow
5.  Deck Memo
6.  Offer Sheet 77
7.  ID Matrix Declaration
8.  Loan Dossier 2025

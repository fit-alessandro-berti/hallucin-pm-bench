### Decision points in Contoso Airlines’ refund process (non-standard variant)

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility — Eligibility decision**
   - **Decision made:** Whether the claim is eligible after checking it against required records.
   - **Required documents:**  
     - *RefundRequestForm*  
     - *FlightManifest*  
     - *WeatherReport*

2. **High-value branch — Amount threshold decision**
   - **Decision made:** Whether the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**.  
     - If **> €1 000** ⇒ **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required and must include the *AuditTrail*.  
     - If **≤ €1 000** ⇒ skip **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
   - **Required documents (for the high-value path):**  
     - *AuditTrail*

3. **Process_Refund_Payment — Authorization / gating decision (start permitted or not)**
   - **Decision made:** Whether payment processing is allowed to begin, based on prerequisites:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** must have succeeded, and  
     - **if high-value (> €1 000)**, **Senior_Auditor_Review** must be completed.
   - **Required documents to execute payment:**  
     - *BankTransferAuthorization*

---

### Complete list of required documents (all artefacts defined above)

- *RefundRequestForm*  
- *FlightManifest*  
- *WeatherReport*  
- *EligibilityChecklist*  
- *AuditTrail* (only required for refunds **> €1 000**)  
- *BankTransferAuthorization*  
- *RefundConfirmationPDF*

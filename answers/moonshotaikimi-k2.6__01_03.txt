**a) Decision points**

* **Step 4 (conditional branch):** The process skips Manual-Underwriting-Review only when the Tri-Color risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is €250 000 or less.  
* **Step 6 (Decision-Gate “TriVote”):** The application passes the gate if at least two of the three designated officers—Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer—approve it.

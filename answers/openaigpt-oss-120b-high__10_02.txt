**RACI Matrix – AtlasRemit Cross‑Border Payment Process**  

| **Task (Activity)** | **Transaction Analyst** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **IT Support** |
|----------------------|------------------------|------------------------|----------------|
| 1. Receive & Log Transfer instruction | **R / A** | I | I |
| 2. Sanctions Screening | **R / A** | C | I |
| 3. **KYC Review** (sender & beneficiary) | C | **R / A** | I |
| 4. High‑Risk Approval | C | **R / A** | I |
| 5. Release Payment | **R / A** | C | I |
| 6. SWIFT Transmission | C | I | **R / A** |
| 7. Archive case file & notify Regulatory Liaison | I | I | **R / A** |

**Key**  
- **R** – Responsible (does the work)  
- **A** – Accountable (owns the outcome; only one per task)  
- **C** – Consulted (provides input)  
- **I** – Informed (kept apprised)  

All activities and role titles follow the mandated vocabulary: **KYC Review**, **Regulatory Liaison**, and **Transaction Analyst**.

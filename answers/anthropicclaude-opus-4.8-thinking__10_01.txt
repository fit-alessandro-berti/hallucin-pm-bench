# RACI Matrix — FinCo Order-to-Cash Workflow

**Legend:** R = Responsible · A = Accountable · C = Consulted · I = Informed

| Activity | AM | CO | FC | IT |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Receive Application** | R/A | I | – | I |
| **KYC Review** | C | R/A | I | – |
| **Order Vetting** | R/A | C | I | – |
| **Payment Clearance** | I | C | R/A | – |
| **Record Update** | I | – | C | R/A |
| **Notify Customer** | R/A | – | I | I |

---

**Notes on assignments**
- **Receive Application:** The Account Manager owns intake; IT is informed for system logging.
- **KYC Review:** The Compliance Officer is fully accountable; the Account Manager is consulted on application context.
- **Order Vetting:** The Account Manager confirms data completeness; the Compliance Officer is consulted where compliance flags overlap.
- **Payment Clearance:** The Finance Clerk initiates and owns settlement; Compliance is consulted on any holds.
- **Record Update:** IT Support refreshes the CRM; the Finance Clerk is consulted to confirm settlement data.
- **Notify Customer:** The Account Manager issues confirmation; Finance and IT are informed of completion.

*Each activity has a single Accountable role to preserve clear ownership.*

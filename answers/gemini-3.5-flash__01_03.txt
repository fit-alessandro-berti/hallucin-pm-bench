Based on the defined small-business loan-approval process for Acme Bank, here are the answers:

### a) Decision Points (Gates or Conditional Branches)

1. **Manual-Underwriting-Review Skip Condition (Step 4)**: The manual underwriting review (Step 5) is bypassed if the risk rating from the Tri-Color-Risk-Check is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
2. **Decision-Gate "TriVote" (Step 6)**: The application successfully passes this gate if it receives approvals from any two of the three mandatory voters (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer).

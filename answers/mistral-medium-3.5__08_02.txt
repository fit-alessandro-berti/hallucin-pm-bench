T5 requires tokens in both P2 and P5. P5 is produced by T4, which requires a token in P4. P4 can only be populated by either T3 (from P2) or T7 (from P3, which itself requires T2 to fire from P1). Since T3 is the only direct way to populate P4 from P2 (T7 depends on P3, which is not guaranteed to fire before T3), T3 must fire to enable T4, which then enables T5. Thus, T5 cannot fire before T3.

### Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation**
   - A triage agent checks the claim against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
   - This determines whether the refund claim is eligible.

2. **High-value refund routing**
   - If the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**, the case must proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**, including the **AuditTrail**.
   - Claims of €1,000 or less do not require this review.

3. **Payment authorization**
   - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** must succeed before payment can begin.
   - For high-value claims, **Senior_Auditor_Review** must also be completed.
   - Payment additionally requires a signed **BankTransferAuthorization**.

### Required documents and artefacts

- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail** — required only for claims greater than €1,000
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — must be signed before payment
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger after payment processing

The legacy activity **CustomerService_Agent_Decide** is not part of Contoso’s process.

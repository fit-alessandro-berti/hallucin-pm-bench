**a) Decision Points**

1. **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check yields a Low or Medium risk rating and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, the process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).
2. **Decision Gate "TriVote" (Step 6):** The loan advances only if at least two out of the three required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) provide their approval.

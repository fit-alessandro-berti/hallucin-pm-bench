# **a) Decision Points**

**1. Step 4 – Conditional Branch (Skip Logic)**  
If the risk rating is Low or Medium AND the requested amount is €250,000 or less, then Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5) is skipped; otherwise it is performed.

**2. Step 6 – Decision Gate "TriVote"**  
The application passes if at least two out of the three required approvals (from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) are obtained.

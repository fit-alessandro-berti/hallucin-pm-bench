Add interrupting boundary timer events to every pre-triage activity that may wait: `Task_Receive`, `Task_Check`, and `Task_RequestDocs`. Configure each timer with the same absolute deadline, `claimSubmissionTime + PT48H`, rather than 48 hours after that activity starts. Route each timer’s outgoing sequence flow directly to `Task_Triage` and label it “Auto-escalated.”

Because the timers are interrupting, the currently delayed task is cancelled and the claim immediately enters `Task_Triage`. When normal processing advances, the previous timer is cancelled and the next activity’s timer retains the original submission-based deadline. No new task or subprocess is required.

## Acme Bank Micro-loan Fast-Track: Decision Points & Required Documents

Here's a summary of the decision points within the process, and a list of all required documents:

**Decision Points:**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate:** Do the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points? (If no, escalate to Harmonisation Committee).
2. **Amount Threshold Decision:** Is the loan amount < €15 000? (If yes, auto-approve. If no, proceed to Micro-loan Board).
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA):** Does the Micro-loan Board vote to *approve* or *reject* the loan?

**Required Documents:**

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

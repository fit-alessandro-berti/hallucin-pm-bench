MEMORANDUM

TO: COO, Orion Credit
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
SUBJECT: Process Performance and SLA Breach Analysis

Based on an evaluation of joint SLA breaches and high waiting times, the three worst-performing activities are Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment. 

Legal Approval records 600 seconds of average waiting time and a throughput time of 900 seconds, exceeding its 600-second Target SLA by 300 seconds. Risk Review has an average waiting time of 480 seconds and a throughput time of 600 seconds, exceeding its 300-second Target SLA by 300 seconds. Credit Assessment shows an average waiting time of 200 seconds and a throughput time of 300 seconds, exceeding its 240-second Target SLA by 60 seconds.

To address these performance gaps, the following data-driven actions are recommended:
* Legal Approval: Streamline the legal review process to reduce the 600-second waiting time and bring the 900-second throughput time within the 600-second Target SLA.
* Risk Review: Optimize review workflows to cut down the 480-second waiting time and align the 600-second throughput time with the 300-second Target SLA.
* Credit Assessment: Accelerate assessment procedures to decrease the 200-second waiting time and lower the 300-second throughput time below the 240-second Target SLA.

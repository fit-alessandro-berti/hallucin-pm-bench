Here’s the **RACI table** for AtlasRemit’s cross-border payment process, strictly adhering to the mandated vocabulary:

```markdown
| **Task**                                                                 | **Transaction Analyst** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **IT Support** | **Informed Stakeholders** |
|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------|-----------------------|----------------|---------------------------|
| **1. Receive and log customer transfer instruction in the system**        | R                      | I                     | I              | -                         |
| **2. Screen payment against sanctions list**                              | R                      | C                     | I              | -                         |
| **3. Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary**                       | -                      | R                     | I              | -                         |
| **4. Approve high-risk transaction (if flagged)**                         | -                      | A                     | I              | -                         |
| **5. Release payment and ensure SWIFT message is sent**                  | R                      | I                     | R              | -                         |
| **6. Archive case file**                                                  | -                      | -                     | R              | Regulatory Liaison        |
```

### **Key:**
- **R (Responsible):** Executes the task.
- **A (Accountable):** Approves or signs off (final authority).
- **C (Consulted):** Provides input or feedback.
- **I (Informed):** Receives updates (no action required).

### **Notes:**
- The **Regulatory Liaison** is **Accountable** for high-risk approvals (Task 4) and **Informed** of archival (Task 6).
- **IT Support** is **Responsible** for SWIFT message transmission (Task 5) and archival (Task 6).
- **Transaction Analyst** handles initial logging, screening, and payment release (Tasks 1, 2, 5).

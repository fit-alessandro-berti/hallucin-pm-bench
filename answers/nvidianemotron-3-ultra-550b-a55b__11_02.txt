**MEMORANDUM**  
**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**Date:** 2025-04-15  
**Re:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling – Top Three Improvement Targets  

**1. Request_Documents** – **Primary bottleneck.** Throughput collapses to 190/day (vs. 270+ upstream), driven by a 150-minute average wait and 22 % rework rate (highest in process). SLA breaches hit 5.5 %.  
**Action:** Deploy a digital document portal with mandatory checklists at submission to eliminate back-and-forth; target < 30 min wait and < 5 % rework.

**2. Review_Documents** – **Quality & variability hotspot.** Highest SLA breach rate (12.4 %), longest processing time (25 min), and widest spread (stdev 9 min). Rework at 5.3 % compounds downstream delays.  
**Action:** Introduce standardized review templates with auto-validated fields; pilot a 20-minute SLA cap to compress tail-time variability.

**3. Initial_Assessment** – **Upstream queue driver.** 18-minute wait (2nd highest) feeds the Request_Documents backlog. SLA breach (3.2 %) and rework (1.8 %) exceed downstream peers (Approve_Claim 0.9 %/0.4 %).  
**Action:** Implement skill-based triage routing at intake to balance assessor load; target ≤ 5 min wait and ≤ 1 % rework.

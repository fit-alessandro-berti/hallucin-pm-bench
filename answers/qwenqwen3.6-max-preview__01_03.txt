**a) Decision Points**
- **Step 4 (Conditional Branch):** The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review if the Tri-Color-Risk-Check yields a Low or Medium risk rating and the requested amount is €250,000 or less; otherwise, it proceeds to underwriting.
- **Step 6 (TriVote Decision Gate):** The loan advances if at least two out of the three designated officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) grant approval.

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**
1. Form 14B (Step 1)
2. Scorecard–Cyan (Step 3)
3. Scorecard–Magenta (Step 3)
4. Scorecard–Yellow (Step 3)
5. Deck Memo (Step 5)
6. Offer Sheet 77 (Step 7)
7. ID Matrix Declaration (Step 8)
8. Loan Dossier 2025 (Step 9)

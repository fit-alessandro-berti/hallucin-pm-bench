### RACI Matrix for NimbusPay Process
| Activity | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Intake Vetting | Front-Office Analyst | Front-Office Analyst | - | - |
| KYC Review | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | - | - |
| AML Screening | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | - | - |
| Risk Scoring | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | - | - |
| Approval (for scores exceeding 70) | Compliance Officer | Compliance Officer | Front-Office Analyst, Compliance Analyst | Treasury Ops |
| Payment Scheduling | Treasury Ops | Compliance Officer | Front-Office Analyst, Compliance Analyst | - |
| Fund Release and Notification | Treasury Ops | Compliance Officer | - | Front-Office Analyst |

Note: 
- "Responsible" indicates the role that performs the activity.
- "Accountable" indicates the role accountable for the outcome.
- "Consulted" indicates roles that are consulted during the activity.
- "Informed" indicates roles that are informed of the activity's outcome.
- A dash (-) indicates no role is assigned for that category in the specific activity.

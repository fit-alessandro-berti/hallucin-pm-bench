*(No activity added or removed, no sequence flow re-directed — only `camunda:dueDate` attribute values changed; `slaStart` is the hire/start timestamp already available on the process instance.)*

## 2. Rationale

Replacing task-creation-relative durations (`P1D…P5D`, which stack up to a 12-day worst case and let every upstream delay silently reset the clock) with **absolute deadlines anchored to the hire date** caps the end-to-end path at the contractual 7 days and makes each handoff breach visible immediately instead of being absorbed downstream.

*Note:* the deeper fix — running the 5-day equipment lead time in parallel with contract preparation — cannot be expressed here, because any single sequence-flow reversal in this purely linear chain would orphan a task; that change would require adding gateways, which the rules forbid.

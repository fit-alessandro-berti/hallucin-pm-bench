# AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process – RACI Matrix

| Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|------|--------------------|--------------------|-----------|
| **Log transfer instruction in system** | **R** | I | — |
| **Screen payment against sanctions list** | **R** | C | — |
| **Conduct KYC Review on sender and beneficiary** | C | **R** | — |
| **Approve high-risk flagged transaction** | I | **R** | — |
| **Release approved payment** | **R** | I | C |
| **Send SWIFT message** | — | — | **R** |
| **Archive case file** | — | I | **R** |

---

### Legend
- **R** = Responsible (executes the task)  
- **A** = Accountable (owns the outcome; not shown when identical to R)  
- **C** = Consulted (provides input before decision)  
- **I** = Informed (notified of outcome after completion)  
- **—** = Not involved

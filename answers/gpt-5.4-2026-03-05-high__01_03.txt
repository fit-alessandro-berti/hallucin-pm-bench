**a) Decision points**

There are **two explicit gate/branch points**, plus the risk-classification outcome that feeds one of them:

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check outcome:** using **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow**, the application is classified as **Low, Medium, or High** risk.  
2. **Conditional branch after the risk check:** if the risk is **Low or Medium** **and** the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, **step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review)** is skipped; otherwise, step 5 is performed.  
3. **TriVote decision gate:** **Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer** all participate, and the application **passes if any two of the three approve**.

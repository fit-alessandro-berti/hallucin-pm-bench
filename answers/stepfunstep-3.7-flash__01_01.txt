### Decision Points
All decision points align with the defined Contoso Airlines process, with no additional activities introduced:
1. **Eligibility validation decision**: Made during the *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility* step, where the triage agent assesses the claim against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport* to confirm if it meets eligibility criteria for further processing.
2. **Finance pre-approval decision**: Made during the *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* step, where the finance controller uses the *EligibilityChecklist* to make a go/no-go decision on proceeding with the refund.
3. **High-value claim routing decision**: Made after finance pre-approval, to check if the requested refund amount exceeds €1,000. A positive result routes the claim to *Senior_Auditor_Review* (which requires the *AuditTrail*); a negative result allows the claim to proceed directly to payment processing.

---
### Required Documents/Artefacts
All artefacts are explicitly referenced in the defined process:
1. *RefundRequestForm*: Completed by the customer during the *Customer_Submit_Claim* step.
2. *FlightManifest*: Used by the triage agent during eligibility validation.
3. *WeatherReport*: Used by the triage agent during eligibility validation.
4. *EligibilityChecklist*: Used by the finance controller during pre-approval.
5. *AuditTrail*: Required for the *Senior_Auditor_Review* step for all claims with a refund amount greater than €1,000.
6. Signed *BankTransferAuthorization*: Required to execute the refund payment in the *Process_Refund_Payment* step.
7. *RefundConfirmationPDF*: Generated and emailed to the passenger in the *Issue_Confirmation_Letter* step.

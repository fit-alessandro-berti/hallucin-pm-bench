As a senior process-mapping consultant for FinCo, I have developed the RACI matrix for the order-to-cash workflow. The activities and assignments below strictly utilize our mandated operational terminology.

### **RACI Legend**
* **R**esponsible: The role that performs the work to complete the activity.
* **A**ccountable: The role ultimately answerable for the correct and thorough completion of the activity (only one per activity).
* **C**onsulted: Those whose subject-matter opinions are sought prior to or during the activity (two-way communication).
* **I**nformed: Those who are kept up-to-date on progress or decisions (one-way communication).

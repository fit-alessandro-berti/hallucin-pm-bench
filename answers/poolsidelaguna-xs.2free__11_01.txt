
Orion Insurance's process analysis identifies three critical bottlenecks in claim handling:

#1 Request_Documents (Avg: 36.5h, 95th: 120.0h)  
#2 Send_Closure_Letter (Avg: 12.2h, 95th: 44.0h)  
#3 Assess_Liability (Avg: 7.8h, 95th: 16.4h)  

**Recommended actions:**  
- Implement digital document submission with automated validation to reduce Request_Documents by 20% (cut 7.3h).  
- Automate closure letters using templates to reduce Send_Closure_Letter by 20% (cut 2.4h).  
- Streamline Assess_Liability workflow and provide specialized training to reduce by 20% (cut 1.6h).  

These interventions target the longest waits, directly improving customer experience.

# Contoso Airlines – Flight-Refund Process

## Decision Points

There is **one** explicit decision point in Contoso's process:

1. **High-Value Branch Decision** (evaluated after *Finance_Controller_PreApprove*)
   - **Condition:** Is the refund amount **greater than €1 000**?
   - **If YES →** the case must go through **Senior_Auditor_Review** before payment can be processed.
   - **If NO →** the case proceeds directly to **Process_Refund_Payment**.

Implicit gating conditions (not branches, but prerequisites) around **Process_Refund_Payment**:
- *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* must have succeeded, **and**
- For high-value claims, *Senior_Auditor_Review* must have been completed.

| # | Document | Used In |

| 1 | **RefundRequestForm** | Customer_Submit_Claim |

*Note:* The activity "CustomerService_Agent_Decide" is **not** part of Contoso's process and has been deliberately excluded.

## a) Decision points (gates / conditional branches)

1) **Step 4 (branch after Tri-Color-Risk-Check):** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, then **skip Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5)**; otherwise proceed to step 5.

2) **Step 6 (Decision-Gate “TriVote”):** The application **passes** if **at least two of the three** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) **approve**; fewer than two approvals means it does not pass.

**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** COO, Acme Insurance
**FROM:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst
**SUBJECT:** Q1-2025 Claims Analysis
**DATE:** 2025-01-25

**Executive Summary:** Three activities exhibit critical failure points threatening system capacity and SLA compliance.

1.  **Request_Documents**: Throughput drops to 190/day; wait time spikes to 150 minutes (outlier). Rework is highest at 22%.
    *Action:* Deploy automated document recognition to eliminate manual collection delays and reduce rework.

2.  **Review_Documents**: SLA breach is highest at 12.4%. Processing time is 25 minutes with 5.3% rework.
    *Action:* Reduce assignment volume per agent or allocate cases based on complexity to clear bottlenecks.

3.  **Initial_Assessment**: Queue time (18 min) and SLA breach (3.2%) are disproportionately high compared to downstream steps.
    *Action:* Increase triage headcount to minimize early-stage queuing.

These issues limit end-to-end throughput to 179 cases/day. Prioritizing these three activities will recover lost capacity and reduce step-specific variance.

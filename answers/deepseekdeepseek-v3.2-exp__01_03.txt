### a) Summary of Decision Points

1.  **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome:** The combination of three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) produces a single risk rating of Low, Medium, or High.
2.  **Underwriting Bypass Gate:** The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review if the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
3.  **TriVote Decision Gate:** The loan is approved to proceed only if at least two of the three designated officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant their approval.

***

**Improvement:** Insert a **timer‑intermediate event** after *Activity_VerifyID* that triggers a **boundary error‑intermediate event** on the *Activity_CreateAccount* task, causing it to fail the SLA if the 48‑hour deadline is not met.

```xml
<bpmn:intermediateCatchEvent id="Timer_48h" name="48‑hour SLA timer">
  <bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
    <bpmn:timeDuration>PT48H</bpmn:timeDuration>
  </bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
</bpmn:intermediateCatchEvent>

<bpmn:boundaryEvent id="Error_48h" attachedToRef="Activity_CreateAccount" cancelActivity="true">
  <bpmn:errorEventDefinition>
    <bpmn:errorCode>SLA_BREACH</bpmn:errorCode>
  </bpmn:errorEventDefinition>
</bpmn:boundaryEvent>
```

*Justification:* The timer intermediate event enforces the 48‑hour deadline, and the attached boundary error event aborts the “Create Account” task when the deadline expires, ensuring SLA compliance without adding any new activities.

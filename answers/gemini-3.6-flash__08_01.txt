### Justification
At marking $M_2$, transition **T2** has executed while **T3** has not yet executed. As a result, place `p3` holds a token while place `p4` remains empty. Because `p4` contains zero tokens, the inhibitor arc condition `–|p4` is satisfied, making **T5** enabled and eligible to fire immediately—well before **T3** ever fires.

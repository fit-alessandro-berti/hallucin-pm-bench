## a) Decision Points

| # | Step | Type | Rule (one sentence) |
|---|------|------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Step 4** | Conditional branch | If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check yields a Low or Medium risk rating **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250 000, the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is skipped entirely; otherwise it must be performed. |
| 2 | **Step 6** | Decision‑Gate ("TriVote") | The application passes the gate when at least **two out of the three** designated approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) vote in favour. |

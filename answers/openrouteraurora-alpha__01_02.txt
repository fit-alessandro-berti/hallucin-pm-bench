**Decision points (exact activity names)**  

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – compare the two risk scores; if the difference is ≤ 2 points the process continues, otherwise it is escalated.  
2. **Harmonisation Committee** – tie‑break decision when the scores differ by > 2 points.  
3. **Amount Threshold Decision** –  
   • If **amount < €15 000** → auto‑approval.  
   • If **amount ≥ €15 000** → forward to the board.  
4. **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)** – board votes *approve* or *reject*.

## Change Summary: Loan_Process (v1 → v2)

### Added tasks
- **Task_VerifyIdentity** ("Verify Identity") — new task inserted between the start event and *Review Application*.
- **Task_RequestDocs** ("Request Additional Documents") — new outcome branch from the decision gateway (flow labelled *"missing docs"*).
- **Task_FinalReject** ("Final Reject") — new rejection task on the gateway's *"ineligible"* branch.

### Removed tasks
- **Task_RejectLoan** ("Reject Loan") — removed; its rejection role appears to be taken over by the new *Task_FinalReject*.

### Gateway-type changes
- **Gateway_Decision**: changed from **Exclusive Gateway → Inclusive Gateway**. This is a semantic change: in v1 exactly one path (approve *or* reject) could be taken; in v2 one *or more* of the three branches (Auto-Approve, Request Additional Documents, Final Reject) may activate concurrently.

*Notes (ignored per instructions):* the renaming of Task_ApproveLoan ("Approve Loan" → "Auto-Approve Loan"), the gateway label change ("Eligible?" → "Eligibility Check"), the definitions ID change (Def_1 → Def_2), and the renumbering of sequence flows were treated as cosmetic and excluded from the headings above.

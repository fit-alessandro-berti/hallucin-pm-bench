As a senior process-management consultant for AtlasRemit, I have mapped the informal workflow to a standardized RACI matrix. All terminology has been strictly aligned with your vocabulary mandate.

| Process Step | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|--------------|---------------------|--------------------|------------|
| Receive and log transfer instruction | R, A | I | – |
| Screen payment against sanctions list | R | C | – |
| Conduct KYC Review | I | R, A | – |
| Approve high-risk transaction | I | R, A | – |
| Release payment and transmit SWIFT message | R | C | R |
| Archive case file and notify Regulatory Liaison | – | I | R, A |

**Legend:**  
**R** = Responsible (executes the task)  
**A** = Accountable (owns the outcome/approval)  
**C** = Consulted (provides input/oversight)  
**I** = Informed (receives notification)  
**–** = No direct involvement in this step

**a) Summary of Every Decision Point (Gate or Conditional Branch)**

1. **Risk Rating Threshold Gate (Step 4):**  
   *If* the Tri-Color-Risk-Check outcome is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, then skip Manual-Underwriting-Review.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):**  
   The loan application passes to the next stage if at least two out of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve the application.

**a) Summary of Every Decision Point (Gate or Conditional Branch)**

1. **Risk Rating Decision (Post-Tri-Color-Risk-Check):**  
   *If* the outcome of the Tri-Color-Risk-Check is **Low** or **Medium** *and* the requested loan amount is **€250,000 or less**, then skip Manual-Underwriting-Review.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):**  
   The loan application proceeds to the binding offer stage *only if* **at least two out of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer)** provide approval.

---

**b) List of All Documents Required (in Order of First Appearance)**

1. **Form 14B** – Submitted at the start of the process to initiate the application.
2. **Deck Memo** – Created during Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).
3. **Offer Sheet 77** – Prepared in Step 7 as the binding offer document.
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** – Required in Step 8 for applicant confirmation.
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** – Finalized in Step 9 to conclude the agreement.

**Executive Memo to the COO**

Analysis of SLA breaches and waiting times reveals three activities that jointly exceed performance targets the most:

- **Risk Review** – average waiting time 480 s (SLA 300 s) → 180 s over SLA; average throughput 600 s (SLA 300 s) → 300 s over SLA.  
- **Legal Approval** – average waiting time 600 s (SLA 600 s) is met, but average throughput 900 s (SLA 600 s) → 300 s over SLA.  
- **Credit Assessment** – average waiting time 200 s (SLA 90 s) → 110 s over SLA; average throughput 300 s (SLA 240 s) → 60 s over SLA.

**Recommendations**  
- Conduct a root‑cause study of the Risk Review step and reallocate staffing to reduce waiting time by at least 150 s, targeting a 450 s total throughput.  
- Introduce a parallel processing lane for Legal Approval to cut throughput by 300 s, aiming for 600 s total.  
- Deploy an automated decision‑support tool in Credit Assessment to lower waiting time by 80 s, bringing total throughput to 260 s.

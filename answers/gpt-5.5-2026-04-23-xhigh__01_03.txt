**a) Decision points**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check risk classification:** The application is assigned a risk rating of **Low, Medium, or High** based on **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow**.

2. **Manual-underwriting conditional branch:** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** and the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, the process **skips Manual-Underwriting-Review**; otherwise, it proceeds to **Manual-Underwriting-Review**.

3. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”:** The **Credit Lead**, **Regulatory Liaison**, and **Sustainability Officer** all participate, and the loan passes the gate if **any two of the three approve**.

**b) Documents required, in order first appearing**

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**  
5. **Deck Memo**  
6. **Offer Sheet 77**  
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77**  
8. **ID Matrix Declaration**  
9. **Loan Dossier 2025**

**Question 2:** No such sequence exists.

**Explanation:**
To fire **T5**, the required input places are **P2** and **P5**.
1. To obtain a token in **P2**, transition **T1** must fire.
2. To obtain a token in **P5**, the sequence **T1 $\to$ T2 $\to$ T7 $\to$ T4** must fire.
3. Crucially, both the path to **P2** and the path to **P5** originate from **T1**. Once **T1** fires, **P2** contains a token.
4. Transition **T3** requires **P2** as an input.
5. However, **T3** is the only transition that can consume the token from **P2** to produce a token in **P4**, which is necessary for **T4** to fire and eventually reach **P5**.

Since **T3** must fire to progress the "P2-branch" toward **P5**, **T5** cannot fire before **T3**.

# Decision Points in Acme's Micro-loan Fast-Track Process

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** (Step 5)
   - **Condition**: Compare risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B
   - **Decision**: 
     - If scores differ by ≤ 2 points → Continue to step 6
     - If scores differ by > 2 points → Escalate to Harmonisation Committee for tie-break, then continue

2. **Amount Threshold Decision** (Step 7)
   - **Condition**: Check loan amount
   - **Decision**:
     - If amount < €15,000 → System auto-approves
     - If amount ≥ €15,000 → Proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** (Step 8)
   - **Decision**: Board votes to approve or reject
   - *(Only reached when amount ≥ €15,000)*

---

# Required Documents at Submission Time

1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
3. Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

**To: COO — Q1 Claims Handling Performance**

The three weakest activities are:

1. **Request Documents** — It has the longest wait time (**150 min**), very high rework (**22.0%**), and lower throughput (**190/day**) than upstream activities.  
   **Action:** Implement a mandatory document-completeness checklist and single consolidated request before contacting customers, targeting rework reduction from 22.0% toward the next-highest rate (5.3%).

2. **Review Documents** — It has the longest processing time (**25 min**), highest variability (**9 min**), highest SLA breaches (**12.4%**), and relatively low throughput (**185/day**).  
   **Action:** Introduce standardized review templates and risk-based triage, with priority handling for cases likely to exceed the SLA.

3. **Initial Assessment** — Among the remaining activities, it shows the highest wait time (**18 min**), processing time (**12 min**), rework (**1.8%**), and SLA breaches (**3.2%**).  
   **Action:** Add a queue-management/staffing trigger to reduce its average wait time from 18 minutes toward the 4-minute level seen in Check Coverage.

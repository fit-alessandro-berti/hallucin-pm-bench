## Summary of Changes between BPMN v1 and BPMN v2

### Added tasks
*   Task\_VerifyIdentity
*   Task\_RequestDocs
*   Task\_FinalReject

### Removed tasks
*   Task\_RejectLoan

### Gateway-type changes
*   The gateway with ID `Gateway_Decision` changed from **ExclusiveGateway** (v1) to **InclusiveGateway** (v2).

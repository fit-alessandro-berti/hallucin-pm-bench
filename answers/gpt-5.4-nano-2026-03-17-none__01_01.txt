## Decision points (every place where the process branches or gates)

1. **High-value vs standard refund amount (controls whether a senior review is required)**
   - **Decision rule:** If the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**, the case proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (which must include the *AuditTrail*).
   - **Implication:** For **€1,000 or less**, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is not required.

2. **Payment eligibility gate (controls when payment may start)**
   - **Decision/gating rule:** **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin **only if**:
     - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has **succeeded**, **and**
     - for **high-value claims**, **Senior_Auditor_Review** has **been completed**.
   - **Implication:** Payment does not proceed until these conditions are met.

> There are no other decision points described in the provided Contoso process (and **no** “CustomerService_Agent_Decide” activity exists in this process).

## Required documents / artefacts (all defined supporting documents)

1. **RefundRequestForm** (online form completed by the passenger) — *used in* **Customer_Submit_Claim**
2. **FlightManifest** — checked in **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
3. **WeatherReport** — checked in **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
4. **EligibilityChecklist** — signed off in **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
5. **AuditTrail** — required as part of **Senior_Auditor_Review** for high-value claims
6. **BankTransferAuthorization** — required before **Process_Refund_Payment** can execute payment
7. **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed in **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**

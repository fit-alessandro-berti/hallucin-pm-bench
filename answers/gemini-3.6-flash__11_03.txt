**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** Chief Operating Officer  
**FROM:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**SUBJECT:** Process SLA Breaches and Waiting Time Analysis  

Performance data reveals that **Legal Approval**, **Risk Review**, and **Credit Assessment** are the three worst-performing activities, driven jointly by target SLA breaches and high waiting times. 

- **Legal Approval** exceeds its 600-second target SLA by **300 seconds** (900 seconds average throughput time), carrying the highest average waiting time of 600 seconds across 465 cases.  
- **Risk Review** exceeds its 300-second target SLA by **300 seconds** (600 seconds average throughput time), driven by an average waiting time of 480 seconds across 480 cases.  
- **Credit Assessment** exceeds its 240-second target SLA by **60 seconds** (300 seconds average throughput time), driven by an average waiting time of 200 seconds across 1,175 cases.  

Recommendations:  
* **Legal Approval:** Reduce the 600-second average waiting time by at least 300 seconds to lower total average throughput from 900 seconds to the 600-second target SLA across all 465 cases.  
* **Risk Review:** Reduce the 480-second average waiting time by 300 seconds to bring average throughput from 600 seconds down to the 300-second target SLA across 480 cases.  
* **Credit Assessment:** Reduce the 200-second average waiting time by at least 60 seconds to lower average throughput from 300 seconds to the 240-second target SLA across 1,175 cases.

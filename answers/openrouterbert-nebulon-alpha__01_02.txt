### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Determines if the risk scores from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** differ by ≤ 2 points (proceed) or > 2 points (escalate to **Harmonisation Committee**).
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Determines if the loan amount is:
   - **< €15,000** (auto-approved by system).
   - **≥ €15,000** (escalated to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**).

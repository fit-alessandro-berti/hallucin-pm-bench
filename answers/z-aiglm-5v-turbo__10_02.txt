### AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process: RACI Matrix

**Role Definitions:**
*   **Transaction Analyst:** Front-line processor responsible for data entry and initial screening.
*   **Regulatory Liaison:** Oversight role responsible for compliance checks and risk approval.
*   **IT Support:** Technical role responsible for infrastructure, messaging protocols, and data storage.

| Task / Activity | **Transaction Analyst** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **IT Support** |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **1. Receive and log transfer instruction** | **R / A** | I | I |
| **2. Perform sanctions screening** | **R** | I | I |
| **3. Perform KYC Review** (Sender & Beneficiary) | I | **R / A** | I |
| **4. Approve high-risk transactions** | I | **R / A** | I |
| **5. Release payment** | **R** | I | I |
| **6. Transmit SWIFT message** | I | I | **R** |
| **7. Archive case file** | I | I | **R** |
| **8. Confirm archiving completion** | I | **I** | **R** |

**Key:**
*   **R**esponsible: Does the work to complete the task.
*   **A**ccountable: Ultimately answerable for the correct completion of the task.
*   **C**onsulted: Provides input and two-way communication.
*   **I**nformed: Kept up-to-date on progress and completion (one-way communication).

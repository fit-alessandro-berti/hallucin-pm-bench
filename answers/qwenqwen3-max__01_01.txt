**Decision Points in Contoso Airlines’ Refund Process**

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**  
   - *Decision*: Whether the refund claim is eligible based on the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.  
   - *Outcome*: Proceeds only if eligibility is confirmed.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**  
   - *Decision*: Whether to pre-approve the refund using the *EligibilityChecklist*.  
   - *Outcome*: Must succeed before proceeding to payment; also triggers the high-value branch check.

3. **High-value refund check** (implicit decision point after pre-approval)  
   - *Decision*: Whether the refund amount exceeds **€1,000**.  
   - *Outcome*:  
     - If **yes**: Route to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (mandatory).  
     - If **no**: Skip **Senior_Auditor_Review** and proceed directly toward payment.

**Required Documents**

- *RefundRequestForm* – submitted by the customer.  
- *FlightManifest* – used during eligibility validation.  
- *WeatherReport* – used during eligibility validation.  
- *EligibilityChecklist* – used by the finance controller for pre-approval.  
- *AuditTrail* – required only if the claim is high-value (refund > €1,000).  
- *BankTransferAuthorization* – signed document required before processing payment.  
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* – generated and emailed upon completion.

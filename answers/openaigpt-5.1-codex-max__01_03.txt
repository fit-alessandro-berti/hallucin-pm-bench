**a) Decision points / rules (one sentence each)**  
1) Tri-Color-Risk-Check assigns a Low/Medium/High risk rating based on the three scorecards.  
2) If the risk is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the Manual-Underwriting-Review is skipped; otherwise it is performed.  
3) At Decision-Gate “TriVote,” any two approvals out of Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer are sufficient to pass.

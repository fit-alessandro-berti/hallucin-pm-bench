Based on Contoso Airlines’ specific process description, here is the summary of the decision point and the complete list of required documents/artefacts:

---

### 1. Decision Point

* **High-Value Refund Assessment (Threshold: €1 000)**
  * **Condition:** Is the refund amount **greater than €1 000**?
  * **Path A (Yes - High Value):** The case proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (including the *AuditTrail*). **Process_Refund_Payment** can only begin after both **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded **and** **Senior_Auditor_Review** has completed.
  * **Path B (No - Standard Value):** The case bypasses the senior auditor review and moves to **Process_Refund_Payment** as soon as **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** succeeds.

---

### 2. Required Documents / Artefacts

1. **RefundRequestForm** – Completed online by the passenger during *Customer_Submit_Claim*.
2. **FlightManifest** – Used by the triage agent during *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility*.
3. **WeatherReport** – Used by the triage agent during *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility*.
4. **EligibilityChecklist** – Signed off by the finance controller during *Finance_Controller_PreApprove*.
5. **AuditTrail** – Included during *Senior_Auditor_Review* for high-value claims (> €1 000).
6. **BankTransferAuthorization** – Signed authorization required to execute *Process_Refund_Payment*.
7. **RefundConfirmationPDF** – Emailed to the passenger during *Issue_Confirmation_Letter*.

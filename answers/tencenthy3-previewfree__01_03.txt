### a) Decision Points (Gate or Conditional Branch)
1. **Step 4 conditional branch**: If an application’s Tri-Color-Risk-Check (step 3) returns a Low or Medium risk rating and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5) is skipped; otherwise, the application proceeds to step 5.
2. **Step 6 TriVote decision gate**: The TriVote gate passes an application if at least two of the three mandatory approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant approval, as any two out of three approvals suffice to pass.

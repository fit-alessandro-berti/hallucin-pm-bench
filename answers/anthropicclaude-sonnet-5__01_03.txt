# a) Decision Points in Acme's Small-Business Loan Process

**1. Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3)**
This gate evaluates three inputs — Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow — to assign an overall risk rating of Low, Medium, or High.

**2. Manual-Underwriting-Review Skip Condition (Step 4)**
Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review) is skipped only if the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000; otherwise, Step 5 must be performed.

**3. TriVote Decision-Gate (Step 6)**
This gate requires sign-off from three roles — Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer — but the process passes as long as **any two of the three** approve (i.e., a 2-out-of-3 majority rule).

**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** Chief Operating Officer, Orion Insurance
**FROM:** Senior Process-Mining Analyst
**DATE:** October 26, 2023
**SUBJECT:** Critical Latency Bottlenecks: Top 3 Activities by Waiting Time

Executive Summary

Process mining data reveals three critical bottlenecks strictly defined by excessive **waiting time** (idle time between activities). To reduce cycle times by ≥20%, we must address the following ranked inefficiencies:

**1. Request_Documents (#1 Priority)**
*   **Performance:** Avg **36.5h** | 95th Percentile **120.0h**
*   **Recommendation:** **Automate Triggers.** Replace manual batching with event-driven automation to send document requests instantly after initial screening.

**2. Send_Closure_Letter**
*   **Performance:** Avg **12.2h** | 95th Percentile **44.0h**
*   **Recommendation:** **Deploy RPA.** Implement Robotic Process Automation to generate and dispatch closure letters immediately upon payment/rejection status change, eliminating manual queue delays.

**3. Assess_Liability**
*   **Performance:** Avg **7.8h** | 95th Percentile **16.4h**
*   **Recommendation:** **Dynamic Load Balancing.** Route claims to available assessors based on real-time capacity rather than static distribution lists to minimize inbox idle time.

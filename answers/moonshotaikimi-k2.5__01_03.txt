 **a) Decision points in Acme’s process**

1. **Step 4 – Conditional bypass:** Manual‑Underwriting‑Review is executed unless the Tri‑Color risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount does not exceed €250 000.  
2. **Step 6 – TriVote Gate:** The application passes when at least two of the three mandatory approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant approval.

**b) Documents required in the process (order of first appearance)**  

1. **Form 14B** (Step 1)  
2. **Deck Memo** (Step 5)  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7)  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8)  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9)  

*Note:* Step 8 requires the applicant to return a signed copy of Offer Sheet 77; the document type remains Offer Sheet 77 referenced above.

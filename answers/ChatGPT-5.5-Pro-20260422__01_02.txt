## Decision points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**
   Compare the two independent risk scores from:

   * **Senior Underwriter A**
   * **Shadow Underwriter B**

   Decision rule:

   * If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
   * If the scores differ by **more than 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**
   Check the requested micro-loan amount.

   Decision rule:

   * If **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves and proceeds to **Disburse Funds**.
   * If **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**
   The **Micro-loan Board** votes.

   Decision rule:

   * If the board votes **approve**, proceed to **Disburse Funds**.
   * If the board votes **reject**, the process does not proceed to **Disburse Funds**.

## Required documents at submission time

* **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
* **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**
* **Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)**
* **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
* **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
* **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

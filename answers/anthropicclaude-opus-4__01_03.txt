**a) Decision Points in Acme's Process:**

1. **Risk-Based Routing (Step 4):** If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check results in Low or Medium risk AND the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, the Manual-Underwriting-Review is skipped.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):** Requires approval from at least two out of three officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) to proceed.

**b) Documents Required (in order of first appearance):**

1. **Form 14B** - received at application (Step 1)
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** - input for risk check (Step 3)
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** - input for risk check (Step 3)
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** - input for risk check (Step 3)
5. **Deck Memo** - created during manual underwriting review (Step 5)

As a senior process-management consultant for **AtlasRemit**, I have formalized the informal process description into a structured RACI matrix. This ensures clear ownership, accountability, and streamlined workflows for our cross-border payment operations. 

Per our strict operational vocabulary standards, all roles and activities have been updated to reflect the mandated terminology.

### **AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process: RACI Matrix**

**Defined Roles:**
*   **Transaction Analyst:** Executes front-line transaction logging, screening, and payment release.
*   **Regulatory Liaison:** Oversees mandatory regulatory checks, high-risk approvals, and final notifications.
*   **IT Support:** Manages technical messaging infrastructure and system archiving.

| Process Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **1. Receive and log transfer instruction** | R | A | - |
| **2. Screen payment against sanctions list** | R | A | - |
| **3. Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary** | - | R, A | - |
| **4. Approve high-risk transactions** | I | R, A | - |
| **5. Release approved payment** | R | A | - |
| **6. Ensure SWIFT message is sent** | A | I | R |
| **7. Archive case file and notify stakeholders** | A | I | R |

**RACI Legend:**
*   **R (Responsible):** The role that performs the work to complete the task.
*   **A (Accountable):** The role ultimately answerable for the correct and thorough completion of the task (only one "A" per task).
*   **C (Consulted):** Those whose opinions are sought prior to a decision or action.
*   **I (Informed):** Those who are kept up-to-date on progress or notified upon completion. 

*Note: In tasks 3 and 4, the **Regulatory Liaison** acts as both the doer (Responsible) and the ultimate owner (Accountable), as they possess the sole authority for these specific regulatory functions.*

**a) Decision Points Summary**  
1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check outcome**: Assigns Low, Medium, or High risk rating based on Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow inputs.  
2. **Risk and Amount Conditional (Step 4)**: If the risk rating is Low or Medium *and* the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, skip Manual-Underwriting-Review; otherwise, proceed to it.  
3. **TriVote (Step 6)**: A loan application is approved if at least two of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) vote to approve.  

**b) All Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**  
1. **Form 14B** (Receive-Application)  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** (Tri-Color-Risk-Check input)  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** (Tri-Color-Risk-Check input)  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** (Tri-Color-Risk-Check input)  
5. **Deck Memo** (Manual-Underwriting-Review)  
6. **Offer Sheet 77** (Prepare-Binding-Offer and Request-Applicant-Confirmation)  
7. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Request-Applicant-Confirmation)  
8. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalize-Agreement)

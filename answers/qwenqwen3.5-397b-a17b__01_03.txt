**a) Decision Points**

1.  **Step 4 Conditional Branch:** The Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is skipped only if the risk rating is Low or Medium **AND** the requested amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Step 6 TriVote Gate:** The process proceeds to the offer stage if at least two out of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant approval.

Subject: Critical Performance Gaps in Credit Application Workflows

This review identifies the three worst-performing credit application workflow activities, evaluated jointly on SLA breaches and elevated waiting times. The top underperformer is Legal Approval: its average throughput time hits 900 seconds, 300 seconds above its 600-second target, with a 600-second waiting time. Next is Risk Review, with a 600-second average throughput time (300 seconds over its 300-second target) and a 480-second waiting time. Third is Credit Assessment, with a 300-second average throughput time (60 seconds above its 240-second target) and a 200-second waiting time.

Concrete, data-backed recommendations:
•  For Legal Approval: Redesign approval checklists to eliminate redundant steps, tailored to its 465 active cases
•  For Risk Review: Deploy a tiered review framework to prioritize lower-complexity applications first, aligned with its 480 active cases
•  For Credit Assessment: Automate routine validation checks to reduce cycle time, supporting its 1175 active cases

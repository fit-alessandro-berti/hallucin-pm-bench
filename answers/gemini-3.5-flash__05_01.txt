To enforce the 48-hour SLA without adding new activities, introduce interrupting **Timer Boundary Events** on the pre-triage tasks (`Task_Receive`, `Task_Check`, and `Task_RequestDocs`). 

Configure these timers to fire exactly 48 hours after `Start_Claim`. If any of these tasks are still active when the 48-hour limit is reached, the active timer will interrupt the process and route the flow directly to a new **Escalation End Event** (e.g., `End_Escalated`). 

This guarantees the claim is auto-escalated if it fails to reach `Task_Triage` in time, satisfying the SLA using only standard BPMN events.

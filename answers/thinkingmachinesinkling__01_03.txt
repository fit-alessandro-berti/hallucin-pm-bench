**a) Decision points (gates / branches)**

- **Manual-underwriting skip (Step 4):** Step 5 is skipped *if and only if* the Tri-Color-Risk-Check yields a **Low** or **Medium** rating **and** the requested amount is **≤ €250 000**; otherwise the review proceeds to Step 5.  
- **TriVote approval gate (Step 6):** The application passes when **any two out of the three** required approvers—**Credit Lead**, **Regulatory Liaison**, and **Sustainability Officer**—give their approval.

MEMO

To: Chief Operating Officer
From: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
Date: October 24, 2023
Subject: Process Performance Audit: Critical SLA Breaches

Analysis of recent throughput data identifies Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment as the three critical bottlenecks within our workflow. These activities represent the most significant operational risks due to high average waiting times and substantial SLA deviations.

Legal Approval is the worst-performing activity, exceeding its 600s SLA by 300s, with a 600s waiting time. Risk Review follows, exceeding its 300s SLA by 300s while carrying a 480s waiting time. Credit Assessment exceeds its 240s SLA by 60s, with a 200s waiting time.

Based on these metrics, I recommend the following:

*   Address the 600s waiting time in Legal Approval by introducing automated document templates to reduce the 900s throughput time.
*   Mitigate the 300s SLA breach in Risk Review by re-evaluating the 480s waiting time to ensure high-priority cases are prioritized over the 480 existing cases.
*   Optimize the Credit Assessment stage to close the 60s SLA gap by streamlining internal data retrieval to lower the 300s average throughput.

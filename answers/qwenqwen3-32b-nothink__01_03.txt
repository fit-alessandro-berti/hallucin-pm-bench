### **a) Decision Points in Acme Bank’s Small-Business Loan Approval Process**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**  
   - *Rule:* Risk is rated as Low, Medium, or High based on three scorecards: Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow.

2. **Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 4 conditional)**  
   - *Rule:* Skipped **if** the risk is **Low or Medium** **and** the requested loan amount is **≤ €250,000**; otherwise, a manual underwriting review is required.

3. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6)**  
   - *Rule:* Requires **at least two of three** approvals from: Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer.

---

### **b) All Documents in the Process (in Order of Appearance)**

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**  
5. **Deck Memo** (optional — only used if Manual-Underwriting-Review is performed)  
6. **Offer Sheet 77**  
7. **ID Matrix Declaration**  
8. **Loan Dossier 2025**

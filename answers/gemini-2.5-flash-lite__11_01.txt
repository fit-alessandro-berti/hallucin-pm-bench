**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** COO, Orion Insurance
**From:** Senior Process Mining Analyst
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Subject:** Executive Summary: Claim Process Waiting Time Bottlenecks

Our analysis of the last 12 months reveals significant waiting time bottlenecks in the claims process. The three worst-performing activities, ranked by average waiting time, are:

1.  **Request_Documents**: Avg. 36.5h, 95th Percentile 120.0h. **Action:** Implement automated document validation and clearer requirements for submission to reduce request loops by 20%.
2.  **Send_Closure_Letter**: Avg. 12.2h, 95th Percentile 44.0h. **Action:** Streamline the final review step for closure letters to accelerate dispatch by 20%.
3.  **Assess_Liability**: Avg. 7.8h, 95th Percentile 16.4h. **Action:** Introduce standardized assessment templates and decision trees to expedite analysis by 20%.

Addressing these will significantly improve customer experience and operational efficiency.

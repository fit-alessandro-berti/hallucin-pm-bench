### **a) Decision Points in Acme Bank’s SMALL-BUSINESS Loan-Approval Process**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check → Conditional Branch (Step 4)**
   *Rule:* If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** **AND** the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, skip **Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5)**.

2. **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6)**
   *Rule:* The loan passes if **at least two out of three** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve.

---

### **b) All Required Documents in Order of First Appearance**

1. **Form 14B** *(Step 1: Receive-Application)*
2. **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow** *(Step 3: Tri-Color-Risk-Check)*
3. **Deck Memo** *(Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review, if applicable)*
4. **Offer Sheet 77** *(Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)*
5. **ID Matrix Declaration** *(Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation, alongside Signed Offer Sheet 77)*
6. **Loan Dossier 2025** *(Step 9: Finalize-Agreement)*

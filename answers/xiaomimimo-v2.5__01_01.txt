Based on the provided process definition for Contoso Airlines, here are the **decision points** and **required documents**.

### **Decision Points**
There is **one explicit decision point** that branches the process:

*   **High-Value Claim Assessment:** The claim is evaluated to determine if the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**.
    *   **Yes:** The process must route to the **Senior_Auditor_Review** step.
    *   **No:** The process proceeds directly from Finance_Controller_PreApprove towards Process_Refund_Payment.

*Note: Steps like Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility and Finance_Controller_PreApprove involve judgment and checks, but the process description does not define them as points where the workflow *branches*. Their outcome determines if the process continues or stops, not a change in sequence.*

### **Required Documents & Artifacts**
The following documents are explicitly required to be produced or used in the process:

1.  **RefundRequestForm** – Submitted by the customer.
2.  **FlightManifest** – Checked by the Triage Agent.
3.  **WeatherReport** – Checked by the Triage Agent.
4.  **EligibilityChecklist** – Signed off by the Finance Controller.
5.  **AuditTrail** – Included in the Senior Auditor’s review (for high-value claims only).
6.  **BankTransferAuthorization** – Required before payment can be processed.
7.  **RefundConfirmationPDF** – E-mailed to the passenger as the final step.

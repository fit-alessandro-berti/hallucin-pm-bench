**Subject: Q1 Claims Handling performance priorities**

The three worst-performing activities are:

1. **Request_Documents** — Clear bottleneck: highest wait time at **150 min**, largest rework rate at **22.0%**, and throughput drops to **190/day** versus 270/day earlier in the flow.  
   **Action:** Implement a guided document checklist with automated completeness validation before requests are sent; track weekly reduction in repeat requests.

2. **Review_Documents** — Worst execution performance: highest processing time at **25 min**, highest variability at **9 min stdev**, and highest SLA breach rate at **12.4%**.  
   **Action:** Introduce triage: fast-track complete/simple files and route complex files to senior reviewers; monitor breach rate by case type.

3. **Initial_Assessment** — The third worst is differentiated by the next-highest wait time (**18 min**), processing time (**12 min**), rework (**1.8%**), and SLA breach rate (**3.2%**) among remaining activities.  
   **Action:** Standardize assessment rules/checklists and review cases exceeding 12 minutes to remove recurring causes.

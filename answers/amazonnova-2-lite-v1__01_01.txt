### Summary of Decision Points & Required Documents in Contoso Airlines' Flight-Refund Process

#### **Decision Points**

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - **Decision**: Determine whether the customer's claim is eligible for a refund based on the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.
   - **Outcome**: 
     - **Eligible** → Proceed to **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**.
     - **Ineligible** → Process terminates; no refund issued.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - **Decision**: Approve or reject the refund request using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
   - **Outcome**:
     - **Approved** → Proceed to **Process_Refund_Payment** (or **Senior_Auditor_Review** if high-value).
     - **Rejected** → Process terminates; no refund issued.

3. **High-value refund threshold check (€1,000)**
   - **Decision**: Evaluate whether the refund amount exceeds €1,000.
   - **Outcome**:
     - **> €1,000** → Proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
     - **≤ €1,000** → Skip **Senior_Auditor_Review**; proceed directly to **Process_Refund_Payment**.

4. **Senior_Auditor_Review (conditional)**
   - **Decision**: Review the case and approve or reject based on the *AuditTrail*.
   - **Outcome**:
     - **Approved** → Proceed to **Process_Refund_Payment**.
     - **Rejected** → Process terminates; no refund issued.

---

#### **Required Documents**

1. **RefundRequestForm**  
   - Submitted by the passenger during **Customer_Submit_Claim**.

2. **FlightManifest**  
   - Used by the **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** to verify flight details.

3. **WeatherReport**  
   - Used by the **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** to assess weather-related eligibility.

4. **EligibilityChecklist**  
   - Used by **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** to validate the claim against policy rules.

5. **AuditTrail** (conditional)  
   - Required only when the refund amount is **greater than €1,000** and used during **Senior_Auditor_Review**.

6. **BankTransferAuthorization** (signed)  
   - Required for **Process_Refund_Payment** to execute the refund.

7. **RefundConfirmationPDF**  
   - Generated and emailed to the passenger during **Issue_Confirmation_Letter** after the refund is processed.

---

### Constraint Reminder
- **Process_Refund_Payment** can only start **after**:
  - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** succeeds **AND**
  - For refund amounts **> €1,000**, after **Senior_Auditor_Review** completes successfully.

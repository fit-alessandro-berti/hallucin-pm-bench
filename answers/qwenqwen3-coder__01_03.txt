### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Loan-Approval Process**

1. **Step 4 – Conditional Skip of Manual Underwriting**  
   *Rule:* If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, then skip the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).

2. **Step 6 – TriVote Decision Gate**  
   *Rule:* A minimum of two out of three approvals from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer is required to pass this gate.

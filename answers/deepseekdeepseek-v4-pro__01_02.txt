**Decision points** in Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process (using the exact activity names):

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** (step 5) – evaluates whether the risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points. If yes, continue to step 6. If not, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision, then continue.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** (step 7) – checks the loan amount. If the amount is **< €15 000**, the system auto-approves. If the amount is **≥ €15 000**, the case proceeds to step 8.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval** (MBA) (step 8) – the Micro-loan Board votes to **approve** or **reject** the application.

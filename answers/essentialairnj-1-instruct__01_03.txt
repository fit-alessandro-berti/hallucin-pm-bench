### a) Decision Points in Acme Bank's SMALL-BUSINESS loan-approval process:

- **Receive-Application**: The decision to move forward with the application based on the completion of Form 14B.
- **Assign-Deck-Reviewer**: The delegation of the application to a Deck Officer for initial processing.
- **Tri-Color-Risk-Check**: The evaluation of the application using Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards to determine risk as either Low, Medium, or High.
- **Condition for Manual-Underwriting-Review**: Decision to proceed only if risk is Low or Medium AND requested amount is less than or equal to €250,000.
- **TriVote Decision Gate**: Approval decision based on approvals from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, where any two out of three approvals are sufficient to pass.
- **Request-Applicant-Confirmation**: The requirement for the applicant to sign the Binding Offer Sheet 77 and provide the ID Matrix Declaration.
- **Finalize-Agreement**: The completion of the loan approval process with the binding signed Loan Dossier 2025.

### b) Documents Required in Acme Bank’s SMALL-BUSINESS loan-approval process:

1. Form 14B
2. Deck Memo
3. Offer Sheet 77
4. ID Matrix Declaration
5. Loan Dossier 2025

Based on Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process, here is the summary of the **decision points** and the complete list of **required documents**:

### Decision Points

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate (Step 5)**
   * **Condition:** Compare the risk scores (0–9) assigned independently by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B.
   * **Outcome 1:** If the two scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue directly to the Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC).
   * **Outcome 2:** If the scores differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before continuing.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7)**
   * **Condition:** Evaluate the total requested loan amount.
   * **Outcome 1:** If the **amount < €15 000**, the system automatically approves the loan and proceeds to Disburse Funds.
   * **Outcome 2:** If the **amount ≥ €15 000**, the process proceeds to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

*(Note: Step 8 also functions as a decision point where the Micro-loan Board votes to either "approve" or "reject" the loan.)*

---

### Required Documents at Submission Time

▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

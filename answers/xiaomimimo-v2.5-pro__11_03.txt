**To: Orion Credit COO**  
**From: Senior Process-Performance Analyst**  
**Subject: Priority Process Bottlenecks Requiring Immediate Action**

Our analysis identifies three critical activities with the highest combined impact from SLA breaches and excessive waiting times. These are severely hampering end-to-end cycle time.

*   **Legal Approval:** Breaches its 600-second SLA by 300 seconds (900s throughput), compounded by the highest waiting time in the process (600s).
*   **Risk Review:** Breaches its 300-second SLA by 300 seconds (600s throughput) and has the second-highest waiting time (480s).
*   **Credit Assessment:** Breaches its 240-second SLA by 60 seconds (300s throughput) and experiences significant waiting (200s).

**Recommendations:**
*   For **Legal Approval**, implement automated document pre-validation against a checklist to reduce manual review load and waiting time.
*   For **Risk Review**, introduce a rules-based auto-approval track for low-risk cases to directly reduce both throughput and waiting time.
*   For **Credit Assessment**, refine the credit-scoring model's threshold parameters to decrease the need for manual overrides, targeting a direct reduction in throughput time.

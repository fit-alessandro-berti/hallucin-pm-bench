Of course. Here is the information regarding Acme Bank's small-business loan-approval process, as per your request.

### a) Summary of Decision Points

1.  **Tri-Color Risk-Check Conditional Branch:** The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review if the calculated risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **TriVote Decision Gate:** The application is approved and proceeds only if it receives approval from **at least two out of the three** designated officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer).

### b) List of All Required Documents (in Order of First Appearance)

1.  **Form 14B:** The initial application form received from the business.
2.  **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow:** The three inputs used for the Tri-Color-Risk-Check.
3.  **Deck Memo:** The document prepared for the Manual-Underwriting-Review (if required).
4.  **Offer Sheet 77:** The binding loan offer prepared after a successful TriVote.
5.  **ID Matrix Declaration:** A required document from the applicant, submitted alongside the signed Offer Sheet 77 for confirmation.
6.  **Loan Dossier 2025:** The final, binding agreement document that is finalized upon applicant confirmation.

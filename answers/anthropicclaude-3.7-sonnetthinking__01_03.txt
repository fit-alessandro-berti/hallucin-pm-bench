# Acme Bank's Small-Business Loan Approval Process

## a) Decision Points Summary

1. **Risk-Amount Bypass Decision (Step 4):** If the risk is rated Low or Medium AND the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, then the Manual-Underwriting-Review step is skipped.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):** The loan application requires review by all three officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer), with any two approvals out of the three being sufficient for the loan to proceed.

## b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)

1. **Form 14B** (Receive-Application)
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** (Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** (Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** (Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
5. **Deck Memo** (Manual-Underwriting-Review)
6. **Offer Sheet 77** (Prepare-Binding-Offer)
7. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
8. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalize-Agreement)

**a) Decision points (gates or conditional branches)**

1. **Conditional skip (Step 4)** – If the Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check yields *Low* or *Medium* risk **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250 000, the Manual‑Underwriting‑Review is skipped; otherwise it must be performed.  
2. **TriVote gate (Step 6)** – The loan is approved if at least two of the three officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) vote in favour; if fewer than two approve, the loan is declined.

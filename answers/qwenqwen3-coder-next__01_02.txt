**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**  

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   – Decision: Continue to step 6 *or* escalate to Harmonisation Committee  
   – Condition: If Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, proceed.  
   – Else (difference > 2), escalate to Harmonisation Committee for tie-break.  

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   – Decision: Auto-approve *or* proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval  
   – Condition: If requested amount **< €15 000**, auto-approve.  
   – Else (**≥ €15 000**), proceed to step 8 (MBA).  

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** *(only triggered for ≥ €15 000)*  
   – Decision: *Approve* or *Reject*  
   – Trigger: Amount threshold ≥ €15 000 after underwriter alignment and NFC.

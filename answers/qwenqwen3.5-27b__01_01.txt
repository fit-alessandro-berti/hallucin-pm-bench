### Decision Points

Based on the defined Contoso Airlines flight-refund process, there are three specific points where the process flow is determined by a condition or approval status:

1.  **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
    *   **Logic:** The triage agent checks the claim against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.
    *   **Outcome:** Determines if the claim proceeds to the Finance Controller.
2.  **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
    *   **Logic:** The finance controller signs off using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
    *   **Outcome:** Payment processing may begin only after this step has **succeeded**.
3.  **High-value branch**
    *   **Logic:** Checks if the refund amount is **greater than â‚¬1 000**.
    *   **Outcome:** If true, the case proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (which must include the *AuditTrail*). If false, this step is bypassed.

### Required Documents

The following artefacts are explicitly required within the process steps:

*   **RefundRequestForm** (Customer_Submit_Claim)
*   **FlightManifest** (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
*   **WeatherReport** (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
*   **EligibilityChecklist** (Finance_Controller_PreApprove)
*   **AuditTrail** (Senior_Auditor_Review, required for high-value claims)
*   **BankTransferAuthorization** (Process_Refund_Payment)
*   **RefundConfirmationPDF** (Issue_Confirmation_Letter)

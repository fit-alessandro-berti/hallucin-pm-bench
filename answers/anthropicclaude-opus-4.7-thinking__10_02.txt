# RACI Matrix — AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process

**Legend:** R = Responsible · A = Accountable · C = Consulted · I = Informed

| # | Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|----------|:-------------------:|:------------------:|:----------:|
| 1 | Receive transfer instruction and log it in the system | R, A | I | C |
| 2 | Screen payment against sanctions list | R, A | C | — |
| 3 | Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary | C | R, A | — |
| 4 | Approve high-risk flagged transaction | I | R, A | — |
| 5 | Release the approved payment | R, A | I | C |
| 6 | Send the SWIFT message | C | I | R, A |
| 7 | Archive the case file | I | I | R, A |
| 8 | Notify Regulatory Liaison of case closure | — | I | R, A |

---

### Notes on Role Assignments
- **Transaction Analyst** owns intake, sanctions screening, and payment release — the operational backbone of the process.
- **Regulatory Liaison** is exclusively accountable for the **KYC Review** and any high-risk approval decisions, ensuring regulatory integrity.
- **IT Support** is accountable for technical execution (SWIFT transmission) and records retention (archival), and is responsible for informing the Regulatory Liaison upon closure.

Based strictly on the provided process definition, here are the decision points and required documents:

### 🔹 Decision Points
1. **Eligibility Validation Gate** – The triage agent assesses whether the claim is eligible by cross-referencing it against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*. A negative outcome halts progression; a positive outcome forwards the claim to the finance controller.
2. **High-Value Routing Branch** – The system/agent evaluates whether the refund amount exceeds €1,000. 
   • **> €1,000**: Case is routed to *Senior_Auditor_Review*.
   • **≤ €1,000**: Case bypasses the audit step and proceeds directly to the next stage.
3. **Payment Execution Gate** – Payment may only commence after verifying three conditions: *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* has succeeded, *Senior_Auditor_Review* is complete (if triggered), and a signed *BankTransferAuthorization* is present.

### 📄 Required Documents & Artefacts
• *RefundRequestForm* – Passenger-submitted claim initiation  
• *FlightManifest* – System data referenced for eligibility validation  
• *WeatherReport* – System data referenced for eligibility validation  
• *EligibilityChecklist* – Tool used by the finance controller to issue pre-approval  
• *AuditTrail* – Mandatory artefact required during *Senior_Auditor_Review*  
• *BankTransferAuthorization* – Signed prerequisite authorizing payment execution  
• *RefundConfirmationPDF* – Generated confirmation emailed to the passenger upon completion

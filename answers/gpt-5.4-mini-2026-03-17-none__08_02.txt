**Q2 (bonus):**  
One minimal firing sequence is:

**T1, T2, T4, T5**

Explanation:  
- **T1** puts tokens in **P1** and **P2**  
- **T2** moves the **P1** token to **P3**  
- **T4** is not yet enabled until **P4** exists, so we need **T7** or **T3** first.  
Actually, the shortest valid sequence before **T5** requires **T7**:

**T1, T2, T7, T4, T5**

This fires **T5** before **T3**.

**a) Decision points (gates or conditional branches)**  

1. **Conditional branch after Tri-Color-Risk-Check (step 4):**  
   If the risk rating is **Low** or **Medium** **and** the requested loan amount is **≤ €250,000**, then skip Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5); otherwise proceed to step 5.  

2. **TriVote decision gate (step 6):**  
   The gate requires approvals from the **Credit Lead**, **Regulatory Liaison**, and **Sustainability Officer**; however, the rule is that **any two out of three approvals** are sufficient to pass the gate.

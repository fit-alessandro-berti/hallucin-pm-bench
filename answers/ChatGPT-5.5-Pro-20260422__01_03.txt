## a) Decision points

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** The application is assigned a risk rating of **Low, Medium, or High** based on **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow**.

2. **Manual-underwriting skip branch:** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** and the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, Acme skips **Manual-Underwriting-Review**; otherwise, the application goes through manual underwriting.

3. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”:** The loan passes the gate if **any two of the three mandatory participants** approve: **Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer**.

## b) Required documents in first-appearance order

1. **Form 14B**
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**
5. **Deck Memo**
6. **Offer Sheet 77**
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77**
8. **ID Matrix Declaration**
9. **Loan Dossier 2025**

Excluded from this list: KYC/AML documents and separate credit-bureau pulls, because the process explicitly says those are not part of Acme’s in-house process.

### Decision Points in Contoso Airlines’ Refund Process
All formal decision points governing process progression, aligned with the defined workflow, are:
1. **Eligibility approval (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility):** A triage agent decides if a submitted claim meets refund eligibility criteria; only approved eligible claims advance past this step.
2. **Pre-approval sign-off (Finance_Controller_PreApprove):** A finance controller decides whether to pre-approve an eligible claim, a mandatory prerequisite for all subsequent steps.
3. **High-value claim classification:** After pre-approval, a mandatory threshold check decides if the refund amount exceeds €1000, triggering an additional audit review step for qualifying high-value claims.
4. **High-value claim audit approval (Senior_Auditor_Review):** For claims over €1000, the senior auditor decides whether to approve the claim, completing the required additional review to enable refund processing.

### Full List of Required Documents
All formal artefacts defined for the process are:
- RefundRequestForm (to submit the initial refund claim)
- FlightManifest
- WeatherReport
- EligibilityChecklist
- AuditTrail (required exclusively for high-value refunds over €1000)
- BankTransferAuthorization
- RefundConfirmationPDF

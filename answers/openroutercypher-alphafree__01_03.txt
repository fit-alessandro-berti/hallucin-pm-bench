**a) Decision Points in Acme's SMALL-BUSINESS Loan Approval Process:**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome:**

- **Rule:** If the tri-color risk outcome is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, bypass Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).

2. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”:**

- **Rule:** Proceed to the next stage if at least two of the three decision-makers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve, requiring all three votes to render a decision.

**b) Required Documents:**

1. **Form 14B** – Application Submission
2. **Deck Memo** – Manual Underwriting Review (if applicable)
3. **Offer Sheet 77** – Binding Offer
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** – Applicant Confirmation
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** – Final Agreement

**Added tasks**  
• `Task_VerifyIdentity` (“Verify Identity”) inserted immediately after the start event.  
• `Task_RequestDocs` (“Request Additional Documents”) added as a new execution path branching directly from the gateway.  
• `Task_FinalReject` (“Final Reject”) introduced as the new termination task for ineligible applications.  

**Removed tasks**  
• `Task_RejectLoan` (“Reject Loan”) completely removed (functionally replaced by `Task_FinalReject`).  

**Gateway-type changes**  
• `Gateway_Decision` changed from `exclusiveGateway` to `inclusiveGateway`.

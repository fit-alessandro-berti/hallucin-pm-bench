### Answer: Yes
The shortest firing sequence is **`T1 → T2b → T4 → T5`** (or equivalently `T1 → T4 → T2b → T5`; the order of `T2b` and `T4` does not matter after `T1`).

---
### Breakdown of validity:
1.  Initial marking only enables `T1`, which fires first to produce 1 token each in `pa` and `pb`.
2.  After `T1`, both `T2b` (uses `pa` to create `pd`) and `T4` (uses `pb` to create `pe`) are enabled, and can fire in either order.
3.  After these two transitions, both `pd` and `pe` have 1 token each, which fully enables `T5`—*without ever firing `T3`*.
4.  Firing `T5` now occurs before `T3` is ever triggered in this sequence.

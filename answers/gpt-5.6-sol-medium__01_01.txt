### Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation** — **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** checks the claim using the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
2. **Finance pre-approval** — **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** must succeed, with sign-off using the **EligibilityChecklist**.
3. **High-value determination**
   - **Amount greater than €1,000:** **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required and must include the **AuditTrail**.
   - **Amount €1,000 or less:** no **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required.
4. **Payment readiness** — **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin only when:
   - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded;
   - **Senior_Auditor_Review** is complete for a high-value claim; and
   - the signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

### Required documents

- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail** — for claims greater than €1,000
- Signed **BankTransferAuthorization**
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger during **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**

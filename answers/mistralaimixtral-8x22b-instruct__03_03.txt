```xml
    <sequenceFlow id="wrc_flow15" sourceRef="wrc_update_inventory" targetRef="wrc_close_claim" />
    <sequenceFlow id="wrc_flow16" sourceRef="wrc_close_claim" targetRef="wrc_end_success" />
  </process>
</definitions>
```

This XML represents the exact process flow described in the requirements:
1. Starts with an online warranty claim
2. Validates the claim (with loop for missing information)
3. Checks warranty eligibility
4. For eligible claims, performs parallel shipment creation and courier scheduling
5. Updates inventory and closes the claim
6. Has two distinct end points (rejection and successful completion)

All element IDs are prefixed with "wrc_" as requested, and task names match the requirements verbatim.

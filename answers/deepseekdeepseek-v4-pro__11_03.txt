To: COO  
From: Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
Subject: Top Three Activities with SLA Breaches and High Waiting Times  

Risk Review, Legal Approval, and Credit Assessment jointly exhibit the worst performance—large SLA breaches and high waiting times. Risk Review exceeds its 300‑s SLA by 300 s (100%), with an average waiting time of 480 s (80% of throughput). Legal Approval exceeds its 600‑s SLA by 300 s (50%), and waiting accounts for 600 s (67% of throughput). Credit Assessment misses its 240‑s SLA by 60 s (25%), with a 200‑s wait (67% of throughput). To relieve these bottlenecks, I recommend three data-driven actions:

• Increase Risk Review capacity by adding one full‑time reviewer, directly targeting the 480‑s wait and reclaiming the 300‑s breach.  
• Streamline Legal Approval with pre‑qualification rules for standard cases, reducing the dominant 600‑s waiting time.  
• Deploy an automated scoring tool for Credit Assessment to cut the 200‑s manual wait and bring throughput below 240 s.  

These interventions address the root waiting-time drivers and align each activity with its SLA.

**Legend**

- **R** – Responsible – performs the work.  
- **A** – Accountable – final decision‑maker, owns the outcome.  
- **C** – Consulted – provides input before the activity proceeds.  
- **I** – Informed – kept up‑to‑date on the activity’s result.  

**R/A** in a cell means the role is both **Responsible** and **Accountable** for that activity.  

All activities use the preferred terminology: **KYC Review**, **Order Vetting**, and **Payment Clearance** as required.

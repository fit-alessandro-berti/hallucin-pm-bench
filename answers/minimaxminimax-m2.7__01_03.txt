**a) Decision points (gate / conditional branch)**  

| # | Decision point | One‑sentence rule |
|---|----------------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check** | The three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) are evaluated to assign a risk rating of **Low**, **Medium**, or **High**. |
| 2 | **Skip‑Manual‑Underwriting branch** | If the risk rating is **Low** or **Medium** **and** the requested loan amount ≤ €250 000, the process **skips** the Manual‑Underwriting‑Review; otherwise it **proceeds** to that review. |
| 3 | **TriVote Decision Gate** | The loan can advance only if **at least two** of the three required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve; any fewer than two approvals results in rejection. |

**b) Documents (first appearance order)**  

1. **Form 14B** – application form received in step 1.  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** – first input to the Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check (step 3).  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** – second input to the Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check (step 3).  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** – third input to the Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check (step 3).  
5. **Deck Memo** – produced (or required) for the Manual‑Underwriting‑Review (step 5).  
6. **Offer Sheet 77** – the binding offer prepared in step 7.  
7. **ID Matrix Declaration** – required together with the signed Offer Sheet 77 for applicant confirmation (step 8).  
8. **Loan Dossier 2025** – final agreement dossier compiled in step 9.  

*(The “Signed Offer Sheet 77” is simply the signed copy of Offer Sheet 77 and does not constitute a separate document in this list.)*

**a)** Decision points in Acme’s process are as follows:

1. **Risk-Based Bypass Gate**  
 • If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check returns a risk rating of Low or Medium AND the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, the Manual Underwriting Review step is skipped.

2. **TriVote Committee Decision Gate**  
 • To pass this gate and proceed to the next step (Prepare Binding Offer), at least two approvals out of the three required (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) must be received.

**b)** Documents that appear in the process, in the order they are first referenced:

1. Form 14B  
2. Scorecard–Cyan  
3. Scorecard–Magenta  
4. Scorecard–Yellow  
5. Deck Memo  
6. Offer Sheet 77  
7. ID Matrix Declaration  
8. Loan Dossier 2025

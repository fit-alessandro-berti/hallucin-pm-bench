As a senior process-management consultant for **AtlasRemit**, I have structured the cross-border payment process according to your mandated vocabulary. 

Below is the RACI matrix detailing the operational workflow:

### AtlasRemit: Cross-Border Payment Process RACI Matrix

| Process Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Log transfer instruction | **R** | I | I |
| Sanctions screening | **R** | A | I |
| **KYC Review** (Sender & Beneficiary) | C | **A** | I |
| High-risk transaction approval | C | **A** | I |
| Payment release | **R** | I | C |
| SWIFT message transmission | I | I | **R** |
| Case file archiving | I | I | **R** |
| Notify **Regulatory Liaison** of completion | I | **A** | **R** |

***

**Key for RACI roles:**
*   **R (Responsible):** The role that performs the activity.
*   **A (Accountable):** The role that approves/owns the outcome of the activity.
*   **C (Consulted):** The role whose expertise is sought prior to completion.
*   **I (Informed):** The role kept updated on progress or completion.

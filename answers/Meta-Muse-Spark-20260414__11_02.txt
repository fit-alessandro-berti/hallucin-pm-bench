**To: COO, Acme Insurance**  
**Re: Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance – Priority Actions**

Based strictly on dashboard metrics, the three worst-performing activities are:

**1. Request_Documents**  
*Why:* Highest `avg_wait_time_min` at 150, `rework_rate_pct` of 22.0%, and `sla_breach_pct` of 5.5%. Throughput also drops from 270 to 190 cases/day here.  
*Action:* Implement pre-submission checklists and automated completeness validation at intake to reduce wait time and rework loops.

**2. Review_Documents**  
*Why:* Highest `sla_breach_pct` at 12.4%, longest `avg_processing_time_min` at 25, and high variability `stdev_processing_time_min` of 9. `Rework_rate_pct` is 5.3%.  
*Action:* Segment documents by complexity and add SLA-aligned work queues; assign simpler cases to junior staff to lower processing time and variance.

**3. Initial_Assessment**  
*Why:* `Avg_wait_time_min` of 18 is the second-highest, with `sla_breach_pct` at 3.2% and `rework_rate_pct` at 1.8%, despite only 12 min processing time.  
*Action:* Increase staffing or introduce triage rules during peak hours to cut queue time, which directly drives its SLA breaches.

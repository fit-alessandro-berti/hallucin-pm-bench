**MEMO — Claims Process: Top Waiting-Time Bottlenecks**

To: COO, Orion Insurance
From: Process-Mining Analysis Team

Ranked by waiting time, our three worst activities are:

**#1 Request_Documents** — Avg 36.5 h; 95th pct 120.0 h.
*Action:* Deploy an automated customer portal with upfront document checklists and SMS/email reminders at 24-h intervals, eliminating manual outreach delays (target: ≥20 % reduction).

**#2 Send_Closure_Letter** — Avg 12.2 h; 95th pct 44.0 h.
*Action:* Auto-generate closure letters from case-resolution data via templated robotic process automation, triggered immediately upon case close.

**#3 Assess_Liability** — Avg 7.8 h; 95th pct 16.4 h.
*Action:* Introduce rules-based triage to auto-route low-complexity claims to a fast-track queue, reserving adjusters for complex cases and smoothing workload peaks.

We recommend piloting #1 first—its tail (120 h) drives the greatest customer-experience risk.

### a) Decision Points
1. **Risk-Based Conditional Branch (Step 4)**: The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) if the risk rating from the Tri-Color-Risk-Check is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6)**: The process advances to preparing the binding offer if at least any two approvals are obtained from the three required roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer).

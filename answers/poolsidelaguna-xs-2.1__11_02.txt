**Executive Memo: Claims Handling Performance Analysis**  

Q1-2025 identifies three underperforming activities in the Claims Handling process:  

1. **Request_Documents**  
   - **Why**: Highest wait time (150 mins) and rework rate (22%), with 5.5% SLA breaches.  
   - **Action**: Automate document collection and implement real-time tracking to reduce delays and rework.  

2. **Review_Documents**  
   - **Why**: High processing time (25 mins) and variability (stdev=9), leading to 12.4% SLA breaches and 5.3% rework.  
   - **Action**: Standardize review protocols and deploy AI-assisted validation to streamline and stabilize processing.  

3. **Initial_Assessment**  
   - **Why**: Elevated wait time (18 mins) and 3.2% SLA breaches, though lower than Request_Documents and Review_Documents.  
   - **Action**: Optimize staffing levels during peak hours to reduce queue time and improve throughput consistency.  

These actions target root causes directly tied to the metrics, ensuring measurable improvements.

### Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation — `Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility`**
   - Determines whether the passenger’s claim is eligible.
   - Based on the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.

2. **Financial pre-approval — `Finance_Controller_PreApprove`**
   - Determines whether the refund receives finance sign-off.
   - Based on the **EligibilityChecklist**.
   - Payment cannot begin unless this step succeeds.

3. **High-value branching decision**
   - **Refund amount > €1,000:** `Senior_Auditor_Review` is required before payment.
   - **Refund amount ≤ €1,000:** no senior-auditor step is required; payment may proceed after successful finance pre-approval and receipt of the signed authorization.

`Senior_Auditor_Review` is a mandatory high-value review, but the description does not define an additional approval criterion or outcome for it. Likewise, presence of the signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is a payment precondition rather than a separately defined decision activity.

### Required documents

| Document | Where required |
|---|---|
| **RefundRequestForm** | Completed during `Customer_Submit_Claim` |
| **FlightManifest** | Consulted during eligibility validation |
| **WeatherReport** | Consulted during eligibility validation |
| **EligibilityChecklist** | Used for finance-controller pre-approval |
| **AuditTrail** | Required for `Senior_Auditor_Review` when the refund is greater than €1,000 |
| **BankTransferAuthorization** | Must be signed and present before `Process_Refund_Payment` |
| **RefundConfirmationPDF** | Emailed during `Issue_Confirmation_Letter` |

The legacy **`CustomerService_Agent_Decide`** activity is not part of Contoso’s process.

**Yes**, it is possible for transition **T5** to fire before **T3**. 

**Shortest firing sequence:**
`T1, T2b, T4, T5`

**Explanation of the sequence:**
1. **T1** fires: consumes the token in `p0`, producing tokens in `pa` and `pb`.
2. **T2b** fires: consumes the token in `pa`, producing a token in `pd` (taking the shortcut).
3. **T4** fires: consumes the token in `pb`, producing a token in `pe`.
4. **T5** fires: consumes the tokens in `pd` and `pe`, producing a token in `pf`. 

At no point in this sequence does transition **T3** (which requires a token in `pc`) fire.

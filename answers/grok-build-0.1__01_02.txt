**Decision points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process:**

- **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: If the risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points, continue to the next step; otherwise, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision before continuing.
- **Amount Threshold Decision**: If the amount is < €15 000, the system auto-approves; if the amount is ≥ €15 000, proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).
- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The board votes to approve or reject.

**Every required document:**
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

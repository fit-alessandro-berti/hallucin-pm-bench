**Yes**, it is possible.

**Firing sequence:** T1, T2, T5

**Justification:**  
After T1 fires, the marking is (p1=1, p2=1).  
T2 can now fire (consuming the token from p1), producing the marking (p2=1, p3=1).  
At this point p4 = 0, so the inhibitor arc on T5 is satisfied and T5 is enabled.  
Thus T5 can fire *before* T3 has ever fired.

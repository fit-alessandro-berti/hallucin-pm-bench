**Executive memo — waiting-time priorities**

#1 **Request_Documents** — Avg wait **36.5h**; 95th percentile **120.0h**.  
Action: trigger automated reminders at 24h and escalation at 48h for open document requests; target avg wait **≤29.2h**.

#2 **Send_Closure_Letter** — Avg wait **12.2h**; 95th percentile **44.0h**.  
Action: batch-generate closure letters twice daily from a queue aged by wait time; target avg wait **≤9.8h**.

#3 **Assess_Liability** — Avg wait **7.8h**; 95th percentile **16.4h**.  
Action: implement an oldest-first liability assessment queue with same-day escalation for items waiting >8h; target avg wait **≤6.2h**.

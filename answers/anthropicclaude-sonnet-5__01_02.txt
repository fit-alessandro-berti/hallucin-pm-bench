# Acme Bank's Micro-loan Fast-Track: Decision Points & Required Documents

## Decision Points

**1. Underwriter Alignment Gate** (following Step 4 – Dual Underwriter Split)
- If Senior Underwriter A's score and Shadow Underwriter B's score differ by **≤ 2 points** → continue to step 6 (Neighbourhood Feedback Check)
- If the scores differ by **more than 2 points** → escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue

**2. Amount Threshold Decision** (Step 7)
- If **amount < €15 000** → system auto-approves
- If **amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to step 8, **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**, where the board votes *approve* or *reject*

## Required Documents at Submission Time

1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
3. Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

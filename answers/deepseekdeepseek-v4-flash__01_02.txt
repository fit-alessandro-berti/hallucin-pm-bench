**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**  

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – If the two risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, the process continues to step 6. Otherwise, the case is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, after which the process continues.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – If the loan amount is **< €15 000**, the system auto-approves. If **≥ €15 000**, the process proceeds to step 8 (Final Micro-loan Board Approval).  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – The board votes **approve** or **reject**.

**Decision points in the Contoso Airlines refund process**

| # | Decision point (activity) | What is decided | Documents that must be available at that point |
|---|---------------------------|----------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| 1 | **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** | Whether the claim is *eligible* for a refund (based on flight‑record and weather conditions) | • *RefundRequestForm* (submitted by the passenger)  <br>• *FlightManifest*  <br>• *WeatherReport* |
| 2 | **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** | Whether the claim *passes* the finance eligibility checklist and can be pre‑approved | • *EligibilityChecklist* (filled out by the controller)  <br>• *RefundRequestForm* (for reference) |
| 3 | **High‑value branch decision** – “Is the refund amount > €1 000?” | Determines if the case must go to senior audit | • *RefundRequestForm* (contains the claimed amount) |
| 4 | **Senior_Auditor_Review** *(only for high‑value claims)* | Whether the high‑value claim is *approved* after audit | • *AuditTrail* (evidence of the audit)  <br>• *EligibilityChecklist* (already approved)  <br>• *RefundRequestForm* |
| 5 | **Process_Refund_Payment** | Whether the payment can be **executed** (all required approvals are in place) | • *BankTransferAuthorization* (signed by finance)  <br>• For high‑value claims: *AuditTrail* and the senior‑auditor’s approval must already be present |

**All required documents across the whole process**

1. *RefundRequestForm* – submitted by the passenger (initial artefact).  
2. *FlightManifest* – used by the triage agent.  
3. *WeatherReport* – used by the triage agent.  
4. *EligibilityChecklist* – completed by the finance controller.  
5. *AuditTrail* – generated when a claim exceeds €1 000 and is reviewed by the senior auditor.  
6. *BankTransferAuthorization* – signed by finance before payment is made.  
7. *RefundConfirmationPDF* – generated and emailed after payment (output artefact).  

No other activities, artefacts, or documents are part of the defined Contoso Airlines refund workflow.

# Acme Bank – Small-Business Loan-Approval Process Analysis

## a) Decision Points (Gates & Conditional Branches)

| # | Location | Decision Point | Rule (one sentence) |
|---|----------|----------------|---------------------|
| **1** | **Step 3 – Tri-Color-Risk-Check** | Risk-rating gate | The combined reading of Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, and Scorecard–Yellow yields a single risk rating of **Low, Medium, or High**. |
| **2** | **Step 4 → Step 5 (conditional branch)** | Underwriting-skip gate | Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review) is **skipped only when the risk is Low or Medium *and* the requested amount is ≤ €250,000**; otherwise the case must pass through Manual-Underwriting-Review. |
| **3** | **Step 6 – "TriVote" Decision Gate** | Approval gate | The gate is passed when **any two of the three required approvers** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant their approval. |

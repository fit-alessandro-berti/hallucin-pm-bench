Attach an interrupting Boundary Timer Event to `Task_Receive` and `Task_RequestDocs`. Set the timer duration to 48 hours relative to `Start_Claim`. If either task stalls and the timer fires, it cancels the current activity and routes the claim directly to `Task_Triage` via a new sequence flow. This forces the claim into triage exactly at the SLA deadline, effectively serving as the required auto-escalation without introducing any new tasks or subprocesses.

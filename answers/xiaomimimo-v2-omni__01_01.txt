**Decision Points in Contoso Airlines’ Refund Process:**

1. **Eligibility Validation** (by Triage Agent):  
   The agent checks the customer’s claim against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport* to determine if the refund request meets the basic criteria.

2. **Pre-Approval Decision** (by Finance Controller):  
   Using the *EligibilityChecklist*, the controller decides whether to pre-approve the refund request.

3. **High-Value Threshold Check**:  
   If the refund amount exceeds €1,000, the process must branch to **Senior_Auditor_Review** before payment.

---

**All Required Documents / Artefacts:**

1. **RefundRequestForm** (completed by the customer)  
2. **FlightManifest** (used for eligibility check)  
3. **WeatherReport** (used for eligibility check)  
4. **EligibilityChecklist** (used for finance pre-approval)  
5. **AuditTrail** (required only for high-value refunds > €1,000)  
6. **BankTransferAuthorization** (required to execute payment)  
7. **RefundConfirmationPDF** (emailed to the customer at closure)

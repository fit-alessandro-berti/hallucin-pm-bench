Based strictly on the provided Acme Bank SMALL-BUSINESS loan-approval process:

**a) Decision Points:**
1.  **Step 4 Conditional Branch:** A loan application is exempt from Manual Underwriting Review if its risk rating is Low or Medium *and* the requested amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Step 6 "TriVote" Gate:** The loan proposal proceeds to the next step if at least two of the three required stakeholders (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) formally approve it.

**b) Required Documents in Order of First Appearance:**
1.  **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive-Application)
2.  **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review – *only created if the Step 4 condition is not met*)
3.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)
4.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation – *requested alongside the signed Offer Sheet*)
5.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize-Agreement)

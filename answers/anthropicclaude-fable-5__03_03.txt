**Notes on the model:**
- **Completeness loop (step 2):** `wrc_gw_complete` routes incomplete claims to *requests missing information*, which flows back into *validates the claim* for re-validation (the wait for information is captured implicitly by the loop, as no separate wait event was mandated).
- **Warranty decision (step 3):** `wrc_gw_warranty` splits into *within warranty* (continue) and *out of warranty* (→ *send rejection notice* → `wrc_end_rejected`).
- **Parallel block (step 4):** `wrc_gw_parallel_split` / `wrc_gw_parallel_join` enclose *Create shipment order* and *Schedule courier pickup*.
- **Exactly two end events:** `wrc_end_rejected` and `wrc_end_completed`. No additional escalation or error paths were modeled.

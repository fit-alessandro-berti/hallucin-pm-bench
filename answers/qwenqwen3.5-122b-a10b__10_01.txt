# FinCo Order-to-Cash Workflow: RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager | Compliance Officer | Finance Clerk | IT Support |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Receive Application** | R/A | I | I | I |
| **KYC Review** | I | R/A | I | I |
| **Order Vetting** | R/A | I | I | I |
| **Payment Clearance** | I | I | R/A | I |
| **Record Update** | I | I | I | R/A |
| **Notify Customer** | R/A | I | I | I |

**Legend**
*   **R** = Responsible (Performs the task)
*   **A** = Accountable (Owns the decision/outcome)
*   **I** = Informed (Notified of completion)
*   **C** = Consulted (Provides input prior to action)

**Notes**
*   The **Account Manager** owns the customer relationship and final communication.
*   The **Compliance Officer** holds authority on risk assessment.
*   The **Finance Clerk** manages settlement actions.
*   **IT Support** maintains system integrity and data refreshes.

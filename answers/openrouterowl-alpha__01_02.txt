## Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track

| # | Step / Activity | Decision Rule | Outcome if TRUE | Outcome if FALSE |
|---|-----------------|--------------|----------------|-------------------|
| **5** | **Underwriter Alignment Gate** | |Score difference ≤ 2 points| → Continue to step **6** (Neighbourhood Feedback Check) | → Escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie‑break decision, then continue |
| **7** | **Amount Threshold Decision** | |Amount **< €15 000**| → System **auto‑approves** (skip step 8) | → Amount **≥ €15 000**: proceed to step **8** (Micro‑loan Board Approval) |
| **8** | **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)** | |Board vote = **Approve**| → Go to step **9** (Disburse Funds) | → Board vote = **Reject**: process ends (no disbursement) |

## Required Documents (to be submitted at time of application)

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
2. **Proof of Address** (dated ≤ 3 months old)
3. **Latest Income Statement** (single pay‑stub or tax slip)
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
5. **Dual Underwriter Score‑Sheet**
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

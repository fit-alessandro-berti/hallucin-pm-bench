
**TO:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**FROM:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**RE:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling – Priority Improvement Actions  

**Top 3 Underperforming Activities**  

1. **Request_Documents** – Extreme wait time (150 min) signals capacity/starvation bottleneck; 22 % rework indicates poor upfront guidance. *Action:* Assign dedicated document specialists and standardize request templates to cut queue and rework.  

2. **Review_Documents** – Longest processing time (25 min) and highest SLA breaches (12.4 %); high variability (σ=9) reflects inconsistent workload. *Action:* Deploy review checklist and parallel‐stream routing to lower cycle time and variability.  

3. **Initial_Assessment** – Second-highest SLA breach (3.2 %) plus 1.8 % rework points to assessment misjudgments. *Action:* Introduce decision-tree tool and targeted reassessor training to improve first-pass accuracy.

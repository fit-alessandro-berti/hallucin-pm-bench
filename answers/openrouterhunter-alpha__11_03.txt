**To:** Chief Operating Officer  
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**Subject:** Process Performance Analysis & Improvement Recommendations  

Based on the latest operational data, the three activities with the most severe combined performance issues are **Credit Assessment**, **Risk Review**, and **Legal Approval**. Each demonstrates significant breaches of their target Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and incurs high average waiting times, indicating process bottlenecks.  

*   **Credit Assessment:** Total cycle time is 500s (300s throughput + 200s waiting), exceeding its 240s SLA by **260s**. The waiting time constitutes 40% of its total time.  
*   **Risk Review:** Total cycle time is 1080s (600s throughput + 480s waiting), exceeding its 300s SLA by **780s**. It has the highest absolute waiting time.  
*   **Legal Approval:** Total cycle time is 1500s (900s throughput + 600s waiting), exceeding its 600s SLA by **900s**. The waiting time is equal to its throughput time.  

These delays directly impact case volume, as downstream activities see sharply reduced case counts. To address this:  

- **Credit Assessment:** Implement an automated, rules-based pre-qualification check to reduce manual assessment load by an estimated 40%.  
- **Risk Review:** Introduce a dedicated triage role to handle simple cases, reducing waiting time for the core review team.  
- **Legal Approval:** Develop a standardized clause library and approval checklist to decrease average throughput time for routine offers.  

Focusing on these high-impact activities will improve overall flow and SLA adherence.

### Part (a) Decision Point Summaries
There are two formal decision points in Acme’s small-business loan process, each defined below:
1. The post-Tri-Color-Risk-Check conditional branch skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review step if and only if the loan receives a Low or Medium risk rating and the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, requiring Manual-Underwriting-Review for all other cases (High risk, or any risk rating paired with a requested amount over €250,000).
2. The TriVote Decision-Gate passes a loan to the next step if at least two of the three mandatory voting stakeholders (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve the loan, as only a majority rejection from the trio results in a failed gate.

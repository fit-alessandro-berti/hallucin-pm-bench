**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** Chief Operating Officer
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst
**Re:** Priority Bottlenecks by SLA Breach and Waiting Time

Three activities jointly drive the worst performance through combined SLA breaches and excessive waiting. Legal Approval is most severe: throughput of 900s exceeds its 600s SLA by 300s, with 600s of waiting. Risk Review follows: throughput of 600s exceeds its 300s SLA by 300s, with 480s waiting. Credit Assessment ranks third: throughput of 300s exceeds its 240s SLA by 60s, with 200s waiting. Collectively these stages erode end-to-end cycle time and threaten downstream conversion.

Recommended actions:

- **Legal Approval:** Add reviewer capacity or parallelize sign-offs to cut the 600s waiting time and close the 300s breach.
- **Risk Review:** Triage the 480 cases by risk tier so straightforward files bypass full review, reducing the 480s wait and 300s breach.
- **Credit Assessment:** Automate scoring inputs to trim the 200s wait and recover the 60s overage.

<bpmn:boundaryEvent id="TimerBoundaryEvent" attachedToRef="Activity_VerifyID" cancelActivity="false">

Adding a boundary timer event to the "Verify Identity" task starts a countdown of 48 hours, and if not met, it transitions control to the existing "Account-Manager Review" task, effectively utilizing unused resources and ensuring compliance with the SLA.

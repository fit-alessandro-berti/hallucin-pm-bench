 MEMO
TO: Chief Operating Officer, Orion Credit
FROM: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
RE: Three Worst-Performing Process Activities

The activities that most exceed their SLAs while also carrying high waiting times are Risk Review, Legal Approval, and Credit Assessment. Risk Review averages 600 seconds of throughput against a 300-second SLA—300 seconds over target—with 480 seconds of waiting time. Legal Approval averages 900 seconds of throughput versus a 600-second SLA, also 300 seconds over target, with 600 seconds of waiting time. Credit Assessment averages 300 seconds of throughput against a 240-second SLA, 60 seconds above target, with 200 seconds of waiting time.

Recommendations:

- Risk Review: Reallocate reviewer capacity or introduce a low-risk fast track so the 480-second wait no longer pushes throughput beyond the 300-second SLA.

- Legal Approval: Implement pre-approved legal templates and parallel review to reduce the 600-second wait and meet the 600-second SLA.

- Credit Assessment: Automate eligibility scoring for straightforward cases to cut the 200-second wait and bring throughput inside the 240-second SLA.

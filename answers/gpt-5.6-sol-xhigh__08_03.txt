**Yes.** The shortcut transition **T2b** produces `pd` without requiring **T3**.

One shortest firing sequence is:

\[
\boxed{T1,\ T2b,\ T4,\ T5}
\]

Markings along the sequence:

1. Initially: `{p0}`
2. After **T1**: `{pa, pb}`
3. After **T2b**: `{pd, pb}`
4. After **T4**: `{pd, pe}` — **T5** is enabled
5. Fire **T5**: `{pf}`

Thus **T5** fires with no prior firing of **T3**. This sequence is shortest because enabling **T5** requires both `pd` and `pe`; before any **T3**, these require **T2b** and **T4**, respectively, and both depend on **T1**.

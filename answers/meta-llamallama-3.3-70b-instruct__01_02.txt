The decision points in Acme's Micro-loan Fast-Track process are:

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Decide whether to continue or escalate to the Harmonisation Committee based on the difference between the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**: Determine whether to auto-approve or proceed to the Micro-loan Board Approval based on the loan amount.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: Decide to approve or reject the loan application.

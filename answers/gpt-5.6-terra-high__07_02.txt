### Added tasks
- **Verify Identity** (`Task_VerifyIdentity`) added before **Review Application**.
- **Request Additional Documents** (`Task_RequestDocs`) added as an eligibility outcome.

### Removed tasks
- **Reject Loan** (`Task_RejectLoan`) removed.
- **Final Reject** is a newly introduced task rather than a cosmetic rename of **Reject Loan**, because it has a different BPMN ID and revised process routing.

### Gateway-type changes (e.g., Exclusive → Inclusive)
- **Gateway_Decision** changed from an **Exclusive Gateway** to an **Inclusive Gateway**.

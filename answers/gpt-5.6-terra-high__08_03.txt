One shortest firing sequence is:

\[
T1,\; T2b,\; T4,\; T5
\]

Marking progression:

- Initial: \(p0=1\)
- After \(T1\): \(pa=1,\ pb=1\)
- After \(T2b\): \(pb=1,\ pd=1\)
- After \(T4\): \(pd=1,\ pe=1\)
- \(T5\) is enabled and fires, producing \(pf=1\)

Transition \(T3\) has not fired. The shortcut \(T2b\) provides the required token in \(pd\) without first producing a token in \(pc\), so \(T5\) can occur before \(T3\).

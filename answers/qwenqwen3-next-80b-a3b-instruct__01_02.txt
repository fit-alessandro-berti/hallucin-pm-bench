**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   – Decision: If the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by **≤ 2 points**, proceed.  
   – Else: Escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break; then proceed.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   – If loan amount is **< €15 000**: Auto-approve.  
   – If loan amount is **≥ €15 000**: Proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   – Board votes: **Approve** or **Reject**. Only applicable for amounts ≥ €15 000.

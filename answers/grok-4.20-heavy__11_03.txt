The process performance data identifies Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment as the three worst-performing activities based jointly on SLA breaches and high waiting time. Legal Approval records an average throughput time of 900 seconds against a target SLA of 600 seconds, exceeding by 300 seconds, with an average waiting time of 600 seconds. Risk Review shows an average throughput time of 600 seconds exceeding its 300-second SLA by 300 seconds and an average waiting time of 480 seconds. Credit Assessment has an average throughput time of 300 seconds exceeding its 240-second SLA by 60 seconds with an average waiting time of 200 seconds. To address these issues the following actions are recommended:
- Reduce the average waiting time of 600 seconds in Legal Approval to align throughput time with the 600-second SLA.
- Reduce the average waiting time of 480 seconds in Risk Review to align throughput time with the 300-second SLA.
- Reduce the average waiting time of 200 seconds in Credit Assessment to align throughput time with the 240-second SLA.

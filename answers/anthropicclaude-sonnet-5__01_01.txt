# Decision Points and Required Documents in Contoso Airlines' Refund Process

## Decision Points

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - Decision: Whether the claim is eligible for a refund, based on the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**
   - Decision: Whether to sign off on the claim using the *EligibilityChecklist*.

3. **High-value branch check** (implicit decision point after Finance_Controller_PreApprove)
   - Decision: Whether the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**.
     - If **yes** → route to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (requiring the *AuditTrail*).
     - If **no** → proceed directly toward **Process_Refund_Payment**.

4. **Senior_Auditor_Review** (only for high-value claims)
   - Decision: Whether the high-value claim passes audit review, based on the *AuditTrail*.

5. **Process_Refund_Payment readiness check**
   - Decision: Whether payment can begin — requires that **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded **and**, for high-value claims, that **Senior_Auditor_Review** has been completed, **and** the *BankTransferAuthorization* is signed and present.

## Required Documents/Artefacts

- **RefundRequestForm** — completed by the customer at claim submission.
- **FlightManifest** — used by the triage agent to validate eligibility.
- **WeatherReport** — used by the triage agent to validate eligibility.
- **EligibilityChecklist** — used by the finance controller for pre-approval.
- **AuditTrail** — required for Senior_Auditor_Review (high-value claims only).
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — signed authorization required before payment execution.
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger as confirmation.

**Note:** No activities or artefacts beyond those defined in the process description have been introduced. The legacy "CustomerService_Agent_Decide" activity is confirmed **not** part of this process.

Here is the RACI matrix using only the authorized vocabulary:

| Activity | Front-Office Analyst | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | Treasury Ops |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Intake Vetting | A/R | I | — | — |
| KYC Review | I | A/R | C | — |
| AML Screening | I | A/R | C | — |
| Risk Scoring | I | A/R | C | — |
| Approval (score > 70 escalation) | I | R | A | I |
| Schedule payment | I | — | C | A/R |
| Release funds & notify originating analyst | I | — | I | A/R |

**Legend:** R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed.

**Notes**
- **Intake Vetting** is owned end-to-end by the Front-Office Analyst.
- **KYC Review**, **AML Screening**, and **Risk Scoring** are executed by the Compliance Analyst, with the Compliance Officer available as Consulted given downstream escalation authority.
- The **Approval** activity is triggered only when **Risk Scoring** exceeds 70; the Compliance Officer is Accountable, while the Compliance Analyst remains Responsible for presenting the case.
- **Schedule payment** and **Release funds & notify originating analyst** are owned by Treasury Ops, with the originating Front-Office Analyst kept Informed.

**Decision Points**
• **High-value threshold check:** Evaluating whether the refund amount is greater than €1 000. If true, the process routes to the *Senior_Auditor_Review* activity; if false, this step is bypassed. 

*(Note: The preconditions for Process_Refund_Payment act as a synchronization constraint/AND-join rather than a routing decision point.)*

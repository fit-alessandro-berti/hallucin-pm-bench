## Answer: **No**

The FACT is **not accurate**. While "Validate_Payment" follows "Receive_Order" in some cases, it does **not always** occur *immediately* after it with no intervening activities.

### Justification

| CaseID | Activity Sequence | Contradicts FACT? |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **C001** | Receive_Order → Validate_Payment → Pack_Items | No — Validate_Payment is immediately after Receive_Order ✓ |
| **C002** | Receive_Order → **Pack_Items** → Validate_Payment | **Yes** — Pack_Items occurs between Receive_Order and Validate_Payment ✗ |

## Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track

There are **three decision points** in the process:

---

### 1. Underwriter Alignment Gate *(Step 5)*
| Condition | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by **≤ 2 points** | Proceed directly to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check** |
| Scores differ by **> 2 points** | Escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break, then continue |

---

### 2. Amount Threshold Decision *(Step 7)*
| Condition | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Loan amount **< €15 000** | System **auto-approves** |
| Loan amount **≥ €15 000** | Proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval** |

---

### 3. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) *(Step 8)*
| Condition | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Board votes **approve** | Proceed to **Disburse Funds** |
| Board votes **reject** | Process ends; funds not disbursed |

---

## Required Documents at Submission

1. **Digital Application Form** (ML-APP-01)
2. **Proof of Address** (no older than 3 months)
3. **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS)** template
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

> ⚠️ Note: No credit-bureau documentation is required — credit history checks are explicitly skipped at **Quick KYC Verification**.

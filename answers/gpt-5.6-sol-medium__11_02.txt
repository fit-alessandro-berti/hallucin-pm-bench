**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance

The three weakest activities, based on combined waiting time, processing performance, rework, and SLA breaches, are:

1. **Request_Documents** — It has by far the longest wait (150 minutes), the highest rework rate (22.0%), and throughput falls to 190 cases/day versus 270 at the preceding activities. Its 5.5% SLA-breach rate is also elevated.  
   **Action:** Introduce a mandatory document-completeness check before submission, targeting rework below 10% and tracking whether throughput improves.

2. **Review_Documents** — It has the longest processing time (25 minutes), greatest processing variability (9 minutes), highest SLA-breach rate (12.4%), and second-highest rework rate (5.3%).  
   **Action:** Standardize reviews with a checklist and exception-routing rules, targeting a reduction in both the 9-minute variability and 12.4% breach rate.

3. **Initial_Assessment** — Its 18-minute wait, 12-minute processing time, 3.2% SLA-breach rate, and 1.8% rework rate are materially worse than most remaining activities.  
   **Action:** Align staffing schedules to measured arrival volumes, with an initial target of reducing average waiting time below 10 minutes.

These three are clearly differentiated from the other activities across multiple indicators.

**a) Decision points (gates or conditional branches) — one sentence each:**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome** — Determines risk rating (Low, Medium, High) based on three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow), which governs whether Manual-Underwriting-Review is triggered.  
2. **Conditional Skip of Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review)** — Skips Manual-Underwriting-Review *only* if risk rating is Low or Medium *and* the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.  
3. **TriVote Decision Gate** — Requires at least two approvals out of three (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) to proceed to binding offer preparation.  

—

**b) All required documents — in order of first appearance:**

1. **Form 14B** — Submitted with initial application (Step 1).  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** — Input to Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3).  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** — Input to Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3).  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** — Input to Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3).  
5. **Deck Memo** — Produced during Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5; only if triggered).  
6. **Offer Sheet 77** — Prepared upon passing TriVote (Step 7), then signed by applicant (Step 8).  
7. **ID Matrix Declaration** — Submitted by applicant alongside signed Offer Sheet 77 (Step 8).  
8. **Loan Dossier 2025** — Final document package upon agreement finalization (Step 9).  

*(Note: KYC/AML documents are explicitly excluded — handled externally.)*

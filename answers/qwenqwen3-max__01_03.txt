**a) Decision Points (Gates or Conditional Branches)**  

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check outcome**: Determines the risk rating (Low, Medium, or High) based on the three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow), which influences whether Manual-Underwriting-Review is required.  
2. **Conditional skip of Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 4)**: If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, the application bypasses Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).  
3. **TriVote Decision-Gate (Step 6)**: Requires at least two approvals out of three mandatory roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) for the loan to proceed.  

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**  

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**  
5. **Deck Memo**  
6. **Offer Sheet 77**  
7. **ID Matrix Declaration**  
8. **Loan Dossier 2025**

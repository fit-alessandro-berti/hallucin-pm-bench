Based on the process description provided for Acme Bank’s small-business loan-approval workflow, here is the summary of decision points and the chronological list of documents.

### a) Decision Points
1.  **Manual Review Bypass (Branch):** The Manual-Underwriting-Review is skipped only if the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount does not exceed €250,000.
2.  **TriVote Decision-Gate (Gate):** A loan application is approved to proceed if at least two of the three mandatory voters (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) grant their approval.

### b) Required Documents (In order of first appearance)
1.  **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive-Application)
2.  **Scorecard–Cyan** (Step 3: Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
3.  **Scorecard–Magenta** (Step 3: Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
4.  **Scorecard–Yellow** (Step 3: Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
5.  **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review)
6.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)
7.  **Signed Offer Sheet 77** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
8.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
9.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize-Agreement)



# AtlasRemit — Cross-Border Payment Process: RACI Matrix

## Roles

| Abbreviation | Role |
|:---:|---|
| **TA** | Transaction Analyst |
| **RL** | Regulatory Liaison |
| **IT** | IT Support |

## RACI Table

| # | Task | Transaction Analyst (TA) | Regulatory Liaison (RL) | IT Support (IT) |
|:-:|------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 1 | Receive customer transfer instruction and log it in the system | **R / A** | I | — |
| 2 | Screen payment against the sanctions list | **R / A** | C | — |
| 3 | Perform **KYC Review** on sender and beneficiary | C | **R / A** | — |
| 4 | Approve high-risk flagged transaction | I | **R / A** | — |
| 5 | Release the approved payment | **R** | **A** | I |
| 6 | Send SWIFT message | I | — | **R / A** |
| 7 | Archive the case file | I | I | **R / A** |

> **R** = Responsible · **A** = Accountable · **C** = Consulted · **I** = Informed · **—** = No involvement

## Key to Assignments

1. **Receive & log instruction** — The **Transaction Analyst** both executes and owns this step; the **Regulatory Liaison** is informed that a new transfer has entered the pipeline.
2. **Sanctions screening** — The **Transaction Analyst** runs the screening; the **Regulatory Liaison** is consulted if a potential match is found.
3. **KYC Review** — The **Regulatory Liaison** conducts and owns the full **KYC Review** on both sender and beneficiary; the **Transaction Analyst** is consulted for supporting transaction details.
4. **High-risk approval** — The **Regulatory Liaison** must formally approve any transaction flagged as high-risk; the **Transaction Analyst** is informed of the decision to proceed or halt.
5. **Release payment** — The **Transaction Analyst** executes the release; the **Regulatory Liaison** remains accountable (having granted approval); IT Support is informed so it can prepare the outbound message.
6. **Send SWIFT message** — **IT Support** is responsible for generating and transmitting the SWIFT message and owns delivery confirmation.
7. **Archive case file** — **IT Support** archives all documentation; both the **Transaction Analyst** and **Regulatory Liaison** are informed once archiving is complete.

**Decision Points** in Contoso Airlines’ refund process:

1. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** — decision made by the finance controller based on the *EligibilityChecklist* (whether the claim is pre-approved).
2. **High-value decision** — decision if the refund amount exceeds €1 000 (whether the claim requires **Senior_Auditor_Review**).

**Required Documents** for the process:

- *RefundRequestForm*  
- *FlightManifest*  
- *WeatherReport*  
- *EligibilityChecklist*  
- *BankTransferAuthorization*  
- *RefundConfirmationPDF*  
- *AuditTrail* (only for high-value claims, during **Senior_Auditor_Review**)

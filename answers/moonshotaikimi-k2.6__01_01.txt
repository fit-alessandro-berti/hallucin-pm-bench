 **Decision points**

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** – the triage agent decides whether the claim is valid by checking it against the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.
2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** – the finance controller decides whether to sign off (pre-approve) on the claim using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
3. **Refund-value threshold (€ 1 000)** – the process evaluates whether the refund amount exceeds € 1 000; this branching decision dictates whether **Senior_Auditor_Review** is required before payment can proceed.

**Required documents / artefacts**

- *RefundRequestForm*
- *FlightManifest*
- *WeatherReport*
- *EligibilityChecklist*
- *AuditTrail* (required for **Senior_Auditor_Review** in high-value cases)
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (must be signed before **Process_Refund_Payment**)
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* (produced in **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**)

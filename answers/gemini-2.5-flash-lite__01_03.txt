Here are the answers to your questions about Acme Bank's SMALL-BUSINESS loan-approval process:

**a) Decision Points:**

*   **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** The outcome of this check determines the overall risk rating of the loan application as Low, Medium, or High.
*   **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review if the risk rating is Low or Medium AND the requested amount is €250,000 or less.
*   **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6):** The loan passes this gate if at least two out of the three required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) provide their approval.

**b) Documents Required (in order of first appearance):**

1.  Form 14B
2.  Deck Memo
3.  Offer Sheet 77
4.  ID Matrix Declaration
5.  Loan Dossier 2025

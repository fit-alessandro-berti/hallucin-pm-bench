# AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process — RACI Matrix

## Process Overview

This RACI matrix defines roles and responsibilities for the cross-border payment processing workflow at AtlasRemit. All activities use the mandated vocabulary as specified in the AtlasRemit Vocabulary Mandate.

---

## RACI Matrix

| **Task** | **Transaction Analyst** | **Regulatory Liaison** | **IT Support** |
|----------|:----------------------:|:----------------------:|:--------------:|
| Receive and log customer's transfer instruction | R | — | — |
| Screen payment against sanctions list | R | C | — |
| Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary | — | R | — |
| Approve high-risk flagged transactions | A | R | — |
| Release approved payment | R | I | — |
| Ensure SWIFT message is sent | — | — | R |
| Archive case file | — | I | R |
| Notify Regulatory Liaison upon completion | R | I | — |

---

## Role Definitions

**Transaction Analyst**: Responsible for receiving transfer instructions, logging payments in the system, conducting sanctions screening, releasing approved payments, and initiating notifications upon workflow completion.

**Regulatory Liaison**: Accountable for high-risk transaction approvals, performs all KYC Review activities, and must be informed of payment releases and case completion. Provides consultation during sanctions screening when complex determinations are required.

**IT Support**: Responsible for ensuring SWIFT message transmission and archiving case files. This role operates under the direction of approved payments and maintains technical systems supporting the payment workflow.

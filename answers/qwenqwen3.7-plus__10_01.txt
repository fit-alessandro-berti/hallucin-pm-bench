| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |

**RACI Legend:**
*   **R** = Responsible (performs the work)
*   **A** = Accountable (owns the outcome; for these discrete tasks, the Responsible party holds accountability)
*   **C** = Consulted (provides input prior to completion)
*   **I** = Informed (notified after completion)
*   **–** = Not involved

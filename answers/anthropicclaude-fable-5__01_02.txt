# Acme Micro-loan Fast-Track: Decision Points & Required Documents

## Decision Points

**1. Underwriter Alignment Gate (Step 5)**
- Compares the two independent risk scores (0–9) from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**:
  - If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → continue to the **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
  - If the scores differ by **more than 2 points** → escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue.

**2. Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7)**
- Based on the requested loan amount:
  - **Amount < €15 000** → the system **auto-approves**.
  - **Amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

**3. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (Step 8)**
- Applies only to amounts **≥ €15 000**:
  - The board votes **approve** → proceed to **Disburse Funds**.
  - The board votes **reject** → the loan is not disbursed.

## Required Documents (at submission time)

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
2. **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
3. **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

---

*Note: Consistent with the Fast-Track variant, there is **no credit-bureau pull** anywhere in the process — the **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)** confirms only the ID card and mobile phone, skipping all credit history checks.*

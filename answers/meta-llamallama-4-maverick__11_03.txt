To: COO, Orion Credit

The current process performance data indicates that certain activities are underperforming based on their SLA breach and waiting times. 
The three worst-performing activities, based jointly on SLA breaches and high waiting times, are Legal Approval, Risk Review, and Credit Assessment. 
Legal Approval exceeds its SLA by 300 seconds (900 - 600), Risk Review by 300 seconds (600 - 300), and Credit Assessment by 60 seconds (300 - 240). 
To address these issues, we recommend: 
* Assigning additional resources to Legal Approval to reduce its average throughput time.
* Streamlining the Risk Review process to eliminate unnecessary steps and reduce waiting times.
* Implementing a more efficient credit scoring model for Credit Assessment to reduce its average throughput time. 
These actions will help mitigate SLA breaches and improve overall process efficiency. 
The data supports these targeted interventions to enhance performance.

 **a) Decision points in Acme’s small-business loan process**

1. **Underwriting bypass rule (step 4):**  
   A loan skips Manual-Underwriting-Review only if the Tri-Color risk rating is **Low or Medium** **and** the requested amount is **≤ €250 000**; otherwise step 5 is required.

2. **TriVote decision gate (step 6):**  
   The loan passes if it receives **at least two approvals out of three** from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer.

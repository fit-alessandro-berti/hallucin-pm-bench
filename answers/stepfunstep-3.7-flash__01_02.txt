### Decision Points (using exact prescribed Acme Micro-loan Fast-Track activity names)
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Compares the independent 0–9 risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the score difference is 2 points or less, the process proceeds to the next step; if the difference exceeds 2 points, the case is escalated to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision before proceeding.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision**: Evaluates the requested loan amount. If the amount is less than €15,000, the system auto-approves the application; if the amount is €15,000 or higher, the process proceeds to Final Micro-loan Board Approval.
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The Micro-loan Board votes to either approve or reject applications for amounts equal to or above €15,000.

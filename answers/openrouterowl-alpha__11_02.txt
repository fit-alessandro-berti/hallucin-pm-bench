**Subject: Q1-2025 Claims Process Performance – Critical Bottlenecks & Immediate Actions**

**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**Date:** April 5, 2025

**Three Worst-Performing Activities:**

1. **Request Documents**  
   *Why:* 150-minute average wait time (highest), driven by 22% rework rate—nearly one in five cases repeats this step due to missing/incomplete submissions.  
   *Action:* Implement a structured digital checklist pre-upload to reduce rework; target rework rate cut to <10%.

2. **Review Documents**  
   *Why:* 12.4% SLA breach rate—highest in process—amplified by high variability (9-min stdev), indicating inconsistent reviewer practices.  
   *Action:* Standardize review criteria via decision-support tool and calibration training; aim to halve stdev and SLA breaches.

3. **Initial Assessment**  
   *Why:* 27% throughput drop here (270 → 190 in next step), with 18-minute wait and 3.2% SLA breaches suggesting capacity or prioritization issues.  
   *Action:* Introduce automated triage to prioritize low-complexity claims; reduce wait to <15 min and align with downstream capacity.

These data-driven fixes target root causes: rework, inconsistency, and misalignment—not just symptoms.

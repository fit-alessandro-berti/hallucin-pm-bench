# Order-to-Cash Workflow: RACI Matrix

## RACI Key
| Code | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **R** | Responsible – performs the work |
| **A** | Accountable – owns the outcome |
| **C** | Consulted – provides input |
| **I** | Informed – needs notification |

---

## RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|----------|:--------------------:|:-----------------------:|:------------------:|:---------------:|
| Receive Application | R, A | – | – | – |
| KYC Review | I | R, A | – | – |
| Order Vetting | R, A | C | – | – |
| Payment Clearance | I | – | R, A | – |
| Record Update | C | – | C | R, A |
| Notify Customer | R, A | – | – | – |

---

## Summary of Responsibilities

**Account Manager (AM):** Leads the customer-facing activities—receiving applications, vetting orders, and sending final notifications. Also serves as a consulted party for CRM updates.

**Compliance Officer (CO):** Owns the KYC Review process and provides compliance expertise during Order Vetting. Remains informed of application receipts.

**Finance Clerk (FC):** Owns Payment Clearance and contributes payment expertise during Record Update. Remains informed of vetted orders and notifications.

**IT Support (IT):** Owns Record Update activities, ensuring the CRM reflects all process outcomes.

**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling performance — key bottlenecks

The three worst-performing activities are:

1. **Request_Documents** — Clear bottleneck: highest wait time (**150 min**), very high rework (**22.0%**), and elevated SLA breaches (**5.5%**), despite only **5 min** average processing.  
   **Action:** Reduce queueing by automating document requests/reminders and adding intake capacity or SLA prioritization.

2. **Review_Documents** — Weak execution quality: longest processing time (**25 min**), highest variability (**9 min stdev**), and high SLA breaches (**12.4%**). Rework is also material at **5.3%**.  
   **Action:** Standardize review rules/checklists and target training or case pre-screening to cut variation and rework.

3. **Initial_Assessment** — Underperforms next most due to relatively high wait time (**18 min**) and non-trivial SLA breaches (**3.2%**), with moderate processing time (**12 min**) and rework (**1.8%**).  
   **Action:** Rebalance staffing or automate triage to reduce queue time and improve SLA adherence.

Other activities are materially better on these measures.

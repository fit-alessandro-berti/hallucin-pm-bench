**Decision points (using the exact activity names)**  

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – compare the two risk scores (Senior Underwriter A vs. Shadow Underwriter B).  
2. **Harmonisation Committee** – invoked only when the score difference is > 2 points; the committee makes the tie‑break decision.  
3. **Amount Threshold Decision** – determines whether the application is auto‑approved (amount < €15 000) or must proceed to the board.  
4. **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)** – the board votes *approve* or *reject* for amounts ≥ €15 000.

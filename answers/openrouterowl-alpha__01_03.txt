a) Decision points in Acme Bank’s small‑business loan‑approval process (each described in one sentence):

1. **Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check**: Computes a risk rating (Low, Medium, or High) from three proprietary scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow).  
2. **Conditional skip of Manual‑Underwriting‑Review**: If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested loan amount is at most €250 000, the Manual‑Underwriting‑Review is skipped; otherwise (i.e., risk = High **or** amount > €250 000), it must be performed.  
3. **TriVote Decision‑Gate**: The loan proceeds only if at least two out of three designated officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve the deal.

b) Required documents (in order of first appearance in the process):

- **Form 14B**  
- **Deck Memo**  
- **Offer Sheet 77**  
- **ID Matrix Declaration**  
- **Loan Dossier 2025**

**a)** Decision points in Acme’s process:

- After the Tri-Color-Risk-Check produces a risk rating, the process branches: if the rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5) is skipped and the application proceeds directly to the TriVote gate.
- At the TriVote Decision-Gate (step 6), the application passes if at least two of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) give approval; otherwise it does not proceed.

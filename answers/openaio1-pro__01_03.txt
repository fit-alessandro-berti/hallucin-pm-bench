a) Decision Points (one sentence each):  
• Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3): Determines if the application’s risk rating is Low, Medium, or High based on three scorecards.  
• Conditional Branch Before Underwriting (Step 4): If the risk is Low or Medium and the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the process skips the Manual Underwriting Review (Step 5).  
• “TriVote” Decision Gate (Step 6): Requires approvals from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, and passes if at least two out of the three vote “yes.”

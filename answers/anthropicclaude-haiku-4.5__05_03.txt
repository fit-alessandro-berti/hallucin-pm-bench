# SLA Compliance Improvement

**Changed/Added XML Elements:**

```xml
<bpmn:boundaryEvent id="BoundaryEvent_SLATimer" name="48h SLA Timer" attachedToRef="Activity_CreateAccount" cancelActivity="false">
  <bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
    <bpmn:timeDuration>PT48H</bpmn:timeDuration>
  </bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
</bpmn:boundaryEvent>

<bpmn:sequenceFlow id="Flow_SLAEscalation" sourceRef="BoundaryEvent_SLATimer" targetRef="Activity_AMReview"/>
```

**Justification:**  
Attach a non-interrupting 48-hour boundary timer to "Create Account in Core System" that automatically escalates incomplete accounts to the existing (currently unused) Account-Manager Review activity, ensuring SLA breach visibility and remediation without introducing new workflow elements.

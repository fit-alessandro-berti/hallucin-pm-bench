**Decision Points**

1. **Eligibility Validation** — During *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility*, the triage agent must decide if the claim is valid by checking it against the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.
2. **Pre-Approval Sign-Off** — During *Finance_Controller_PreApprove*, the finance controller must decide whether to sign off on the refund using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
3. **High-Value Check** — The process evaluates whether the refund amount is greater than €1,000. If it is, the claim is routed to *Senior_Auditor_Review*; if not, it proceeds directly to payment.

**Required Documents**

*   **RefundRequestForm** (completed by the customer at submission)
*   **FlightManifest** (used for eligibility validation)
*   **WeatherReport** (used for eligibility validation)
*   **EligibilityChecklist** (used for finance controller pre-approval)
*   **AuditTrail** (required for the *Senior_Auditor_Review* on high-value claims)
*   **BankTransferAuthorization** (must be signed and present before payment processing)
*   **RefundConfirmationPDF** (emailed to the passenger at the end of the process)

Based on the process description provided for Contoso Airlines, here is the summary of decision points and the list of required documents:

### **Decision Points**
*   **Eligibility Check:** Performed by the **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** activity, where the agent determines if the claim qualifies for a refund by cross-referencing the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.
*   **High-Value Threshold:** A conditional branch triggered if the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**. Claims exceeding this amount are routed to the **Senior_Auditor_Review** activity. 

### **Required Documents**
*   **RefundRequestForm:** Completed by the passenger during the submission phase.
*   **FlightManifest:** Used to validate eligibility.
*   **WeatherReport:** Used to validate eligibility.
*   **EligibilityChecklist:** Used by the finance controller for pre-approval.
*   **AuditTrail:** Required specifically for high-value claims during the senior auditor's review.
*   **BankTransferAuthorization:** A signed document required before the finance team can execute the payment.
*   **RefundConfirmationPDF:** The final document emailed to the passenger upon completion.

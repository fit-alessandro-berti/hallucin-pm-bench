**Added tasks**
* **Verify Identity** (`Task_VerifyIdentity`): Added at the start of the process, executing before the application review.
* **Request Additional Documents** (`Task_RequestDocs`): Added as a new outgoing branch from the decision gateway.
* **Final Reject** (`Task_FinalReject`): Added as a new terminal task node for the rejection path.

**Removed tasks**
* **Reject Loan** (`Task_RejectLoan`): Removed from the process (replaced by the newly added `Task_FinalReject` node).

**Gateway-type changes**
* **Gateway_Decision**: Changed from an **Exclusive Gateway** (`<exclusiveGateway>`) in v1 to an **Inclusive Gateway** (`<inclusiveGateway>`) in v2. 

*(Note: Name changes to existing nodes, such as "Approve Loan" to "Auto-Approve Loan" and "Eligible?" to "Eligibility Check", were ignored as cosmetic renamings per the instructions.)*

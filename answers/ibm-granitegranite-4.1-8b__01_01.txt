**Decision Points in Contoso Airlines’ Flight‑Refund Process**

| # | Decision Point | Description |
|---|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Eligibility Check (Triage_Validation)** | The triage agent determines whether the RefundRequestForm is eligible by cross‑referencing the FlightManifest and the WeatherReport. |
| 2 | **Eligibility Approval (Finance_PreApprove)** | The finance controller reviews the EligibilityChecklist and decides whether to pre‑approve the claim. |
| 3 | **High‑Value Claim Branch** | Conditional routing: if the refund amount exceeds €1 000, the claim proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review**; otherwise it proceeds directly to Refund Payment. |

**Required Documents for Each Step**

| Document | Used In Which Activity |
|----------|-----------------------|
| **RefundRequestForm** | Customer_Validate Claim |
| **FlightManifest** | Triage_Validate_Eligibility |
| **WeatherReport** | Triage_Validate_Eligibility |
| **EligibilityChecklist** | Finance_PreApprove |
| **AuditTrail** | (Only for high‑value claims) Senior_Auditor_Review |
| **BankTransferAuthorization** | Process_Refund_Payment |
| **RefundConfirmationPDF** | Issue_Confirmation_Letter |

These are the sole decision points and artefacts specified in the defined process.

### Decision Points

Based on the Acme Bank Micro-loan Fast-Track process, the explicit decision points are:

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Evaluates the risk scores (0–9) independently assigned by **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**. 
    *   If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, the process continues to the Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC). 
    *   If the scores differ by more than 2 points, the application is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before continuing.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision**: Evaluates the requested amount. 
    *   If the **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves and proceeds to disburse funds. 
    *   If the **amount ≥ €15 000**, the process proceeds to the Micro-loan Board.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: Required only when the amount is **≥ €15 000**. The board evaluates the application and votes to either *approve* or *reject*.

### Required Documents

The following documents must be provided at submission time:

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

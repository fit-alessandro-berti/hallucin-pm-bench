## RACI Matrix — FinCo Order-to-Cash

| # | Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|---|----------|----------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Receive Application | **R / A** | I | I | — |
| 2 | KYC Review | C | **R / A** | I | I |
| 3 | Order Vetting | **R / A** | C | I | — |
| 4 | Payment Clearance | C | I | **R / A** | I |
| 5 | Record Update | C | I | I | **R / A** |
| 6 | Notify Customer | **R / A** | I | I | I |

**Legend:** R = Responsible (does the work) · A = Accountable (owns the outcome) · C = Consulted · I = Informed · — = No involvement

### Notes on the assignments

1. **Receive Application** — AM owns intake and is the single point of contact; CO and FC are informed so they can anticipate downstream workload.
2. **KYC Review** — CO holds sole accountability to preserve independence of the control; AM is consulted for customer context and document chasing.
3. **Order Vetting** — AM verifies data completeness; CO is consulted where completeness gaps touch on regulatory evidence.
4. **Payment Clearance** — FC initiates and owns settlement; AM is consulted on commercial terms or exceptions.
5. **Record Update** — IT refreshes the CRM; AM is consulted to confirm the customer record reflects the agreed commercial position.
6. **Notify Customer** — AM issues confirmation; all other roles are informed that the case is closed.

### Design principles applied

- Exactly one **A** per activity — no shared accountability, so escalation paths stay unambiguous.
- **A** and **R** are co-located on the same role in every step, which suits a lean four-role team and avoids hand-off overhead.
- Segregation of duties is preserved between KYC Review (CO), Order Vetting (AM), and Payment Clearance (FC) — no single role controls a customer from onboarding through settlement.

### Suggested follow-ups

- Confirm whether a second **A** is needed above FC for Payment Clearance above a defined value threshold (e.g. a Finance Manager sign-off tier).
- Clarify whether CO should be **C** rather than **I** on Record Update, if CRM fields carry regulatory evidence.
- Agree the SLA clock start/stop points per activity so the matrix can be paired with cycle-time targets.


# RACI Table

| Archive case file | IT Support | Regulatory Liaison | - | - |
| Notify compliance officer | IT Support | Regulatory Liaison | - | Regulatory Liaison |

**Explanation of RACI Elements:**  
- **Responsible**: The role directly performing the task (using mandated terms: `Transaction Analyst` for payment/clerk roles, `Regulatory Liaison` for compliance roles).  
- **Accountable**: The role owning the task (typically the same as Responsible, unless specified for accountability).  
- **Consulted**: The role consulted by the Responsible role before performing the task (using mandated terms; e.g., `Regulatory Liaison` provides input on KYC Review/approval).  
- **Informed**: The role notified of the task result (using mandated terms; e.g., `Regulatory Liaison` is informed of compliance notifications).

**To:** COO, Orion Insurance  
**Subject:** Top waiting-time bottlenecks (last 12 months)

The three worst-performing activities by **waiting time** are:

**#1 Request_Documents** — **Avg wait: 36.5h; 95th percentile: 120.0h**  
**Action:** Launch an automated document chase at 24h and 72h, plus a mandatory missing-item checklist in the request. Given the extreme tail (120.0h), reducing incomplete and unresponded requests should cut waiting time by **20%+**.

**#2 Send_Closure_Letter** — **Avg wait: 12.2h; 95th percentile: 44.0h**  
**Action:** Move closure letters to straight-through generation at decision completion, with a queue SLA trigger at 8h. This should remove batching delays and reduce waiting time by **at least 20%**.

**#3 Assess_Liability** — **Avg wait: 7.8h; 95th percentile: 16.4h**  
**Action:** Introduce priority routing for claims already holding complete documentation and auto-assign from a live capacity queue. Faster assignment should deliver a **20%+** waiting-time reduction.

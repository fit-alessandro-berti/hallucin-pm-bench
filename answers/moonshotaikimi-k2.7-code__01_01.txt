 **Decision points**

1. **Claim-eligibility decision** — *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility*  
   - Compares the *RefundRequestForm* against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.  
   - Outcome: the claim is either eligible (proceed) or not eligible.

2. **Pre-approval decision** — *Finance_Controller_PreApprove*  
   - Uses the *EligibilityChecklist*.  
   - Outcome: the refund is either pre-approved (proceed) or not pre-approved.

3. **High-value routing decision** — based on refund amount  
   - Checks whether the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**.  
   - Outcome: if yes, *Senior_Auditor_Review* is required (with the *AuditTrail*); if no, the case proceeds directly to payment once pre-approved.

4. **Payment-start condition** — for *Process_Refund_Payment*  
   - Starts only after *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* has succeeded, any required *Senior_Auditor_Review* is complete, and the signed *BankTransferAuthorization* is present.  
   - Outcome: payment is executed or remains blocked until all conditions are met.

**Required documents / artefacts**

- *RefundRequestForm*
- *FlightManifest*
- *WeatherReport*
- *EligibilityChecklist*
- *AuditTrail* (required only for the high-value branch)
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (must be signed before payment)
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* (issued to the passenger)

No legacy activities (such as *CustomerService_Agent_Decide*) are included, because they are not part of Contoso’s defined process.

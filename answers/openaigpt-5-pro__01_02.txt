Decision points:
- Quick KYC Verification (KYC): confirm ID card and mobile phone; skip all credit history checks.
- Underwriter Alignment Gate: after Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B each assign a risk score (0–9), if the two scores differ by ≤ 2 points, continue; else escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision, then continue.
- Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC): collect at least 3 local references before proceeding.
- Amount Threshold Decision: if amount < €15 000, the system auto-approves; if amount ≥ €15 000, proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).
- Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA): board votes approve or reject.

Required documents at submission time:
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

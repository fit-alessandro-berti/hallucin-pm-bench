### 1. Decision Points

There are two critical decision points (gates) in this process that determine the flow of the claim:

*   **A. Eligibility Validation (at Triage)**
    *   **Location:** During the **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** activity.
    *   **Logic:** The triage agent must determine if the claim is valid by checking it against the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.
    *   **Outcome:** The process can only proceed to financial approval if the claim is deemed eligible.

*   **B. High-Value Threshold Check (Routing)**
    *   **Location:** Between **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** and payment processing.
    *   **Logic:** The system (or agent) evaluates if the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**.
    *   **Outcome:**
        *   **Yes (>€1,000):** The case is routed to the **High-value branch**, requiring **Senior_Auditor_Review** (and the *AuditTrail*) before payment can be authorized.
        *   **No (≤€1,000):** The case bypasses the auditor and proceeds directly to payment authorization once the Finance Controller signs off.

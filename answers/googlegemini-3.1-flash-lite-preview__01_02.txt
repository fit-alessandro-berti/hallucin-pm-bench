Based on the Micro-loan Fast-Track process, here is the summary of decision points and the list of required documents.

### Decision Points

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate (Step 5):** The system compares the risk scores assigned by Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B. If the scores differ by > 2 points, the application must be escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7):**
    *   If the loan amount is **< €15 000**, the process moves to system auto-approval.
    *   If the loan amount is **≥ €15 000**, the application must proceed to the **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (Step 8):** The board casts a vote to either approve or reject the application.

***

### Required Documents (at submission time)

*   **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
*   **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
*   **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
*   **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
*   **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

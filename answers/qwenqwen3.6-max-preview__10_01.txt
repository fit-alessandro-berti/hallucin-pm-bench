**RACI Legend**
- **R** = Responsible (executes the task)
- **A** = Accountable (owns the outcome and approves completion)
- **C** = Consulted (provides input or expertise before/during execution)
- **I** = Informed (notified of completion or status changes)
- **–** = No direct involvement

**Consultant Notes on Handoffs**
- The **Account Manager** drives intake, completes **Order Vetting**, and owns customer communication, ensuring a single point of contact for the client.
- The **Compliance Officer** holds full accountability for **KYC Review**; the AM is consulted to supply documentation and clarify client details.
- The **Finance Clerk** takes ownership at **Payment Clearance**, with the AM kept informed to manage client expectations.
- **IT Support** is accountable for the **Record Update**, consulting the Finance Clerk to verify settlement data before refreshing the CRM.
- Each row contains exactly one Accountable role to prevent decision bottlenecks and maintain clear audit trails across the workflow.

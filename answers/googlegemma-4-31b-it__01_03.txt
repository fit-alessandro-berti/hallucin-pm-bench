**a) Decision Points**

*   **Risk/Amount Branch:** If the risk rating is Low or Medium AND the requested amount is €250,000 or less, the process skips Manual Underwriting Review.
*   **TriVote Gate:** The application passes if at least two of the three required officials (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) provide approval.

**b) Required Documents**

1. Form 14B
2. Scorecard–Cyan
3. Scorecard–Magenta
4. Scorecard–Yellow
5. Deck Memo
6. Offer Sheet 77
7. ID Matrix Declaration
8. Loan Dossier 2025

Based on Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process, here are the decision points and the list of required documents.

### Decision Points

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate (Step 5)**
   * **Condition:** Compare the independent risk scores (0–9) assigned during the **Dual Underwriter Split** (by **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**).
   * **Path A:** If the two scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, the process continues directly to the **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
   * **Path B:** If the scores differ by **> 2 points**, the file is escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before continuing to the **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7)**
   * **Path A (Amount < €15 000):** The system auto-approves the micro-loan, proceeding directly to **Disburse Funds**.
   * **Path B (Amount ≥ €15 000):** The process proceeds to step 8 for **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (Step 8)**
   * *Only triggered if amount is ≥ €15 000.* 
   * **Decision:** The board votes to either **approve** (proceeding to **Disburse Funds**) or **reject** the application.

---

### Required Documents

The following exact documents are required at submission time:
* **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
* **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
* **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
* **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
* **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
* **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

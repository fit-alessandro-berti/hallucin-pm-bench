### Added tasks
- **Task_VerifyIdentity** (“Verify Identity”) — added before the application review.
- **Task_RequestDocs** (“Request Additional Documents”) — added as a new outcome after the decision gateway.
- **Task_FinalReject** (“Final Reject”) — added as a new rejection/end outcome after the decision gateway.

### Removed tasks
- **Task_RejectLoan** (“Reject Loan”) — removed (its role is replaced by **Task_FinalReject**).

### Gateway-type changes (e.g., Exclusive → Inclusive)
- **Gateway_Decision** changed from **exclusiveGateway** (v1) to **inclusiveGateway** (v2).
- The gateway’s outgoing paths expanded from **2 branches** (“yes” / “no”) to **3 branches** (“eligible” / “missing docs” / “ineligible”).

**MEMO TO COO – Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance**

The Claims Handling process shows a major bottleneck at mid-process causing throughput loss.

**1. Request_Documents – Worst bottleneck**
`avg_wait_time_min` 150 is 5x higher than any other activity, `rework_rate_pct` 22.0% is highest, and throughput drops from 270 to 190 cases/day. `sla_breach_pct` 5.5% compounds delay.
**Action:** Launch guided upload portal with completeness checks to cut 22% rework and queue.

**2. Review_Documents – Worst efficiency & SLA**
`avg_processing_time_min` 25 and `stdev_processing_time_min` 9 are highest, `sla_breach_pct` 12.4% is highest, throughput remains low at 185, `rework_rate_pct` 5.3% second-highest.
**Action:** Implement standardized review checklist and split complex cases to reduce variability and SLA breaches.

**3. Initial_Assessment – Secondary delay**
`avg_wait_time_min` 18 is second-highest and `avg_processing_time_min` 12 is second-highest, with `sla_breach_pct` 3.2%.
**Action:** Add triage staffing/rules to cut 18-min wait and stabilize downstream flow.

**AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process: RACI Matrix**

| Process Activity | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|---|---|---|
| Receive & Log Transfer Instruction | R, A | I | – |
| Screen Payment Against Sanctions List | R | A | I |
| Conduct KYC Review | C | R, A | I |
| Approve High-Risk Transaction | I | R, A | – |
| Release Payment | R, A | I | C |
| Transmit SWIFT Message | C | I | R, A |
| Archive Case File | – | I | R, A |

**RACI Legend**
- **R (Responsible):** Executes the activity.
- **A (Accountable):** Owns the outcome, holds final sign-off authority, and is answerable for correctness. Exactly one **A** is assigned per row.
- **C (Consulted):** Provides two-way communication or subject-matter input prior to execution.
- **I (Informed):** Receives one-way notification upon completion or status change.

**Allocation Notes (Consultant Guidance)**
- The **Transaction Analyst** owns operational intake, payment release, and initial sanctions screening, with the **Regulatory Liaison** retaining accountability for screening accuracy.
- The **Regulatory Liaison** exclusively executes and owns the **KYC Review** and high-risk approvals, consulting the **Transaction Analyst** only for transactional context.
- **IT Support** manages technical routing and archival workflows, ensuring the **Regulatory Liaison** is formally notified upon case closure per audit requirements.
- This matrix strictly enforces the mandated vocabulary and isolates accountability to prevent role overlap during cross-border settlement.

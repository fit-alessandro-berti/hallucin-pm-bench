**Decision Points**

- **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**: decides whether the claim is eligible by checking it against the FlightManifest and WeatherReport.
- **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**: decides whether to pre-approve the claim using the EligibilityChecklist.
- **High-value branch**: decides whether the refund amount is greater than €1 000; if so, the case must proceed to Senior_Auditor_Review (which must include the AuditTrail).

**Required Documents**

- RefundRequestForm
- FlightManifest
- WeatherReport
- EligibilityChecklist
- AuditTrail (required for claims > €1 000)
- BankTransferAuthorization
- RefundConfirmationPDF

All steps and artefacts above are taken exclusively from the defined Contoso process. No other activities or documents are included.

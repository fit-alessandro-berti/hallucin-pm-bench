### Decision Points

The key decision points in the process are:

*   **Quick KYC Verification (KYC):** A pass/fail decision to confirm the applicant's ID card and mobile phone.
*   **Community Impact Assessment (CIA):** A qualitative judgment on the loan's community value, conducted by an Impact Officer.
*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate:** A system check on the risk scores from the two underwriters.
    *   If the scores differ by more than 2 points, the application is escalated for a tie-break decision by the **Harmonisation Committee**.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision:** An automated decision based on the loan value.
    *   Applications for **< €15 000** are auto-approved.
    *   Applications for **≥ €15 000** are forwarded for final approval.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA):** A final *approve* or *reject* vote by the board for all applications of €15,000 or more.

### Required Documents

The following documents are required at the time of submission:

*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

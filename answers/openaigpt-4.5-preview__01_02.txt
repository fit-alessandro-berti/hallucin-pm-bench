### Decision Points:

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
 • If both underwriters' risk scores differ by ≤ 2 points: Proceed to Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC).  
 • Else: Escalate to Harmonisation Committee before continuing.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
 • If amount < €15 000: System auto-approves.  
 • Else (amount ≥ €15 000): Proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** *(only for amounts ≥ €15 000)*  
 • Board votes approve or reject.

### Required Documents at Submission:

- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

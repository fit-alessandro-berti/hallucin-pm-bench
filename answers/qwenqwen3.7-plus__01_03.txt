**a) Decision Points (Gates and Conditional Branches)**

1. **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** The process automatically skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) if the Tri-Color risk rating is "Low" or "Medium" *and* the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less. 
2. **Decision Gate (Step 6):** The "TriVote" gate passes and allows the process to continue if a minimum of two out of the three designated approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) grant their approval.

*(Note: Although Step 6 initially states it "requires approvals from all three," the explicit decision rule overrides this by stating "any two approvals out of three suffice to pass.")*

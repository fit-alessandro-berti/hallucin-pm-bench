

# RACI Matrix — FinCo Order-to-Cash Workflow

| # | Activity | AM | CO | FC | IT |
|---|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 1 | **Receive Application** | **R / A** | I | – | – |
| 2 | **KYC Review** | C | **R / A** | – | – |
| 3 | **Order Vetting** | **R / A** | C | I | – |
| 4 | **Payment Clearance** | I | – | **R / A** | – |
| 5 | **Record Update** | I | – | – | **R / A** |
| 6 | **Notify Customer** | **R / A** | – | I | I |

> **Key:** **R** = Responsible · **A** = Accountable · **C** = Consulted · **I** = Informed · **–** = No involvement

---

## Assignment Rationale

1. **Receive Application** — The **Account Manager** owns intake and is accountable for capturing all initial data; the **Compliance Officer** is informed so they can prepare for the upcoming review.

2. **KYC Review** — The **Compliance Officer** is responsible for and accountable for the review. The **Account Manager** is consulted because they hold the client relationship and can supply supporting documents.

3. **Order Vetting** — The **Account Manager** checks data completeness (R/A). The **Compliance Officer** is consulted for any residual risk flags, and the **Finance Clerk** is informed so settlement preparation can begin.

4. **Payment Clearance** — The **Finance Clerk** initiates and owns settlement (R/A). The **Account Manager** is informed of the clearance outcome to manage client expectations.

5. **Record Update** — **IT Support** is responsible for refreshing the CRM (R/A). The **Account Manager** is informed once the record is current.

6. **Notify Customer** — The **Account Manager** sends the confirmation email (R/A). Both the **Finance Clerk** and **IT Support** are informed to close out their respective task queues.

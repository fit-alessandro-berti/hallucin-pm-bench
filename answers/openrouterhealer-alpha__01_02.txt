### Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track Process

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   - If the two risk scores (from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B) differ by **≤ 2 points**, the process continues.  
   - Otherwise, escalation to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie‑break decision is triggered before proceeding.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   - If the requested loan amount is **< €15,000**, the system auto‑approves and funds are disbursed.  
   - If the amount is **≥ €15,000**, the application proceeds to the **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval** step.

3. **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   - The Micro‑loan Board votes to **approve** or **reject** the application.

### Required Documents at Submission Time

- Digital Application Form (ML‑APP‑01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay‑stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score‑Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

A shortest firing sequence where **T5 fires before T3** is:

**T1, T2b, T4, T5**

### Why this works
- **T1** fires first:  
  `p0 → pa + pb`
- **T2b** uses `pa` and produces `pd`:  
  `pa → pd`
- **T4** uses `pb` and produces `pe`:  
  `pb → pe`
- Now both inputs of **T5** are present: `pd` and `pe`, so **T5** can fire:  
  `pd + pe → pf`

At this point, **T3 has not fired at all**, so **T5 has indeed fired before T3**.

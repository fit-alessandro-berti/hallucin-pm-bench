Add an interrupting Boundary Timer Event to `Task_RequestDocs`. Waiting for missing documents is the primary bottleneck that threatens the 48-hour SLA from `Start_Claim`. Set the timer's duration to a strict limit (e.g., PT40H) that reserves an adequate buffer for `Task_Receive` and `Task_Check`. If the timer fires before the documents are received, the token is interrupted and routed to an Escalation End Event, ensuring the claim is auto-escalated instead of breaching the SLA. Additionally, reconnect sequence flow `f6` from `Task_RequestDocs` back to `Task_Check` (instead of `Task_Triage`) so that received documents are actually re-verified before triage.

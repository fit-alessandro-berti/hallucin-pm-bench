Decision points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process  
1. Quick KYC Verification (KYC) – confirms ID card **and** mobile phone.  
2. Underwriter Alignment Gate – compares Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B risk scores; if the difference is **> 2 points**, escalates to the **Harmonisation Committee**.  
3. Amount Threshold Decision – if amount **≥ €15 000**, the application proceeds to the **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**; if **< €15 000**, the system **auto-approves**.

Required documents at submission time  
▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

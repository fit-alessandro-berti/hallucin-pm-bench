**RACI Matrix – AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process**

| Task                                      | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|-------------------------------------------|---------------------|--------------------|------------|
| Receive and Log Transfer Instruction      | R/A                 | I                  | -          |
| Screen Payment Against Sanctions List     | R/A                 | I                  | -          |

| Approve High-Risk Transaction             | I                   | R/A                | -          |
| Release Payment                           | R                   | A                  | I          |
| Ensure SWIFT Message is Sent              | I                   | I                  | R/A        |

**Notes:**  
- R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed  
- Only mandated terminology (Transaction Analyst, Regulatory Liaison, KYC Review) has been used for roles and activities.

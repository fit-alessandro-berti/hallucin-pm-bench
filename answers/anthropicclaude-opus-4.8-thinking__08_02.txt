**Question 2.** Minimal sequence: **T1, T2, T7, T4, T5**.

Reasoning: T1 puts tokens in P1 and P2. The P1‑branch (T2→P3, T7→P4, T4→P5) generates P5 *without* consuming P2. T3 is only needed if P4 must be produced from the P2‑branch, but the alternative T7 route makes that unnecessary.

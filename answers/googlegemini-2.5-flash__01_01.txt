Here are the decision points and required documents in Contoso Airlines’ flight-refund process:

**Decision Point:**
*   Whether the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**.
    *   If yes, the case proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
    *   If no, it directly proceeds to **Process_Refund_Payment** (after Finance_Controller_PreApprove).

**Required Documents:**
*   RefundRequestForm
*   FlightManifest
*   WeatherReport
*   EligibilityChecklist
*   AuditTrail (required for Senior_Auditor_Review)
*   BankTransferAuthorization
*   RefundConfirmationPDF

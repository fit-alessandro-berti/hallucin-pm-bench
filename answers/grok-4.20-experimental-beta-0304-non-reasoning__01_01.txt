**Decision Points in Contoso’s Process**

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**  
   - Decision: Is the claim eligible based on the FlightManifest and WeatherReport?

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**  
   - Decision: Does the claim pass the EligibilityChecklist (pre-approval)?

3. **High-value branch decision** (after Finance_Controller_PreApprove)  
   - Decision: Is the refund amount greater than €1,000?  
     - Yes → route to Senior_Auditor_Review (must include AuditTrail)  
     - No → proceed directly to Process_Refund_Payment

**Required Documents / Artefacts**

- RefundRequestForm
- FlightManifest
- WeatherReport
- EligibilityChecklist
- AuditTrail (only for claims > €1,000)
- BankTransferAuthorization
- RefundConfirmationPDF

All steps strictly follow the defined process. The legacy activity “CustomerService_Agent_Decide” does not exist and is not used.

**RACI Matrix – FinCo Order‑to‑Cash Workflow**

| Activity          | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|-------------------|----------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Receive Application | **R / A** | I | I | I |
| KYC Review          | I | **R / A** | I | I |
| Order Vetting       | **R / A** | I | I | I |
| Payment Clearance   | I | I | **R / A** | I |
| Record Update       | I | I | I | **R / A** |
| Notify Customer     | **R / A** | I | I | I |

**Key**:  
- **R** – Responsible (does the work)  
- **A** – Accountable (owns the outcome)  
- **C** – Consulted (provides input) – *none assigned in this baseline*  
- **I** – Informed (kept aware of progress)  

*Roles not directly handling an activity are marked as Informed.*

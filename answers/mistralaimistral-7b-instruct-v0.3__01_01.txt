 Decision Points in Contoso Airlines' Flight-Refund Process:

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**: This decision point checks if the refund request is valid based on the FlightManifest and WeatherReport.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**: This decision point checks if the refund request is eligible for pre-approval using an EligibilityChecklist.

3. **High-value branch**: If the refund amount is greater than â‚¬1 000, the case proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review**. This decision point is based on the refund amount.

Required Documents:

1. *RefundRequestForm*
2. *FlightManifest*
3. *WeatherReport*
4. *EligibilityChecklist*
5. *AuditTrail* (for high-value claims only)
6. *BankTransferAuthorization*
7. *RefundConfirmationPDF* (emailed to the passenger after the refund payment is processed)

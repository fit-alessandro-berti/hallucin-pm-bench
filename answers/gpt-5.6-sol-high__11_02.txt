**To:** COO, Acme Insurance  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance

The three clear under-performers are:

1. **Request_Documents** — Highest queue time (150 minutes) and rework rate (22.0%), with throughput falling to 190 cases/day from 270 at the preceding activities. SLA breaches are also elevated at 5.5%. **Action:** Introduce a first-time-completeness checklist and track repeat requests weekly, targeting rework below 10%.

2. **Review_Documents** — Highest processing time (25 minutes), processing variability (9 minutes), and SLA-breach rate (12.4%). It also has a 30-minute wait and 5.3% rework. **Action:** Segment reviews by expected complexity and establish standardized review steps, targeting lower variability and SLA breaches.

3. **Initial_Assessment** — Third-worst across several service measures: 18-minute wait, 12-minute processing time, 1.8% rework, and 3.2% SLA breaches. These are materially worse than most other activities. **Action:** Reallocate capacity using daily queue volumes, with an initial target of reducing average wait below 10 minutes.

Priority should be given to **Request_Documents** and **Review_Documents**, where the performance gaps are largest.

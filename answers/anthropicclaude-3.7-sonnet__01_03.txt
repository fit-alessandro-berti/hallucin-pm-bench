## Decision Points in Acme Bank's Small-Business Loan Approval Process

**a) Decision Points:**

1. **Risk/Amount Conditional Branch (Step 4):** If the risk rating is either Low or Medium AND the requested amount is €250,000 or less, the Manual-Underwriting-Review step is skipped.

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):** A loan application passes when at least two out of three required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) approve the application.

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance):**

1. Form 14B (for Receive-Application)
2. Scorecard–Cyan (for Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
3. Scorecard–Magenta (for Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
4. Scorecard–Yellow (for Tri-Color-Risk-Check)
5. Deck Memo (for Manual-Underwriting-Review)
6. Offer Sheet 77 (for Prepare-Binding-Offer)
7. ID Matrix Declaration (for Request-Applicant-Confirmation)
8. Loan Dossier 2025 (for Finalize-Agreement)

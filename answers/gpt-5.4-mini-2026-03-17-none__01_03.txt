### a) Decision points in Acme’s process

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check outcome:** assigns a risk rating of **Low, Medium, or High** based on the three scorecards.  
2. **Skip-Manual-Underwriting branch:** **if risk is Low or Medium and the requested amount is €250,000 or less, step 5 is skipped**; otherwise, the application proceeds to Manual-Underwriting-Review.  
3. **TriVote gate:** the application passes if **any two of the three approvers** — Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer — approve.  

### b) Documents required anywhere in the process, in first-appearance order

1. **Form 14B**  
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**  
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**  
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**

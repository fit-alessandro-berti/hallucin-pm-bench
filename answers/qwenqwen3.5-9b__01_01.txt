Based on the provided Contoso Airlines flight-refund process definition, here is the summary of decision points and required documents.

### 1. Decision Points

The process contains the following logical gates and branching decisions. Note that the legacy activity **CustomerService_Agent_Decide** is excluded.

*   **Eligibility Validation Decision**
    *   **Stage:** `Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility`
    *   **Logic:** The agent validates the claim by cross-referencing the customer data against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
    *   **Condition:** The process continues to the Finance Controller only after eligibility is validated; otherwise, the claim does not proceed to pre-approval.

*   **Financial Approval Decision**
    *   **Stage:** `Finance_Controller_PreApprove`
    *   **Logic:** The finance controller signs off on the **EligibilityChecklist**.
    *   **Condition:** Payment processing cannot begin without a successful sign-off on this checklist.

*   **High-Value Amount Decision**
    *   **Stage:** Precedes `Process_Refund_Payment` (High-value branch)
    *   **Logic:** The specific threshold check: **if the refund amount is greater than €1 000**.
    *   **Branching Path:**
        *   **> €1 000:** Case proceeds to `Senior_Auditor_Review` (requires **AuditTrail**).
        *   **≤ €1 000:** Case proceeds directly to `Process_Refund_Payment`.

*   **Payment Authorization Decision**
    *   **Stage:** `Process_Refund_Payment`
    *   **Logic:** Execution is conditional on the presence of signed authorization.
    *   **Condition:** Process begins only after `Finance_Controller_PreApprove` has succeeded **and** `BankTransferAuthorization` is present (for high-value claims, `Senior_Auditor_Review` must also be completed).

---

### 2. Required Documents

The following artefacts are defined as required within the process steps or decision criteria. No other documents exist in this process.

*   **RefundRequestForm** (Artifect: Customer_Submit_Claim)
*   **FlightManifest** (Artifect: Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
*   **WeatherReport** (Artifect: Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
*   **EligibilityChecklist** (Artifect: Finance_Controller_PreApprove)
*   **AuditTrail** (Artifect: Senior_Auditor_Review — *Required only if refund amount > €1 000*)
*   **BankTransferAuthorization** (Artifect: Process_Refund_Payment)
*   **RefundConfirmationPDF** (Artifect: Issue_Confirmation_Letter)

Adding a non-interrupting boundary timer event to the "Create Account in Core System" task triggers an escalation to the existing Account-Manager Review if the task does not complete within 48 hours of starting it (immediately after "Verify Identity"), thereby enforcing the SLA without halting the main process flow.

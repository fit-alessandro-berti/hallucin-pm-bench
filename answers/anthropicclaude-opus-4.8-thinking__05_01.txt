**Attach an interrupting timer boundary event (duration `PT48H`, measured from submission) to `Task_RequestDocs`** — the only step that can stall, since it waits on the claimant. When the 48-hour limit is hit, the boundary event fires and routes a new flow directly into `Task_Triage`, replacing/short-circuiting `f6`. This auto-escalates the claim into triage on time even if documents never arrive, guaranteeing the SLA. No new tasks are introduced — only a boundary event and one reconnected sequence flow.

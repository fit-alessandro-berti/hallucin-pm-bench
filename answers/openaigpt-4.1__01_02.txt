### Decision Points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track Process

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   – Decision: Does ID card **and** mobile phone verify successfully? (Proceed if yes.)

2. **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)**  
   – Decision: Does the Impact Officer endorse the application’s community impact?

3. **Dual Underwriter Split**  
   – Decision:  
     • **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** each assign a risk score (0–9).

4. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   – Decision:  
     • If risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → proceed.  
     • Else → escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision.

5. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**  
   – Decision: Are at least **3 local references** collected and assessed?

6. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   – Decision:  
     • If **amount < €15 000** → system auto-approves.  
     • If **amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

7. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   – Decision: Does the Micro-loan Board vote **approve** or **reject**?

---

### Required Documents (**at submission time**)

- **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
- **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
- **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
- **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
- **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

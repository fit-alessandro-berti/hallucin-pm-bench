Here is the RACI matrix for FinCo’s order-to-cash workflow, structured with activities as rows and roles as columns, and strictly applying the preferred terminology.

| Process Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Receive Application** | R, A | I | I | I |
| **KYC Review** | A | R | I | I |
| **Order Vetting** | R, A | C | I | I |
| **Payment Clearance** | A | I | R | I |
| **Record Update** | A | I | I | R |
| **Notify Customer** | R, A | I | I | I |

**Legend:**
* **R** = Responsible (performs the work)
* **A** = Accountable (ultimately answerable for the outcome)
* **C** = Consulted (provides input prior to the task)
* **I** = Informed (notified of the outcome after the task)

*Rationale notes:* The Account Manager is assigned as Accountable for the overarching workflow and Responsible for the steps they directly handle. The Compliance Officer is Consulted during Order Vetting to ensure all compliance parameters from the prior step are met before the order proceeds.

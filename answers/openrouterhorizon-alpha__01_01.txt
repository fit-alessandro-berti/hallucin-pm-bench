Decision points
1) High-value branch decision: Is the refund amount greater than €1 000?
• If yes: the case must proceed to Senior_Auditor_Review (includes the AuditTrail).
• If no: the case skips Senior_Auditor_Review and can proceed toward payment after Finance_Controller_PreApprove.

Required documents and artefacts
• RefundRequestForm (submitted by the passenger in Customer_Submit_Claim)
• FlightManifest (used in Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
• WeatherReport (used in Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
• EligibilityChecklist (used in Finance_Controller_PreApprove)
• AuditTrail (must be included in Senior_Auditor_Review for high-value claims)
• BankTransferAuthorization (must be signed before Process_Refund_Payment)
• RefundConfirmationPDF (emailed in Issue_Confirmation_Letter)

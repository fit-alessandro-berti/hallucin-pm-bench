Yes, it is possible for T5 to fire before T3.  

Shortest (and only minimal-length) sequence:  
T1, T2b, T4, T5  

Verification against the given net and initial marking:  
- M0: p0=1  
- T1 (requires p0): produces pa, pb → M1: pa=1, pb=1  
- T2b (requires pa): produces pd → M2: pb=1, pd=1  
- T4 (requires pb): produces pe → M3: pd=1, pe=1  
- T5 (requires pd, pe): produces pf → M4: pf=1  

In this sequence T5 fires at step 4 while T3 has not fired (and its input pc is never produced before T5 fires). Hence T5 can occur before T3.

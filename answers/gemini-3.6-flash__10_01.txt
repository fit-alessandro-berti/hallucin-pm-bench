### Order-to-Cash RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **1. Receive Application** | **A / R** | I | I | I |
| **2. KYC Review** | C | **A / R** | I | I |

| **4. Payment Clearance** | C | I | **A / R** | I |
| **5. Record Update** | C | I | I | **A / R** |
| **6. Notify Customer** | **A / R** | I | I | I |

---

### RACI Definitions
* **R – Responsible:** The role that performs the activity.
* **A – Accountable:** The role with final approval and ownership of the activity.
* **C – Consulted:** The role providing required input or supporting data.
* **I – Informed:** The role kept updated on progress or completion.

### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – If the two risk scores (from *Senior Underwriter A* and *Shadow Underwriter B*) differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee**.
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – If the loan amount is **≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**; otherwise, auto-approve.

### **Required Documents**
▪ **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
▪ **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**
▪ **Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)**
▪ **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
▪ **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
▪ **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

*(Note: No credit-bureau pull or standard loan documents are required.)*

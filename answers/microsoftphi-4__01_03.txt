Certainly! Let's break down the decision points and list the required documents as requested.

### a) Decision Points

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Outcome Decision**: If the risk rating is either Low or Medium and the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, skip the Manual-Underwriting-Review step.

2. **Decision-Gate "TriVote"**: Requires approvals from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, with the rule that any two approvals out of the three suffice to pass.

### b) Required Documents

1. **Form 14B**: Used at the reception of the application.
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**: Input for the Tri-Color-Risk-Check.
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**: Input for the Tri-Color-Risk-Check.
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**: Input for the Tri-Color-Risk-Check.
5. **Deck Memo**: Prepared during the Manual-Underwriting-Review step.
6. **Offer Sheet 77**: Prepared at the Binding-Offer stage.
7. **ID Matrix Declaration**: Required when requesting Applicant Confirmation.
8. **Loan Dossier 2025**: Used to finalize the agreement.

These steps and documents outline the decision-making process and necessary paperwork in the Acme Bank’s SMALL-BUSINESS loan-approval process.

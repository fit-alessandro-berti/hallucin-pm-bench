**Decision Points**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   *If the two risk scores differ by ≤ 2 points → proceed to the next step; if the difference is > 2 points → escalation to the Harmonisation Committee.*

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   *If the requested loan amount is **< €15 000** → auto‑approve;  
   if **≥ €15 000** → route to the Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA).*

3. **Final Micro‑loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   *The Micro‑loan Board votes **approve** or **reject** the application.*

---

**Required Documents (submission time)**

- Digital Application Form **(ML-APP-01)**  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay‑stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score‑Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

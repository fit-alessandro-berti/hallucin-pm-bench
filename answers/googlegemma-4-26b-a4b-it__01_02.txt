Based on the May 2025 Micro-loan Fast-Track process, here are the decision points and the required documentation:

### **Decision Points**
*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate:** A check to determine if the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by $\leq 2$ points. If the difference is greater, the case must be escalated to the **Harmonisation Committee**.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision:** A determination based on the requested loan amount:
    *   If the **amount < €15,000**, the system triggers an **auto-approval**.
    *   If the **amount $\geq$ €15,000**, the case must proceed to the **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA):** The board conducts a vote to either **approve** or **reject** loans $\geq$ €15,000.

### **Required Documents**
*   Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
*   Proof of Address ($\leq 3$ months old)
*   Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
*   Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
*   Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
*   Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

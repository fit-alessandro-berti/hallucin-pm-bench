**a) Decision Points**
• **Step 4 (Conditional Branch):** The manual underwriting review (Step 5) is bypassed if and only if the Tri‑Color risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested loan amount does not exceed €250 000.  
• **Step 6 (“TriVote” Decision Gate):** The application advances to offer preparation only if at least two of the three mandatory reviewers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) approve.

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**
1. Form 14B  
2. Deck Memo  
3. Offer Sheet 77  
4. ID Matrix Declaration  
5. Loan Dossier 2025  
*(Note: The Tri‑Color Scorecards are designated as process inputs rather than documents, and the “Signed Offer Sheet 77” in Step 8 is merely the executed state of the Offer Sheet 77 first created in Step 7.)*

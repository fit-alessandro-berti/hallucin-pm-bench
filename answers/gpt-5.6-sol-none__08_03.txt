**Yes.** One shortest firing sequence is:

**T1 → T2b → T4 → T5**

Markings:

1. After **T1**: `pa=1, pb=1`
2. After **T2b**: `pd=1, pb=1`
3. After **T4**: `pd=1, pe=1`
4. **T5** is enabled and fires, without **T3** having fired.

This is shortest because **T5** requires tokens in both `pd` and `pe`: obtaining them without T3 requires **T1**, **T2b**, and **T4** first. (The order of **T2b** and **T4** may be swapped.)

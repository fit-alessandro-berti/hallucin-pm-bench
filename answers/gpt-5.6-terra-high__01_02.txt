## Decision points

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   Confirm both the ID card and mobile phone. Credit history checks are skipped.

2. **Dual Underwriter Split**  
   - **Senior Underwriter A** assigns a risk score from 0–9.  
   - **Shadow Underwriter B** independently assigns a risk score from 0–9.

3. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   - If the two risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  
   - If they differ by **more than 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue.

4. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**  
   Collect at least **3 local references** before the amount decision.

5. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   - If the amount is **< €15 000**, the system auto-approves.  
   - If the amount is **≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

6. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   For amounts **≥ €15 000**, the Micro-loan Board votes **approve** or **reject**. Approved applications proceed to **Disburse Funds**.

## Required documents at submission time

- Digital Application Form (**ML-APP-01**)  
- Proof of Address (**≤ 3 months old**)  
- Latest Income Statement (**single pay-stub or tax slip**)  
- Community Impact Statement (**CIS**) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (**NFF**)

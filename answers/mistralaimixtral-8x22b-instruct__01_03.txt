### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Process**
1. **Step 4 (Conditional Skip):** If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the loan amount is **≤ €250,000**, skip **Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5)**.
2. **Step 6 (TriVote Decision Gate):** The loan passes if **at least two out of three** (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve.

---

### **b) Documents in Order of First Appearance**
1. **Form 14B** (Application)
2. **Deck Memo** (Manual-Underwriting-Review, if required)
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Binding-Offer)
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Applicant Confirmation)
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalization)

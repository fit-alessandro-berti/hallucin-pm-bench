**a) Decision Points**

1.  **Step 4 (Conditional Branch):** The process skips the Manual-Underwriting-Review if the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Step 6 (Decision-Gate "TriVote"):** The application passes this gate if at least two of the three designated approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, or Sustainability Officer) grant approval.

MEMO

To: COO, Orion Credit
From: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
Subject: Top SLA Breaches in Loan Origination

Review of throughput versus SLA targets and waiting times surfaces three activities that simultaneously miss SLA and carry excessive idle time. Risk Review runs 300s over its 300s SLA (100% breach) with 480s waiting—80% of its 600s cycle. Legal Approval runs 300s over its 600s SLA (50% breach) and posts the highest absolute waiting time at 600s, roughly two-thirds of its 900s cycle. Credit Assessment exceeds its 240s SLA by 60s (25%) while spending 200s of its 300s cycle waiting (67%).

Recommended actions:

- **Risk Review** – Automate the initial assessment for the 480 incoming cases, removing the 480s queue before manual handling.
- **Legal Approval** – Add reviewer capacity or a fast-track lane for the 465 cases reaching this step, targeting the 600s waiting component.
- **Credit Assessment** – Run bureau checks in parallel to compress the 200s waiting time across the 1,175 cases processed.

**a) Decision Points in Acme Bank’s SMALL-BUSINESS Loan-Approval Process**  
1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3):** The risk rating (Low, Medium, or High) derived from the Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards determines the next step.  
2. **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** If the risk rating is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the process skips manual underwriting (step 5); otherwise, it proceeds to manual review.  
3. **TriVote Gate (Step 6):** At least two of the three required approvals (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) must be granted to proceed.  

---

**b) Documents Required in Order of First Appearance**  
1. **Form 14B** (Received in **Step 1**).  
2. **Deck Memo** (Created in **Step 5**: Manual-Underwriting-Review).  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Prepared in **Step 7**: Prepare-Binding-Offer).  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Required in **Step 8**: Request-Applicant-Confirmation).  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Finalized in **Step 9**: Finalize-Agreement).  

*Note:* The three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) are inputs to the Tri-Color-Risk-Check and are not listed as standalone process documents. KYC/AML and credit-bureau checks are explicitly excluded from this in-house process.

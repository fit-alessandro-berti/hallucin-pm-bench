### **Decision Points in Contoso Airlines’ Flight-Refund Process**
1. **High-Value Check**
   - **Decision:** Is the refund amount **> €1,000**?
     - **If yes** → Proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
     - **If no** → Skip to **Process_Refund_Payment** (after **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**).

---

### **Required Documents**
1. **RefundRequestForm** (from **Customer_Submit_Claim**)
2. **FlightManifest** (used in **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**)
3. **WeatherReport** (used in **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**)
4. **EligibilityChecklist** (signed in **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**)
5. **AuditTrail** (required for **Senior_Auditor_Review**, high-value cases only)
6. **BankTransferAuthorization** (signed, required for **Process_Refund_Payment**)
7. **RefundConfirmationPDF** (generated in **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**)

## Acme Bank Small-Business Loan Approval Process – Analysis

Here’s the analysis of Acme Bank’s loan approval process, based strictly on the provided definition:

**a) Decision Points:**

*   **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** The application receives a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High based on the combined assessment of the Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow Scorecards.
*   **Conditional Skip (Step 4):** If the risk rating is Low or Medium *and* the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is skipped.
*   **TriVote (Step 6):** The loan passes the Decision-Gate if at least two out of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) provide approval.

**b) Documents Required (in order of first appearance):**

1.  **Form 14B**
2.  **Scorecard–Cyan**
3.  **Scorecard–Magenta**
4.  **Scorecard–Yellow**
5.  **Deck Memo**
6.  **Offer Sheet 77**
7.  **ID Matrix Declaration**
8.  **Loan Dossier 2025**

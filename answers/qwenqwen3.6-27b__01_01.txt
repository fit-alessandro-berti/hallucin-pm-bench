Here is the summary of every required documents and decision points defined in Contoso Airlines’ flight-refund process.

### Required Documents
*   RefundRequestForm
*   FlightManifest
*   WeatherReport
*   EligibilityChecklist
*   AuditTrail
*   BankTransferAuthorization
*   RefundConfirmationPDF

### Decision Points
1.  **Triage Eligibility Validation**: The triage agent validates the claim directly against the **FlightManifest** and the **WeatherReport.
2.  **Financial Pre-Approval Decision**: A Finance Controller signs off on the **EligibilityChecklist**.
3.  **High-Value Threshold Check**: The system determines if the refund amount is **greater than €1,000**.
4.  **Senior Auditor Review (Conditional)**: **IF** the refund amount is greater than €1,000, the case proceeds to decision point 4: **Senior_Auditor_Review**, which reviews the **AuditTrail**.
5.  **Payment Execution Decision**: The finance team executes the refund payment **only** when a signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present, AND the **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** step has succeeded, **AND** a senior auditor review has been completed in all cases, **AND** the **Senior_Auditor_Review** is complete. (Note: The legacy activity “CustomerService_Agent_Decide” mentioned in textbooks does **does not exist**.

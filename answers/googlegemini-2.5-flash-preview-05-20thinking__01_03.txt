Here's an analysis of Acme Bank’s small-business loan-approval process:

**a) Summary of every decision point:**

1.  **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is skipped if the applicant's risk rating is Low or Medium AND the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6):** This gate passes if at least two out of the three required officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer) provide their approval.

**b) All documents required in the process, in order of first appearance:**

1.  Form 14B
2.  Scorecard–Cyan
3.  Scorecard–Magenta
4.  Scorecard–Yellow
5.  Deck Memo
6.  Offer Sheet 77
7.  ID Matrix Declaration
8.  Loan Dossier 2025

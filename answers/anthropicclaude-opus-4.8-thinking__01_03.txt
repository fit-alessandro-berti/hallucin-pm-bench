## a) Decision Points

**1. Conditional Branch — "Underwriting Skip" (between Steps 4 & 5)**
If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** *and* the requested amount is **≤ €250,000**, the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5) is skipped; otherwise it is performed.

**2. Decision-Gate "TriVote" (Step 6)**
Approval is sought from three roles — Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer — and the application passes if **any two of the three approve**.

*(Note: The Tri-Color-Risk-Check in Step 3 is a risk-classification activity that produces the {Low / Medium / High} rating feeding decision point #1, rather than a gate in itself.)*

---

## b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)

1. **Form 14B** — Receive-Application (Step 1)
2. **Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow** — inputs to Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3)
3. **Deck Memo** — Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5)
4. **Offer Sheet 77** — Prepare-Binding-Offer (Step 7)
5. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** *and* **ID Matrix Declaration** — Request-Applicant-Confirmation (Step 8)
6. **Loan Dossier 2025** — Finalize-Agreement (Step 9)

*(If the three Scorecards are treated as data inputs rather than formal "documents," the document list reduces to Form 14B, Deck Memo, Offer Sheet 77, ID Matrix Declaration, and Loan Dossier 2025.)*

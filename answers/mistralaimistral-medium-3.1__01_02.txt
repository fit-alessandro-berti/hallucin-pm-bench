### **Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**
   - If **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** scores differ by **> 2 points** → escalate to **Harmonisation Committee** for tie-break.
   - If scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → proceed to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision**
   - If loan **< €15 000** → **auto-approve**.
   - If loan **≥ €15 000** → escalate to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** (for amounts ≥ €15 000)
   - Board votes **approve** or **reject** → if approved, proceed to **Disburse Funds**.

### **Required Documents at Submission**
▪ **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
▪ **Proof of Address** (≤ 3 months old)
▪ **Latest Income Statement** (single pay-stub or tax slip)
▪ **Community Impact Statement (CIS)** (template)
▪ **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet** (from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**)
▪ **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)** (minimum **3 local references**)

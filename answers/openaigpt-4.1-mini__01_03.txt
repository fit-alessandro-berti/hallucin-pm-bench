Certainly! Here is the summary based on Acme Bank’s SMALL-BUSINESS loan-approval process:

---

### a) Decision points summary

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3)**  
   Assigns a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High based on three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow).

2. **Conditional skip of Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 4)**  
   If the risk rating is Low or Medium *and* the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less, then skip Manual-Underwriting-Review; otherwise, proceed to it.

3. **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6)**  
   Requires approval votes from Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer, passing if any two out of the three approve.

---

### b) List of all required documents in order of first appearance

1. **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive-Application)  
2. **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review)  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)  
4. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)  
5. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)  
6. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize-Agreement)

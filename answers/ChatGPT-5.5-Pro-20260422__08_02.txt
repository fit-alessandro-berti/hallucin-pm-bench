After **T1**, the marking is `{P1, P2}`. The token in **P2** can wait while the other branch progresses:

`T1 → T2 → T7 → T4`

This reaches marking `{P2, P5}`. Since **T5** requires `{P2, P5}`, it is enabled even though **T3** has not fired.

**Q2. Minimal demonstrating sequence:**

`[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]`

Here **T5** fires before any firing of **T3**. Note that after **T5** fires, it consumes the token in **P2**, so **T3** cannot fire later in that same run.

Here is the summary of Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track decision points and required documentation, based strictly on the May 2025 non-standard protocol.

### **Key Decision Points**

There are three specific gates where the process branches or requires a judgment call:

**1. Underwriter Alignment Gate (Step 5)**
*   **Trigger:** After **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** independently assign risk scores (0–9).
*   **Logic:**
    *   **Aligned (Variance ≤ 2):** Proceed immediately to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.
    *   **Misaligned (Variance > 2):** Escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break before proceeding.

**2. Amount Threshold Decision (Step 7)**
*   **Trigger:** After successful **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)** (minimum 3 local references collected).
*   **Logic:**
    *   **< €15 000:** System **Auto-Approves**; move directly to Disbursement.
    *   **≥ €15 000:** Proceed to the **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

**3. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) (Step 8)**
*   **Trigger:** Loan amount is €15,000 or greater.
*   **Logic:** The Board votes to either **Approve** or **Reject** the application. (Note: This step replaces branch manager authority).

---

### **Required Documents (Submission Time)**

The following documents must be submitted to initiate the **Receive Micro-loan Application (RMA)** stage:

1.  **Digital Application Form** (Code: ML-APP-01)
2.  **Proof of Address** (Must be ≤ 3 months old)
3.  **Latest Income Statement** (Single pay-stub or tax slip)
4.  **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
5.  **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
6.  **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

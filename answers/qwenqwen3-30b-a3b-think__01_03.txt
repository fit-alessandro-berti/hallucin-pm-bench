### **a) Decision Points in Acme’s Process**  
1. **Conditional Skip (Step 4):**  
   *If* the risk rating is **Low** or **Medium** **AND** the requested amount ≤ €250,000, **skip** the Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).  

2. **TriVote Decision Gate (Step 6):**  
   Requires **at least two approvals** out of three mandatory roles (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) to proceed.  

---

### **b) Documents Required in Order of First Appearance**  
1. **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive-Application)  
2. **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual-Underwriting-Review)  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare-Binding-Offer)  
4. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)  
5. **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request-Applicant-Confirmation)  
6. **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize-Agreement)  

---  
**Notes:**  
- The **three Scorecards** (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) are inputs to the Tri-Color-Risk-Check (Step 3) but are not explicitly listed as "documents required" in the process.  
- The **Sustainability Officer’s involvement** is mandatory but not a document.  
- **KYC/AML checks** are outsourced and excluded from the in-house process.

### a) Summary of Decision Points

There are two primary decision points in Acme Bank’s loan-approval process:

*   **Manual-Underwriting-Review Trigger:** A loan application bypasses manual underwriting if its risk is rated Low or Medium and the requested amount is €250,000 or less.
*   **Decision-Gate “TriVote”:** An application is approved to proceed if at least two of the three required decision-makers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) vote to approve it.

**a) Decision points (gate or conditional branch)**  

1. **Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check** – The applicant’s risk rating is set to **Low, Medium, or High** based on the three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow).  
2. **Conditional skip of Manual‑Underwriting** – If the risk rating is **Low or Medium** **and** the requested amount is **≤ €250 000**, the process bypasses the Manual‑Underwriting‑Review step; otherwise it proceeds to that review.  
3. **TriVote Decision Gate** – The loan passes only if **at least two** of the three required approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) give their approval.  

---

**b) Documents required (in order of first appearance)**  

1. **Form 14B** – received with the application (step 1).  
2. **Deck Memo** – produced for the Manual‑Underwriting‑Review (step 5).  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** – prepared as the binding offer (step 7).  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** – signed by the applicant together with the Offer Sheet 77 (step 8).  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** – compiled to finalize the agreement (step 9).

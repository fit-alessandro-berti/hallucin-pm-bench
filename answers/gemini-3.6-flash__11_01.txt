**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** COO, Orion Insurance  
**From:** Senior Process-Mining Analyst  
**Subject:** Priority Waiting-Time Bottlenecks & Action Plan  

Process analysis identifies the three worst-performing activities strictly by waiting time:

1. **Request_Documents (#1 Worst)**
   * **Metrics:** 36.5h avg | 120.0h 95p waiting time
   * **Action:** Deploy an automated customer portal with automated SMS/email reminders to accelerate document submissions.

2. **Send_Closure_Letter (#2)**
   * **Metrics:** 12.2h avg | 44.0h 95p waiting time
   * **Action:** Implement trigger-based document generation to instantly email closure letters upon claim completion.

3. **Assess_Liability (#3)**
   * **Metrics:** 7.8h avg | 16.4h 95p waiting time
   * **Action:** Launch dynamic, load-balanced queue routing to immediately assign incoming assessments to available adjusters.

Implementing these targeted interventions will reduce waiting times by over 20% for each activity.

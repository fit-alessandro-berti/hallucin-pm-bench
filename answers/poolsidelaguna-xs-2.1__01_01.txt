### **Decision Points in Contoso’s Process**  
1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**  
   - **Decision**: Is the claim eligible?  
     - *Outcome*: If **ineligible**, the process stops. If **eligible**, proceed to Finance_Controller_PreApprove.  

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**  
   - **Decision**: Does the claim meet financial pre-approval criteria?  
     - *Outcome*: If **not approved**, the process stops. If **approved**, proceed to payment processing (with or without Senior_Auditor_Review, depending on claim value).  

3. **High-value Claim Check**  
   - **Decision**: Is the refund amount **greater than €1 000**?  
     - *Outcome*: If **yes**, the case proceeds to **Senior_Auditor_Review**. If **no**, skip to **Process_Refund_Payment**.  

4. **Process_Refund_Payment**  
   - **Decision**: Are all prerequisites met?  
     - *Prerequisites*: Finance_Controller_PreApprove **and** (for high-value claims) Senior_Auditor_Review.  
     - *Outcome*: Only begins if prerequisites are fully completed.  

---

### **Required Documents**  
1. **RefundRequestForm** (submitted by the passenger during **Customer_Submit_Claim**).  
2. **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport** (used by **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**).  
3. **EligibilityChecklist** (used by **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**).  
4. **AuditTrail** (required for **Senior_Auditor_Review** if the refund exceeds €1 000).  
5. **BankTransferAuthorization** (required to execute **Process_Refund_Payment**).  
6. **RefundConfirmationPDF** (issued after payment completion via **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**).  

---

**Constraints Observed**:  
- **Process_Refund_Payment** cannot start until both **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** and **Senior_Auditor_Review** (if applicable) are complete.  
- Legacy activity **CustomerService_Agent_Decide** is not part of this process.

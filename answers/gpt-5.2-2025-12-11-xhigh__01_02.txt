## Decision points (using the exact activity names)

- **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
  Proceed only once the ID card **and** mobile phone are confirmed (no credit history checks are performed).

- **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
  - If **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B** scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → continue.  
  - If the difference is **> 2 points** → escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue.

- **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**  
  Continue only after collecting at least **3 local references**.

- **Amount Threshold Decision**  
  - If **amount < €15 000** → system auto-approves.  
  - If **amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

- **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
  Board votes **approve** or **reject**.

## Required documents at submission time (every item)

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**
2. **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**
3. **Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)**
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

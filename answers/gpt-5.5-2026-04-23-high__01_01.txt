## Decision points in Contoso’s refund process

| Decision point | Condition / question | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Eligibility validation** | Does the claim satisfy eligibility checks against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**? | If eligibility is validated, the case can proceed to finance pre-approval. |
| **Finance pre-approval** | Has **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** succeeded using the **EligibilityChecklist**? | **Process_Refund_Payment** may not begin unless this pre-approval has succeeded. |
| **High-value refund branch** | Is the refund amount **greater than €1,000**? | If yes, the case must go to **Senior_Auditor_Review** before payment. If no, payment can proceed after successful finance pre-approval and signed payment authorization. |
| **High-value completion gate** | For claims over €1,000, has **Senior_Auditor_Review** been completed with the **AuditTrail**? | Payment may begin only after this review is completed. |
| **Payment authorization gate** | Is the signed **BankTransferAuthorization** present? | **Process_Refund_Payment** can be executed once it is present and all prior required approvals/reviews are complete. |

## Required documents

### Required for all claims
- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **BankTransferAuthorization**
- **RefundConfirmationPDF**

### Required only for high-value claims over €1,000
- **AuditTrail**

## Decision points

1. **Eligibility validation**  
   The triage agent checks the claim against the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport** and determines whether it satisfies the eligibility requirements.

2. **Finance pre-approval**  
   The finance controller reviews the **EligibilityChecklist** and either successfully signs off or does not. Successful pre-approval is required before payment.

3. **High-value determination**  
   The refund amount is checked against **€1,000**:
   - If it is **greater than €1,000**, the case must undergo **Senior_Auditor_Review**, including the **AuditTrail**.
   - Otherwise, the senior-auditor branch is not required.

4. **Payment authorization gate**  
   **Process_Refund_Payment** may begin only when:
   - **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** has succeeded;
   - for a refund greater than €1,000, **Senior_Auditor_Review** is complete; and
   - a signed **BankTransferAuthorization** is present.

There is no decision activity named **CustomerService_Agent_Decide** in Contoso’s process.

## Required documents and artefacts

- **RefundRequestForm** — completed by the passenger.
- **FlightManifest** — used for eligibility validation.
- **WeatherReport** — used for eligibility validation.
- **EligibilityChecklist** — used by the finance controller for pre-approval.
- **AuditTrail** — required for claims greater than €1,000 during senior-auditor review.
- **BankTransferAuthorization** — must be signed before payment.
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** — emailed to the passenger after payment.

To: Chief Operating Officer, Orion Credit
From: Senior Process-Performance Analyst
Subject: Process Performance Bottlenecks

This memo identifies three activities critically exceeding SLA targets driven by high waiting times. Legal Approval exceeds its SLA by 300s (900s actual vs. 600s target), burdened by a 600s waiting time. Risk Review similarly exceeds its SLA by 300s (600s actual vs. 300s target), with a 480s waiting time impacting all 480 cases. Finally, Credit Assessment exceeds its SLA by 60s (300s actual vs. 240s target), presenting a 200s waiting time across 1175 cases.

To address these bottlenecks, I recommend the following data-driven actions:
* Reallocate existing staff to Legal Approval to directly reduce the 600s waiting time.
* Implement prioritized queuing in Risk Review to mitigate the 480s waiting time across all 480 cases.
* Optimize resource allocation in Credit Assessment to eliminate the 200s waiting time impacting 1175 cases.

To meet the 48-hour SLA without adding new tasks, introduce a **non-interrupting boundary timer event** on `Task_Check` set to a 48-hour duration. 

When the timer fires, reconnect its outgoing sequence flow directly to `Task_Triage` (bypassing `GW_Docs` and `Task_RequestDocs`). This ensures that if document completeness checks take too long, the claim is automatically forced forward to `Task_Triage` within the mandatory timeframe.

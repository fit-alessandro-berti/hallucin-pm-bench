# RACI Matrix for AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Processes
| Task Description | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|-------------------|----------------------|---------------------|------------|
| Receive the customer’s cross-border transfer instruction and log it in the AtlasRemit system | R, A | I | I |
| Screen the payment instruction against international sanctions lists | R, A | I | I |
| Conduct mandatory KYC Review for both the payment sender and beneficiary | I | R, A | I |
| Approve any cross-border payment flagged as high-risk | I | R, A | I |
| Release the approved cross-border payment to the designated beneficiary bank | R, A | I | I |
| Ensure the official SWIFT payment message is transmitted to the beneficiary bank | I | I | R, A |
| Archive the complete case file for the processed payment | I | I | R, A |

### Definitions for this matrix:
- **R = Responsible**: Executes the task
- **A = Accountable**: Ultimate owner/approver of the task (only one per task)
- **I = Informed**: Kept updated on task completion
*(No Consult (C) entries apply to any tasks per the defined process)*

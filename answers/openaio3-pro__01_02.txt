Decision points in the Micro-loan Fast-Track (non-standard variant, May 2025):

1. Quick KYC Verification (KYC) – confirm ID card and mobile phone; pass or fail.  
2. Community Impact Assessment (CIA) – Impact Officer decides acceptability.  
3. Senior Underwriter A risk score (0–9) is set.  
4. Shadow Underwriter B risk score (0–9) is set.  
5. Underwriter Alignment Gate –  
   • If the two scores differ by ≤ 2 points, continue.  
   • Otherwise escalate to the Harmonisation Committee, then continue.  
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC) – must secure ≥ 3 local references.  
7. Amount Threshold Decision –  
   • If amount < €15 000, system auto-approves.  
   • If amount ≥ €15 000, move to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).  
8. Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) – board votes approve or reject.

Required documents (must be present at submission):

• Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
• Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
• Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
• Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
• Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
• Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

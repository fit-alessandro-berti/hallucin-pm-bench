**MEMO — To: COO, Orion Insurance**
**From: Process-Mining Analytics**
**Re: Top 3 Waiting-Time Bottlenecks & Recommended Actions**

Analysis of the last 12 months identifies the following activities as the worst performers by waiting time:

**#1 — Request_Documents**
Avg wait: **36.5 h** | 95p wait: **120.0 h**
*Action:* Deploy an automated customer portal with pre-filled document checklists and 24/48-hour reminder triggers, replacing manual email follow-ups. Expected wait reduction: 40–50%.

**#2 — Send_Closure_Letter**
Avg wait: **12.2 h** | 95p wait: **44.0 h**
*Action:* Auto-generate and dispatch closure letters via templated workflow immediately upon payment/rejection status change, removing the manual queue. Expected reduction: 60%+.

**#3 — Assess_Liability**
Avg wait: **7.8 h** | 95p wait: **16.4 h**
*Action:* Introduce rules-based auto-routing to pre-assign adjusters by claim type/complexity at screening, eliminating work-basket dwell time. Expected reduction: 25–30%.

Implementation sequencing by impact recommended.

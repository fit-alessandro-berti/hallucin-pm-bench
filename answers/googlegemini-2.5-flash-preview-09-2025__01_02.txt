Based on the Acme Bank’s Micro-loan Fast-Track process (May 2025 variant), here is the summary of the decision points and the list of required documents:

---

### Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track

| Step | Activity Name | Decision/Condition | Outcomes/Next Step |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **5** | **Underwriter Alignment Gate** | Do the scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by **≤ 2 points**? | **Yes:** Continue to step 6 (Neighbourhood Feedback Check). |
| | | | **No (Difference > 2 points):** Escalate to the Harmonisation Committee, then continue (to step 6). |
| **7** | **Amount Threshold Decision** | Is the amount requested **< €15 000**? | **Yes:** System auto-approves (then proceed to Disburse Funds). |
| | | Is the amount requested **≥ €15 000**? | **No:** Proceed to step 8 (Final Micro-loan Board Approval). |
| **8** | **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** | Board votes. | *Approve* or *Reject*. |

---

### Required Documents at Submission Time

The following documents must be available at submission time:

1. Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
2. Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
3. Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
4. Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
5. Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
6. Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

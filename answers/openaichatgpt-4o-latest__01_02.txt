**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   – Decision: Confirm both ID card and mobile phone match applicant profile.

2. **Community Impact Assessment (CIA)**  
   – Decision: Impact Officer assesses if the community benefit merits continued processing.

3. **Dual Underwriter Split**  
   – Decision:  
     • Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B each assign an independent risk score (0–9).

4. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   – Decision:  
     • If the risk scores differ by ≤ 2 points, proceed.  
     • If the difference is > 2 points, escalate case to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision.

5. **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**  
   – Decision: Confirm the submission of at least 3 valid local references.

6. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   – Decision:  
     • If **amount < €15 000**, system auto-approves.  
     • If **amount ≥ €15 000**, send to Final Micro-loan Board Approval.

7. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   – Decision: Micro-loan Board votes to either approve or reject the application.

---

**Required Documents at Submission Time**

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**  
2. **Proof of Address** (dated within the last 3 months)  
3. **Latest Income Statement** (either a single pay-stub or a tax slip)  
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS)** using the provided template  
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet** (includes scores from both underwriters)  
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)** documenting at least 3 local references

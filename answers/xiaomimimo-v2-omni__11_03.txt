**Executive Memo: Process Performance Review**

Analysis of the Q3 loan origination data identifies three activities with critical SLA breaches compounded by excessive waiting times, representing our primary operational bottlenecks.

*   **Legal Approval** is the most severe outlier, exceeding its 600-second SLA by 900 seconds, with an average waiting time of 600 seconds.
*   **Risk Review** surpasses its 300-second target by 780 seconds, featuring a waiting time of 480 seconds.
*   **Credit Assessment** breaches its 240-second SLA by 260 seconds, with a waiting time of 200 seconds.

To address these systemic delays, the following targeted interventions are recommended:

*   For **Legal Approval**, initiate a parallel document pre-check by the legal team during the preceding *Credit Assessment* stage to reduce sequential waiting.
*   For **Risk Review**, deploy an automated preliminary risk scoring model on all applications to fast-track low-risk cases and focus reviewer capacity on complex assessments.
*   For **Credit Assessment**, integrate real-time data feeds from credit bureaus to automate 50% of standard income and employment verification, directly cutting processing and waiting time.

**a) Decision Points (Gates/Conditional Branches)**

1.  **Step 4 Conditional Branch:** The decision to skip Manual Underwriting Review is made if the Tri-Color Risk Check yields a "Low" or "Medium" risk rating **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
2.  **Step 6 Decision Gate ("TriVote"):** The decision to proceed to prepare the offer requires a minimum of two approvals from the three designated reviewers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer).

**b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**

1.  **Form 14B** (Step 1: Receive Application)
2.  **Deck Memo** (Step 5: Manual Underwriting Review)
3.  **Offer Sheet 77** (Step 7: Prepare Binding Offer)
4.  **Signed Offer Sheet 77** (Step 8: Request Applicant Confirmation)
5.  **ID Matrix Declaration** (Step 8: Request Applicant Confirmation)
6.  **Loan Dossier 2025** (Step 9: Finalize Agreement)

**Decision points**  
- Quick KYC Verification (KYC)  
- Community Impact Assessment (CIA) by an Impact Officer  
- Dual Underwriter Split: Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B assign risk scores (0–9)  
- Underwriter Alignment Gate (escalates to Harmonisation Committee if scores differ by > 2)  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC) with at least 3 local references  
- Amount Threshold Decision (auto-approval if amount < €15 000; otherwise triggers further review)  
- Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA) for amounts ≥ €15 000  

**Required documents**  
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

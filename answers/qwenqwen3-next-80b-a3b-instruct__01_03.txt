**a) Decision Points (Gates or Conditional Branches) — One Sentence Each:**

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Gate**: The loan’s risk rating (Low, Medium, or High) is determined by combining outputs from three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow), and this rating triggers the next conditional branch.  
2. **Skip-Underwriting Conditional**: If the risk rating is Low *or* Medium *and* the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, then Manual-Underwriting-Review is bypassed; otherwise, it is required.  
3. **TriVote Decision Gate**: The loan advances only if at least two of the three approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) approve; a majority of two out of three is required, regardless of which two approve.  

> *Note: No other decision gates exist in this process. Steps 1, 6, and 8 involve documentation or workflow, but are not decision points with conditional logic. Only the above three contain explicit rules that alter the process flow.*

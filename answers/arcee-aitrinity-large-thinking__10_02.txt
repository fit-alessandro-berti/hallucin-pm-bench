
**RACI Matrix for AtlasRemit Cross-Border Payment Process**

| Task                                               | Responsible       | Accountable       | Consulted | Informed         |
|----------------------------------------------------|-------------------|-------------------|-----------|------------------|
| 1. Log transfer instruction                        | Transaction Analyst | Transaction Analyst | –         | –                |
| 2. Screen payment against sanctions list           | Transaction Analyst | Transaction Analyst | –         | –                |
| 3. Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary    | Regulatory Liaison  | Regulatory Liaison  | –         | –                |
| 4. Approve high-risk transaction                   | Regulatory Liaison  | Regulatory Liaison  | –         | Transaction Analyst |
| 5. Release payment                                 | Transaction Analyst | Transaction Analyst | –         | Regulatory Liaison  |
| 6. Send SWIFT message                              | Transaction Analyst | Transaction Analyst | –         | –                |
| 7. Archive case file and notify Regulatory Liaison | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison  | –         | Regulatory Liaison  |

**Notes:**
- All roles use only mandated terms: **Transaction Analyst** and **Regulatory Liaison**.
- IT Support functions are incorporated under the **Transaction Analyst** role for consistency with the mandated vocabulary.
- "Consulted" is marked as "–" where no specific consultation is described in the process.

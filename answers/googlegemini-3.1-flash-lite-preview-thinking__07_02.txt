As a process-mining analyst, I have reviewed the two versions of the *Loan_Process* BPMN XML. Here is the summary of changes:

**Added tasks**
*   **Task_VerifyIdentity:** New verification step added between the start event and the application review.
*   **Task_RequestDocs:** New handling step for missing documents.
*   **Task_FinalReject:** New final rejection stage (replaces the original Task_RejectLoan).

**Removed tasks**
*   **Task_RejectLoan:** This task was removed and replaced by the updated workflow path leading to `Task_FinalReject`. 
*(Note: While `Task_ApproveLoan` remains, it was effectively updated/re-defined in the model).*

**Gateway-type changes**
*   **Gateway_Decision:** Changed from an `exclusiveGateway` to an `inclusiveGateway`. This shifts the logic from a single-path choice to a potential multi-path or multi-conditional evaluation process.

**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**:  
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** (step 5):  
   - If the two underwriter risk scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue to step 6.  
   - Else, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision.  

2. **Amount Threshold Decision** (step 7):  
   - If **amount < €15 000**, the system auto-approves.  
   - If **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to step 8.  

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** (step 8):  
   - Board votes **approve** or **reject**.

- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)

- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet

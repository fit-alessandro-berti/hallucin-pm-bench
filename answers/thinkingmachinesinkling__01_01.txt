**Decision points (only those defined in the process)**

1. **Eligibility validation** — In *Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility* the agent decides if the claim is eligible by checking it against the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.
2. **Pre-approval sign-off** — In *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* the controller decides whether to sign off using the *EligibilityChecklist*.
3. **High-value branch** — If the refund amount is **> €1 000**, the case is routed to *Senior_Auditor_Review*; otherwise that step is skipped.
4. **Senior audit completion** — For high-value claims, *Senior_Auditor_Review* (which must include the *AuditTrail*) must be finished; its completion is a required gate.
5. **Payment-entry gate** — *Process_Refund_Payment* may begin only after *Finance_Controller_PreApprove* has succeeded and, for high-value claims, *Senior_Auditor_Review* has been completed, with a signed *BankTransferAuthorization* present.

**Required documents / artefacts (no others added)**

- *RefundRequestForm*
- *FlightManifest*
- *WeatherReport*
- *EligibilityChecklist*
- *AuditTrail* (required within *Senior_Auditor_Review* for claims > €1 000)
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (signed)
- *RefundConfirmationPDF*

*(The undefined legacy activity “CustomerService_Agent_Decide” is not part of this process and is not included.)*

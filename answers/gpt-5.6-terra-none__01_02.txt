### Decision points

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   - Confirm the applicant’s ID card and mobile phone.  
   - Credit history checks are skipped.

2. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   - Compare the scores assigned by **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**.  
   - If scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, continue to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  
   - If scores differ by **more than 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue.

3. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   - If the amount is **< €15 000**, the system auto-approves.  
   - If the amount is **≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

4. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   - The Micro-loan Board votes to **approve** or **reject**.  
   - Approved cases proceed to **Disburse Funds**.

### Required documents at submission time

- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

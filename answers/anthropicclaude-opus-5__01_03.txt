## a) Decision Points

| # | Where | Type | Rule (one sentence) |
|---|-------|------|---------------------|
| D1 | Step 3 → 4 | Conditional branch (routing) | If the Tri-Color-Risk-Check yields **Low or Medium** risk **AND** the requested amount is **≤ €250 000**, step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review) is skipped and the file goes straight to the TriVote gate; otherwise Manual-Underwriting-Review is performed. |
| D2 | Step 6 | Multi-party approval gate (**"TriVote"**) | The file passes the gate when **at least two of the three** designated approvers — Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer — give approval; failing that, the file does not proceed to the offer stage. |
| D3 | Step 8 | Implicit applicant-side gate | The process continues to Finalize-Agreement only if the applicant returns the **signed** Offer Sheet 77 together with the ID Matrix Declaration; without that confirmation the cycle stops. |

*Note on the risk rating itself:* Step 3 produces a **classification** (Low / Medium / High), not a decision — it is the *input* to decision point D1.

**Two internal tensions worth flagging in the specification of D2:**
- The step header says approvals are required "from all three," while the stated decision rule says "any two out of three suffice." As written, the **decision rule (2-of-3) governs**; the "all three" phrasing is best read as *all three must participate/vote*, not that all three must approve.
- Consistent with that reading: the Sustainability Officer's **participation** is mandatory in every loan cycle (including micro-loans), but their **approval** is not individually decisive — they can be outvoted by the other two.

## b) Documents Required (order of first appearance)

1. **Form 14B** — Receive-Application (step 1)
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** — Tri-Color-Risk-Check (step 3)
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** — Tri-Color-Risk-Check (step 3)
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** — Tri-Color-Risk-Check (step 3)
5. **Deck Memo** — Manual-Underwriting-Review (step 5) *(conditional: only when D1 routes the file through step 5)*
6. **Offer Sheet 77** — Prepare-Binding-Offer (step 7)
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** — Request-Applicant-Confirmation (step 8) *(the same instrument returned executed, not a new document)*
8. **ID Matrix Declaration** — Request-Applicant-Confirmation (step 8)
9. **Loan Dossier 2025** — Finalize-Agreement (step 9)

**Documents explicitly *not* part of this process:** no KYC/AML documentation (outsourced to a third-party vendor) and no credit-bureau report or pull beyond the three Scorecards used in the Tri-Color-Risk-Check.

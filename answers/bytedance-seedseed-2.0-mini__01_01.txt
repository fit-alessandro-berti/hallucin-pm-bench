### Decision Points in Contoso Airlines’ Flight Refund Process
1.  **Triage Agent Eligibility Validation Decision**: The triage agent compares the customer’s submitted claim against the FlightManifest and WeatherReport to confirm initial eligibility, routing valid claims to the finance pre-approval stage.
2.  **Finance Controller Pre-Approval Decision**: Using the EligibilityChecklist, the finance controller reviews the validated claim and grants pre-approval for further processing.
3.  **High-Value Refund Threshold Decision**: After pre-approval, the team checks if the refund amount exceeds €1,000 to route the claim:
    - For claims over the threshold: Send the case to Senior Auditor Review (with attached AuditTrail)
    - For claims at/under the threshold: Proceed directly to refund payment preparation

---
### Required Documents/Artefacts
All defined artefacts from the official process:
1.  RefundRequestForm (submitted by the customer to start the claim)
2.  FlightManifest (used for initial eligibility checks)
3.  WeatherReport (used for initial eligibility checks)
4.  EligibilityChecklist (used for finance pre-approval)
5.  AuditTrail (mandatory attachment for high-value Senior Auditor Review)
6.  BankTransferAuthorization (required prior to executing refund payments)
7.  RefundConfirmationPDF (emailed to the customer post-processing)

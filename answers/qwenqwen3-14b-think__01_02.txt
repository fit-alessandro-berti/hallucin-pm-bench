**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**  
1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** – Determine if the two underwriter scores differ by ≤ 2 points; escalate to Harmonisation Committee if not.  
2. **Amount Threshold Decision** – Auto-approve loans < €15 000; escalate to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** for ≥ €15 000.  
3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** – Board votes to approve or reject loans ≥ €15 000.  

**Required Documents at Submission Time**  
▪ Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
▪ Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
▪ Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
▪ Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
▪ Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
▪ Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

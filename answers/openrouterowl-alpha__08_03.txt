**Yes**, it is possible for transition **T5** to fire before **T3** in a reachable marking.

**Shortest firing sequence**:  
T1 → T2b → T4 → T5

**Explanation**:  
- In the initial marking, only T1 is fireable.
- After T1 fires, places **pa** and **pb** are marked.
- Now, both T2 and T2b are enabled from pa, and T4 is enabled from pb.
- Importantly, **T2b** (not T2) can fire directly to produce **pd** without going through **pc**.
- After T2b fires, **pd** is marked.
- Meanwhile, **pb** (still present) enables **T4**, which can now fire (concurrently or subsequently) to produce **pe**.
- Once both **pd** and **pe** are marked, **T5** becomes enabled and can fire.
- Crucially, **T3** was never enabled because **pc** was never marked (since T2 was never fired).

Thus, in this firing order, **T5 fires before T3**, satisfying the requirement.

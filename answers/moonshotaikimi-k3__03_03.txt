**Design notes (per the constraints):**

- **Step 2 loop:** The incomplete path routes through `requests missing information` and back into `validates the claim`, capturing the "wait until received, then re-validate" behavior without inventing extra events.
- **Gateways:** `Claim complete?` and `within warranty?` are exclusive gateways (with `complete` / `within warranty` as default flows); the step 4 fork/join uses a parallel gateway pair.
- **Exactly two end events:** `Claim rejected` (after `send rejection notice`) and `Claim closed` (after `close the claim`).
- All element IDs carry the `wrc_` prefix, and every task name reproduces the bolded source text verbatim.

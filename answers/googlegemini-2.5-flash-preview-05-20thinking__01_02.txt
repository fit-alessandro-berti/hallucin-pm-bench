**Decision Points in Acme's Micro-loan Fast-Track Process:**

*   **Underwriter Alignment Gate**: Decides whether to continue the process or escalate to the Harmonisation Committee based on the difference between the two underwriters' risk scores.
*   **Amount Threshold Decision**: Decides whether the application is auto-approved or proceeds to the Micro-loan Board Approval based on the loan amount.
*   **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**: The board votes to approve or reject the application.

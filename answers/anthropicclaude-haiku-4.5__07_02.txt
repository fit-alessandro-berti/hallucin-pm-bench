# BPMN Process Changes Summary

## Added tasks
- **Task_VerifyIdentity** – "Verify Identity" (inserted before Review Application)
- **Task_RequestDocs** – "Request Additional Documents" (new conditional branch)
- **Task_FinalReject** – "Final Reject" (replaces simple Reject with named variant)

## Removed tasks
- **Task_RejectLoan** – "Reject Loan" (replaced by Task_FinalReject)

## Gateway-type changes
- **Gateway_Decision**: **Exclusive → Inclusive**
  - v1: Single decision point (yes/no branching, mutually exclusive)
  - v2: Multiple simultaneous paths possible (eligible, missing docs, and ineligible can overlap in inclusive logic)
  - Also renamed: "Eligible?" → "Eligibility Check"

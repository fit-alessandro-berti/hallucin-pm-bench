Here are the details regarding Acme Bank’s small-business loan-approval process:

### **a) Decision Points Summary**

1.  **Step 4 – Conditional Routing:** The process determines whether to **skip** the Manual Underwriting Review if—and only if—the applicant’s risk rating is **Low or Medium** **AND** the requested amount is **€250,000 or less**; otherwise, the manual review is mandatory.
2.  **Step 6 – “TriVote” Approval Gate:** The loan is approved to proceed only if it receives affirmative votes from **at least two** of the three mandatory approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer).

***

### **b) Required Documents (in order of first appearance)**

1.  **Form 14B** *(Received during Application)*
2.  **Deck Memo** *(Created/Required during Manual Underwriting)*
3.  **Offer Sheet 77** *(Prepared during Offer stage)*
4.  **ID Matrix Declaration** *(Required during Applicant Confirmation)*
5.  **Loan Dossier 2025** *(Created during Finalization)*

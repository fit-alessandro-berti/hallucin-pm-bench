Attach a boundary timer event to **Task_RequestDocs** (ID: `Task_RequestDocs`) configured with a `timeDate` expression (e.g., `${submissionTime.plusHours(48)}`) that references the claim submission timestamp plus 48 hours. Connect this timer to a new escalation end event (e.g., `End_Escalated`). Since `Task_RequestDocs` is the only waiting state before `Task_Triage`, this guarantees auto-escalation if the claim hasn’t reached Triage within the SLA. No new tasks or subprocesses are added.

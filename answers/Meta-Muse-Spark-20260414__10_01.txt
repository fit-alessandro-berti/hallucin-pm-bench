Here is the RACI matrix for FinCo’s order-to-cash workflow using the required terminology:

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Receive Application** | R, A | I | I | I |
| **KYC Review** | C, I | R, A | I | I |
| **Order Vetting** | R, A | C | I | I |
| **Payment Clearance** | C, I | I | R, A | I |
| **Record Update** | I | I | C, I | R, A |
| **Notify Customer** | R, A | I | I | I |

**Legend**  
- **R** = Responsible: Does the work  
- **A** = Accountable: Final approver/owner  
- **C** = Consulted: Provides input before work is done  
- **I** = Informed: Updated after work is completed

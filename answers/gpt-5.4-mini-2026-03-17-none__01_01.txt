Contoso’s refund process has one decision point:

1. **High-value branch decision**
   - **Condition:** whether the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**
   - **If yes:** the case must go to **Senior_Auditor_Review** and include the **AuditTrail**
   - **If no:** the case skips that review and can proceed toward payment after **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**

Required documents / artefacts defined in the process:

- **RefundRequestForm**
- **FlightManifest**
- **WeatherReport**
- **EligibilityChecklist**
- **AuditTrail**
- **BankTransferAuthorization**
- **RefundConfirmationPDF**

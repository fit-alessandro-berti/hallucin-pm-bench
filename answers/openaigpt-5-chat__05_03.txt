Here’s the **minimal BPMN 2.0 XML change** to meet the 48h SLA without adding new tasks:  

```xml
<bpmn:boundaryEvent id="Boundary_Timer_48h" attachedToRef="Activity_CreateAccount">
  <bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
    <bpmn:timeDuration>PT48H</bpmn:timeDuration>
  </bpmn:timerEventDefinition>
</bpmn:boundaryEvent>

<bpmn:sequenceFlow id="Flow_TimerToAM" sourceRef="Boundary_Timer_48h" targetRef="Activity_AMReview"/>
```

**Justification:**  
By attaching a **48-hour timer boundary event** to “Create Account in Core System” and routing overdue cases to the existing “Account-Manager Review” task, SLA breaches are automatically detected and escalated without introducing any new activities.

**Decision Points in Acme’s Micro-loan Fast-Track Process**

1. **Underwriter Alignment Gate** –  
   • If the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by **≤ 2 points**, proceed to step 6.  
   • If they differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision before proceeding.

2. **Amount Threshold Decision** –  
   • If the requested **amount < €15 000**, the system **auto-approves**.  
   • If the requested **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

3. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)** –  
   • The Micro-loan Board votes to either **approve** or **reject** the application.

**Required Documents at Submission Time**

- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)  
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)  
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)  
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template  
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet  
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

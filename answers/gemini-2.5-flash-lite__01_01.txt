Here are the decision points in Contoso Airlines' flight-refund process and their associated required documents:

**Decision Point:**

*   **Refund amount greater than €1 000?**
    *   **If Yes (High-value claim):** Proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**.
    *   **If No (Standard claim):** Bypass **Senior_Auditor_Review**.

**Required Documents per Activity:**

*   **Customer_Submit_Claim:** *RefundRequestForm*
*   **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility:** *FlightManifest*, *WeatherReport*
*   **Finance_Controller_PreApprove:** *EligibilityChecklist*
*   **Senior_Auditor_Review (for high-value claims):** *AuditTrail*
*   **Process_Refund_Payment:** *BankTransferAuthorization*
*   **Issue_Confirmation_Letter:** *RefundConfirmationPDF*

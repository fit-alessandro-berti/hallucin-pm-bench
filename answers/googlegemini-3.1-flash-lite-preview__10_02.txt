Subject: RACI Matrix for Cross-Border Payment Workflow  

The following matrix delineates the roles and responsibilities for the AtlasRemit payment workflow, ensuring adherence to standardized terminology and internal oversight protocols.

### RACI Matrix: Cross-Border Payment Workflow

| Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Receive and log transfer instruction | **R** | I | I |
| Screen payment against sanctions list | **R** | A | I |
| Conduct **KYC Review** (Sender & Beneficiary) | C | **A** | I |
| Approve high-risk transaction | C | **A** | I |
| Release payment | **R** | I | C |
| Transmit SWIFT message | I | I | **R** |
| Archive case file | I | I | **R** |
| Notification of case closure | I | **A** | R |

***

**Role Definitions:**
*   **Responsible (R):** The person who performs the work to achieve the task.
*   **Accountable (A):** The person who is ultimately answerable for the correct and thorough completion of the task.
*   **Consulted (C):** People who provide input or feedback based on their specific expertise.
*   **Informed (I):** People who are kept up-to-date on progress or completion of tasks.

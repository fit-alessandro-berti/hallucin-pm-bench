# RACI Matrix — Order-to-Cash Workflow

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Receive Application | **R, A** | I | — | — |
| 2. KYC Review | I | **R, A** | — | — |
| 3. Order Vetting | **R, A** | C | C | — |
| 4. Payment Clearance | I | C | **R, A** | — |
| 5. Record Update | I | — | C | **R, A** |
| 6. Notify Customer | **R, A** | I | I | I |

**Legend:** R = Responsible · A = Accountable · C = Consulted · I = Informed

### Notes
- The **Account Manager** owns the customer-facing bookends (Receive Application, Notify Customer) and data-completeness checks at **Order Vetting**.
- The **Compliance Officer** is fully accountable for **KYC Review** and consulted on Order Vetting to confirm no compliance flags remain.
- The **Finance Clerk** drives **Payment Clearance** and is consulted on the Record Update to ensure settlement data reconciles in the CRM.
- **IT Support** is accountable for the Record Update step only, keeping the CRM source-of-truth accurate.

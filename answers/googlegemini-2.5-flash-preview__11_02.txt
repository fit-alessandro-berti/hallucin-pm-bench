**MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** COO, Acme Insurance
**FROM:** Senior Process Performance Analyst
**DATE:** October 25, 2024
**SUBJECT:** Q1 2025 Claims Handling Process Performance Summary

This memo highlights key performance issues within the Claims Handling process during Q1 2025.

**Worst Performing Activities:**

1.  **Request Documents:** Exhibits the longest average wait time (150 minutes) and the highest rework rate (22.0%), significantly impacting throughput.
    *   **Recommendation:** Investigate triggers for document requests and explore automation for initial document verification to reduce wait and rework.

2.  **Review Documents:** Shows a high average processing time (25 minutes), a significant standard deviation (9 minutes), and the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%).
    *   **Recommendation:** Analyze root causes of processing variability and SLA breaches. Consider implementing standardized checklists or automated document analysis tools.

3.  **Initial Assessment:** Records a relatively high average wait time (18 minutes) and SLA breach rate (3.2%). While others are worse, this is a critical early step.
    *   **Recommendation:** Review resource allocation and workload balancing for Initial Assessment activities to reduce wait times and improve adherence to SLAs.

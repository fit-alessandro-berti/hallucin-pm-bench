**MEMO**
**To:** COO
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Process Bottlenecks

The three worst-performing activities are Request Documents, Review Documents, and Initial Assessment.

1. **Request Documents:** Suffers from extreme queueing (150 min wait) and a massive 22% rework rate. This indicates missing or invalid inputs are stalling the workflow.
*Action:* Implement automated pre-submission validation on the customer portal to eliminate incomplete requests before they enter the queue.

2. **Review Documents:** Has the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), driven by prolonged execution (25 min) and high variability (stdev 9 min), suggesting inconsistent manual evaluations.
*Action:* Deploy mandatory decision-support checklists to standardize reviews and reduce processing variance.

3. **Initial Assessment:** Exhibits an 18-minute wait time and 3.2% SLA breach despite a fast 12-minute execution time, pointing strictly to a capacity constraint rather than an efficiency issue.
*Action:* Reallocate adjuster staffing to this step to clear the upstream queue.

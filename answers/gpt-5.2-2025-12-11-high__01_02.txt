## Decision points (using the exact activity names)

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   - Decision: KYC passes only if **ID card** and **mobile phone** are confirmed (with **no credit history checks**). If not confirmed, the application cannot proceed.

2. **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   - Decision: Compare the two risk scores from **Senior Underwriter A** and **Shadow Underwriter B**.  
     - If the scores differ by **≤ 2 points** → continue.  
     - If the scores differ by **> 2 points** → escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee** for a tie-break decision, then continue.

3. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   - Decision: Based on the requested amount.  
     - If **amount < €15 000** → the system **auto-approves**.  
     - If **amount ≥ €15 000** → proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

4. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   - Decision: Board vote outcome is **approve** or **reject**.

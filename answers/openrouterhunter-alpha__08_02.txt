Yes, it is possible.  

A minimal firing sequence is: **T1 → T2 → T7 → T4 → T5**.  

In this sequence, after T1 and T2 fire, T7 fires instead of T3, creating a token in P4 without consuming P2. T4 then fires, producing P5. With P2 still marked (from T1) and P5 now marked, T5 can fire before T3 ever fires.

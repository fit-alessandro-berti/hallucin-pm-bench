**Decision points**

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** — determines whether the claim is eligible based on the *FlightManifest* and *WeatherReport*.
2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** — determines whether the claim meets the eligibility checklist for payment processing.
3. **High-value branch** — triggered if the refund amount is **greater than €1 000**, requiring **Senior_Auditor_Review**.

**Required documents**

- *RefundRequestForm* (submitted by the customer)
- *FlightManifest*
- *WeatherReport*
- *EligibilityChecklist* (used by Finance_Controller)
- *AuditTrail* (required within Senior_Auditor_Review for high-value claims)
- *BankTransferAuthorization* (required for Process_Refund_Payment)
- *RefundConfirmationPDF* (issued to the passenger)

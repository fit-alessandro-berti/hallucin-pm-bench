A minimal firing sequence is:

**[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]**

After **T1**, tokens are in **P1** and **P2**. The token from **P1** proceeds through **T2 → T7 → T4**, producing a token in **P5**, while the token in **P2** remains untouched. Thus, both input places of **T5**—**P2** and **P5**—contain tokens, enabling **T5** before **T3** has fired.

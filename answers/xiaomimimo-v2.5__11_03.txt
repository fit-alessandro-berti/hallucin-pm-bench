**To:** Chief Operating Officer  
**From:** Process-Performance Analysis  
**Subject:** Key Process Bottlenecks and Recommended Actions  

Based on joint analysis of SLA breaches and waiting times, three activities require urgent intervention.  

1.  **Risk Review:** Throughput of 600 seconds breaches the 300-second SLA by **300 seconds**; waiting time is 480 seconds.  
2.  **Legal Approval:** Throughput of 900 seconds breaches the 600-second SLA by **300 seconds**; waiting time is 600 seconds.  
3.  **Credit Assessment:** Throughput of 300 seconds breaches the 240-second SLA by **60 seconds**; waiting time is 200 seconds.  

**Recommended Actions:**  
*   For Risk Review: Deploy parallel case review tracks to reduce both active processing and queue time.

*   For Credit Assessment: Automate initial data aggregation to reduce manual processing steps.

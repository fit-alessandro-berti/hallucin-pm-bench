**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**Subject:** Q1-2025 Claims Handling Performance – Critical Bottlenecks  

The three worst-performing activities are **Request_Documents**, **Review_Documents**, and **Initial_Assessment**.

1.  **Request_Documents:** It has the highest wait time (150 min) and rework rate (22.0%), indicating customers submit incomplete documents, causing queue delays and repetition.
    *   *Action:* Implement an automated document-check at submission to flag omissions instantly.

2.  **Review_Documents:** It shows the longest processing time (25 min) with high variability (stdev 9 min) and the highest SLA breach rate (12.4%), suggesting inconsistent, complex reviews.
    *   *Action:* Develop a standardized review protocol and decision matrix to reduce processing time and variability.

3.  **Initial_Assessment:** It has a high wait time (18 min) and the third-highest SLA breach rate (3.2%), creating an early backlog that impacts downstream flow.
    *   *Action:* Analyze case complexity distribution and fast-track straightforward claims to reduce queue time.

These three activities form a primary bottleneck, stalling the entire process from the second step.

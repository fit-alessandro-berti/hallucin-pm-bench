Decision points
- Eligibility validation (Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility)
  - Criteria: Claim matches FlightManifest and weather conditions in WeatherReport.
  - Outcome: Only eligible claims proceed to Finance_Controller_PreApprove.

- Finance pre-approval (Finance_Controller_PreApprove)
  - Criteria: Finance controller signs off using the EligibilityChecklist.
  - Outcome: If pre-approved, the case can move toward payment (subject to the high-value rule and payment prerequisites).

- High-value branch
  - Criteria: Refund amount greater than €1 000.
  - Outcome: If true, the case must go to Senior_Auditor_Review and include the AuditTrail before payment can proceed; if false (≤ €1 000), this review is skipped.

- Payment readiness gate (Process_Refund_Payment)
  - Criteria: Finance_Controller_PreApprove completed; for high-value claims, Senior_Auditor_Review completed; signed BankTransferAuthorization present.
  - Outcome: Only when all applicable conditions are met is the refund payment executed.

Required documents
- RefundRequestForm
- FlightManifest
- WeatherReport
- EligibilityChecklist
- AuditTrail (only for high-value claims in Senior_Auditor_Review)
- BankTransferAuthorization (signed, required for payment)
- RefundConfirmationPDF

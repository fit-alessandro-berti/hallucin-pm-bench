**a) Decision points:**  
1. **Step 4 conditional branch:** If the risk rating is *Low* or *Medium* **and** the requested loan amount is ≤ €250,000, skip Manual-Underwriting-Review (Step 5).  
2. **Step 6 decision gate (TriVote):** The loan must receive at least two approvals out of three from the Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer to pass.

**b) Documents in order of first appearance:**  
1. **Form 14B** (application)  
2. **Deck Memo** (manual underwriting review)  
3. **Offer Sheet 77** (binding offer)  
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** (applicant confirmation)  
5. **Loan Dossier 2025** (final agreement)

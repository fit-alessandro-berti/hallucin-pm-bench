**Legend:**

- **Responsible**: Performs the tasks.
- **Accountable**: Final decision-maker, ensures completion.
- **Consulted**: Provides input or guidance.
- **Informed**: Receives updates or outcomes of tasks.

This structure aligns with the mandated vocabulary and clearly defines the roles for each process step.

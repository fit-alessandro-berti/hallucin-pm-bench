Below is the RACI matrix for the AtlasRemit cross-border payment process, strictly adhering to the mandated vocabulary. 

| Process Task | Transaction Analyst | Regulatory Liaison | IT Support |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **1. Log transfer instruction** | R/A | I | - |
| **2. Screen payment against sanctions list** | R | A | - |
| **3. Perform KYC Review on sender and beneficiary** | C | R/A | - |
| **4. Approve high-risk transaction** | I | R/A | - |
| **5. Release payment** | R | I | I |
| **6. Ensure SWIFT message is sent** | - | - | R/A |
| **7. Archive case file** | - | I | R/A |

**RACI Legend:**
* **R (Responsible):** The role performing the work.
* **A (Accountable):** The role ultimately answerable for the task's completion and quality.
* **C (Consulted):** The role providing input or expertise for the task.
* **I (Informed):** The role kept updated on progress or completion.

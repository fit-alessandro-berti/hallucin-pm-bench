**Subject:** Q1 Claims Performance: Top 3 Bottlenecks  

Three activities require immediate intervention:

1.  **Request_Documents**: Severe bottleneck. **Wait time is 150 min** (vs. ≤30 elsewhere) and **rework is 22.0%** (vs. ≤5.3%), causing throughput to collapse from 270 to 190 cases/day.  
    *Action:* Implement automated document requests with validation rules to eliminate incomplete submissions driving the 22% rework loop.

2.  **Review_Documents**: Highest SLA risk. **SLA breaches peak at 12.4%**. Processing averages **25 min** with high variability (**stdev 9 min**), indicating inconsistent execution.  
    *Action:* Deploy standardized review checklists to reduce variability and processing time.

3.  **Initial_Assessment**: Queueing inefficiency. **Wait time is 18 min** (second highest) and **SLA breaches are 3.2%** (third highest), disproportionate to its 12-min processing time.  
    *Action:* Optimize auto-routing rules to reduce the 18-minute queue.

All other activities show SLA breaches ≤1.5% and rework ≤0.8%, performing within acceptable limits.

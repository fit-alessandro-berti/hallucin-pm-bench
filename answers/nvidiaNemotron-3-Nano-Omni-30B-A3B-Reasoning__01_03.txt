**a) Decision points (gates / conditional branches)**  
1. **Assign‑Deck‑Reviewer gate** – after receiving Form 14B, the application is assigned to a Deck Officer; this is the first internal hand‑off.  
2. **Tri‑Color‑Risk‑Check gate** – the three scorecards are evaluated; the combined risk rating is classified as Low, Medium, or High.  
3. **Amount‑Threshold conditional branch** – if the requested amount is ≤ €250 000 **and** the risk rating is Low or Medium, the application bypasses Manual‑Underwriting; otherwise it proceeds to step 5.  
4. **TriVote approval gate** – after manual underwriting (or directly after the risk check when the amount‑threshold condition is met), approvals from any two of the three designated officers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) are required to pass.  

**b) Documents in order of first appearance**  
1. Form 14B  
2. Scorecard‑Cyan  
3. Scorecard‑Magenta  
4. Scorecard‑Yellow  
5. Deck Memo

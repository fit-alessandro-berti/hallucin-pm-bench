Decision points
- Underwriter Alignment Gate: If the risk scores from Senior Underwriter A and Shadow Underwriter B differ by ≤ 2 points, continue. Else, escalate to the Harmonisation Committee for a tie-break decision, then continue.
- Amount Threshold Decision: If amount < €15 000, the system auto-approves. If amount ≥ €15 000, proceed to Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA).
- Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA): The Micro-loan Board votes approve or reject.
- Harmonisation Committee (within the Underwriter Alignment Gate path when triggered): Issues the tie-break decision before continuing.

Required documents at submission time
- Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)
- Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)
- Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)
- Community Impact Statement (CIS) template
- Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet
- Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)

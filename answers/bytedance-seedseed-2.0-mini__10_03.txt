# RACI Matrix
| Activity | Front-Office Analyst | Compliance Analyst | Compliance Officer | Treasury Ops |
|----------|----------------------|--------------------|--------------------|--------------|
| Intake Vetting | R, A | I | — | — |
| KYC Review | I | R, A | I | — |
| AML Screening | I | R, A | I | — |
| Risk Scoring | I | R, A | I | — |
| Escalate cases with Risk Scoring >70 to Compliance Officer | I | R, A | I | — |
| Compliance Officer Approval | I | I | R, A | I |
| Treasury Ops Schedule Payment | I | I | I | R, A |
| Treasury Ops Release Funds | I | I | I | R, A |
| Treasury Ops Notify Originating Analyst | I | I | I | R, A |

### Definitions used:
- **R = Responsible**: Executes the activity
- **A = Accountable**: Owns final approval/answerability for the activity
- **I = Informed**: Receiving post-activity updates
- **— = No involvement in the activity**

All restricted vocabulary terms are used exactly as required per policy: *Intake Vetting*, *KYC Review*, *AML Screening*, *Risk Scoring*. No forbidden synonyms or alternative phrasing are included.

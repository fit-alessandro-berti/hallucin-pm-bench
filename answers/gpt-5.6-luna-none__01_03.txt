### a) Decision points

1. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check:** The Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow scorecards produce a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High.  
2. **Underwriting branch:** Manual Underwriting Review is skipped when the risk is Low or Medium **and** the requested amount is no more than €250,000; otherwise, it is performed.  
3. **TriVote Decision-Gate:** The application passes when at least two of the three mandatory participants—Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer—approve.

### b) Required documents, in order of first appearance

1. **Form 14B**
2. **Scorecard–Cyan**
3. **Scorecard–Magenta**
4. **Scorecard–Yellow**
5. **Deck Memo** *(required only when the underwriting branch is not skipped)*
6. **Offer Sheet 77**
7. **Signed Offer Sheet 77**
8. **ID Matrix Declaration**
9. **Loan Dossier 2025**

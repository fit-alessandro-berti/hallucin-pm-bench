**Question 2:** A minimal firing sequence is:

**[T1, T2, T7, T4, T5]**

After T1, tokens are in P1 and P2. The path T2 → T7 → T4 moves the P1 token to P5 while leaving the P2 token untouched. The resulting marking is {P2, P5}, enabling T5. Thus, T5 fires without T3 having fired.

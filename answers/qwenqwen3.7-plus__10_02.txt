### **RACI Legend**
*   **R (Responsible):** The role that performs the work to complete the task.
*   **A (Accountable):** The role that is ultimately answerable for the correct and thorough completion of the task (only one per task).
*   **C (Consulted):** The role that provides subject-matter input prior to task completion (two-way communication).
*   **I (Informed):** The role that is notified after the task is completed (one-way communication). 

*Vocabulary Compliance Note: The matrix exclusively utilizes "KYC Review", "Regulatory Liaison", and "Transaction Analyst" for their respective activities and roles, strictly avoiding all non-mandated synonyms as directed.*

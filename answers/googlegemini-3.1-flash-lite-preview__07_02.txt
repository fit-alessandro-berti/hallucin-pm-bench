As a process-mining analyst, I have reviewed the two versions of the *Loan_Process* BPMN XML. Below is the summary of the structural changes:

**Added tasks**
*   **Task_VerifyIdentity:** Introduced as the initial step following the start event.
*   **Task_RequestDocs:** Introduced as a new branch to handle cases with missing documentation.
*   **Task_FinalReject:** Introduced to replace the original rejection task.

**Removed tasks**
*   **Task_RejectLoan:** This task was removed from the process flow and replaced by the new *Final Reject* task.

**Gateway-type changes**
*   **Gateway_Decision:** The gateway type has been changed from an **Exclusive Gateway** to an **Inclusive Gateway**.

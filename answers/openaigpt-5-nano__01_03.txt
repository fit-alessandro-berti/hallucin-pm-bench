a) Decision points (gate or conditional branch) and their rules

- Step 3 Tri-Color-Risk-Check: Tri-Color-Risk-Check outputs a risk rating of Low, Medium, or High based on inputs Scorecard–Cyan, Scorecard–Magenta, Scorecard–Yellow.

- Step 4 Conditional branch: If risk ∈ {Low, Medium} and requested-amount ≤ €250,000, skip Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review); otherwise proceed to Step 5.

- Step 6 Decision-Gate “TriVote”: The loan passes when at least two of the three approvers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer) have approved; if fewer than two approve, the loan does not pass.

b) Documents required, in the order they first appear

1) Form 14B

2) Deck Memo

3) Offer Sheet 77

4) Signed Offer Sheet 77

5) ID Matrix Declaration

6) Loan Dossier 2025

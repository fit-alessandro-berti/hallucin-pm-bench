**From:** Senior Process-Performance Analyst  
**Subject:** Critical Bottlenecks in Credit Process  

Our analysis identifies three activities that jointly breach SLAs and suffer from high waiting times, creating major bottlenecks.

1.  **Risk Review** exceeds its 300s SLA by 300s (total 600s) with an average waiting time of 480s.
2.  **Legal Approval** exceeds its 600s SLA by 300s (total 900s) with an average waiting time of 600s.
3.  **Credit Assessment** exceeds its 240s SLA by 60s (total 300s) with an average waiting time of 200s.

These delays directly impact case cycle time and customer experience.

*   **For Risk Review:** Implement automated pre-screening rules to reduce the manual review queue, targeting a 50% reduction in the 480s waiting time.
*   **For Legal Approval:** Introduce a parallel digital approval workflow to replace sequential reviews, aiming to cut the 600s waiting time.
*   **For Credit Assessment:** Refine the initial scoring model to automate straightforward cases, reducing the 200s waiting time and assessment workload.

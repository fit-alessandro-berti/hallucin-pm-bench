**Decision Points**  
1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility** – the triage agent decides whether the claim is eligible by checking the *RefundRequestForm* against the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.  
2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** – the finance controller decides whether to pre‑approve the claim using the *EligibilityChecklist*.  
3. **High‑value threshold check** – an automatic branching decision: if the refund amount **> €1 000**, the case is routed to **Senior_Auditor_Review**; otherwise it skips that step.  
4. **Senior_Auditor_Review** – the senior auditor decides whether the high‑value claim passes audit scrutiny, referencing the *AuditTrail*.

**Required Documents / Artefacts**  
1. *RefundRequestForm* (submitted by the passenger)  
2. *FlightManifest* (used in triage)  
3. *WeatherReport* (used in triage)  
4. *EligibilityChecklist* (used by the finance controller)  
5. *AuditTrail* (required for Senior_Auditor_Review on high‑value claims)  
6. *BankTransferAuthorization* (must be signed before payment execution)  
7. *RefundConfirmationPDF* (emailed to the passenger after payment)

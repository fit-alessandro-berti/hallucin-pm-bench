As a senior process-mapping consultant for FinCo, I have constructed the RACI (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed) matrix for our order-to-cash workflow. 

This matrix ensures clear ownership and communication across all departments, using our updated, standard terminology.

### FinCo Order-to-Cash RACI Matrix

| Activity | Account Manager (AM) | Compliance Officer (CO) | Finance Clerk (FC) | IT Support (IT) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **1. Receive Application** | **R / A** | - | - | - |
| **2. KYC Review** | **I** | **R / A** | - | - |
| **3. Order Vetting** | **R / A** | **C** | - | - |
| **4. Payment Clearance** | **I** | - | **R / A** | - |
| **5. Record Update** | **I** | - | **I** | **R / A** |
| **6. Notify Customer** | **R / A** | - | - | - |

***

### Key Legend:
*   **R (Responsible):** The role that performs the activity/work.
*   **A (Accountable):** The role with final approval power and ultimate ownership of the step's success (only one "A" designated per activity).
*   **C (Consulted):** The role that provides input, feedback, or specialized knowledge prior to or during the step.
*   **I (Informed):** The role kept up-to-date on progress or outcomes after the step is completed.

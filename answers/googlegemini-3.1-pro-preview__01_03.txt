**a) Decision Points (Gates and Conditional Branches)**

*   **Conditional Branch (Step 4):** 
    Step 5 (Manual-Underwriting-Review) is skipped if the applicant's risk rating from Step 3 is "Low" or "Medium" **and** the requested loan amount is €250,000 or less.
*   **Decision-Gate “TriVote” (Step 6):** 
    The loan application passes if it receives approval from any two of the three mandated reviewers (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer).

**b) Required Documents (in order of appearance)**

1. **Form 14B** *(Step 1 - Receive-Application)*
2. **Scorecard–Cyan** *(Step 3 - Tri-Color-Risk-Check inputs)*
3. **Scorecard–Magenta** *(Step 3 - Tri-Color-Risk-Check inputs)*
4. **Scorecard–Yellow** *(Step 3 - Tri-Color-Risk-Check inputs)*
5. **Deck Memo** *(Step 5 - Manual-Underwriting-Review; Note: skipped if Step 4 conditions are met)*
6. **Offer Sheet 77 / Signed Offer Sheet 77** *(Step 7 & 8 - Prepare-Binding-Offer / Request-Applicant-Confirmation)*
7. **ID Matrix Declaration** *(Step 8 - Request-Applicant-Confirmation)*
8. **Loan Dossier 2025** *(Step 9 - Finalize-Agreement)*

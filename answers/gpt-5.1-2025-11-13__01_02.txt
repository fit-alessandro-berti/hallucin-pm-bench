Decision points in the **Micro-loan Fast-Track** process  
(using the exact activity names):

1. **Quick KYC Verification (KYC)**  
   - Decision: Acceptable ID card and mobile phone confirmed?  
   - If not, the process cannot proceed.

2. **Dual Underwriter Split** / **Underwriter Alignment Gate**  
   - **Senior Underwriter A** assigns a risk score (0–9).  
   - **Shadow Underwriter B** assigns a risk score (0–9).  
   - **Underwriter Alignment Gate** decision:  
     - If the two scores differ by **≤ 2 points**, proceed to **Neighbourhood Feedback Check (NFC)**.  
     - If they differ by **> 2 points**, escalate to the **Harmonisation Committee**, which issues a tie-break decision, then proceed.

3. **Amount Threshold Decision**  
   - If **amount < €15 000**, the system **auto-approves** (no **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**).  
   - If **amount ≥ €15 000**, proceed to **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**.

4. **Final Micro-loan Board Approval (MBA)**  
   - Decision: Micro-loan Board votes *approve* or *reject*.  
   - If *approve*, proceed to **Disburse Funds**.  
   - If *reject*, funds are not disbursed.

---

Required documents at submission time:

1. **Digital Application Form (ML-APP-01)**  
2. **Proof of Address (≤ 3 months old)**  
3. **Latest Income Statement (single pay-stub or tax slip)**  
4. **Community Impact Statement (CIS) template**  
5. **Dual Underwriter Score-Sheet**  
6. **Neighbourhood Feedback Form (NFF)**

Yes.  
Because of the shortcut transition **T2b** (`pa -> pd`), **T5** does **not** depend on **T3**.

One shortest firing sequence is:

1. **T1** : `p0 -> pa + pb`
2. **T2b** : `pa -> pd`
3. **T4** : `pb -> pe`
4. **T5** : `pd + pe -> pf`

So **T5 fires before T3**, and **T3 has not fired at all** in this sequence.

Why this is shortest:
- **T1** is necessary to create `pa` and `pb`
- then one transition is needed to get `pd` (`T2b`)
- one transition is needed to get `pe` (`T4`)
- then **T5** can fire

So the minimum length is **4 transitions**.

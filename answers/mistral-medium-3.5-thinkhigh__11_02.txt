The three worst-performing activities are **Review_Documents**, **Request_Documents**, and **Initial_Assessment**.

**Initial_Assessment** shows elevated wait (18 min), processing (12 min) times, and above-average rework (1.8%) and SLA breaches (3.2%). *Action*: Streamline the assessment workflow to reduce wait and processing times.

These targeted actions address the most critical inefficiencies per the Q1 dashboard.

### a) Summary of Decision Points in Acme’s Process

1. **Receive-Application** – Submission of the application with required documents.
2. **Assign-Deck-Reviewer** – Assigns a Deck Officer to evaluate the application.
3. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check** – Assesses risk at Low, Medium, or High based on scorecards.
4. **Decision-Gate “TriVote”** – The outcome is conditional to receive two prior approvals to pass (Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, Sustainability Officer).
5. **Manual-Underwriting-Review** – Additional review by underwriter for final approval.
6. **Prepare-Binding-Offer (Offer Sheet 77)** – Value and terms of the loan are communicated to the applicant.
7. **Request-Applicant-Confirmation** – Idea to confirm acceptance, including documents and KYC.
8. **Finalize-Agreement** – Final signing and documentation are finalized into the Loan Dossier.

---

### b) Required Documents, in Order of First Appearance

1. **Form 14B** – Mandatory application document.
2. **Deck Memo** – Result of manual underwriting review.
3. **Offer Sheet 77** – Formal binding offer issued after underwriting.
4. **ID Matrix Declaration** – All KYC/AML records to verify applicant identity.
5. **Signed Offer Sheet 77** – Applicant signs the binding offer.
6. **Loan Dossier 2025** – Final detailed agreement with all supporting documentation.
7. **Tri-Color-Risk-Check Scorecard** – Internal assessment results from scoring.
8. **Tri-Color-Risk-Rating** – Determination of Low, Medium, or High risk.
9. **Preparation of Ownership/Confidentiality Agreements** – Required for the binding offer and confirmation steps.

a) Decision points

- Tri-Color-Risk-Check outcome: The three scorecards (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) are evaluated to produce a single risk rating of Low, Medium, or High.  
- Conditional branch after risk check: If the risk rating is Low or Medium and the requested amount is ≤ €250,000, the process skips Manual-Underwriting-Review; otherwise step 5 is performed.  
- Decision Gate “TriVote”: Credit Lead, Regulatory Liaison, and Sustainability Officer each vote, and the application passes if at least two of the three approve.

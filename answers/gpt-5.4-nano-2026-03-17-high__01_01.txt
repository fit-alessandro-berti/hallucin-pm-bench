## Decision points (all that exist in the described process)

1. **Triage decision — eligibility**
   - **Where:** **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**
   - **Decision:** Whether the refund claim is **eligible** based on the **FlightManifest** and **WeatherReport**.
   - **Effect:** The claim proceeds only if eligibility is validated.

2. **High-value branch**
   - **Where:** **Finance_Controller_PreApprove** → branch to **Senior_Auditor_Review**
   - **Decision:** Whether the **refund amount is greater than €1,000**
   - **Effect:**
     - If **> €1,000** → proceed to **Senior_Auditor_Review** (must include the **AuditTrail**).
     - If **≤ €1,000** → skip **Senior_Auditor_Review** and continue toward payment.

## Required documents / artefacts (as defined)

- **RefundRequestForm** (completed online by the passenger)
- **FlightManifest** (used by triage to validate eligibility)
- **WeatherReport** (used by triage to validate eligibility)
- **EligibilityChecklist** (signed by **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**)
- **AuditTrail** (must be included in **Senior_Auditor_Review** for high-value claims)
- **BankTransferAuthorization** (signed; payment starts only once it is present)
- **RefundConfirmationPDF** (emailed to the passenger in **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**)

### Decision Points in Contoso Airlines' Flight-Refund Process

1. **Triage_Agent_Validate_Eligibility**: 
   - Decision Point: Determining if the claim is eligible based on the *FlightManifest* and the *WeatherReport*.
   - Result:
     - If eligible, proceeds to the next step.
     - If ineligible, the process ends here.

2. **Finance_Controller_PreApprove**:
   - Decision Point: Reviewing the eligibility based on the *EligibilityChecklist* to decide if the refund should be pre-approved.
   - Result:
     - If pre-approved, determines the value to decide the next step (normal or high-value branch).
     - If not pre-approved, the process ends.

3. **High-value branch** (conditional on the outcome of pre-approval):
   - Decision Point: Review performed by the Senior Auditor on whether high-value claims meet the necessary criteria and audit standards.
   - Result:
     - If approved by Senior Auditor, moves to payment processing.
     - If not approved, the process ends.

4. **Process_Refund_Payment**:
   - Decision Point: N/A; this is a procedural activity dependent on the preceding approvals.

5. **Issue_Confirmation_Letter**:
   - Decision Point: N/A; this is a follow-up activity post successful payment processing.

### Required Documents in the Flight-Refund Process

1. **RefundRequestForm**: Submitted by the customer initiating the refund process.
2. **FlightManifest**: Used by the triage agent to validate eligibility related to the booked flight details.
3. **WeatherReport**: Used by the triage agent to assess any external factors affecting the flight which might impact refund eligibility.
4. **EligibilityChecklist**: Used by the finance controller to determine the eligibility for pre-approval of the refund.

6. **BankTransferAuthorization**: Required to process the refund payment through bank transfer.

The LLM answer fails to provide a complete RACI matrix. It only includes Responsible (R) and Accountable (A) assignments, missing entirely the Consulted (C) and Informed (I) designations that are an essential part of any RACI chart. Moreover, even the R and A assignments it does give are frequently inaccurate when compared to the ground truth (e.g., Compliance Officer should be Responsible for KYC Review, not Accountable; Finance Clerk should be Responsible for Payment Clearance, not Accountable; IT Support should be Responsible for Record Update, not Accountable; and the Account Manager’s dual R/A roles are reduced to a single letter or swapped). The near-total absence of required information and the multiple assignment errors render the answer virtually useless for the intended process-mapping purpose. While the mandated terminology (“KYC Review”, “Order Vetting”, “Payment Clearance”) is used correctly without forbidden terms, this marginal compliance does not compensate for the fundamental structural and content deficiencies.

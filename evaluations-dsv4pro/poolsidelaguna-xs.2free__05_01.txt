The LLM answer places the timer on **Task_Check** instead of **Task_Receive**, which means the 48‑hour clock starts only after “Receive Claim” finishes. This does **not** guarantee that “Triage Claim” is reached within 48 hours of submission, because the initial “Receive Claim” could consume most or all of that window before the timer even starts. The required SLA escalation must begin at the moment of claim submission, so the boundary timer must be attached to the very first task (Task_Receive). The LLM’s suggestion thus fails the core requirement and constitutes a critical design error. The remainder of the answer (bypassing GW_Docs and flowing to Task_Triage) is otherwise sensible but cannot salvage the fundamental misplacement.

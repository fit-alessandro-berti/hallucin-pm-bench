The LLM JSON deviates substantially in top-level schema (processId/processName + sequenceFlows vs. process_id/flows), element attribute names and types (eventDefinition/timerDuration/direction versus GT’s concise “event” keys), and especially the cancel semantics (invented nested eventSubProcess with interrupting startEvent versus GT’s flat intermediateCatchEvent + terminateEndEvent pair). These structural mismatches, plus many extraneous fields and non-canonical IDs, violate the strict “model only what is described” rule and produce a representation that cannot be considered faithful.
